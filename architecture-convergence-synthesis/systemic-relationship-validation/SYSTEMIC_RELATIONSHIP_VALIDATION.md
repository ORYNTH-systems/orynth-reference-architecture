# Systemic Relationship Validation

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to validate relationships between ORYNTH architecture components, graph structures, dependencies, and synthesized reference states.

---

## Relationship Validation Principle

Systemic relationship validation ensures that architecture connections preserve logical consistency, structural coherence, and continuity across the complete ORYNTH architecture model.

A system is validated through the integrity of its relationships.

---

## Validation Domains

Includes:

- architecture relationships;
- dependency alignment;
- graph consistency;
- governance relationships;
- reference continuity;
- transformation pathways;
- invariant preservation;
- synthesis coherence.
