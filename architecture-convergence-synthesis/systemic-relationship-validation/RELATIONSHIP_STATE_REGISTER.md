# Relationship State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track architecture relationships, validation conditions, connection states, and synthesis outcomes.

---

## State Principle

Relationship states preserve connections between architecture entities, dependency structures, validation evidence, and resulting integrated conditions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- relationship identity;
- connected architectures;
- relationship classification;
- dependency condition;
- validation result;
- integrity status;
- synthesis reference;
- continuity condition.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE RELATIONSHIP

↓

CLASSIFY CONNECTION

↓

EVALUATE CONDITION

↓

VALIDATE RELATIONSHIP

↓

REGISTER STATE

↓

MAINTAIN ARCHITECTURAL COHERENCE


---

## Principle

A relationship state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH architecture connections and their validated systemic conditions.
