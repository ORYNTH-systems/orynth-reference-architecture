# Discovery Governance System

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the governance framework for evaluating and integrating discoveries within ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Discovery Governance Principle

Discovery requires governance to ensure emerging insights maintain:

- evidence alignment;
- contextual integrity;
- validation requirements;
- architectural compatibility;
- knowledge continuity.

Discovery may expand understanding without bypassing established governance.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- discovery evaluation;
- hypothesis review;
- evidence assessment;
- validation authority;
- integration approval;
- reference state transition.

---

## Discovery Governance Lifecycle


DISCOVERY IDENTIFIED

↓

EXPLORATION REVIEWED

↓

EVIDENCE ASSESSED

↓

DISCOVERY VALIDATED

↓

GOVERNANCE DECISION

↓

KNOWLEDGE INTEGRATION


---

## Governance Requirements

Discovery governance must preserve:

- discovery origin;
- observation context;
- evidence lineage;
- validation criteria;
- integration decision.

---

## Principle

The Discovery Governance System ensures new understanding enters the architecture through controlled and traceable processes.
