# Discovery Control Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define controls governing discovery activities.

---

## Control Principle

Discovery controls maintain balance between exploration and architectural integrity.

---

## Control Model


DISCOVERY OBSERVATION

PATTERN ANALYSIS

EVIDENCE REVIEW

GOVERNANCE ASSESSMENT

VALIDATION

↓

AUTHORIZED DISCOVERY STATE


---

## Control Categories

### Exploration Control

Defines boundaries for discovery activities.

### Evidence Control

Ensures discoveries maintain supporting evidence.

### Validation Control

Determines discovery reliability.

### Integration Control

Governs transition into architectural knowledge.

### Continuity Control

Preserves discovery history.

---

## Control Factors

Includes:

- novelty assessment;
- evidence quality;
- compatibility;
- validation method;
- architectural impact.

---

## Principle

The Discovery Control Model prevents unsupported assumptions from becoming architectural knowledge.
