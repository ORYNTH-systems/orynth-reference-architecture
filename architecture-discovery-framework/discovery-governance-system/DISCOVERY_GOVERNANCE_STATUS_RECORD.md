# Discovery Governance Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for discovery governance activities.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- discovery identifier;
- originating observation;
- evidence record;
- governance review;
- validation outcome;
- integration decision;
- final status.

---

## Governance Lifecycle


DISCOVERY SUBMITTED

↓

REVIEW INITIATED

↓

EVIDENCE ANALYZED

↓

DECISION RECORDED

↓

DISCOVERY INTEGRATED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Governance States

Possible states:

- SUBMITTED;
- REVIEWING;
- ANALYZING;
- VALIDATING;
- APPROVED;
- INTEGRATED;
- REJECTED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Discovery Governance Status Records preserve accountability between emerging insights and accepted architectural knowledge.
