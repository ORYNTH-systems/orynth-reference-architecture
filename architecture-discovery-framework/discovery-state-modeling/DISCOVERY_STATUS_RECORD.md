# Discovery Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for discovery states.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- discovery identifier;
- originating knowledge state;
- observation source;
- discovery hypothesis;
- evidence relationship;
- validation status;
- final disposition.

---

## Discovery Lifecycle

DISCOVERY IDENTIFIED

↓

OBSERVATION RECORDED

↓

EXPLORATION PERFORMED

↓

PATTERN ASSESSED

↓

DISCOVERY VALIDATED

↓

KNOWLEDGE INTEGRATED

---

## Discovery States

Possible states:

- UNKNOWN;
- OBSERVED;
- EXPLORING;
- HYPOTHESIZED;
- EVALUATING;
- VALIDATED;
- INTEGRATED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Discovery Status Records preserve accountability between emerging insights and established architectural knowledge.
