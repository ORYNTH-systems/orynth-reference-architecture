# Discovery Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structural model for governed discovery processes.

---

## Discovery Principle

Discovery emerges through structured interaction between existing knowledge, observation, exploration, and validation.

---

## Discovery Model

EXISTING KNOWLEDGE STATE

+

OBSERVATION

+

EXPLORATION

+

PATTERN RECOGNITION

+

VALIDATION

↓

NEW KNOWLEDGE POSSIBILITY

---

## Discovery Categories

### Exploratory Discovery

Investigates unknown relationships or possibilities.

### Structural Discovery

Identifies hidden architectural relationships.

### Operational Discovery

Reveals improvement opportunities.

### Knowledge Discovery

Expands understanding.

### Evolutionary Discovery

Identifies future capability pathways.

---

## Discovery Factors

Includes:

- novelty;
- evidence;
- context;
- reproducibility;
- validation pathway.

---

## Principle

The Discovery Model provides a controlled method for expanding architectural understanding.
