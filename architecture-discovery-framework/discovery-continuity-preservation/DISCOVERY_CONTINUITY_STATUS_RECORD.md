# Discovery Continuity Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for discovery continuity preservation.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- discovery identifier;
- originating observation;
- discovery lineage;
- validation history;
- integration history;
- current state;
- future review requirements.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


DISCOVERY RECORD CREATED

↓

ORIGIN LINKED

↓

VALIDATION HISTORY ATTACHED

↓

KNOWLEDGE RELATIONSHIP PRESERVED

↓

CONTINUITY REVIEWED

↓

STATUS MAINTAINED


---

## Continuity States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- PRESERVED;
- VERIFIED;
- INTEGRATED;
- CONTINUING;
- EVOLVING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Discovery Continuity Status Records preserve accountability between emerging discoveries and long-term architectural knowledge.
