# Discovery Continuity Preservation

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework for preserving discovery lineage across future ORYNTH / Morning Star architectural states.

---

## Continuity Principle

A discovery remains meaningful when its relationship to origin, evidence, validation, and integration is preserved.

Discovery continuity maintains:

- discovery origin;
- exploration context;
- evidence lineage;
- governance history;
- knowledge transition;
- future accessibility.

---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- discovery history;
- validation lineage;
- knowledge integration history;
- architectural evolution;
- future discovery reference.

---

## Continuity Requirements

Discovery continuity must preserve:

- original observation;
- reasoning path;
- evidence relationships;
- validation records;
- architectural impact;
- revision history.

---

## Principle

Discovery Continuity Preservation ensures future architectural states remain connected to the discoveries that shaped them.
