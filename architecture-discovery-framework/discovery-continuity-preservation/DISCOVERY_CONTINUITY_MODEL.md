## Purpose

Define the structural model for maintaining discovery continuity.

---

## Continuity Principle

Discoveries evolve through time while maintaining traceable relationships to their originating conditions.

## Continuity Categories

### Origin Continuity

Preserves discovery beginnings.

### Evidence Continuity

Preserves supporting information.

### Validation Continuity

Preserves verification history.

### Knowledge Continuity

Preserves integration relationships.

### Evolution Continuity

Preserves future development paths.

---

## Continuity Factors

Includes:

- traceability;
- historical accuracy;
- semantic preservation;
- validation accessibility;
- future review capability.

---

## Principle

The Discovery Continuity Model ensures discoveries remain understandable across future architectural generations.
