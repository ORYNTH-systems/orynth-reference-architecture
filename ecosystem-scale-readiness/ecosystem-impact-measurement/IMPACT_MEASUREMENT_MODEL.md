# Impact Measurement Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to measure ecosystem effects, outcomes, adoption patterns, and value creation resulting from ORYNTH deployment and expansion.

---

## Impact Principle

Impact measurement preserves the relationship between ecosystem activities, observed outcomes, measurable indicators, and long-term effects.

---

## Impact Domains

Includes:

- technical impact;
- research impact;
- operational impact;
- knowledge impact;
- ecosystem impact.

---

## Impact Lifecycle


DEFINE IMPACT OBJECTIVE

↓

IDENTIFY MEASUREMENT DOMAIN

↓

COLLECT OBSERVATIONS

↓

ANALYZE OUTCOMES

↓

VALIDATE IMPACT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An impact measurement model converts ecosystem activity into traceable evidence of outcomes.
