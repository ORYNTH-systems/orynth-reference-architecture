# Outcome Validation Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ecosystem outcomes and impact assessments are validated and preserved.

---

## Validation Principle

Outcome validation connects measured results with evidence, context, assumptions, and review criteria.

---

## Validation Domains

Includes:

- observed outcomes;
- supporting evidence;
- measurement conditions;
- interpretation;
- validation status.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


IDENTIFY OUTCOME

↓

COLLECT EVIDENCE

↓

ASSESS CONDITIONS

↓

VALIDATE RESULT

↓

DOCUMENT FINDINGS

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

Outcome validation ensures ecosystem impact claims remain evidence-based and traceable.
