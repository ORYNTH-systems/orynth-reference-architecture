# Impact Metric Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure used to register, classify, and maintain ecosystem impact measurements.

---

## Metric Principle

Impact metrics provide consistent references for evaluating progress, outcomes, and ecosystem development.

---

## Metric Domains

Includes:

- adoption metrics;
- performance metrics;
- knowledge metrics;
- collaboration metrics;
- sustainability metrics.

---

## Metric Lifecycle


DEFINE METRIC

↓

DOCUMENT PURPOSE

↓

COLLECT DATA

↓

↓

REVIEW SIGNIFICANCE

↓

UPDATE RECORD

## Principle

An impact metric register provides structured visibility into ecosystem growth and effectiveness.
