# Scaling Capacity Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure used to track operational capacity and expansion requirements.

---

## Capacity Principle

Capacity records preserve visibility into available resources, limitations, dependencies, and scaling conditions.

---

## Capacity Domains

Includes:

- technical capacity;
- operational capacity;
- organizational capacity;
- knowledge capacity;
- governance capacity.
