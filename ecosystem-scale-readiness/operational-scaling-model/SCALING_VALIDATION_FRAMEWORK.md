# Scaling Validation Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the validation process used to confirm that scaling activities preserve ORYNTH system integrity.

---

## Validation Principle

Scaling validation ensures expanded operations remain aligned with architecture, governance, evidence standards, and intended capabilities.

---

## Validation Domains

Includes:

- performance validation;
- architecture validation;
- governance validation;
- operational validation;
- continuity validation.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


DEFINE SCALING OBJECTIVE

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

VERIFY REQUIREMENTS

↓

REVIEW SYSTEM CONDITION

↓

APPROVE SCALE STATE

↓

PRESERVE VALIDATION


---

## Principle

A scaling validation framework ensures growth remains controlled, measurable, and sustainable.
