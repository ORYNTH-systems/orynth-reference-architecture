# Operational Scaling Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to evaluate and manage ORYNTH operational expansion across increasing levels of adoption and complexity.

---

## Scaling Principle

Operational scaling preserves system integrity by expanding capability, capacity, and reach while maintaining governance, validation, and architectural continuity.

---

## Scaling Domains

Includes:

- infrastructure scaling;
- process scaling;
- governance scaling;
- knowledge scaling;
- collaboration scaling.

---

## Scaling Lifecycle


IDENTIFY SCALE REQUIREMENT

↓

ASSESS CURRENT CAPACITY

↓

DEFINE EXPANSION PATH

↓

VALIDATE CONDITIONS

↓

IMPLEMENT SCALE

↓

MONITOR PERFORMANCE


---

## Principle

An operational scaling model enables controlled growth without sacrificing ecosystem coherence.
