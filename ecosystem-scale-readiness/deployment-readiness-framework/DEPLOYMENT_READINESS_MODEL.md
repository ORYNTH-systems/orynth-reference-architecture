# Deployment Readiness Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to evaluate readiness for ORYNTH deployment across operational environments.

---

## Deployment Principle

Deployment readiness requires validated architecture, defined requirements, operational support, governance alignment, and evidence of system preparedness.

---

## Readiness Domains

Includes:

- technical readiness;
- operational readiness;
- governance readiness;
- documentation readiness;
- validation readiness.

---

## Deployment Lifecycle


IDENTIFY DEPLOYMENT TARGET

↓

ASSESS REQUIREMENTS

↓

VERIFY SYSTEM CONDITION

↓

VALIDATE READINESS

↓

AUTHORIZE DEPLOYMENT

↓

MONITOR OPERATION


---

## Principle

A deployment readiness model ensures expansion occurs through controlled and verifiable pathways.
