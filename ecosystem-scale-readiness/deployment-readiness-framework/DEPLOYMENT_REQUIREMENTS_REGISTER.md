# Deployment Requirements Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for deployment requirements and readiness conditions.

---

## Requirements Principle

Deployment requirements preserve clarity regarding necessary capabilities, constraints, dependencies, and validation criteria.

---

## Requirement Domains

Includes:

- infrastructure requirements;
- operational requirements;
- governance requirements;
- security requirements;
- validation requirements.

---

## Requirements Lifecycle


IDENTIFY REQUIREMENT

↓

DOCUMENT CONDITION

↓

ASSESS COMPLETENESS

↓

VERIFY AVAILABILITY

↓

APPROVE READINESS

↓

MAINTAIN RECORD


---

## Principle

A deployment requirements register creates traceability between deployment objectives and required conditions.
