# Knowledge Distribution Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to distribute ORYNTH knowledge assets, research outputs, documentation, and reference materials.

---

## Distribution Principle

Knowledge distribution preserves accessibility, context, accuracy, and traceability while enabling broader ecosystem participation.

---

## Distribution Domains

Includes:

- technical documentation;
- research publications;
- educational materials;
- reference architectures;
- ecosystem resources.

---

## Distribution Lifecycle


IDENTIFY KNOWLEDGE ASSET

↓

PREPARE MATERIAL

↓

VERIFY CONTEXT

↓

PUBLISH RESOURCE

↓

TRACK ACCESS

↓

MAINTAIN VERSION

## Principle

A structured knowledge distribution model enables responsible expansion of ecosystem understanding.
