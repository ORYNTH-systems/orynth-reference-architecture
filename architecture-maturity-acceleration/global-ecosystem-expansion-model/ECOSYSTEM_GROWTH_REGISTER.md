## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track ORYNTH ecosystem growth activities, adoption pathways, and expansion states.

---

## Growth Principle

Growth records preserve expansion context, participating entities, implementation conditions, and resulting ecosystem changes.

- ecosystem participants;
- growth milestones;
- capability expansion;
- regional development.
