# Collaboration Network Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track research relationships, contributors, and collaboration pathways.

---

## Network Principle

Collaboration networks preserve relationship context, contribution history, shared objectives, and research alignment.

---

## Network Domains

Includes:

- collaborator identity;
- research domain;
- contribution type;
- relationship state;
- collaboration history.

---

## Network Lifecycle


REGISTER COLLABORATION

↓

DEFINE RELATIONSHIP

↓

DOCUMENT CONTRIBUTION

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

TRACK EVOLUTION

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A collaboration network register maintains visibility into research ecosystem development.
