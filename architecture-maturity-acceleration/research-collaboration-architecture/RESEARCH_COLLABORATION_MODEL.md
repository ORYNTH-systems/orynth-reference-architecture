Define the framework used to support structured research collaboration across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

Research collaboration enables knowledge exchange, validation, and advancement while preserving attribution, governance, and architectural continuity.

- knowledge exchange;
- validation collaboration;

- discovery pathways.
