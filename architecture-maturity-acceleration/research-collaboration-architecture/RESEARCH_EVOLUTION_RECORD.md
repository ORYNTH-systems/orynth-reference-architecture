# Research Evolution Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how research collaborations, discoveries, and knowledge pathways evolve over time.

---

## Evolution Principle

Research evolution records preserve discoveries, methodological changes, collaboration outcomes, and future research directions.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- research milestones;
- collaboration outcomes;
- knowledge expansion;
- methodological refinement;
- future opportunities.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


CAPTURE RESEARCH EVENT

↓

DOCUMENT DISCOVERY

↓

ASSESS CONTRIBUTION

↓

REFINE KNOWLEDGE PATH

↓

VALIDATE RESULT

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A research evolution record maintains continuity between past discoveries and future research development.
