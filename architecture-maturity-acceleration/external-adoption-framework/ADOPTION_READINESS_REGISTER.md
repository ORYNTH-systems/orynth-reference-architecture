# Adoption Readiness Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to evaluate readiness for external adoption.

---

## Readiness Principle

Adoption readiness records preserve evidence of capability, alignment, requirements, and implementation conditions.

---

## Readiness Domains

Includes:

- organizational readiness;
- technical readiness;
- governance readiness;
- operational readiness;
- validation readiness.

---

## Readiness Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ADOPTION TARGET

↓

ASSESS REQUIREMENTS

↓

EVALUATE CAPABILITY

↓

DOCUMENT GAPS

↓

VERIFY READINESS

↓

UPDATE REGISTER


---

## Principle

An adoption readiness register provides structured visibility into external integration conditions.
