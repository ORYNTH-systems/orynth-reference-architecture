# External Adoption Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to support external adoption of ORYNTH architectural systems while preserving governance, integrity, and implementation alignment.

---

## Adoption Principle

External adoption requires clear architecture understanding, validated implementation pathways, preserved constraints, and measurable alignment with ORYNTH principles.

---

## Adoption Domains

Includes:

- adoption readiness;
- organizational alignment;
- implementation pathways;
- governance requirements;
- operational integration.

---

## Adoption Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ADOPTION CONTEXT

↓

ASSESS READINESS

↓

DEFINE IMPLEMENTATION PATH

↓

VALIDATE ALIGNMENT

↓

SUPPORT DEPLOYMENT

↓

PRESERVE ADOPTION HISTORY


---

## Principle

An external adoption framework enables expansion while preserving architectural identity and operational integrity.
