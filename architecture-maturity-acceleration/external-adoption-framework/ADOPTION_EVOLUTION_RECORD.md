# Adoption Evolution Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how external adoption pathways evolve over time.

---

## Evolution Principle

Adoption evolution records preserve lessons, implementation changes, feedback, and resulting improvements.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- adoption milestones;
- implementation feedback;
- compatibility changes;
- improvement actions;
- future adoption pathways.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


CAPTURE ADOPTION EVENT

↓

DOCUMENT EXPERIENCE

↓

ANALYZE IMPROVEMENT

↓

REFINE APPROACH

↓

VERIFY CHANGE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

An adoption evolution record ensures expansion remains connected to accumulated knowledge and architectural continuity.
