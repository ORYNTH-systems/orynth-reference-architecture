# Deployment Validation Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how deployment outcomes and validation evidence are recorded.

---

## Validation Principle

Deployment validation records preserve evidence that deployed systems meet required architectural, operational, and governance conditions.

---

## Validation Domains

Includes:

- deployment state;
- system behavior;
- integration condition;
- operational outcome;
- corrective actions.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


CAPTURE DEPLOYMENT EVENT

↓

VERIFY SYSTEM STATE

↓

ASSESS OPERATIONAL RESULT

↓

DOCUMENT EVIDENCE

↓

CONFIRM ACCEPTANCE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A deployment validation record maintains traceability between deployment action and verified system state.
