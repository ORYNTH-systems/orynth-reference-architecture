# Deployment Governance Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to govern deployment of ORYNTH architectural systems while preserving security, integrity, traceability, and operational alignment.

---

## Deployment Principle

Deployment governance ensures that system releases occur through controlled pathways with defined requirements, validation conditions, and preserved evidence.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- deployment authorization;
- environment readiness;
- operational controls;
- validation requirements;
- post-deployment review.

---

## Deployment Lifecycle
ASSESS DEPLOYMENT REQUIREMENT

↓

VERIFY ENVIRONMENT READINESS

↓

AUTHORIZE DEPLOYMENT

↓

EXECUTE DEPLOYMENT

↓

VALIDATE RESULT

↓

PRESERVE DEPLOYMENT RECORD


---

## Principle

A deployment governance model enables scalable adoption while maintaining architectural integrity.
