# Synchronized Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track synchronized intelligence states, alignment conditions, operational relationships, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Synchronized intelligence states preserve relationships between integrated intelligence systems, timing conditions, governance mechanisms, and resulting coherent configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- synchronization identity;
- intelligence component states;
- alignment conditions;
- architecture state;
- governance condition;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.
