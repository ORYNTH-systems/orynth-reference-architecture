## Purpose

Define the framework used to govern evolutionary intelligence architectures through recursive observation, transformation evaluation, and continuity preservation.

## Governance Principle

Recursive Evolutionary Intelligence Governance enables intelligence architectures to evaluate their own evolutionary transitions while preserving identity, integrity, and architectural continuity.

Evolution requires recursive governance to distinguish advancement from uncontrolled divergence.
