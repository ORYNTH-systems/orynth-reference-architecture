# Convergent Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track convergent intelligence states, integrated relationships, governance conditions, and architectural lineage.

---

## State Principle

Convergent intelligence states preserve relationships between intelligence layers, architectural structures, governance mechanisms, and resulting unified configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- convergence identity;
- intelligence layer states;
- architectural state;
- governance condition;
- integration status;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.
