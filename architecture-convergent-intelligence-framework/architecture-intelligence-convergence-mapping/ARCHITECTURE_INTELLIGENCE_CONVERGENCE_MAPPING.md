## Purpose

Define the framework used to map convergence relationships between ORYNTH architecture systems and intelligence structures.

---

## Convergence Principle

Architecture–Intelligence Convergence Mapping represents architecture and intelligence as interconnected domains that converge through validated relationships, shared operational context, and preserved identity.

Convergence enables coordinated capability without eliminating structural boundaries.
