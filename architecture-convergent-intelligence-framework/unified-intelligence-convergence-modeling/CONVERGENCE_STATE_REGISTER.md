# Intelligence Convergence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track unified intelligence convergence states, relationships, validation conditions, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Convergence states preserve relationships between intelligence domains, architecture contexts, governance conditions, and resulting unified intelligence configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- convergence identity;
- intelligence domains;
- relationship mappings;
- integration conditions;
- validation status;
- resulting intelligence state;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE CONVERGENCE RECORD

↓

CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE STATES

↓

MAP DOMAIN RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ASSESS CONVERGENCE CONDITION

↓

VALIDATE RESULT

↓

REGISTER CONVERGENCE STATE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A convergence state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence domains and their unified representations.
