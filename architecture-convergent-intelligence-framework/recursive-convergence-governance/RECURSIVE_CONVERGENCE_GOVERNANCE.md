## Purpose

Define the framework used to govern convergence states across ORYNTH intelligence domains, architecture structures, and continuity systems.

---

## Governance Principle

Recursive Convergence Governance enables convergence states to continuously evaluate, refine, and preserve themselves through recursive governance cycles.

Governance maintains convergence through structured observation, evaluation, validation, and controlled transformation.
