# Convergence Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track recursive convergence governance states, transitions, validation conditions, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Governance states preserve relationships between convergent intelligence states, architecture structures, recursive decisions, and resulting system configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- convergence state;
- architecture context;
- intelligence context;
- recursive evaluation;
- transition decision;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE GOVERNANCE RECORD

↓

CAPTURE CONVERGENCE CONDITION

↓

MAP ARCHITECTURE–INTELLIGENCE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ANALYZE RECURSIVE EFFECTS

↓

VALIDATE GOVERNANCE TRANSITION

↓

REGISTER CONVERGENCE GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY

## Principle

A convergence governance state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH convergent intelligence states and their recursive governance processes.
