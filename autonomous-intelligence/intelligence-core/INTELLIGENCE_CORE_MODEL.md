# Intelligence Core Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the foundational architecture through which ORYNTH intelligence systems organize information, evaluate conditions, and generate structured understanding.

---

## Intelligence Core Principle

Intelligence emerges from the coordinated processing of information, context, relationships, reasoning, and feedback.

---

## Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- perception;
- interpretation;
- analysis;
- synthesis;
- evaluation;
- adaptation.

---

## Intelligence Lifecycle


RECEIVE INFORMATION

↓

PROCESS CONTEXT

↓

ANALYZE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

GENERATE UNDERSTANDING

↓

EVALUATE RESULT

↓

UPDATE INTELLIGENCE STATE


---

## Principle

The intelligence core provides the foundation for adaptive analysis while preserving traceability and governance.
