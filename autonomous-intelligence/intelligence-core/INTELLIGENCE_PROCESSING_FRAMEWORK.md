# Intelligence Processing Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how intelligence inputs are transformed into evaluated outputs.

---

## Processing Principle

Intelligence processing converts observations and knowledge structures into actionable understanding through controlled pathways.

---

## Processing Stages

Includes:

- input acquisition;
- contextualization;
- reasoning;
- synthesis;
- evaluation;
- output generation.

---

## Processing Lifecycle


COLLECT INPUT

↓

NORMALIZE INFORMATION

↓

APPLY REASONING

↓

GENERATE ANALYSIS

↓

VERIFY OUTPUT

↓

PRESERVE RESULT


---

## Principle

Structured intelligence processing maintains consistency between information, reasoning, and outcomes.
