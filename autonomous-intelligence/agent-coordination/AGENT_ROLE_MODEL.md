# Agent Role Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how autonomous intelligence roles are represented and governed.

---

## Role Principle

Clear roles establish responsibility boundaries and preserve accountability within distributed intelligence systems.

---

## Role Categories

Includes:

- analysis role;
- coordination role;
- verification role;
- learning role;
- governance role.

---

## Role Lifecycle


DEFINE ROLE

↓

ASSIGN FUNCTION

↓

PERFORM RESPONSIBILITY

↓

EVALUATE PERFORMANCE

↓

UPDATE ROLE STATE


---

## Principle

Explicit roles preserve clarity and accountability across autonomous intelligence structures.
