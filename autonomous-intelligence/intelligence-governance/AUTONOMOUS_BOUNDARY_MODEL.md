# Autonomous Boundary Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define limits and constraints that govern autonomous intelligence operation.

---

## Boundary Principle

Autonomous systems require explicit boundaries to preserve safety, alignment, and predictable behavior.

---

## Boundary Domains

Includes:

- authority boundaries;
- operational boundaries;
- knowledge boundaries;
- decision boundaries;
- adaptation boundaries.

---

## Boundary Lifecycle


DEFINE LIMITS

↓

EVALUATE REQUEST

↓

CHECK CONDITIONS

↓

ALLOW OR RESTRICT

↓

MONITOR RESULT

↓

UPDATE RECORD


---

## Principle

Clear autonomous boundaries enable capability expansion while preserving governance integrity.
