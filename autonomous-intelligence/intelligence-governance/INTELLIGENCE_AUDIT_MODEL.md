# Intelligence Audit Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH intelligence processes, decisions, and outcomes are reviewed for transparency and accountability.

---

## Audit Principle

Intelligence auditing preserves traceability between inputs, reasoning, decisions, actions, and outcomes.

---

## Audit Domains

Includes:

- input review;
- reasoning review;
- decision review;
- execution review;
- outcome review.

---

## Audit Lifecycle


CAPTURE ACTIVITY

↓

COLLECT EVIDENCE

↓

RECONSTRUCT PROCESS

↓

EVALUATE ALIGNMENT

↓

DOCUMENT FINDINGS

↓

PRESERVE AUDIT HISTORY


---

## Principle

Intelligence auditing maintains accountability throughout autonomous operation.
