# Intelligence Governance Model

Version:

v1.0.0

## Purpose

Define the governance structures that maintain alignment, accountability, and controlled evolution of ORYNTH autonomous intelligence systems.

---

## Intelligence Governance Principle

Autonomous intelligence requires defined principles, boundaries, evaluation pathways, and accountability structures.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- intelligence oversight;
- operational boundaries;
- decision accountability;
- change management;
- audit continuity.
