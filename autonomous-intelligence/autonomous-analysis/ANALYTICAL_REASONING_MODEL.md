# Analytical Reasoning Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how autonomous analytical processes evaluate information and generate conclusions.

---

## Analytical Reasoning Principle

Analytical reasoning connects evidence, relationships, constraints, and objectives into evaluated understanding.

---

## Reasoning Factors

Includes:

- evidence quality;
- contextual relevance;
- causal relationships;
- constraints;
- potential outcomes.

---

## Analytical Reasoning Lifecycle


DEFINE QUESTION

↓

GATHER INFORMATION

↓

ANALYZE VARIABLES

↓

EVALUATE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

FORM CONCLUSION

↓

REVIEW ACCURACY


---

## Principle

Analytical reasoning provides structured pathways from information to understanding.
