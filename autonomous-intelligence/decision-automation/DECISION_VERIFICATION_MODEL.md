# Decision Verification Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how automated decisions are reviewed, validated, and verified.

---

## Verification Principle

Decision verification ensures selected actions remain consistent with requirements, constraints, and available evidence.

---

## Verification Domains

Includes:

- input verification;
- reasoning verification;
- constraint verification;
- outcome verification.

---

## Verification Lifecycle


CAPTURE DECISION

↓

VERIFY INPUTS

↓

REVIEW LOGIC

↓

ASSESS CONDITIONS

↓

CONFIRM RESULT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Decision verification maintains trust by connecting automated choices to evidence and evaluation.
