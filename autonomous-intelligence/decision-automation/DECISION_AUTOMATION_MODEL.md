# Decision Automation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH intelligence systems evaluate conditions and support automated decision pathways.

---

## Decision Automation Principle

Automated decisions require structured evaluation, defined constraints, verified inputs, and traceable outcomes.

---

## Decision Domains

Includes:

- condition evaluation;
- option assessment;
- action selection;
- constraint verification;
- outcome review.

---

## Decision Automation Lifecycle


IDENTIFY OBJECTIVE

↓

COLLECT CONDITIONS

↓

EVALUATE OPTIONS

↓

SELECT ACTION

↓

VERIFY EXECUTION

↓

REVIEW OUTCOME


---

## Principle

Decision automation extends operational capability while preserving governance and accountability.
