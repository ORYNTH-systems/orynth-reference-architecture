# Action Selection Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates possible actions and selects appropriate pathways.

---

## Action Selection Principle

Actions are selected through evaluation of objectives, evidence, constraints, and expected consequences.

---

## Selection Factors

Includes:

- objective alignment;
- available information;
- system state;
- risk conditions;
- expected outcomes.

---

## Action Selection Lifecycle


DEFINE AVAILABLE ACTIONS

↓

EVALUATE CONDITIONS

↓

COMPARE OPTIONS

↓

SELECT PATHWAY

↓

EXECUTE OR DEFER

↓

REVIEW RESULT


---

## Principle

Structured action selection prevents uncontrolled decisions and preserves alignment.
