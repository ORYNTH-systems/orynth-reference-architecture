# Optimization Metric Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the metrics used to evaluate architectural improvement.

---

## Metric Principle

Optimization metrics must identify meaningful improvement while maintaining alignment with architectural objectives.

---

---

## Metric Categories

### Efficiency Metrics

Measures reduction of unnecessary cost, effort, or complexity.

### Reliability Metrics

Measures consistency and operational stability.

### Integrity Metrics

Measures preservation of correctness and trust.

### Adaptability Metrics

Measures ability to respond to changing conditions.

### Continuity Metrics

Measures preservation across architectural evolution.

---

## Metric Factors

Includes:

- baseline definition;
- measurement method;
- comparison criteria;
- improvement threshold;
- evidence quality.

---

## Principle

The Optimization Metric Model ensures improvements are demonstrated rather than assumed.
