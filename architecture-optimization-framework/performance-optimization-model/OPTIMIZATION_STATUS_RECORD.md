# Optimization Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for performance optimization activities.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- optimization identifier;
- affected architecture state;
- performance baseline;
- proposed improvement;
- evidence source;
- validation result;
- final status.

---

## Optimization Lifecycle


OPTIMIZATION IDENTIFIED

↓

BASELINE RECORDED

↓

IMPROVEMENT DESIGNED

↓

CHANGE EVALUATED

↓

OPTIMIZATION VERIFIED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Optimization States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- ANALYZING;
- PROPOSED;
- IMPLEMENTING;
- VALIDATING;
- OPTIMIZED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Optimization Status Records preserve accountability between architectural changes and demonstrated performance improvement.
