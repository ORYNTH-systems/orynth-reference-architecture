# Refinement Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for evidence-driven refinement activities.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- refinement identifier;
- originating evidence;
- affected architecture component;
- proposed improvement;
- review authority;
- validation result;
- final disposition.

---

## Refinement Lifecycle


REFINEMENT IDENTIFIED

↓

EVIDENCE REVIEWED

↓

CHANGE PROPOSED

↓

VALIDATION PERFORMED

↓

REFINEMENT ACCEPTED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Refinement States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- REVIEWING;
- PROPOSED;
- APPROVED;
- VALIDATING;
- ACCEPTED;
- REJECTED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Refinement Status Records preserve accountability between evidence, decisions, and architectural evolution.
