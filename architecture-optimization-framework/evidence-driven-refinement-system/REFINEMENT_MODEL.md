# Refinement Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structural model for evidence-based architectural refinement.

---

## Refinement Principle

A refinement is an intentional improvement supported by demonstrated evidence.

---

## Refinement Model


OBSERVED CONDITION

EVIDENCE COLLECTION

ANALYSIS

IMPROVEMENT DECISION

VALIDATION

↓

REFINED ARCHITECTURE STATE


---

## Refinement Categories

### Corrective Refinement

Addresses identified deficiencies.

### Performance Refinement

Improves measured capability.

### Structural Refinement

Improves architecture organization.

### Knowledge Refinement

Improves understanding and interpretation.

### Evolution Refinement

Supports governed future capability.

---

## Refinement Factors

Includes:

- evidence strength;
- impact assessment;
- compatibility;
- governance approval;
- validation outcome.

---

## Principle

The Refinement Model ensures improvement remains evidence-connected and architecturally coherent.
