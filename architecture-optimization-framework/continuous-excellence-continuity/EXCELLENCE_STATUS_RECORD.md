# Excellence Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for continuous excellence tracking.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- excellence identifier;
- architecture state;
- optimization history;
- refinement records;
- performance indicators;
- validation status;
- continuity assessment.

---

## Excellence Lifecycle


EXCELLENCE STATE CREATED

↓

PERFORMANCE TRACKED

↓

IMPROVEMENTS APPLIED

↓

RESULTS VALIDATED

↓

MATURITY ASSESSED

↓

CONTINUITY PRESERVED


---

## Excellence States

Possible states:

- ESTABLISHED;
- OPTIMIZING;
- MATURING;
- SUSTAINED;
- EVOLVING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Excellence Status Records preserve the relationship between continuous improvement and long-term architectural maturity.
