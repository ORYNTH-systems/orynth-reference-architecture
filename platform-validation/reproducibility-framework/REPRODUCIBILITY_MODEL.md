# Reproducibility Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH validation results, system behaviors, and research outputs can be independently reconstructed and evaluated.

---

## Reproducibility Principle

Reproducibility requires preservation of methods, inputs, conditions, evidence, and procedures necessary to recreate results.

---

## Reproducibility Domains

Includes:

- methodological reproducibility;
- evidence reproducibility;
- procedural reproducibility;
- operational reproducibility;
- analytical reproducibility.

---

## Reproducibility Lifecycle


DEFINE METHOD

↓

PRESERVE CONDITIONS

↓

DOCUMENT PROCESS

↓

RECREATE EXECUTION

↓

COMPARE RESULTS

↓

ARCHIVE FINDINGS


---

## Principle

Reproducibility transforms system claims into independently reviewable processes.
