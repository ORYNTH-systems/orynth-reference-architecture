# Result Reconstruction Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH results are reconstructed through preserved inputs, processes, and decision pathways.

---

## Reconstruction Principle

Result reconstruction restores the relationship between initial conditions, system activity, and final outcomes.

---

## Reconstruction Elements

Includes:

- input conditions;
- system state;
- processing pathway;
- evaluation criteria;
- final result.

---

## Reconstruction Lifecycle


COLLECT RECORDS

↓

RESTORE CONDITIONS

↓

TRACE PROCESS

↓

COMPARE OUTPUT

↓

VERIFY RESULT

↓

PRESERVE RECONSTRUCTION


---

## Principle

Result reconstruction enables transparent evaluation of complex system behavior.
