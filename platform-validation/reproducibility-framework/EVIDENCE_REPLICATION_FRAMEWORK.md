# Evidence Replication Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evidence chains are preserved and replicated for validation purposes.

---

## Evidence Replication Principle

Evidence replication ensures that validation conclusions remain connected to traceable and reviewable sources.

---

## Evidence Domains

Includes:

- source evidence;
- execution evidence;
- evaluation evidence;
- outcome evidence;
- historical evidence.

---

## Evidence Replication Lifecycle


IDENTIFY EVIDENCE

↓

PRESERVE SOURCE

↓

RECREATE CONDITIONS

↓

VERIFY MATCH

↓

ASSESS DIFFERENCE

↓

RECORD RESULT


---

## Principle

Replicated evidence strengthens confidence in validation outcomes.
