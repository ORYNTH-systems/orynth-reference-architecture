# Certification Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the certification structure used to recognize validated ORYNTH capabilities, systems, and architectures.

---

## Certification Principle

Certification represents a formal determination that defined requirements have been satisfied through documented validation evidence.

---

## Certification Domains

Includes:

- architecture certification;
- capability certification;
- operational certification;
- governance certification;
- reproducibility certification.

---

## Certification Lifecycle


DEFINE CERTIFICATION SCOPE

↓

ESTABLISH REQUIREMENTS

↓

REVIEW VALIDATION EVIDENCE

↓

EVALUATE CONDITIONS

↓

ISSUE DETERMINATION

↓

PRESERVE CERTIFICATION RECORD


---

## Principle

Certification creates a durable relationship between system capability, evidence, and recognized status.
