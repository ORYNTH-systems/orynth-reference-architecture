# Certification Requirements

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the requirements necessary for ORYNTH systems to achieve certification status.

---

## Requirement Principle

Certification requires demonstrated alignment between architecture, operation, evidence, and governance conditions.

---

## Requirement Domains

Includes:

- documented architecture;
- validated functionality;
- available evidence;
- operational controls;
- governance alignment.

---

RECORD STATUS
