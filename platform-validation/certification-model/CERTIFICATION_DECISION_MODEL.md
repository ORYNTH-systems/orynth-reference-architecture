# Certification Decision Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how certification decisions are evaluated, determined, and recorded.

---

## Decision Principle

Certification decisions must be based on verified requirements, available evidence, and defined evaluation criteria.

---

## Decision Factors

Includes:

- validation results;
- evidence quality;
- requirement satisfaction;
- identified limitations;
- operational readiness.

---

## Decision Lifecycle


COLLECT EVIDENCE

↓

EVALUATE REQUIREMENTS

↓

ASSESS CONDITIONS

↓

DETERMINE STATUS

↓

DOCUMENT RATIONALE

↓

ARCHIVE DECISION


---

## Principle

A structured certification decision process preserves transparency and accountability.
