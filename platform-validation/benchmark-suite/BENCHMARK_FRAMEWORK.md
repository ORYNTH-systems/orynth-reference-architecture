# Benchmark Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH capabilities are measured, compared, and evaluated through structured benchmarks.

---

## Benchmark Principle

Benchmarks provide repeatable measurements that connect system capability, evaluation criteria, and observed performance.

---

## Benchmark Domains

Includes:

- capability measurement;
- operational performance;
- validation comparison;
- improvement tracking;
- system evolution.

---

## Benchmark Lifecycle


DEFINE OBJECTIVE

↓

ESTABLISH METRICS

↓

EXECUTE EVALUATION

↓

COLLECT RESULTS

↓

ANALYZE PERFORMANCE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Structured benchmarks provide measurable evidence of system capability and progression.
