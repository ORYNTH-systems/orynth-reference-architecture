# Capability Measurement Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH capabilities are measured across integrated systems and operational contexts.

---

## Measurement Principle

Capability measurement evaluates whether systems achieve intended functions under defined conditions.

---

## Measurement Domains

Includes:

- functional capability;
- analytical capability;
- operational capability;
- governance capability;
- adaptive capability.

---

## Measurement Lifecycle


DEFINE CAPABILITY

↓

SELECT METRICS

↓

COLLECT OBSERVATIONS

↓

EVALUATE PERFORMANCE

↓

COMPARE RESULTS

↓

UPDATE MODEL


---

## Principle

Capability measurement transforms system potential into observable evidence.
