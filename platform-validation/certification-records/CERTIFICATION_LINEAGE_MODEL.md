# Certification Lineage Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH certification states maintain connection across versions, revisions, and system evolution.

---

## Lineage Principle

Certification lineage preserves the relationship between original validation, subsequent changes, and current certification status.

---

## Lineage Elements

Includes:

- origin certification;
- version history;
- modifications;
- validation updates;
- successor relationships.

---

## Lineage Lifecycle


ESTABLISH ORIGIN

↓

TRACK CHANGE

↓

REVALIDATE CONDITION

↓

UPDATE CERTIFICATION

↓

CONNECT HISTORY

↓

PRESERVE LINEAGE


---

## Principle

Certification lineage maintains continuity as systems evolve over time.
