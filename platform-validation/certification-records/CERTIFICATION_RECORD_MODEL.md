# Certification Record Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH certification decisions, evidence, and status records are preserved.

---

## Certification Record Principle

Certification records maintain a durable connection between validated capability, supporting evidence, decision authority, and certification status.

---

## Record Components

Includes:

- certification scope;
- validation evidence;
- evaluation results;
- certification decision;
- limitations;
- historical status.

---

## Certification Record Lifecycle


CREATE RECORD

↓

ATTACH EVIDENCE

↓

DOCUMENT EVALUATION

↓

ISSUE CERTIFICATION

↓

UPDATE STATUS

↓

ARCHIVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

Certification records preserve institutional memory and enable future verification.
