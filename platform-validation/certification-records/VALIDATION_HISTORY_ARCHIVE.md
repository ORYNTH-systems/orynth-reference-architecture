# Validation History Archive

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH validation activities and historical evaluations are preserved.

---

## Archive Principle

Validation history provides a chronological record of assessments, changes, findings, and improvements.

---

## Archive Domains

Includes:

- validation events;
- system revisions;
- evaluation outcomes;
- corrective actions;
- certification milestones.

---

## Archive Lifecycle


CAPTURE VALIDATION EVENT

↓

STORE EVIDENCE

↓

CONNECT TIMELINE

↓

TRACK EVOLUTION

↓

ENABLE REVIEW

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

Historical validation records support transparency and continuous improvement.
