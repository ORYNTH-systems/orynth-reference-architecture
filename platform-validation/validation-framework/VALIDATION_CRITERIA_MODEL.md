# Validation Criteria Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the criteria used to determine whether ORYNTH systems meet validation requirements.

---

## Criteria Principle

Validation criteria provide measurable conditions for evaluating system completeness, correctness, and readiness.

---

## Criteria Domains

Includes:

- structural completeness;
- functional correctness;
- evidence availability;
- operational stability;
- governance alignment.

---

## Criteria Lifecycle


DEFINE CRITERIA

↓

APPLY EVALUATION

↓

MEASURE RESULTS

↓

COMPARE REQUIREMENTS

↓

DETERMINE STATUS

↓

MAINTAIN CRITERIA HISTORY


---

## Principle

Clear validation criteria create transparent pathways from evaluation to certification.
