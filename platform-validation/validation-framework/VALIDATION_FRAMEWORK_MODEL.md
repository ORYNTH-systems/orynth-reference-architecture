# Validation Framework Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to validate ORYNTH systems, capabilities, and integrated architectures.

---

## Validation Principle

Validation establishes whether a system satisfies defined requirements through evidence, evaluation, and repeatable assessment.

---

## Validation Domains

Includes:

- architectural validation;
- functional validation;
- operational validation;
- governance validation;
- evidence validation.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


DEFINE REQUIREMENTS

↓

ESTABLISH CRITERIA

↓

COLLECT EVIDENCE

↓

PERFORM VALIDATION

↓

EVALUATE RESULTS

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A structured validation framework transforms system claims into measurable and reviewable evidence.
