# Continuity Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for autonomous continuity preservation.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- continuity identifier;
- source architecture state;
- current architecture state;
- transition history;
- preserved properties;
- validation results;
- continuity status.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


CONTINUITY CHECK INITIATED

↓

STATE RELATIONSHIP ANALYZED

↓

PRESERVATION VERIFIED

↓

STATUS ASSIGNED

↓

RECORD PRESERVED


---

## Continuity States

Possible states:

- PRESERVED;
- VERIFIED;
- CONDITIONAL;
- REVIEW REQUIRED;
- DISCONTINUOUS.

---

## Principle

Continuity Status Records maintain evidence that autonomous architecture operation remains connected to its original validated foundation.
