# Continuity Automation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the automated mechanisms for preserving architectural continuity.

---

## Automation Principle

Continuity automation continuously verifies that autonomous state changes remain connected to approved reference states.

---

## Automation Model


STATE CHANGE DETECTED

↓

COMPARE WITH REFERENCE STATE

↓

VERIFY PRESERVED PROPERTIES

↓

VALIDATE TRANSITION

↓

UPDATE CONTINUITY RECORD

↓

MAINTAIN ARCHITECTURAL LINEAGE


---

## Automated Preservation Functions

Includes:

- lineage tracking;
- state comparison;
- evidence association;
- transition validation;
- historical recording.

---

## Continuity Decisions

Possible outcomes:

### Preserved

State maintains required relationships.

### Conditional

Additional validation required.

### Restricted

Transition limited by constraints.

### Broken

Continuity requirements not satisfied.

---

## Principle

The Continuity Automation Model ensures autonomous evolution remains traceable and governed.
