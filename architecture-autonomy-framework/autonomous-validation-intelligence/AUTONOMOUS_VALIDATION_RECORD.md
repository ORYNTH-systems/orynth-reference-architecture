# Autonomous Validation Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for autonomous validation operations.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- validation identifier;
- evaluated state;
- validation criteria;
- evidence references;
- generated decision;
- confidence condition;
- verification status;
- historical record.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


VALIDATION INITIATED

↓

CRITERIA EVALUATED

↓

EVIDENCE REVIEWED

↓

DECISION GENERATED

↓

RESULT VERIFIED

↓

RECORD PRESERVED


---

## Validation States

Possible states:

- INITIATED;
- ANALYZING;
- VALIDATED;
- CONDITIONALLY VALIDATED;
- REVIEW REQUIRED;
- INVALIDATED.

---

## Principle

Autonomous Validation Records preserve the reasoning and evidence behind self-governed architectural decisions.
