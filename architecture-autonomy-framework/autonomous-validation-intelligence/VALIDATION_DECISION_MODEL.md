# Validation Decision Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the decision structure used for autonomous validation outcomes.

---

## Decision Principle

A validation result is determined through comparison between observed conditions, required conditions, and supporting evidence.

---

## Decision Model


OBSERVED ARCHITECTURE STATE

VALIDATION CRITERIA

EVIDENCE RECORDS

↓

AUTONOMOUS ANALYSIS

↓

VALIDATION RESULT


---

## Decision Categories

### Validated

Architecture condition satisfies requirements.

### Conditionally Validated

Requirements satisfied with limitations.

### Requires Review

Insufficient evidence or unresolved condition.

### Invalidated

Requirements not satisfied.

---

## Decision Factors

Includes:

- evidence sufficiency;
- constraint compliance;
- governance status;
- continuity impact;
- reliability condition.

---

## Principle

The Validation Decision Model ensures autonomous judgments remain structured and auditable.
