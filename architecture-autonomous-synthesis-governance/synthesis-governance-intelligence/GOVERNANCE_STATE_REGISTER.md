# Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track synthesis governance states, evaluation conditions, decisions, and validation outcomes.

---

## State Principle

Governance states preserve relationships between synthesized architecture conditions, analytical evaluation, governance decisions, and resulting system states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- synthesis source state;
- evaluation condition;
- constraint assessment;
- decision classification;
- validation status;
- resulting architecture state;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE GOVERNANCE RECORD

↓

CAPTURE SYNTHESIS CONDITION

↓

ANALYZE GOVERNANCE FACTORS

↓

EVALUATE TRANSITION

↓

VALIDATE DECISION

↓

REGISTER GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

PRESERVE GOVERNANCE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A governance state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH synthesis processes and their controlled governance decisions.
