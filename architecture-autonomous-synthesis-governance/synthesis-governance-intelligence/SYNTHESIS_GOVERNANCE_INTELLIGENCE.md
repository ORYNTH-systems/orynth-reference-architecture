# Synthesis Governance Intelligence

Version:

v1.0.0

## Purpose

Define the framework used to govern synthesized architecture states through intelligence-driven evaluation, validation, and controlled decision processes.

---

## Governance Intelligence Principle

Synthesis governance intelligence evaluates architecture synthesis conditions while preserving reference integrity, provenance, constraints, and continuity.

Governance determines admissible evolution.

---

## Governance Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- synthesis state evaluation;
- architecture relationship analysis;
- transition assessment;
- constraint verification;
- validation intelligence;
- reference alignment;
- risk classification;
- governance decision support.
