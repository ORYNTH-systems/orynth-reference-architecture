# Adaptive Transition Management

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to manage transitions between ORYNTH synthesized architecture states through governed adaptation.

---

## Transition Management Principle

Adaptive transition management ensures architecture changes occur through validated pathways while preserving identity, dependencies, provenance, and reference continuity.

Transition is governed transformation.

---

## Transition Domains

Includes:

- synthesis state transitions;
- dependency evaluation;
- architecture continuity;
- governance approval;
- validation conditions;
- rollback awareness;
- state preservation;
- evolutionary readiness.

---

↓

↓
