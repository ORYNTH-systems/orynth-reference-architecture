# Continuous Synthesis Governance Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to provide continuous governance oversight across ORYNTH autonomous synthesis processes.

---

## Governance Continuity Principle

Continuous synthesis governance maintains alignment between architecture evolution, synthesis activity, transition conditions, and reference integrity over time.

Governance is a continuous architectural function.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- synthesis oversight;
- autonomous transition review;
- architecture state management;
- emergent structure evaluation;
- constraint preservation;
- reference continuity;
- validation cycles;
- long-term synthesis stewardship.

## Governance Constraints

Continuous synthesis governance preserves:

- architecture identity;
- semantic integrity;
- provenance;
- dependency relationships;
- uncertainty states;
- validation requirements;
- reference continuity.

---

## Principle

The Continuous Synthesis Governance Model enables ORYNTH architecture to evolve through autonomous synthesis while maintaining controlled governance, traceability, and structural coherence.
