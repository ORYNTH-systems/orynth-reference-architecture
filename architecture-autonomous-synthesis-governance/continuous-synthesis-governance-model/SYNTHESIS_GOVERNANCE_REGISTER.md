# Synthesis Governance Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track continuous synthesis governance states, evaluations, decisions, and historical conditions.

---

## State Principle

Governance states preserve relationships between synthesis activity, architecture conditions, governance evaluations, and resulting decisions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- synthesis condition;
- architecture state;
- evaluation process;
- decision outcome;
- validation status;
- continuity assessment;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

CAPTURE SYNTHESIS CONDITION

↓

EVALUATE ARCHITECTURE IMPACT

↓

VERIFY GOVERNANCE REQUIREMENTS

↓

RECORD GOVERNANCE DECISION

↓

UPDATE REFERENCE STATE

↓

PRESERVE GOVERNANCE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A synthesis governance register maintains traceability between autonomous synthesis activity and continuous governance oversight.
