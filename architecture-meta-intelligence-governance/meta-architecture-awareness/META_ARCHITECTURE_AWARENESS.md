## Purpose

Define the framework used to establish awareness of ORYNTH architecture as an interconnected system of architectures, intelligence layers, governance structures, and evolutionary processes.

---

## Awareness Principle

Meta-architecture awareness enables ORYNTH to understand relationships between its architectural components, identify systemic conditions, and preserve coherence across multiple intelligence and governance layers.

Awareness exists through relationship understanding.
