# Governance Coordination State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track governance coordination states, intelligence relationships, alignment conditions, and validated outcomes.

---

## State Principle

Governance coordination states preserve relationships between intelligence domains, governance processes, coordinated decisions, and resulting systemic conditions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- coordination identity;
- intelligence source domains;
- governance relationships;
- decision conditions;
- validation status;
- coordinated outcome;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE COORDINATION RECORD

↓

CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE CONDITIONS

↓

MAP GOVERNANCE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ANALYZE COORDINATION NEEDS

↓

VALIDATE ALIGNMENT

↓

REGISTER COORDINATED STATE

↓

PRESERVE GOVERNANCE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A governance coordination state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence domains and their coordinated governance states.
