# Presence Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for operational presence states.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- presence identifier;
- embodiment state;
- operating environment;
- resource conditions;
- interaction pathways;
- governance status;
- continuity state.

---

## Presence Lifecycle


PRESENCE RECORD CREATED

↓

ENVIRONMENT LINKED

↓

RESOURCES VERIFIED

↓

OPERATIONAL STATE ACTIVATED

↓

INTERACTIONS MONITORED

↓

PRESENCE HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Presence States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- INITIALIZING;
- AVAILABLE;
- ACTIVE;
- ADAPTING;
- MAINTAINING;
- SUSPENDED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Presence Status Records preserve accountability between embodied architecture and operational existence.
