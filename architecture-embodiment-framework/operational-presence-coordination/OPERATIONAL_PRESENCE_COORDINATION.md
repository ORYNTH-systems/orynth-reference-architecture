## Purpose

Define the framework for coordinating operational presence within ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Presence Principle

Operational presence represents the maintained relationship between an embodied architecture and the environments in which it exists.

Presence coordination manages:

- availability;
- interaction pathways;
- operational state;
- environmental relationship;
- resource alignment.

---

## Presence Domains

Includes:

- system availability;
- interaction channels;
- operational context;
- environmental awareness;
- resource coordination;
- presence continuity.
