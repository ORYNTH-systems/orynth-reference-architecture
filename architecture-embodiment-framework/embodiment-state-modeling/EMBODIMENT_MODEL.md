# Embodiment Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structural model for architectural embodiment.

---

## Embodiment Principle

Embodiment occurs when governed agency becomes represented as a persistent operational entity capable of maintaining identity and interaction.

---

## Embodiment Model

---

## Embodiment Categories

### Digital Embodiment

Represents architecture through computational systems.

### Operational Embodiment

Represents architecture through active processes.

### Institutional Embodiment

Represents architecture through organizational structures.

### Environmental Embodiment

Represents architecture through interaction with external conditions.

### Evolutionary Embodiment

Represents architecture through long-term adaptation.

---

## Embodiment Factors

Includes:

- identity persistence;
- operational availability;
- environmental relationship;
- governance alignment;
- continuity preservation.

---

## Principle

The Embodiment Model ensures architecture manifests without losing its defining identity.
