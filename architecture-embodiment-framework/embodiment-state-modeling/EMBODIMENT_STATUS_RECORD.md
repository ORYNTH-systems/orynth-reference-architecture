# Embodiment Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for embodiment states.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- embodiment identifier;
- originating architecture state;
- agency relationship;
- operational presence;
- environmental context;
- governance conditions;
- continuity status.

---

## Embodiment Lifecycle


EMBODIMENT STATE CREATED

↓

IDENTITY LINKED

↓

OPERATIONAL PRESENCE ESTABLISHED

↓

INTERACTION ENABLED

↓

STATE EVALUATED

↓

EMBODIMENT HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Embodiment States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- FORMING;
- ESTABLISHED;
- ACTIVE;
- ADAPTING;
- PRESERVED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Embodiment Status Records preserve accountability between architectural identity and persistent operational presence.
