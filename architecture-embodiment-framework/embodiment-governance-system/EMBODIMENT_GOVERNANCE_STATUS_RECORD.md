# Embodiment Governance Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for embodiment governance activities.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- embodiment identifier;
- identity state;
- operational presence state;
- governance conditions;
- interaction permissions;
- validation evidence;
- continuity status.

---

## Governance Lifecycle


EMBODIMENT REVIEW INITIATED

↓

IDENTITY ANALYZED

↓

PRESENCE EVALUATED

↓

GOVERNANCE CONDITIONS VERIFIED

↓

STATE DECISION RECORDED

↓

EMBODIMENT HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Governance States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- REVIEWING;
- VALIDATING;
- APPROVED;
- ACTIVE;
- RESTRICTED;
- REVISED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Embodiment Governance Status Records preserve accountability between persistent operational presence and architectural integrity.
