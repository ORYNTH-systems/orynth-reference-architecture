# Embodiment Governance System

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the governance framework for maintaining integrity within ORYNTH / Morning Star architectural embodiment.

---

## Embodiment Governance Principle

Embodiment enables persistent operational presence while preserving:

- architectural identity;
- constitutional boundaries;
- operational legitimacy;
- environmental responsibility;
- continuity requirements.

A persistent presence does not independently define its own authority.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- identity governance;
- operational presence governance;
- interaction boundaries;
- environmental alignment;
- state transitions;
- continuity oversight.

Embodiment governance must preserve:

- originating architecture;
- identity relationships;

- authorization boundaries;
- historical state.

The Embodiment Governance System ensures persistent architectural presence remains controlled, traceable, and aligned with constitutional purpose.
