## Continuity Categories

### Identity Continuity

Preserves what the architecture is.

### Presence Continuity

Preserves where and how the architecture exists.

### Operational Continuity

Preserves active processes and states.

### Governance Continuity

Preserves boundaries and authority history.

### Evolution Continuity

Preserves adaptation over time.

## Continuity Factors

- traceability;
- persistence;
- accountability;
- historical accuracy;
- adaptive consistency.

## Principle

The Embodiment Continuity Model ensures operational manifestation remains connected to architectural origin.
