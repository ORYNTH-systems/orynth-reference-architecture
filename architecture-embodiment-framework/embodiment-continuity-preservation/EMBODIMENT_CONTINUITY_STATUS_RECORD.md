# Embodiment Continuity Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for embodiment continuity preservation.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- embodiment identifier;
- originating architecture state;
- identity history;
- operational presence history;
- governance records;
- environmental interactions;
- continuity status.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


EMBODIMENT RECORD CREATED

↓

IDENTITY LINKED

↓

HISTORY ATTACHED

↓

STATE TRANSITIONS RECORDED

↓

CONTINUITY VERIFIED

↓

LEGACY PRESERVED


---

## Continuity States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- PRESERVED;
- VERIFIED;
- ACTIVE;
- EVOLVING;
- CONTINUING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Embodiment Continuity Status Records preserve accountability between persistent architectural presence and future evolutionary states.
