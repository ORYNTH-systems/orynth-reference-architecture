# Architecture Evolution Queue

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how future architectural changes are recorded, evaluated, and prioritized.

---

## Evolution Principle

Architecture evolution requires deliberate assessment of necessity, compatibility, and long-term impact.

---

## Queue Domains

Includes:

- proposed changes;
- technical improvements;
- governance updates;
- validation enhancements;
- research extensions.

---

## Evolution Queue Lifecycle


SUBMIT CHANGE

↓

REVIEW PROPOSAL

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

PRIORITIZE WORK

↓

IMPLEMENT CHANGE

↓

UPDATE ARCHITECTURE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A managed evolution queue enables continuous improvement while protecting architectural continuity.
