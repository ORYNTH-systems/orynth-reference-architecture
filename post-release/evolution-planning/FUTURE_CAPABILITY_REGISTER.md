# Future Capability Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for future capabilities considered for ORYNTH ecosystem development.

---

## Capability Principle

Future capabilities must maintain alignment with architecture purpose, governance requirements, and validation standards.

---

## Capability Domains

Includes:

- new system capabilities;
- research extensions;
- integration opportunities;
- operational improvements;
- knowledge expansion.

---

## Capability Lifecycle


IDENTIFY POSSIBILITY

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURAL FIT

↓

DEFINE REQUIREMENTS

↓

VALIDATE APPROACH

↓

SCHEDULE DEVELOPMENT

↓

INTEGRATE CAPABILITY


---

## Principle

A capability register preserves future opportunities without compromising current architectural integrity.
