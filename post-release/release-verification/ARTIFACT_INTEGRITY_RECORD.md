# Artifact Integrity Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH release artifacts maintain integrity after publication.

---

## Integrity Principle

Artifact integrity requires preservation of content identity, location, version, and relationship to the release state.

---

## Integrity Domains

Includes:

- documentation artifacts;
- architecture records;
- validation records;
- release records;
- historical records.

---

## Integrity Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ARTIFACT

↓

VERIFY CONTENT

↓

CONFIRM LOCATION

↓

CHECK VERSION

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Artifact integrity ensures released materials remain reliable references for future review and evolution.
