# Change History Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the historical record of significant repository changes and release milestones.

---

## Change Principle

Change history preserves the relationship between modifications, architectural evolution, and released states.

---

## Change Domains

Includes:

- architecture additions;
- framework development;
- validation milestones;
- documentation changes;
- release transitions.

---

## Change Lifecycle


IDENTIFY CHANGE

↓

DOCUMENT PURPOSE

↓

CONNECT VERSION

↓

VERIFY IMPACT

↓

RECORD HISTORY


---

## Principle

Change history maintains continuity between past development and future evolution.
