# Research Lineage Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH research assets maintain traceable origins and evolution history.

---

## Lineage Principle

Every research artifact should maintain connection to its origin, development path, and related contributions.

---

## Lineage Relationships

Includes:

- predecessor relationships;
- successor relationships;
- derivative works;
- supporting evidence;
- related implementations.

---

## Lineage Lifecycle


ORIGIN

↓

DEVELOPMENT

↓

VALIDATION

↓

PUBLICATION

↓

EVOLUTION


---

## Principle

Research lineage preserves continuity between discoveries, implementations, and future extensions.
