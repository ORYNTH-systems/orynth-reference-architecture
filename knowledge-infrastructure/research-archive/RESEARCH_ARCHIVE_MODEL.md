# Research Archive Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH research materials are preserved, organized, and maintained over time.

---

## Archive Principle

Research archives preserve the history, evidence, and evolution of knowledge assets.

---

## Archive Contents

Includes:

- research publications;
- technical documents;
- validation records;
- experimental outcomes;
- historical decisions.

## Principle

A structured research archive enables reconstruction, review, and continued advancement.
