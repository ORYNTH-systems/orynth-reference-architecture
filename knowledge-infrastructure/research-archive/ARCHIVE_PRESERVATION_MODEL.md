# Archive Preservation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define preservation requirements for long-term retention of ORYNTH knowledge assets.

---

## Preservation Principle

Archived materials must remain accessible, traceable, and understandable across future system states.

---

## Preservation Requirements

Maintain:

- source identity;
- version history;
- supporting evidence;
- relationships;
- contextual meaning.

---

## Preservation Lifecycle


IDENTIFY

↓

CAPTURE

↓

VERIFY

↓

STORE

↓

REVIEW

↓

PRESERVE


---

## Principle

Preservation protects knowledge continuity across time.
