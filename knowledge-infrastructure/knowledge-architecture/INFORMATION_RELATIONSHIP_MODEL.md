# Information Relationship Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH information assets relate to one another.

---

## Relationship Principle

Knowledge gains value through traceable relationships between concepts, evidence, systems, and outcomes.

---

## Relationship Types

Includes:

- dependency relationships;
- inheritance relationships;
- validation relationships;
- historical relationships;
- application relationships.
