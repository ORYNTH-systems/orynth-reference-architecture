# Knowledge Architecture Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure used to organize, relate, and preserve ORYNTH knowledge assets.

---

## Knowledge Principle

Knowledge architecture creates a stable structure through which concepts, evidence, research, and implementations remain connected.

---

## Knowledge Domains

Includes:

- foundational concepts;
- technical frameworks;
- research outputs;
- validation evidence;
- operational records.

---

## Knowledge Lifecycle


CREATE

↓

CLASSIFY

↓

CONNECT

↓

VALIDATE

↓

PRESERVE

↓

EXTEND


---

## Principle

A coherent knowledge architecture enables reconstruction, discovery, and future development.
