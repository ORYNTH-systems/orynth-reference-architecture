# Knowledge Mapping Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH knowledge relationships are represented and analyzed.

---

## Mapping Principle

Knowledge maps reveal connections between concepts, systems, evidence, and outcomes.

---

## Mapping Dimensions

Includes:

- conceptual relationships;
- architectural dependencies;
- research lineage;
- implementation connections;
- validation relationships.

---

## Mapping Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ENTITIES

↓

DEFINE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

CREATE MAP

↓

ANALYZE STRUCTURE

↓

UPDATE CONNECTIONS


---

## Principle

Knowledge mapping enables deeper understanding of complex research ecosystems.
