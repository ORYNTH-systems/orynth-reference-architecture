# Search and Retrieval Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define methods for locating and accessing ORYNTH knowledge resources.

---

## Retrieval Principle

Search systems should preserve context, relationships, and source lineage rather than returning isolated information.

---

## Retrieval Areas

Includes:

- document discovery;
- evidence retrieval;
- research lookup;
- relationship navigation;
- historical reference.

---

## Retrieval Lifecycle


DEFINE QUERY

↓

LOCATE SOURCES

↓

ASSESS CONTEXT

↓

RETRIEVE INFORMATION

↓

VERIFY RELEVANCE


---

## Principle

Effective retrieval maintains connection between information and its original meaning.
