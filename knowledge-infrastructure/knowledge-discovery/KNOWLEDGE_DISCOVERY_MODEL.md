# Knowledge Discovery Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH knowledge assets are located, interpreted, and connected for future research and development.

---

## Discovery Principle

Knowledge discovery transforms stored information into accessible understanding through structured retrieval and relationship analysis.

---

## Discovery Functions

Includes:

- information retrieval;
- relationship identification;
- research exploration;
- knowledge navigation;
- pattern discovery.

---

## Discovery Lifecycle


IDENTIFY QUESTION

↓

LOCATE KNOWLEDGE

↓

ANALYZE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

CONNECT INSIGHTS

↓

GENERATE UNDERSTANDING


---

## Principle

Discovery enables stored knowledge to become an active research resource.
