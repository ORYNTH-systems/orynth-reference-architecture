# Validation Record Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure for preserving validation activities and outcomes.

---

## Validation Record Structure

Includes:

- validation identifier;
- subject evaluated;
- methodology;
- evidence reviewed;
- findings;
- conclusion;
- review status.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


DEFINE CRITERIA

↓

COLLECT EVIDENCE

↓

PERFORM REVIEW

↓

DOCUMENT FINDINGS

↓

ARCHIVE RESULT


---

## Principle

Validation records preserve accountability and support future review.
