# Evidence Management Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evidence is collected, organized, maintained, and reviewed.

---

## Evidence Principle

Evidence provides the foundation for validation, reproducibility, and informed evaluation.

---

## Evidence Categories

Includes:

- research evidence;
- implementation evidence;
- validation evidence;
- operational evidence;
- historical evidence.

---

## Evidence Lifecycle


IDENTIFY

↓

COLLECT

↓

CLASSIFY

↓

VERIFY

↓

STORE

↓

REFERENCE


---

## Principle

Managed evidence preserves trust and enables independent examination.
