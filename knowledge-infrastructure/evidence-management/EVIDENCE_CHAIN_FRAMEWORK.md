# Evidence Chain Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how evidence relationships are preserved from origin through validation.

---

## Evidence Chain Principle

Evidence must maintain traceable connection between claims, sources, processes, and outcomes.

---

## Chain Components

Includes:

- source origin;
- transformation history;
- validation activity;
- resulting conclusion;
- related records.

---

## Evidence Chain Lifecycle


SOURCE

↓

PROCESS

↓

ANALYSIS

↓

VALIDATION

↓

CONCLUSION

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Principle

A complete evidence chain enables reconstruction of how conclusions were formed.
