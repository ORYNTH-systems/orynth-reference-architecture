# Ecosystem Governance Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Ecosystem Development Layer

Domain:

Community Ecosystem

---

## Purpose

Define governance relationships within the ORYNTH community.

---

## Governance Principle

Participation requires clarity of roles, responsibilities, and decision boundaries.

---

## Governance Layers

### Foundation Governance

Maintains:

- Morning Star principles;
- architectural invariants;
- continuity.

---

### Application Governance

Maintains:

- applied architecture decisions;
- implementation standards.

---

### Community Governance

Maintains:

- contribution processes;
- collaboration practices;
- ecosystem participation.

---

## Governance Structure


Morning Star

↓

Applied Architectures

↓

Ecosystem Systems

↓

Community Participants


---

## Governance Invariant

Community growth occurs through coordinated participation rather than uncontrolled modification.
