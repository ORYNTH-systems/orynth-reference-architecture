## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH architectures integrate with external systems while preserving boundaries, governance, and continuity.

---

## Integration Principle

Systems should connect through clearly defined relationships without loss of identity or control.

---

## Integration Domains

### Architectural Integration

Defines:

- component relationships;
- dependency boundaries;
- shared capabilities.

---

### Operational Integration

Defines:

- workflows;
- execution pathways;
- system coordination.

---

### Knowledge Integration

Defines:

- information exchange;
- research relationships;
- discovery pathways.
