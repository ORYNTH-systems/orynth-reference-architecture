# Interoperability Framework

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Ecosystem Development Layer

Domain:

Integration Ecosystem

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH systems cooperate with independent systems.

---

## Interoperability Principle

Different systems may collaborate while maintaining independent identity.

---

## Interoperability Domains

### Technical Interoperability

Supports:

- communication;
- compatibility;
- shared operation.

---

### Semantic Interoperability

Supports:

- shared meaning;
- terminology alignment;
- interpretation.

---

### Governance Interoperability

Supports:

- authority alignment;
- accountability;
- coordination.

---

## Interoperability Model


IDENTIFY

↓

ALIGN

↓

CONNECT

↓

VERIFY

↓

COLLABORATE


---

## Interoperability Invariant

Compatibility does not require loss of independence.
