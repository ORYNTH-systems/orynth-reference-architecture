## Purpose

Define how organizations adopt ORYNTH-derived architectures.

---

## Adoption Principle

Organizations adopt capabilities through structured implementation, validation, and governance alignment.

---

## Adoption Stages

### Awareness

Understanding architecture purpose and capabilities.

---

### Evaluation

Assessing compatibility and requirements.

---

### Implementation

Deploying applicable systems.

---

### Validation

Measuring effectiveness and alignment.

---

### Maturation

Continuous improvement and integration.
