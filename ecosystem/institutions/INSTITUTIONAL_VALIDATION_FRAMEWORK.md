# Institutional Validation Framework

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Ecosystem Development Layer

Domain:

Institutional Ecosystem

---

## Purpose

Define validation requirements for institutional implementations.

---

## Validation Principle

Institutional deployment requires measurable evidence of alignment, capability, and outcomes.

---

## Validation Domains

### Architectural Validation

Checks:

- compatibility;
- integration;
- boundary preservation.

---

### Operational Validation

Checks:

- functionality;
- performance;
- reliability.

---

### Governance Validation

Checks:

- authority;
- accountability;
- decision pathways.

---

### Impact Validation

Checks:

- outcomes;
- sustainability;
- broader effects.

---

## Validation Cycle


ASSESS

↓

IMPLEMENT

↓

MEASURE

↓

REVIEW

↓

CERTIFY


---

## Validation Invariant

Institutional adoption remains evidence-based and continuously reviewable.
