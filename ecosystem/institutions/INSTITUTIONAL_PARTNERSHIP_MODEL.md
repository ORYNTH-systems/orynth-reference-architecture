## Purpose

Define partnership structures between ORYNTH and external institutions.

---

## Partnership Principle

Institutional relationships should expand capability while preserving architectural integrity, attribution, and governance clarity.

- validation programs;
- knowledge exchange.

---

### Implementation Partnerships

- integration;
- operational testing.

---

### Strategic Partnerships

Supports:

- long-term collaboration;
- ecosystem development;
- shared objectives.
