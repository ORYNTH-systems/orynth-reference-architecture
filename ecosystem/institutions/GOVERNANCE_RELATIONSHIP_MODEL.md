# Governance Relationship Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Ecosystem Development Layer

Domain:

Institutional Ecosystem

---

## Purpose

Define governance relationships between ORYNTH, institutions, and deployed architectures.

---

## Governance Principle

Distributed participation requires clear authority boundaries and accountability.

---

## Relationship Domains

### Foundation Governance

Maintains:

- Morning Star principles;
- architectural invariants;
- continuity.

---

### Application Governance

Maintains:

- applied architecture decisions;
- implementation rules;
- operational responsibilities.

---

### Institutional Governance

Maintains:

- organizational participation;
- deployment authority;
- local requirements.

---

## Governance Structure


Morning Star

↓

Applied Architectures

↓

Institutions

↓

Operational Implementations


---

## Governance Invariant

Participation expands through defined boundaries rather than uncontrolled modification.
