## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH architectures move from validated systems into operational environments.

---

## Deployment Principle

Deployment expands system capability while maintaining architectural integrity, evidence, and governance.

---

## Deployment Domains

### Research Deployment

Supports:

- experimental environments;
- validation studies;
- prototype systems.

---

### Institutional Deployment

Supports:

- organizational implementations;
- operational adoption;
- integration pathways.

---

### Civilization Deployment

Supports:

- large-scale applications;
- interconnected systems;
- long-term deployment.

---
