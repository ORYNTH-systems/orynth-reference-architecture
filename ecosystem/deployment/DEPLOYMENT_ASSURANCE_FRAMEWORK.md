# Deployment Assurance Framework

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Ecosystem Development Layer

Domain:

Deployment Ecosystem

---

## Purpose

Define assurance requirements for deployed ORYNTH systems.

---

## Assurance Principle

Deployment requires continuous verification after implementation.

---

## Assurance Domains

### Technical Assurance

Validates:

- functionality;
- reliability;
- system health.

---

### Governance Assurance

Validates:

- authority;
- accountability;
- compliance.

---

### Evidence Assurance

Validates:

- records;
- traceability;
- reconstruction.

---

### Continuity Assurance

Validates:

- long-term stability;
- evolution;
- preservation.

---

## Assurance Cycle


DEPLOY

↓

MONITOR

↓

VERIFY

↓

REVIEW

↓

IMPROVE


---

## Assurance Invariant

Deployment success requires continued evidence of integrity.
