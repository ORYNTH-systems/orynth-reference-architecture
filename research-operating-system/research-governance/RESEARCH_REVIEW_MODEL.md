# Research Review Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH research outputs, processes, and findings are reviewed.

---

## Review Principle

Research review evaluates quality, alignment, evidence, and readiness before advancement.

---

## Review Domains

Includes:

- methodology review;
- evidence review;
- technical review;
- governance review;
- outcome review.

---

## Review Lifecycle


SUBMIT RESEARCH ITEM

↓

ASSESS REQUIREMENTS

↓

PERFORM REVIEW

↓

DOCUMENT FINDINGS

↓

DETERMINE STATUS

↓

ARCHIVE REVIEW


---

## Principle

Structured research review supports quality assurance and continuous improvement.
