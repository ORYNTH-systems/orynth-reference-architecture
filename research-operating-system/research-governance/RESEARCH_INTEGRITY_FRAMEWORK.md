# Research Integrity Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the principles and structures that maintain accuracy, transparency, and reliability throughout ORYNTH research activities.

---

## Integrity Principle

Research integrity requires alignment between evidence, methods, analysis, documentation, and conclusions.

---

## Integrity Domains

Includes:

- evidence integrity;
- methodological integrity;
- analytical integrity;
- documentation integrity;
- publication integrity.

---

## Integrity Lifecycle


ESTABLISH STANDARDS

↓

APPLY METHODS

↓

VERIFY EVIDENCE

↓

REVIEW FINDINGS

↓

CORRECT ISSUES

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Research integrity ensures knowledge generation remains accountable and reconstructable.
