# Research Governance Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the governance structures that guide ORYNTH research activities, decisions, and long-term research continuity.

---

## Research Governance Principle

Research governance establishes the processes, responsibilities, and controls required to maintain trustworthy research operations.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- research direction;
- methodology oversight;
- evidence management;
- review processes;
- publication decisions.

---

## Governance Lifecycle


DEFINE RESEARCH RULES

↓

ESTABLISH RESPONSIBILITIES

↓

REVIEW RESEARCH ACTIVITY

↓

EVALUATE COMPLIANCE

↓

MAINTAIN RECORDS

↓

IMPROVE GOVERNANCE


---

## Principle

Research governance preserves integrity while enabling continuous exploration and discovery.
