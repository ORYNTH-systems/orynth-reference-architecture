# Research Continuity Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH preserves research capability, knowledge, and operational continuity across time.

---

## Research Continuity Principle

Research continuity maintains the connection between past discoveries, present activities, and future development.

---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- knowledge continuity;
- operational continuity;
- architectural continuity;
- institutional continuity;
- developmental continuity.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


CAPTURE RESEARCH STATE

↓

PRESERVE KNOWLEDGE

↓

MAINTAIN CONTEXT

↓

TRANSFER UNDERSTANDING

↓

CONTINUE DEVELOPMENT

↓

UPDATE HISTORY

## Principle

Research continuity ensures valuable knowledge remains available throughout ecosystem evolution.
