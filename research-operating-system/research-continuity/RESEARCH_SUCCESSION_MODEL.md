# Research Succession Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH research capability and knowledge can continue through future contributors, systems, and organizational structures.

---

## Succession Principle

Research succession preserves foundational knowledge while allowing continued evolution and improvement.

---

## Succession Domains

Includes:

- knowledge transfer;
- role continuity;
- capability preservation;
- architectural understanding;
- future development.

---

## Succession Lifecycle


IDENTIFY KNOWLEDGE

↓

DOCUMENT CONTEXT

↓

TRANSFER UNDERSTANDING

↓

ENABLE CONTINUATION

↓

MAINTAIN IDENTITY

↓

PRESERVE LEGACY


---

## Principle

Research succession ensures long-term continuity without preventing future innovation.
