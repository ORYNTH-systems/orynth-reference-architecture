# Research Archive Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH research artifacts, findings, decisions, and historical records are preserved.

---

## Archive Principle

Research archives preserve evidence, context, lineage, and evolution of knowledge assets.

---

## Archive Domains

Includes:

- publications;
- experiments;
- validation records;
- decisions;
- historical milestones.

---

## Archive Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ARTIFACT

↓

CLASSIFY RECORD

↓

STORE INFORMATION

↓

MAINTAIN VERSION

↓

ENABLE RETRIEVAL

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A structured archive protects research knowledge from loss while enabling future reconstruction.
