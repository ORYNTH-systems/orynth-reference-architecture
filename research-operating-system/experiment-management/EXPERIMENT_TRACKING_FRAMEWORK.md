# Experiment Tracking Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH tracks experiment states, progress, evidence, and outcomes.

---

## Tracking Principle

Experiment tracking preserves continuity between research intent, execution activity, observations, and final results.

---

## Tracking Domains

Includes:

- experiment identity;
- research objectives;
- methodology;
- execution state;
- evidence records;
- outcomes.

---

## Tracking Lifecycle


REGISTER EXPERIMENT

↓

RECORD CONDITIONS

↓

TRACK EXECUTION

↓

CAPTURE OBSERVATIONS

↓

UPDATE STATUS

↓

ARCHIVE RESULTS


---

## Principle

Experiment tracking enables reconstruction and review of research activity over time.
