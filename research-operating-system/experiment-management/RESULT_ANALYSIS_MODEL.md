# Result Analysis Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates experimental results and converts observations into research understanding.

---

## Analysis Principle

Result analysis connects collected evidence with research objectives, methods, and conclusions.

---

## Analysis Domains

Includes:

- data review;
- outcome evaluation;
- comparison;
- interpretation;
- conclusion development.

---

## Result Analysis Lifecycle


COLLECT RESULTS

↓

VERIFY DATA

↓

ANALYZE OUTCOMES

↓

COMPARE EXPECTATIONS

↓

IDENTIFY FINDINGS

↓

DOCUMENT CONCLUSION


---

## Principle

Structured result analysis transforms experimental activity into validated research knowledge.
