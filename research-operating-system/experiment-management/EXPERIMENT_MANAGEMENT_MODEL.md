# Experiment Management Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH research experiments are designed, coordinated, executed, and evaluated.

---

## Experiment Management Principle

Experiment management provides structured pathways for testing concepts, evaluating outcomes, and generating validated knowledge.

---

## Experiment Domains

Includes:

- experiment design;
- methodology;
- execution control;
- observation;
- evaluation;
- documentation.

---

## Experiment Lifecycle
DEFINE OBJECTIVE

↓

ESTABLISH METHOD

↓

PREPARE CONDITIONS

↓

EXECUTE EXPERIMENT

↓

ANALYZE RESULTS

↓

PRESERVE FINDINGS


---

## Principle

Structured experiment management improves repeatability, transparency, and research quality.
