# Research Process Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the process structure used to guide ORYNTH research activities from concept through validated outcome.

---

## Process Principle

Research processes provide repeatable pathways for exploration, evaluation, and knowledge generation.

---

## Process Domains

Includes:

- hypothesis development;
- evidence gathering;
- experimentation;
- analysis;
- review;
- dissemination.

---

## Research Process Lifecycle


IDENTIFY TOPIC

↓

FORMULATE APPROACH

↓

CONDUCT RESEARCH

↓

EVALUATE RESULTS

↓

REFINE UNDERSTANDING

↓

COMMUNICATE FINDINGS


---

## Principle

A defined research process improves consistency, quality, and institutional continuity.
