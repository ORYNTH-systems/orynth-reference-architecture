# Research Execution Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH research activities are executed while maintaining alignment, evidence, and operational awareness.

---

## Execution Principle

Research execution connects objectives, methods, resources, and outcomes through controlled progression.

---

## Execution Domains

Includes:

- planning;
- resource coordination;
- implementation;
- observation;
- evaluation;
- reporting.

---

## Research Execution Lifecycle


ESTABLISH OBJECTIVE

↓

PREPARE RESOURCES

↓

EXECUTE METHOD

↓

COLLECT RESULTS

↓

EVALUATE OUTCOME

↓

UPDATE RESEARCH STATE


---

## Principle

Controlled research execution enables reliable progression from concept to validated knowledge.
