# Research Workflow Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH research activities are structured, coordinated, and advanced through repeatable workflows.

---

## Research Workflow Principle

Research workflows transform ideas, questions, evidence, and analysis into structured research progression.

---

## Workflow Domains

Includes:

- research initiation;
- investigation;
- analysis;
- validation;
- publication;
- preservation.

---

## Research Workflow Lifecycle


DEFINE QUESTION

↓

COLLECT INFORMATION

↓

PERFORM ANALYSIS

↓

VALIDATE FINDINGS

↓

DOCUMENT RESULTS

↓

PRESERVE KNOWLEDGE


---

## Principle

Structured research workflows enable consistent advancement while preserving traceability.
