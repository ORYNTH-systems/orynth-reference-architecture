# Knowledge Management Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH maintains knowledge assets throughout their lifecycle.

---

## Management Principle

Knowledge management preserves accuracy, context, relationships, and accessibility over time.

---

## Management Domains

Includes:

- knowledge inventory;
- version management;
- relationship mapping;
- access structure;
- archival continuity.

---

## Knowledge Management Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ASSET

↓

STORE INFORMATION

↓

TRACK VERSION

↓

MAINTAIN CONTEXT

↓

UPDATE KNOWLEDGE

↓

PRESERVE LINEAGE

## Principle

Knowledge management transforms accumulated research into a persistent institutional capability.
