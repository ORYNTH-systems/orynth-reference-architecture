# Research Knowledge Pipeline

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the pathway through which ORYNTH research knowledge moves from creation to validated institutional knowledge.

---

## Pipeline Principle

A research knowledge pipeline preserves the transition from observation to understanding to reusable knowledge.

---

## Pipeline Stages

Includes:

- research input;
- analysis;
- validation;
- documentation;
- publication;
- archival.

---

## Pipeline Lifecycle


GENERATE RESEARCH

↓

PROCESS INFORMATION

↓

VALIDATE FINDINGS

↓

DOCUMENT KNOWLEDGE

↓

DISTRIBUTE OUTPUT

↓

ARCHIVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A structured knowledge pipeline enables reliable movement of research through the ecosystem.
