# Knowledge Operations Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH manages the collection, organization, movement, and preservation of research knowledge.

---

## Knowledge Operations Principle

Knowledge operations transform research outputs into structured, accessible, and maintainable knowledge assets.

---

## Operations Domains

Includes:

- knowledge acquisition;
- organization;
- classification;
- preservation;
- retrieval;
- dissemination.

---

## Knowledge Operations Lifecycle


ACQUIRE KNOWLEDGE

↓

STRUCTURE INFORMATION

↓

CLASSIFY CONTENT

↓

MAINTAIN RECORDS

↓

ENABLE ACCESS

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

Structured knowledge operations preserve research continuity and enable future discovery.
