# Architecture Correlation Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to record relationships between ORYNTH architecture domains.

---

## Register Principle

Correlation records preserve source architecture, target architecture, relationship type, interaction context, and validation status.

---

## Correlation Fields

Includes:

- source domain;
- target domain;
- relationship classification;
- dependency condition;
- integration state;
- validation evidence.

---

## Register Lifecycle


REGISTER ARCHITECTURE DOMAIN

↓

IDENTIFY RELATED DOMAIN

↓

CLASSIFY RELATIONSHIP

↓

DOCUMENT CORRELATION

↓

VERIFY CONNECTION

↓

UPDATE REGISTER


---

## Principle

An architecture correlation register maintains traceability between independent architectural structures and their ecosystem relationships.
