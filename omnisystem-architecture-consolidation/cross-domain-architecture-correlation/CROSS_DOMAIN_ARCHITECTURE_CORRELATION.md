# Cross-Domain Architecture Correlation

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to identify, map, and preserve relationships between distinct ORYNTH architectural domains.

---

## Correlation Principle

Cross-domain architecture correlation establishes structural relationships between systems, frameworks, governance models, intelligence layers, and operational capabilities.

---

## Correlation Domains

Includes:

- architectural relationships;
- functional relationships;
- governance relationships;
- intelligence relationships;
- operational relationships;
- evolutionary relationships.

---

## Correlation Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ARCHITECTURE DOMAINS

↓

MAP CROSS-DOMAIN RELATIONSHIPS

↓

CLASSIFY CORRELATION TYPE

↓

ANALYZE SYSTEM INTERACTION

↓

VALIDATE RELATIONSHIP COHERENCE

↓

PRESERVE CORRELATION MODEL


---

## Principle

A cross-domain architecture correlation framework provides visibility into how individual ORYNTH systems collectively operate as an integrated ecosystem.
