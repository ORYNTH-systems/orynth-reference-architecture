# Inventory Evolution Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how the complete system inventory evolves over time.

---

## Evolution Principle

Inventory evolution records preserve newly identified systems, architectural changes, classification refinements, and ecosystem expansion.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- system additions;
- architecture changes;
- classification updates;
- relationship discovery;
- future ecosystem growth.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


CAPTURE INVENTORY CHANGE

↓

DOCUMENT SYSTEM UPDATE

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURAL IMPACT

↓

REFINE INVENTORY MODEL

↓

VALIDATE COMPLETENESS

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

An inventory evolution record ensures ORYNTH system knowledge remains current, structured, and traceable.
