# Ontology Evolution Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how the unified ORYNTH system ontology evolves over time.

---

## Evolution Principle

Ontology evolution records preserve terminology refinement, concept expansion, relationship discovery, and changes to system understanding.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- concept refinement;
- terminology evolution;
- relationship expansion;
- classification updates;
- future ontology development.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


CAPTURE ONTOLOGY CHANGE

↓

DOCUMENT CONCEPT UPDATE

↓

ASSESS SEMANTIC IMPACT

↓

REFINE ONTOLOGY MODEL

↓

VALIDATE CONSISTENCY

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

An ontology evolution record ensures ORYNTH conceptual structures remain coherent, expandable, and historically traceable.
