# Optimization History Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how optimization activities, results, and architectural improvements are preserved.

---

## Optimization Principle

Optimization history maintains context between previous states, implemented improvements, and future refinement opportunities.

---

## Optimization Domains

Includes:

- performance improvements;
- structural improvements;
- process improvements;
- governance improvements;
- operational lessons.

---

## Optimization Lifecycle


CAPTURE BASELINE STATE

↓

IDENTIFY OPTIMIZATION

↓

IMPLEMENT REFINEMENT

↓

MEASURE RESULT

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

An optimization history record ensures improvements remain connected to measurable outcomes and architectural continuity.
