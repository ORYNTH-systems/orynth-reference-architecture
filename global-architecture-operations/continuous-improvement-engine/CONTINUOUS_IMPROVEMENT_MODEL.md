# Continuous Improvement Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to identify, evaluate, implement, and preserve improvements across the ORYNTH architecture ecosystem.

---

## Improvement Principle

Continuous improvement transforms operational observations, validation results, and research insights into controlled architectural advancement.

---

## Improvement Domains

Includes:

- architecture refinement;
- operational optimization;
- governance improvement;
- validation enhancement;
- ecosystem advancement.

---

## Improvement Lifecycle


IDENTIFY IMPROVEMENT OPPORTUNITY

↓

ANALYZE CURRENT STATE

↓

DEFINE IMPROVEMENT PATH

↓

IMPLEMENT CHANGE

↓

VALIDATE RESULT

↓

PRESERVE IMPROVEMENT HISTORY


---

## Principle

A continuous improvement model enables ORYNTH to evolve while maintaining structural coherence and traceability.
