## Purpose

Define how operational intelligence capabilities evolve over time.

---

## Evolution Principle

Intelligence evolution records preserve analytical improvements, new observations, refined models, and accumulated operational knowledge.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- intelligence improvements;
- analytical methods;
- discovered patterns;
- operational lessons;
- future intelligence pathways.

PRESERVE HISTORY
