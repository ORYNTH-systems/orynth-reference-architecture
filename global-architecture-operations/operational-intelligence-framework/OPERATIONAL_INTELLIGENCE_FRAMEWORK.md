# Operational Intelligence Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to collect, interpret, and preserve operational intelligence across the ORYNTH architecture ecosystem.

---

## Intelligence Principle

Operational intelligence transforms observed conditions, system states, and ecosystem signals into structured knowledge for informed architectural decisions.

---

## Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- operational signals;
- system conditions;
- performance observations;
- governance indicators;
- evolutionary insights.

---

## Intelligence Lifecycle


OBSERVE SYSTEM CONDITION

↓

COLLECT OPERATIONAL SIGNALS

↓

ANALYZE PATTERNS

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

INFORM DECISION PROCESS

↓

PRESERVE INTELLIGENCE HISTORY


---

## Principle

An operational intelligence framework provides continuous visibility into ecosystem behavior while maintaining traceability and architectural continuity.
