# Operational Signal Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to capture operational signals across ORYNTH systems.

---

## Signal Principle

Operational signals preserve observations, conditions, trends, and contextual information required for architectural awareness.

---

## Signal Domains

Includes:

- system signals;
- integration signals;
- governance signals;
- performance signals;
- evolution signals.

---

## Signal Lifecycle


IDENTIFY SIGNAL

↓

CAPTURE CONDITION

↓

CLASSIFY INFORMATION

↓

ANALYZE IMPACT

↓

UPDATE ARCHITECTURE VIEW

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An operational signal register provides structured awareness of changing ecosystem conditions.
