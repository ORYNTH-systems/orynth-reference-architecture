# Stewardship Network Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track stewardship relationships, responsibilities, and preservation activities.

---

## Network Principle

Stewardship networks preserve accountability, knowledge transfer, architectural awareness, and continuity across participating structures.

---

## Network Domains

Includes:

- stewardship roles;
- preservation responsibilities;
- knowledge pathways;
- governance relationships;
- continuity conditions.

---
