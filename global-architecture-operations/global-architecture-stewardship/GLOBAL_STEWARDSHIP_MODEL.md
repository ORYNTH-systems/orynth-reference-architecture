# Global Stewardship Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to preserve ORYNTH architectural integrity, continuity, and responsible evolution across a global ecosystem.

---

## Stewardship Principle

Global stewardship maintains the relationship between architectural identity, governance responsibility, operational continuity, and future evolution.

---

## Stewardship Domains

Includes:

- architecture preservation;
- knowledge continuity;
- governance continuity;
- ecosystem responsibility;
- future development pathways.

---

## Stewardship Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ARCHITECTURAL ASSETS

↓

PRESERVE FOUNDATIONAL STRUCTURE

↓

MAINTAIN KNOWLEDGE CONTINUITY

↓

SUPPORT RESPONSIBLE EVOLUTION

↓

VERIFY ARCHITECTURAL ALIGNMENT

↓

PRESERVE STEWARDSHIP HISTORY

## Principle

A global stewardship model ensures the ORYNTH architecture remains coherent through long-term ecosystem growth.
