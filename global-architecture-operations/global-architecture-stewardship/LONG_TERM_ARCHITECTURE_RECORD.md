# Long-Term Architecture Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the permanent record structure for preserving ORYNTH architectural states, decisions, and evolution history.

---

## Preservation Principle

Long-term architecture records maintain identity, context, rationale, and historical continuity across future system changes.

---

## Record Domains

Includes:

- architectural milestones;
- governance decisions;
- system evolution;
- preservation events;
- future reference states.

---

## Record Lifecycle


CAPTURE ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

DOCUMENT CONTEXT

↓

PRESERVE DECISION HISTORY

↓

TRACK EVOLUTION

↓

VERIFY CONTINUITY

↓

MAINTAIN REFERENCE


---

## Principle

A long-term architecture record provides a durable reference for future ORYNTH development and stewardship.
