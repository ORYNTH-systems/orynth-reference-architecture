# Governance Evolution Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ecosystem governance structures evolve over time.

---

## Evolution Principle

Governance evolution records preserve decisions, structural changes, lessons learned, and future governance pathways.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- governance changes;
- decision history;
- participation evolution;
- structural improvements;
- future governance models.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


CAPTURE GOVERNANCE EVENT

↓

DOCUMENT DECISION

↓

ANALYZE IMPACT

↓

REFINE STRUCTURE

↓

VALIDATE CHANGE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A governance evolution record ensures ecosystem coordination develops through traceable and deliberate progression.
