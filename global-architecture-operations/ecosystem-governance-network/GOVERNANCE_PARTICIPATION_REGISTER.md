# Governance Participation Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track governance participants, roles, contributions, and participation states.

---

## Participation Principle

Governance participation records preserve contributor context, responsibilities, decision relationships, and historical involvement.

---

## Participation Domains

Includes:

- participant role;
- governance contribution;
- decision involvement;
- alignment status;
- participation history.

---

## Participation Lifecycle


REGISTER PARTICIPANT

↓

DEFINE ROLE

↓

DOCUMENT CONTRIBUTION

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

TRACK PARTICIPATION

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A governance participation register provides visibility into ecosystem governance involvement.
