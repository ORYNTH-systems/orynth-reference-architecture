# Constitutional Change Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how constitutional-level architectural changes are evaluated, recorded, and preserved.

---

## Change Principle

Constitutional changes require explicit documentation of motivation, impact, compatibility, and continuity.

---

## Change Domains

Includes:

- proposed modification;
- affected principles;
- impact assessment;
- validation evidence;
- approval status.

---

## Change Lifecycle


IDENTIFY CHANGE

↓

ASSESS CONSTITUTIONAL IMPACT

↓

REVIEW COMPATIBILITY

↓

VALIDATE TRANSITION

↓

RECORD DECISION

↓

UPDATE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A constitutional change record ensures architectural evolution remains deliberate, traceable, and continuity-preserving.
