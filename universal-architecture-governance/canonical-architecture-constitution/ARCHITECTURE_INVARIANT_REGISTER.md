## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to preserve architectural properties that must remain consistent throughout system evolution.

---

## Invariant Principle

Architectural invariants identify characteristics that preserve system identity despite modification, expansion, or adaptation.

---

## Invariant Domains

Includes:

- identity invariants;
- governance invariants;
- validation invariants;
- relationship invariants;
- continuity invariants.
