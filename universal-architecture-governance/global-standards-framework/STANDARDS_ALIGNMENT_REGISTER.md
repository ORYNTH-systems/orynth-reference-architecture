# Standards Alignment Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track alignment between ORYNTH architecture and external or internal standards.

---

## Alignment Principle

Standards alignment preserves compatibility, traceability, and measurable relationship between requirements and implementation.

---

## Alignment Domains

Includes:

- standard identity;
- requirement mapping;
- implementation alignment;
- verification state;
- revision history.

---

## Alignment Lifecycle


IDENTIFY STANDARD

↓

MAP REQUIREMENTS

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURE

↓

VERIFY ALIGNMENT

↓

DOCUMENT RESULT

↓

TRACK CHANGES


---

## Principle

A standards alignment register provides visibility into how architectural components correspond to defined standards.
