# Global Standards Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to establish, align, and maintain standards across the ORYNTH architectural ecosystem.

---

## Standards Principle

Global standards provide shared requirements, interfaces, evaluation methods, and compatibility structures while preserving system evolution.

---

## Standards Domains

Includes:

- architectural standards;
- operational standards;
- governance standards;
- validation standards;
- interoperability standards.

---

## Standards Lifecycle


IDENTIFY STANDARD REQUIREMENT

↓

DEFINE STANDARD STRUCTURE

↓

ALIGN IMPLEMENTATION

↓

VERIFY COMPLIANCE

↓

REVIEW EVOLUTION

↓

MAINTAIN STANDARD


---

## Principle

A global standards framework enables consistent adoption while preserving architectural flexibility.
