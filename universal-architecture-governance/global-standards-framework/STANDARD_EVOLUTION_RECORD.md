## Purpose

Define how standards, requirements, and alignment models evolve over time.

## Evolution Principle

Standards evolution preserves historical context while enabling adaptation to new requirements, discoveries, and operational conditions.

- standard revision;
- requirement changes;
- compatibility assessment;
- adoption impact;
- validation results.
