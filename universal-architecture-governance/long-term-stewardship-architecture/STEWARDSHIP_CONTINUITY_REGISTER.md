# Stewardship Continuity Register

Version:

v1.0.0

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track stewardship responsibilities, continuity requirements, and preservation activities.

---

## Continuity Principle

Stewardship continuity preserves the relationship between architectural principles, responsible maintenance, and future evolution.

---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- stewardship role;
- preserved assets;
- continuity requirements;
- transition history;
- validation status.
