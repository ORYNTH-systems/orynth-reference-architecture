# Long-Term Stewardship Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to preserve ORYNTH architectural integrity, continuity, and responsible evolution across extended time horizons.

---

## Stewardship Principle

Long-term stewardship preserves foundational architecture while enabling responsible adaptation, maintenance, and future development.

---

## Stewardship Domains

Includes:

- architectural preservation;
- governance continuity;
- knowledge continuity;
- implementation continuity;
- evolutionary responsibility.

---

## Stewardship Lifecycle


PRESERVE FOUNDATION

↓

MAINTAIN ARCHITECTURE

↓

REVIEW EVOLUTION

↓

TRANSFER KNOWLEDGE

↓

VALIDATE CONTINUITY

↓

SUSTAIN FUTURE DEVELOPMENT


---

## Principle

A long-term stewardship model ensures architectural identity remains preserved across generations of development.
