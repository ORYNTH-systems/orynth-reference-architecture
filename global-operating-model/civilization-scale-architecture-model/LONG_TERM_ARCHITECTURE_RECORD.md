# Long Term Architecture Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the historical record structure for preserving large-scale architectural evolution.

---

## Architecture Continuity Principle

Long-term architecture records preserve decisions, transitions, relationships, and identity through successive stages of development.

---

## Record Domains

Includes:

- architectural milestones;
- system transitions;
- governance changes;
- knowledge evolution;
- integration history.

---

## Record Lifecycle


CAPTURE ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

DOCUMENT TRANSITION

↓

REVIEW EVOLUTION

↓

VERIFY CONTINUITY

↓

UPDATE ARCHIVE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A long-term architecture record maintains continuity between present systems and future evolution.
