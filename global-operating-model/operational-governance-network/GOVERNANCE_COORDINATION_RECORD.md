# Governance Coordination Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how governance interactions, decisions, and coordination events are recorded.

---

## Coordination Principle

Governance coordination records preserve decision context, participating entities, outcomes, and historical continuity.

---

## Coordination Domains

Includes:

- decision events;
- governance actions;
- coordination outcomes;
- review status;
- historical records.

---

## Coordination Lifecycle


INITIATE COORDINATION

↓

DOCUMENT CONTEXT

↓

EXECUTE DECISION

↓

VERIFY OUTCOME

↓

UPDATE STATUS

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A governance coordination record provides traceability across distributed decision processes.
