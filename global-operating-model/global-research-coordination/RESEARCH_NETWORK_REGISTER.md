## Purpose

Define the structure used to register research participants, domains, relationships, and contributions.

---

## Network Principle

Research network records preserve identity, expertise, contribution pathways, and collaboration history.

- researchers;
- institutions;
- projects;
- knowledge domains;
- research outputs.
