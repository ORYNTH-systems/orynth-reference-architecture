# Research Coordination Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to coordinate distributed research activities, knowledge development, and discovery pathways across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Research Coordination Principle

Global research coordination preserves shared context, discovery continuity, methodological alignment, and knowledge exchange across distributed research activities.

---

## Research Domains

Includes:

- theoretical research;
- technical research;
- applied research;
- interdisciplinary research;
- validation research.

---

## Research Coordination Lifecycle


IDENTIFY RESEARCH DOMAIN

↓

CONNECT RESEARCH ACTIVITY

↓

ALIGN OBJECTIVES

↓

SHARE DISCOVERIES

↓

VALIDATE FINDINGS

↓

PRESERVE KNOWLEDGE


---

## Principle

A research coordination model enables distributed discovery while maintaining coherence across the ecosystem.
