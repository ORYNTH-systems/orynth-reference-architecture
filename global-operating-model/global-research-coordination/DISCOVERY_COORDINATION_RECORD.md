# Discovery Coordination Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how research discoveries, insights, and knowledge transitions are recorded and coordinated.

---

## Discovery Principle

Discovery coordination preserves origin, context, validation status, and relationship between findings.

---

## Discovery Domains

Includes:

- discovery source;
- research context;
- evidence reference;
- validation state;
- integration pathway.

---

## Discovery Lifecycle


CAPTURE DISCOVERY

↓

DOCUMENT CONTEXT

↓

ASSESS SIGNIFICANCE

↓

VERIFY RELATIONSHIP

↓

INTEGRATE KNOWLEDGE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A discovery coordination record ensures emerging knowledge remains traceable throughout ecosystem evolution.
