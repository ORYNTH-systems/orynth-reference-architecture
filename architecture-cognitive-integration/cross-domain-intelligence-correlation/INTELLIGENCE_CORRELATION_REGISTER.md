# Intelligence Correlation Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track intelligence relationships, domain correlations, contextual links, and validation states.

---

## State Principle

Correlation states preserve relationships between architecture domains, intelligence sources, analytical context, and integrated outcomes.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- correlation identity;
- source domains;
- relationship type;
- contextual conditions;
- dependency relationship;
- validation status;
- synthesis outcome;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE CORRELATION RECORD

↓

IDENTIFY SOURCE DOMAINS

↓

MAP INTELLIGENCE RELATIONSHIP

↓

ANALYZE CONTEXT

↓

VALIDATE CORRELATION

↓

REGISTER INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

PRESERVE RELATIONSHIP HISTORY


---

## Principle

An intelligence correlation register maintains traceability between ORYNTH architecture domains and their integrated intelligence relationships.
