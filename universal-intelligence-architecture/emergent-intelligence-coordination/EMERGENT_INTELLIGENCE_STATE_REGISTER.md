# Emergent Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track emergent intelligence states, interaction conditions, coordination states, and evolutionary transitions.

---

## State Principle

Emergent intelligence states preserve relationships between intelligence components, information flows, reasoning interactions, and resulting collective intelligence conditions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- intelligence component;
- interaction state;
- coordination condition;
- reasoning state;
- emergence condition;
- continuity status.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

MAP INTERACTION PATTERNS

↓

ASSESS EMERGENT CONDITION

↓

VERIFY COORDINATION ALIGNMENT

↓

UPDATE INTELLIGENCE MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An emergent intelligence state register maintains traceability between intelligence interactions and their resulting ecosystem-level conditions.
