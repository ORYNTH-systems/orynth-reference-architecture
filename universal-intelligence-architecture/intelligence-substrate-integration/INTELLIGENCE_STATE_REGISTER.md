# Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track intelligence states, knowledge conditions, reasoning processes, adaptive transitions, and continuity states.

---

## State Principle

Intelligence states preserve relationships between information sources, knowledge structures, reasoning conditions, adaptive processes, and resulting intelligence capabilities.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- intelligence identity;
- knowledge state;
- reasoning condition;
- adaptive capability;
- evaluation status;
- continuity state;
- evolution readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

CLASSIFY KNOWLEDGE CONDITION

↓

EVALUATE REASONING PROCESS

↓

ASSESS ADAPTIVE CAPABILITY

↓

UPDATE INTELLIGENCE MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An intelligence state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence structures and their evolving operational states.
