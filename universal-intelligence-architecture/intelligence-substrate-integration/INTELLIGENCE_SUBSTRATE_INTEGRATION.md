## Purpose

Define the intelligence substrate framework used to support ORYNTH intelligence structures, knowledge processing, reasoning capabilities, and adaptive systems.

---

## Intelligence Substrate Principle

The intelligence substrate provides the foundational layer through which information becomes organized knowledge, knowledge becomes actionable understanding, and intelligence capabilities can evolve while preserving continuity.

---

## Substrate Domains

Includes:

- information representation;
- knowledge structures;
- reasoning mechanisms;
- learning processes;
- adaptive capabilities;
- memory continuity;
- intelligence boundaries;
- evaluation mechanisms.
