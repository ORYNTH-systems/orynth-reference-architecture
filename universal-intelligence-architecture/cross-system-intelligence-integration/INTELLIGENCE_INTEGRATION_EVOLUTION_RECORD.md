## Purpose

Define how cross-system intelligence integration evolves over time.

---

## Evolution Principle

Intelligence integration evolution records preserve expanded intelligence relationships, improved coordination, reasoning refinement, and future integration pathways.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- intelligence expansion;
- knowledge integration;
- reasoning improvement;
- coordination refinement;
- adaptive intelligence development.
