## Purpose

Define the framework used to integrate intelligence capabilities, knowledge structures, reasoning processes, and adaptive intelligence systems across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Intelligence Integration Principle

Cross-system intelligence integration enables independent intelligence components to exchange information, coordinate reasoning, maintain shared context, and preserve system-level coherence.

---

## Integration Domains

Includes:

- intelligence interoperability;
- knowledge exchange;
- reasoning coordination;
- system awareness;
- adaptive intelligence alignment;
- cross-domain learning.
