# Intelligence Ecosystem State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track unified intelligence ecosystem states, component relationships, adaptive conditions, and evolutionary transitions.

---

## State Principle

Intelligence ecosystem states preserve relationships between intelligence components, knowledge structures, coordination processes, governance conditions, and resulting ecosystem states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- ecosystem identity;
- intelligence components;
- knowledge relationships;
- coordination condition;
- adaptive state;
- validation status;
- continuity state;
- future readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE ECOSYSTEM STATE

↓

MAP INTELLIGENCE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ASSESS SYSTEM CONDITION

↓

UPDATE ECOSYSTEM MODEL

↓

VALIDATE INTELLIGENCE ALIGNMENT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An intelligence ecosystem state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence architecture components and their unified operational representation.
