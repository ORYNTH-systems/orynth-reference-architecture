# Intelligence Operating State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track intelligence operating states, execution conditions, coordination states, and transitions.

---

## State Principle

Intelligence operating states preserve relationships between intelligence processes, operational contexts, governance conditions, adaptive responses, and resulting system states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- intelligence context;
- operational state;
- reasoning condition;
- coordination state;
- adaptation status;
- continuity record.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

ASSESS OPERATING CONDITION

↓

PROCESS INTELLIGENCE ACTIVITY

↓

VERIFY GOVERNANCE ALIGNMENT

↓

UPDATE OPERATING MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An intelligence operating state register maintains traceability between intelligence execution and its operational environment.
