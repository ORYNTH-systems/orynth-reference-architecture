# Knowledge Synthesis State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track synthesized knowledge states, relationships, evaluation conditions, and adaptive transitions.

---

## State Principle

Knowledge synthesis states preserve relationships between source knowledge, integrated representations, contextual meaning, validation conditions, and future evolution pathways.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- knowledge identity;
- source lineage;
- synthesized representation;
- relationship structure;
- contextual condition;
- validation status;
- evolution state.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE STATE

↓

CLASSIFY KNOWLEDGE SOURCE

↓

MAP RELATIONSHIPS

↓

GENERATE SYNTHESIS MODEL

↓

VALIDATE KNOWLEDGE CONDITION

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A knowledge synthesis state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH knowledge structures and their adaptive intelligence representations.
