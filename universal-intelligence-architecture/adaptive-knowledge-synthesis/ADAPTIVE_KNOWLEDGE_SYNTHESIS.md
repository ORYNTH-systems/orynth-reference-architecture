# Adaptive Knowledge Synthesis

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the adaptive knowledge synthesis framework used to integrate, organize, refine, and evolve knowledge structures throughout the ORYNTH intelligence architecture.

---

## Knowledge Synthesis Principle

Adaptive knowledge synthesis transforms distributed information and knowledge structures into coherent representations while preserving origin, context, relationships, and evolutionary history.

Synthesis creates higher-order understanding without eliminating source integrity.

---

## Synthesis Domains

Includes:

- knowledge acquisition;
- information integration;
- semantic organization;
- relationship discovery;
- contextual evaluation;
- knowledge refinement;
- adaptive learning;
- continuity preservation.

---

CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE SOURCES

## Principle

Adaptive knowledge synthesis ensures ORYNTH intelligence capabilities continuously improve while maintaining traceability, context, and structural integrity.
