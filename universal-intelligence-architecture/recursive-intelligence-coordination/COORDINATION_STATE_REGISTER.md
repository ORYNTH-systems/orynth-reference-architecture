# Coordination State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track intelligence coordination states, interaction conditions, synchronization states, and evolutionary transitions.

---

## State Principle

Coordination states preserve relationships between intelligence components, communication pathways, knowledge exchanges, adaptive conditions, and resulting system states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- coordination identity;
- intelligence participants;
- interaction relationship;
- synchronization condition;
- evaluation state;
- continuity status;
- evolution readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE COORDINATION STATE

↓

CLASSIFY INTELLIGENCE RELATIONSHIP

↓

ASSESS INTERACTION CONDITION

↓

UPDATE COORDINATION MODEL

↓

VALIDATE SYSTEM ALIGNMENT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A coordination state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence components and their evolving interaction structures.
