## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH / Morning Star participates within a global architecture network.

---

## Participation Principle

Network participation requires defined roles, relationship boundaries, and contribution expectations.

---

## Participation Model

---

## Participation Roles

### Reference Contributor

Provides architectural knowledge and documentation.

### Interoperability Participant

Supports governed system relationships.

### Research Participant

Contributes analysis and development.

### Standards Participant

Engages with broader architectural alignment.

---

## Participation Factors

Includes:

- contribution value;
- compatibility;
- governance alignment;
- evidence quality;
- continuity preservation.

---

## Principle

The Network Participation Model enables contribution without compromising architectural independence.
