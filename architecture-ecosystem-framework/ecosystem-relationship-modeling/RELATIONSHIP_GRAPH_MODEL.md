# Relationship Graph Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the graph representation of ecosystem relationships.

---

## Graph Principle

External ecosystems are represented as connected systems with explicit relationship types and boundaries.

---

## Relationship Graph

            EXTERNAL SYSTEMS

                   |

                   ↓

          RELATIONSHIP LAYER

                   |

                   ↓

        ORYNTH / MORNING STAR

                   |

                   ↓

          FUTURE ECOSYSTEM NODES

---

## Relationship Types

### Reference Relationship

System provides comparison or knowledge context.

### Integration Relationship

Systems exchange capabilities.

### Collaboration Relationship

Systems cooperate toward shared objectives.

### Translation Relationship

Concepts require mapping between domains.

### Observation Relationship

System is monitored without direct integration.

---

## Graph Properties

Maintains:

- node identity;
- relationship direction;
- relationship purpose;
- interaction constraints;
- historical state.

---

## Principle

The Relationship Graph Model preserves ecosystem structure without removing architectural boundaries.
