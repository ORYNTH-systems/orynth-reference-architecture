# Ecosystem Relationship Modeling

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the relationship model for connecting ORYNTH / Morning Star with external architectural ecosystems.

---

## Relationship Principle

An ecosystem is composed of distinct systems connected through defined relationships.

Relationship modeling preserves identity while enabling cooperation, comparison, and knowledge exchange.

---

## Relationship Domains

Includes:

- architecture relationships;
- institutional relationships;
- technical relationships;
- research relationships;
- standards relationships;
- implementation relationships.

---

## Relationship Lifecycle


SYSTEM IDENTIFIED

↓

RELATIONSHIP CREATED

↓

CONTEXT ANALYZED

↓

INTERACTION MODEL DEFINED

↓

RELATIONSHIP GOVERNED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Relationship Requirements

Relationships must preserve:

- system identity;
- relationship purpose;
- interaction boundaries;
- evidence context;
- governance conditions.

---

## Principle

Ecosystem Relationship Modeling creates a structured map of external architectural connections.
