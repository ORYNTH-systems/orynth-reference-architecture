# Relationship Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for ecosystem relationship management.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- relationship identifier;
- connected systems;
- relationship type;
- purpose;
- interaction conditions;
- governance requirements;
- status history.

---

## Relationship Lifecycle


RELATIONSHIP IDENTIFIED

↓

CONTEXT REVIEWED

↓

RELATIONSHIP CLASSIFIED

↓

GOVERNANCE APPLIED

↓

STATUS MAINTAINED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Relationship States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- ANALYZING;
- MAPPED;
- ACTIVE;
- REVIEW REQUIRED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Relationship Status Records maintain accountability for ecosystem interactions.
