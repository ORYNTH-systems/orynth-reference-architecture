# Interoperability Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structural model for governed interoperability.

---

## Interoperability Principle

Systems interact through defined relationships, translation mechanisms, and governance conditions.

---

## Interoperability Model


SYSTEM A

INTERFACE / TRANSLATION LAYER

GOVERNANCE CONDITIONS

SYSTEM B

↓

VALIDATED INTERACTION


---

## Interoperability Categories

### Informational Interoperability

Exchange of knowledge or evidence.

### Semantic Interoperability

Preservation of meaning across systems.

### Functional Interoperability

Exchange of capabilities or operations.

### Governance Interoperability

Alignment of rules, constraints, and authority.

---

## Interoperability Factors

Includes:

- compatibility;
- translation accuracy;
- authority alignment;
- evidence requirements;
- continuity impact.

---

## Principle

The Interoperability Model defines how independent systems cooperate while maintaining architectural boundaries.
