# Interoperability Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for governed interoperability relationships.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- interoperability identifier;
- participating systems;
- interaction type;
- interface conditions;
- governance requirements;
- validation evidence;
- operational status.

---

## Interoperability Lifecycle


INTEROPERABILITY PROPOSED

↓

SYSTEMS EVALUATED

↓

CONDITIONS DEFINED

↓

VALIDATION COMPLETED

↓

INTERACTION ACTIVE

↓

STATUS PRESERVED


---

## Interoperability States

Possible states:

- PROPOSED;
- EVALUATING;
- DEFINED;
- VALIDATED;
- ACTIVE;
- REVIEW REQUIRED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Interoperability Status Records preserve the operational history of multi-system relationships.
