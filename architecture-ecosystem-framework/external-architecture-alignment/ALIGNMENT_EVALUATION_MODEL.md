# Alignment Evaluation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the evaluation process for determining external architecture compatibility.

---

## Evaluation Principle

Alignment requires understanding both similarities and differences between architectures.

---

## Evaluation Model

---

## Alignment Categories

### Compatible

Architectures share compatible principles or interfaces.

### Complementary

Architectures provide mutually beneficial capabilities.

### Translational

Architectures require mapping between concepts.

### Independent

No direct integration relationship exists.

### Conflicting

Architectural principles require review.

---

## Evaluation Factors

Includes:

- purpose alignment;
- semantic compatibility;
- governance compatibility;
- interoperability potential;
- continuity impact.

---

## Principle

The Alignment Evaluation Model prevents uncontrolled assumptions between distinct architectures.
