# Alignment Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for external architecture alignment activities.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- alignment identifier;
- external architecture;
- relationship category;
- evaluated properties;
- compatibility findings;
- governance assessment;
- final status.

---

## Alignment Lifecycle


ALIGNMENT PROPOSED

↓

EXTERNAL SYSTEM REVIEWED

↓

RELATIONSHIP CLASSIFIED

↓

COMPATIBILITY VERIFIED

↓

STATUS RECORDED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Alignment States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- EVALUATING;
- ALIGNED;
- COMPLEMENTARY;
- REQUIRES REVIEW;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Alignment Status Records preserve the history of external architectural relationships.
