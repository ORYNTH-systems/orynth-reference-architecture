# Cross-System Operational Integration

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to integrate operational processes, system capabilities, intelligence structures, and governance mechanisms across ORYNTH systems.

---

## Integration Principle

Cross-system operational integration enables independent systems to coordinate through defined interfaces, shared operational states, preserved relationships, and validated transitions.

---

## Integration Domains

Includes:

- system interoperability;
- operational coordination;
- execution alignment;
- governance synchronization;
- intelligence exchange;
- continuity management.

---

## Integration Lifecycle


IDENTIFY SYSTEM INTERFACES

↓

MAP OPERATIONAL RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ALIGN SYSTEM CONDITIONS

↓

COORDINATE OPERATIONAL PROCESSES

↓

VERIFY INTEGRATION STATE

↓

PRESERVE OPERATIONAL HISTORY


---

## Principle

A cross-system operational integration layer provides the structure required for coordinated operation across the ORYNTH ecosystem.
