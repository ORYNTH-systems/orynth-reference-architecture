# Operating Intelligence Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track operating intelligence states, signals, adaptive responses, and operational transitions.

---

## Register Principle

Operating intelligence records preserve relationships between observed conditions, analytical interpretation, adaptive decisions, and resulting operational states.

---

## Intelligence Fields

Includes:

- operational signal;
- system condition;
- intelligence assessment;
- adaptive response;
- validation status;
- historical record.

---

## Register Lifecycle


CAPTURE OPERATIONAL SIGNAL

↓

CLASSIFY OPERATING CONDITION

↓

ANALYZE INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

GENERATE ADAPTIVE RESPONSE

↓

VERIFY OPERATIONAL EFFECT

↓

UPDATE REGISTER


---

## Principle

An operating intelligence register maintains traceability between operational conditions and adaptive system behavior.
