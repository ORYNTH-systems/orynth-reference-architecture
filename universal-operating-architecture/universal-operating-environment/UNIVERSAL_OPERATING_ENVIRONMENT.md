# Universal Operating Environment

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the operational environment used to coordinate ORYNTH systems, architectures, intelligence capabilities, governance mechanisms, and adaptive processes as a unified operating structure.

---

## Operating Environment Principle

A universal operating environment provides the shared context in which ORYNTH systems interact, execute, adapt, and preserve continuity across evolving operational conditions.

---

## Environment Domains

Includes:

- execution environment;
- governance environment;
- intelligence environment;
- operational environment;
- adaptive environment;
- ecosystem coordination.

---
