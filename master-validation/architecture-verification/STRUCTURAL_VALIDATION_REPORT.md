# Structural Validation Report

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record format used to evaluate ORYNTH structural completeness and architectural organization.

---

## Structural Validation Principle

Structural validation confirms that architecture layers, components, and relationships form a coherent and maintainable system.

---

## Validation Domains

Includes:

- architecture organization;
- framework placement;
- dependency structure;
- documentation completeness;
- evolution readiness.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


REVIEW STRUCTURE

↓

COMPARE DESIGN

↓

IDENTIFY VARIANCE

↓

ASSESS COMPLETENESS

↓

DOCUMENT FINDINGS

↓

ISSUE VALIDATION STATUS


---

## Principle

Structural validation provides a formal reference for architectural confidence and continued evolution.
