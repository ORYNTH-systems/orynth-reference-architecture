# Architecture Verification Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the verification process used to confirm ORYNTH architectural structure, relationships, and system coherence.

---

## Verification Principle

Architecture verification confirms that defined components, relationships, and capabilities operate according to established architectural requirements.

---

## Verification Domains

Includes:

- structural verification;
- component verification;
- relationship verification;
- capability verification;
- lifecycle verification.

---

## Verification Lifecycle


DEFINE ARCHITECTURE

↓

IDENTIFY REQUIREMENTS

↓

VERIFY COMPONENTS

↓

ASSESS RELATIONSHIPS

↓

DOCUMENT RESULTS

↓

PRESERVE EVIDENCE


---

## Principle

Architecture verification establishes confidence that the integrated system maintains structural integrity.
