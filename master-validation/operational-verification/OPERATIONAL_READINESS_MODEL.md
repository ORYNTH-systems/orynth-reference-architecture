# Operational Readiness Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH operational capability is evaluated before transition into sustained execution.

---

## Readiness Principle

Operational readiness requires alignment between architecture, capabilities, resources, governance, and execution requirements.

---

## Readiness Domains

Includes:

- system availability;
- execution capability;
- resource readiness;
- governance readiness;
- continuity readiness.

---

## Readiness Lifecycle


ASSESS REQUIREMENTS

↓

REVIEW CAPABILITIES

↓

VERIFY CONDITIONS

↓

EVALUATE READINESS

↓

DOCUMENT STATUS

↓

MAINTAIN RECORD


---

## Principle

Operational readiness establishes confidence that architecture can transition into sustained operation.
