# Governance Verification Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the verification process used to confirm that ORYNTH governance structures maintain alignment, accountability, and continuity.

---

## Governance Verification Principle

Governance verification confirms that authority, responsibility, stewardship, and decision pathways remain explicitly defined and traceable.

---

## Verification Domains

Includes:

- governance structure;
- responsibility assignment;
- accountability pathways;
- stewardship continuity;
- decision traceability.

---

## Verification Lifecycle


IDENTIFY GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE

↓

REVIEW RESPONSIBILITIES

↓

VERIFY AUTHORITY PATHWAYS

↓

ASSESS CONTINUITY

↓

DOCUMENT RESULTS

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Governance verification ensures system evolution remains aligned with foundational governance requirements.
