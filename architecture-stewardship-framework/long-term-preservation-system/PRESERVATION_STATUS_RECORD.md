# Preservation Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for long-term preservation activities.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- preservation identifier;
- preserved artifact;
- reference version;
- evidence relationship;
- storage location;
- verification status;
- preservation history.

---

## Preservation Lifecycle


PRESERVATION INITIATED

↓

ARTIFACT IDENTIFIED

↓

INTEGRITY VERIFIED

↓

RECORD CREATED

↓

PERIODIC REVIEW

↓

CONTINUITY MAINTAINED


---

## Preservation States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- PRESERVED;
- VERIFIED;
- REVIEWING;
- UPDATED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Preservation Status Records maintain accountability between architectural history and future accessibility.
