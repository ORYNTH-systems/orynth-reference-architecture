## Preservation Categories

### Artifact Preservation

Maintains documents, specifications, and implementations.

### Semantic Preservation

Maintains intended meaning.

### Evidence Preservation

Maintains validation and verification records.

### Historical Preservation

Maintains evolution history.

### Access Preservation

Maintains future discoverability.

## Preservation Factors

Includes:

- integrity;
- traceability;
- recoverability;
- accessibility;
- continuity.

## Principle

The Preservation Model ensures future users can reconstruct both the architecture and its governing context.
