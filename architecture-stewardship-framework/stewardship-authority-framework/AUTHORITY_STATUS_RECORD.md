# Authority Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for stewardship authority assignments and actions.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- authority identifier;
- assigned steward;
- authority category;
- scope of responsibility;
- governance constraints;
- review history;
- current status.

---

## Authority Lifecycle


AUTHORITY PROPOSED

↓

SCOPE REVIEWED

↓

AUTHORITY ASSIGNED

↓

ACTIVITY TRACKED

↓

AUTHORITY REVIEWED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Authority States

Possible states:

- PROPOSED;
- ASSIGNED;
- ACTIVE;
- REVIEWING;
- TRANSITIONING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Authority Status Records preserve accountability between stewardship responsibility and architectural continuity.
