# Stewardship Authority Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the authority framework required to preserve and govern the ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture across future custodial states.

---

## Authority Principle

Stewardship authority exists to preserve architectural continuity, not to arbitrarily alter foundational identity.

Authority is bounded by:

- constitutional principles;
- evidence requirements;
- governance rules;
- historical continuity;
- reference architecture integrity.

---

## Authority Domains

Includes:

- preservation authority;
- review authority;
- certification authority;
- revision authority;
- archival authority;
- succession authority.

---

## Authority Lifecycle


STEWARDSHIP ROLE ESTABLISHED

↓

AUTHORITY SCOPE DEFINED

↓

RESPONSIBILITY ACCEPTED

↓

DECISIONS GOVERNED

↓

AUTHORITY REVIEWED

↓

SUCCESSION PRESERVED


---

## Authority Requirements

Stewardship authority must preserve:

- original architectural intent;
- semantic continuity;
- evidence lineage;
- governance boundaries;
- public reference integrity.

---

## Principle

The Stewardship Authority Framework ensures custodians maintain continuity without compromising the architecture's foundational identity.
