# Authority Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structural model of stewardship authority.

---

## Authority Principle

Authority must be explicit, bounded, and traceable.

---

## Authority Structure


REFERENCE ARCHITECTURE

    ↓

STEWARD AUTHORITY

    ↓

GOVERNANCE REVIEW

    ↓

AUTHORIZED ACTION

    ↓

RECORDED OUTCOME


---

## Authority Categories

### Preservation Authority

Maintains existing reference state.

### Review Authority

Evaluates proposed changes.

### Certification Authority

Confirms validated architectural states.

### Revision Authority

Approves governed evolution.

### Succession Authority

Maintains continuity across custodial transitions.

---

## Authority Constraints

Authority cannot:

- remove historical lineage;
- bypass validation requirements;
- redefine foundational meaning without governance;
- erase evidence relationships.

---

## Principle

The Authority Model ensures stewardship power remains aligned with preservation responsibility.
