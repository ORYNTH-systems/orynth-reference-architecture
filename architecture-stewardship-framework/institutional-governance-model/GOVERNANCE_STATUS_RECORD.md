# Governance Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for institutional governance activities.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- governance identifier;
- responsible authority;
- decision scope;
- active responsibilities;
- review history;
- governance condition.

---

## Governance Lifecycle


GOVERNANCE INITIATED

↓

AUTHORITY DEFINED

↓

RESPONSIBILITIES ASSIGNED

↓

DECISIONS TRACKED

↓

STATUS REVIEWED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Governance States

Possible states:

- ESTABLISHING;
- ACTIVE;
- REVIEWING;
- UPDATED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Governance Status Records preserve institutional accountability across the lifetime of the architecture.
