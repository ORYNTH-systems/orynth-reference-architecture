# Governance Structure Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structural organization of institutional governance.

---

## Structure Principle

Governance requires clearly defined roles, responsibilities, and relationships.

---

## Governance Structure


ARCHITECTURE STEWARD

    ↓

GOVERNANCE AUTHORITY

    ↓

SPECIALIZED REVIEW FUNCTIONS

    ↓

IMPLEMENTATION PARTICIPANTS

    ↓

REFERENCE COMMUNITY


---

## Governance Roles

### Architecture Steward

Maintains foundational integrity and reference continuity.

### Governance Authority

Reviews decisions affecting architecture direction.

### Review Function

Evaluates technical, semantic, and operational changes.

### Implementation Participant

Applies governed architecture within operational environments.

### Reference Community

Uses, evaluates, and contributes feedback.

---

## Governance Factors

Includes:

- authority scope;
- responsibility assignment;
- review requirements;
- decision pathways;
- accountability mechanisms.

---

## Principle

The Governance Structure Model establishes clear responsibility boundaries for long-term stewardship.
