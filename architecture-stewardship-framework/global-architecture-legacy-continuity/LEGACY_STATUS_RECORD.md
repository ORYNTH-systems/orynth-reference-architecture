# Legacy Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for global architecture legacy continuity.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- legacy identifier;
- originating reference state;
- stewardship history;
- preservation status;
- successor relationships;
- continuity validation;
- historical record.

---

## Legacy Lifecycle


LEGACY RECORD CREATED

↓

ORIGIN DOCUMENTED

↓

STEWARDSHIP TRACKED

↓

CONTINUITY VERIFIED

↓

SUCCESSION RECORDED

↓

LEGACY PRESERVED


---

## Legacy States

Possible states:

- ORIGINATING;
- PRESERVED;
- STEWARDED;
- TRANSITIONING;
- CONTINUING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Legacy Status Records preserve the continuity relationship between past, present, and future architectural states.
