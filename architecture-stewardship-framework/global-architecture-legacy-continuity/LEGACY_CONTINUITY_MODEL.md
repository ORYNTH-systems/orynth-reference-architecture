## Continuity Categories

### Origin Continuity

Preserves the initial architectural purpose.

### Knowledge Continuity

Preserves accumulated understanding.

### Governance Continuity

Preserves decision structures.

### Technical Continuity

Preserves implementation relationships.

### Stewardship Continuity

Preserves responsible custodianship.

- traceability;
- recoverability;
- authority transfer;
- historical accuracy;
- future accessibility.

## Principle

The Legacy Continuity Model ensures future architecture remains connected to its foundational state.
