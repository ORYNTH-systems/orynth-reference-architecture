## Purpose

Define the continuity framework for preserving ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture as a long-term global reference structure.

---

## Legacy Principle

A lasting architecture is preserved through continuity of:

- identity;
- meaning;
- evidence;
- governance;
- stewardship;
- historical lineage.

Legacy is not static preservation. It is the continued ability of future participants to understand, validate, and responsibly evolve the architecture.

---

## Legacy Domains

Includes:

- originating principles;

- stewardship transitions;
- historical records;
- future custodianship;
- public reference continuity.
