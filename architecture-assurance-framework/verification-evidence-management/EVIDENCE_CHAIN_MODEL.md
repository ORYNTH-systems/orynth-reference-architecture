# Evidence Chain Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the relationship model connecting assurance claims to supporting evidence.

---

## Evidence Chain Principle

Every assurance statement should be traceable through a chain connecting requirement, evidence, validation, and conclusion.

---

## Evidence Chain Structure


ASSURANCE CLAIM

↓

REQUIRED CONDITION

↓

SUPPORTING ARTIFACT

↓

VALIDATION ACTIVITY

↓

VERIFICATION RESULT

↓

CERTIFIED CONCLUSION


---

## Evidence Relationship Types

Includes:

### Direct Evidence

Evidence immediately supporting a condition.

### Derived Evidence

Evidence produced through analysis or validation.

### Historical Evidence

Evidence preserving previous architecture states.

### Comparative Evidence

Evidence showing alignment between states.

---

## Principle

The Evidence Chain Model ensures assurance conclusions remain traceable and reconstructable.
