# Verification Evidence Management

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the evidence management framework supporting ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture assurance.

---

## Evidence Principle

Assurance requires traceable evidence demonstrating that architecture conditions, governance states, and validation outcomes are supported by preserved records.

Evidence is not only collected; it is structured, related, and maintained.

---

## Evidence Domains

Includes:

- architecture artifacts;
- validation records;
- governance records;
- observability records;
- change history;
- certification records.

---

## Evidence Lifecycle


ASSURANCE REQUIREMENT

↓

EVIDENCE IDENTIFICATION

↓

EVIDENCE COLLECTION

↓

EVIDENCE LINKING

↓

EVIDENCE VALIDATION

↓

EVIDENCE PRESERVATION

↓

ASSURANCE SUPPORT


---

## Evidence Requirements

Evidence must preserve:

- origin;
- context;
- relationship;
- validation status;
- version lineage;
- reconstruction capability.

---

## Principle

Verification Evidence Management establishes the foundation for defensible architectural assurance.
