# Evidence Validation Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the validation record structure for assurance evidence.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- evidence identifier;
- evidence source;
- related architecture component;
- validation method;
- reviewer or authority;
- validation result;
- preservation status.

---

## Evidence Validation Lifecycle


EVIDENCE IDENTIFIED

↓

SOURCE VERIFIED

↓

RELATIONSHIP CONFIRMED

↓

VALIDATION PERFORMED

↓

RESULT RECORDED

↓

EVIDENCE PRESERVED


---

## Validation States

Possible states:

- VERIFIED;
- PARTIALLY VERIFIED;
- REQUIRES REVIEW;
- INVALIDATED.

---

## Principle

Evidence Validation Records preserve the integrity of the assurance evidence chain.
