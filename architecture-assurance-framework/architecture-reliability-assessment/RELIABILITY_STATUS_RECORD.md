# Reliability Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for architecture reliability assessments.

---

## Status Record Fields

Includes:

- assessment identifier;
- architecture version;
- assessment scope;
- evaluated conditions;
- supporting evidence;
- reliability result;
- identified risks;
- follow-up actions.

---

## Reliability Lifecycle


ASSESSMENT INITIATED

↓

CONDITIONS EVALUATED

↓

EVIDENCE REVIEWED

↓

RELIABILITY STATUS ASSIGNED

↓

ACTIONS TRACKED

↓

ASSESSMENT PRESERVED


---

## Reliability States

Possible states:

- RELIABLE;
- RELIABLE WITH CONDITIONS;
- UNDER REVIEW;
- REASSESSMENT REQUIRED.

---

## Principle

Reliability Status Records preserve the ongoing assurance history of the Morning Star architecture.
