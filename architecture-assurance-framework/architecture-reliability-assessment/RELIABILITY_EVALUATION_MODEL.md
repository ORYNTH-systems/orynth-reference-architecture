# Reliability Evaluation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the evaluation model used to assess architectural reliability.

---

## Evaluation Principle

Reliability evaluation compares observed architecture conditions against established assurance requirements.

---

## Evaluation Model


ASSURANCE BASELINE

CURRENT ARCHITECTURE STATE

VERIFICATION EVIDENCE

↓

RELIABILITY ANALYSIS

↓

RELIABILITY DETERMINATION


---

## Evaluation Categories

### Stable

Architecture maintains expected conditions.

### Resilient

Architecture continues operating through controlled change.

### At Risk

Potential reliability concern identified.

### Requires Review

Insufficient evidence or unresolved condition exists.

---

## Assessment Factors

Includes:

- consistency;
- traceability;
- recoverability;
- validation coverage;
- governance alignment;
- continuity preservation.

---

## Principle

The Reliability Evaluation Model provides a repeatable method for determining architectural confidence.
