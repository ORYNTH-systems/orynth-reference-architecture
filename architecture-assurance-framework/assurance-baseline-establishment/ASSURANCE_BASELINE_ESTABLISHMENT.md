# Assurance Baseline Establishment

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the initial assurance baseline for the ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Assurance Principle

An assurance baseline establishes the validated condition from which architectural reliability, verification, and continuity are assessed.

The baseline represents a known state supported by documentation, governance, observability, and validation evidence.

---

## Baseline Domains

Includes:

- architecture identity;
- constitutional alignment;
- governance state;
- observability state;
- documentation completeness;
- validation evidence;
- continuity condition.

---

## Baseline Establishment Lifecycle


CERTIFIED ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

COLLECT VALIDATION EVIDENCE

↓

VERIFY GOVERNANCE ALIGNMENT

↓

VERIFY OBSERVABILITY CONDITION

↓

ESTABLISH ASSURANCE BASELINE

↓

PRESERVE BASELINE RECORD


---

## Baseline Requirements

The assurance baseline preserves:

- reference architecture;
- version identity;
- validation history;
- evidence lineage;
- governance boundaries;
- continuity state.

---

## Principle

The assurance baseline provides the trusted starting point for evaluating future architectural reliability.
