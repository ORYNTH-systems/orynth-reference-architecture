# Continuity Validation Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the validation record used to confirm assurance continuity.

---

## Continuity Validation Principle

A transition maintains assurance when the relationship between prior and current states can be verified.

---

## Validation Record Fields

Includes:

- previous assurance state;
- current assurance state;
- transition description;
- evidence comparison;
- validation method;
- continuity result;
- approval status.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


PREVIOUS STATE IDENTIFIED

↓

CURRENT STATE ASSESSED

↓

TRANSITION ANALYZED

↓

EVIDENCE COMPARED

↓

CONTINUITY VERIFIED

↓

RECORD PRESERVED


---

## Continuity Results

Possible states:

- CONTINUOUS;
- CONTINUOUS WITH CONDITIONS;
- REQUIRES REVIEW;
- BROKEN.

---

## Principle

Continuity Validation Records ensure future architecture states remain connected to their validated origins.
