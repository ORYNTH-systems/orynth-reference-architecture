# Assurance History Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the historical record structure for assurance states over time.

---

## History Principle

Every assurance state maintains traceable connection to previous assessments, evidence, and validated conditions.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- assurance identifier;
- architecture version;
- baseline reference;
- assessment results;
- evidence references;
- reliability status;
- continuity status;
- transition history.

---

## History Lifecycle


ASSURANCE STATE CREATED

↓

VALIDATION PERFORMED

↓

STATUS RECORDED

↓

REFERENCE PRESERVED

↓

FUTURE COMPARISON ENABLED


---

## Assurance States

Possible states:

- ESTABLISHED;
- VERIFIED;
- MAINTAINED;
- TRANSITIONING;
- SUPERSEDED.

---

## Principle

Assurance History Records preserve the complete lineage of architectural assurance.
