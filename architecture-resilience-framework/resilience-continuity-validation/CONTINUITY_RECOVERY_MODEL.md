# Continuity Recovery Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the relationship between recovery processes and architectural continuity.

---

## Recovery Continuity Principle

Recovery is successful when the restored or adapted state maintains verified connection to the previous valid state.

---

## Continuity Recovery Model


VALIDATED STATE

↓

DISRUPTION

↓

RECOVERY PROCESS

↓

RESTORED / ADAPTED STATE

↓

COMPARISON AGAINST BASELINE

↓

CONTINUITY DECISION


---

## Recovery Outcomes

### Continuous Recovery

Architecture restored while preserving identity.

### Adaptive Continuity

Architecture changed while preserving defining properties.

### Conditional Continuity

Architecture requires additional validation.

### Broken Continuity

Architecture no longer maintains required relationships.

---

## Principle

The Continuity Recovery Model ensures resilience actions preserve architectural lineage.
