# Resilience Validation Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for resilience continuity validation.

---

## Validation Record Fields

Includes:

- validation identifier;
- affected architecture state;
- disruption condition;
- recovery action;
- adaptation action;
- evidence references;
- continuity result;
- final resilience status.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


VALIDATION INITIATED

↓

STATE HISTORY REVIEWED

↓

RECOVERY ANALYZED

↓

CONTINUITY VERIFIED

↓

RESILIENCE STATUS ASSIGNED

↓

RECORD PRESERVED


---

## Validation States

Possible states:

- CONTINUOUS;
- CONTINUOUS WITH CONDITIONS;
- REQUIRES REVIEW;
- DISCONTINUOUS.

---

## Principle

Resilience Validation Records preserve evidence that Morning Star remains coherent through disruption and evolution.
