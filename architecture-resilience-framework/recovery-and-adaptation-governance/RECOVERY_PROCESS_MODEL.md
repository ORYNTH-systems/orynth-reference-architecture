# Recovery Process Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the controlled recovery process for architectural disruption.

---

## Recovery Principle

Recovery is a governed transition from a disrupted state toward a validated stable state.

---

## Recovery Model


DISRUPTED STATE

↓

FAILURE ANALYSIS

↓

RECOVERY PLAN

↓

IMPLEMENTATION

↓

VALIDATION

↓

RESTORED STATE


---

## Recovery Actions

Includes:

### Restore

Return architecture to validated prior condition.

### Repair

Correct identified integrity issue.

### Reconstruct

Rebuild missing or damaged relationships.

### Adapt

Introduce validated improvements.

---

## Principle

The Recovery Process Model preserves architectural continuity during restoration activities.
