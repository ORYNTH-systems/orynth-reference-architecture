# Adaptation Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for governed architectural adaptation.

---

## Adaptation Principle

Adaptation represents intentional evolution validated against resilience and continuity requirements.

---

## Status Record Fields

Includes:

- adaptation identifier;
- triggering condition;
- affected architecture layer;
- proposed modification;
- impact assessment;
- validation evidence;
- final disposition.

---

## Adaptation Lifecycle


ADAPTATION IDENTIFIED

↓

PROPOSAL CREATED

↓

IMPACT REVIEW

↓

GOVERNANCE VALIDATION

↓

IMPLEMENTATION

↓

STATUS PRESERVED


---

## Adaptation States

Possible states:

- PROPOSED;
- REVIEWING;
- APPROVED;
- IMPLEMENTED;
- VERIFIED;
- REJECTED.

---

## Principle

Adaptation Status Records ensure resilience-driven evolution remains controlled, traceable, and governed.
