# Recovery and Adaptation Governance

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the governance framework for recovery and adaptation following architectural disruption.

---

## Recovery Principle

Recovery restores architectural coherence while preserving identity, evidence, governance, and continuity.

Adaptation permits validated change when restoration of the previous state is insufficient.

---

## Recovery Domains

Includes:

- state restoration;
- dependency restoration;
- evidence restoration;
- governance restoration;
- continuity restoration.

---

## Adaptation Domains

Includes:

- controlled modification;
- capability improvement;
- resilience strengthening;
- validated evolution.

---

## Recovery and Adaptation Lifecycle


FAILURE CONDITION

↓

IMPACT ASSESSMENT

↓

RECOVERY DECISION

↓

RESTORATION OR ADAPTATION PATH

↓

VALIDATION

↓

UPDATED RESILIENCE STATE


---

## Governance Requirements

Recovery and adaptation must preserve:

- architectural identity;
- semantic boundaries;
- evidence lineage;
- governance authority;
- continuity relationships.

---

## Principle

Recovery and Adaptation Governance ensures Morning Star can respond to disruption without uncontrolled architectural divergence.
