# Resilience Reference Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the reference model used for resilience evaluation.

---

## Reference Principle

Resilience evaluation requires a known baseline describing what must remain preserved during disruption.

---

## Resilience Reference Domains

### Identity Preservation

Ability to maintain architectural definition and purpose.

### Functional Preservation

Ability to maintain intended capabilities.

### Governance Preservation

Ability to maintain controlled decision processes.

### Evidence Preservation

Ability to maintain traceability and verification records.

### Continuity Preservation

Ability to maintain connection between architecture states.

### Adaptive Capacity

Ability to respond through governed change.

---

## Reference Model


BASELINE CONDITION

DISRUPTION EVENT

↓

RESILIENCE RESPONSE

↓

STATE EVALUATION

↓

CONTINUITY DETERMINATION


---

## Principle

The Resilience Reference Model defines the criteria for determining whether Morning Star remains coherent under changing conditions.
