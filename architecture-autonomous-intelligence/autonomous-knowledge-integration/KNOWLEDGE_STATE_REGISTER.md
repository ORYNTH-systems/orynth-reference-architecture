# Knowledge State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track integrated knowledge states, source relationships, validation outcomes, and intelligence effects.

---

## State Principle

Knowledge states preserve relationships between knowledge sources, validation processes, integrated concepts, intelligence capabilities, and resulting architecture states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- knowledge identity;
- source reference;
- validation condition;
- contextual relationship;
- integration method;
- resulting intelligence capability;
- continuity status;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE KNOWLEDGE RECORD

↓

CAPTURE SOURCE INFORMATION

↓

VALIDATE KNOWLEDGE CONDITION

↓

MAP RELATIONSHIPS

↓

INTEGRATE KNOWLEDGE STATE

↓

ASSESS INTELLIGENCE IMPACT

↓

PRESERVE KNOWLEDGE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A knowledge state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH autonomous intelligence processes and integrated knowledge evolution.
