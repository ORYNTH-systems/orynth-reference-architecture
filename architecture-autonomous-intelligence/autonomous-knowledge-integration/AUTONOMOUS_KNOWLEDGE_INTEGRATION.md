# Autonomous Knowledge Integration

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to integrate validated knowledge into ORYNTH autonomous intelligence processes while preserving provenance, relationships, governance constraints, and continuity.

---

## Knowledge Integration Principle

Autonomous knowledge integration enables intelligence systems to incorporate validated information, establish meaningful relationships, and improve future reasoning capabilities without compromising architectural integrity.

Knowledge becomes intelligence through governed integration.

---

- source provenance;

- contextual alignment;
- intelligence enrichment;
- governance review;
- continuity preservation.
