# Autonomous Continuity State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track autonomous intelligence continuity states, transitions, validation outcomes, and preserved lineage.

---

## State Principle

Continuity states preserve relationships between intelligence formation states, optimization processes, integrated knowledge, evolutionary transitions, and resulting autonomous configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- continuity identity;
- source intelligence state;
- optimization relationship;
- knowledge relationship;
- transition condition;
- validation status;
- resulting intelligence state;
- historical lineage.

---

CREATE CONTINUITY RECORD

LINK HISTORICAL RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ANALYZE AUTONOMOUS TRANSITION

↓

↓

REGISTER AUTONOMOUS STATE

↓

PRESERVE LINEAGE

An autonomous continuity state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH autonomous intelligence evolution and preserved intelligence identity.
