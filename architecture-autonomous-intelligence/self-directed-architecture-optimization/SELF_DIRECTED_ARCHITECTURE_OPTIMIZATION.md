## Purpose

Define the framework used to enable autonomous intelligence processes to evaluate and optimize ORYNTH architecture states through governed refinement.

---

## Optimization Principle

Self-directed architecture optimization enables intelligence systems to identify improvement opportunities, evaluate potential refinements, and apply validated optimization pathways while preserving architecture identity and continuity.

Optimization is autonomous refinement under governance.

- governance alignment;
- adaptive improvement;
- continuity preservation.
