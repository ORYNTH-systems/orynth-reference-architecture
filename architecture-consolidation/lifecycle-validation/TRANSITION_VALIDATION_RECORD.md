# Transition Validation Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how major architectural transitions are documented, reviewed, and preserved.

---

## Transition Principle

Architectural transitions require evidence that changes preserve continuity while enabling advancement.

---

## Transition Record Elements

Includes:

- previous state;
- transition objective;
- changed components;
- validation evidence;
- resulting state.

---

## Transition Lifecycle


IDENTIFY TRANSITION

↓

DOCUMENT CHANGE

↓

VERIFY CONDITIONS

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

APPROVE TRANSITION

↓

ARCHIVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Transition records preserve the historical pathway of architecture evolution.
