# Architecture Maturity Review

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH architecture maturity is evaluated across capability, governance, and operational dimensions.

---

## Maturity Principle

Architecture maturity reflects the degree of structural completeness, validation, integration, and operational readiness.

---

## Maturity Domains

Includes:

- structural maturity;
- capability maturity;
- governance maturity;
- validation maturity;
- ecosystem maturity.

---

## Maturity Review Lifecycle


ASSESS ARCHITECTURE

↓

MEASURE COMPLETENESS

↓

EVALUATE CAPABILITY

↓

REVIEW GOVERNANCE

↓

IDENTIFY IMPROVEMENT

↓

UPDATE MATURITY STATE


---

## Principle

Maturity review provides a structured understanding of architectural progression.
