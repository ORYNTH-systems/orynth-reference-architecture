# Lifecycle Validation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH architecture evolution is validated across development phases, transitions, and maturity states.

---

## Lifecycle Validation Principle

Architecture lifecycle validation confirms that system evolution preserves identity, continuity, and intended capability.

---

## Validation Domains

Includes:

- developmental progression;
- architectural continuity;
- capability evolution;
- governance alignment;
- operational readiness.

---

## Lifecycle Validation Process


IDENTIFY CURRENT STATE

↓

COMPARE ARCHITECTURAL CONDITIONS

↓

EVALUATE TRANSITIONS

↓

VERIFY CONTINUITY

↓

DOCUMENT FINDINGS

↓

PRESERVE VALIDATION RECORD


---

## Principle

Lifecycle validation ensures architectural evolution remains traceable and coherent.
