## Purpose

Define the canonical record of the completed ORYNTH architectural ecosystem.

## Architecture Record Principle

The final architecture record preserves the identity, composition, relationships, validation history, and operational state of the ORYNTH ecosystem.

- foundational frameworks;
- governance structures;
- intelligence systems;
- research infrastructure;
- knowledge infrastructure;
- validation records.
