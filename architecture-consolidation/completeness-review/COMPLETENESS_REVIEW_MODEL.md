# Completeness Review Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates whether architectural components, capabilities, governance structures, and operational layers are complete.

---

## Completeness Principle

Architectural completeness requires alignment between defined purpose, implemented structures, validation evidence, and operational readiness.

---

## Completeness Domains

Includes:

- structural completeness;
- documentation completeness;
- capability completeness;
- governance completeness;
- validation completeness.

---

## Completeness Review Lifecycle


DEFINE REVIEW SCOPE

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURAL ELEMENTS

↓

IDENTIFY MISSING COMPONENTS

↓

EVALUATE IMPACT

↓

VERIFY COMPLETION

↓

PRESERVE REVIEW RECORD


---

## Principle

Completeness review provides a final assessment of architectural readiness and continuity.
