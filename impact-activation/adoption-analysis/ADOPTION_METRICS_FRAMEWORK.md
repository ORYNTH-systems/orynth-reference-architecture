# Adoption Metrics Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define measurable indicators for tracking adoption development.

---

## Metric Categories

### Reach Metrics

Measure:

- organizations engaged;
- domains reached;
- research visibility.

---

### Engagement Metrics

Measure:

- interactions;
- reviews;
- collaborations;
- participation.

---

### Implementation Metrics

Measure:

- deployments;
- integrations;
- operational usage.

---

### Growth Metrics

Measure:

- expansion;
- retention;
- ecosystem development.

---

Adoption metrics provide evidence of ecosystem growth and practical value.
