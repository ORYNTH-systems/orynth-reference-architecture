# Institutional Adoption Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how institutions adopt and integrate ORYNTH architectures.

---

## Adoption Stages

### Awareness

Institution discovers ORYNTH capabilities.

---

### Evaluation

Institution reviews relevance and applicability.

---

### Integration

Institution begins structured implementation.

---

### Operationalization

Institution applies systems within workflows.

---

### Evolution

Institution contributes feedback and improvement opportunities.

---

## Adoption Lifecycle


AWARENESS

↓

EVALUATION

↓

INTEGRATION

↓

OPERATION

↓

CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT


---

## Principle

Institutional adoption requires alignment between architecture, objectives, and operational requirements.
