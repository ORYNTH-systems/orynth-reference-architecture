# Case Study Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure for documenting ORYNTH applications, deployments, and observed outcomes.

---

## Case Study Principle

Case studies preserve practical evidence showing how architecture translates into applied results.

---

## Case Study Structure

Each case study includes:

- context;
- objective;
- implemented architecture;
- methodology;
- evidence;
- results;
- lessons learned.

---

## Case Study Lifecycle


SELECT EXAMPLE

↓

DOCUMENT CONTEXT

↓

DESCRIBE IMPLEMENTATION

↓

RECORD RESULTS

↓

ANALYZE OUTCOMES

↓

PUBLISH FINDINGS


---

## Case Study Categories

Includes:

- research applications;
- operational deployments;
- domain implementations;
- ecosystem collaborations.

---

## Principle

Case studies connect architectural theory with observable application.
