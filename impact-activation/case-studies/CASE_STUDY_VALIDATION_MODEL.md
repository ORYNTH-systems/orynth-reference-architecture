# Case Study Validation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define validation requirements for documented case studies.

---

## Validation Areas

### Context Validation

Verify:

- documented environment;
- objectives;
- constraints.

---

### Implementation Validation

Verify:

- applied architecture;
- methodology;
- execution process.

---

### Outcome Validation

Verify:

- evidence;
- results;
- limitations;
- conclusions.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


DOCUMENT

↓

VERIFY

↓

ANALYZE

↓

CLASSIFY

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Principle

Case study credibility depends on traceable evidence and defined validation boundaries.
