# Outcome Record Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH outcomes are recorded, classified, and preserved.

---

## Outcome Principle

Outcomes provide measurable evidence of architectural application, deployment results, and ecosystem effects.

---

## Outcome Record Structure

Each record includes:

- outcome identifier;
- originating system;
- application context;
- objective;
- evidence;
- result;
- limitations;
- future actions.

---

## Outcome Categories

Includes:

- technical outcomes;
- operational outcomes;
- research outcomes;
- institutional outcomes;
- ecosystem outcomes.

---

## Outcome Lifecycle


GENERATE

↓

DOCUMENT

↓

VALIDATE

↓

CLASSIFY

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Principle

Outcome records preserve measurable evidence for future analysis.
