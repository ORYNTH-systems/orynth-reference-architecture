# Impact Indicator Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define indicators used to evaluate ORYNTH impact.

---

## Indicator Categories

### Capability Indicators

Measure:

- system capability;
- implementation progress;
- technical advancement.

---

### Adoption Indicators

Measure:

- usage;
- integration;
- participation.

---

### Outcome Indicators

Measure:

- achieved results;
- improvements;
- measurable effects.

---

### Sustainability Indicators

Measure:

- continuity;
- maintenance;
- long-term viability.

---

## Principle

Indicators should provide meaningful evidence of change.
