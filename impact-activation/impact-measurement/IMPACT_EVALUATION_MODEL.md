# Impact Evaluation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define evaluation methods for measuring ORYNTH outcomes.

---

## Evaluation Areas

### Baseline Comparison

Determine:

- initial state;
- change over time;
- improvement patterns.

---

### Evidence Review

Evaluate:

- records;
- measurements;
- deployment results;
- stakeholder feedback.

---

### Outcome Assessment

Determine:

- achieved impact;
- limitations;
- future opportunities.

---

## Evaluation Lifecycle


ESTABLISH BASELINE

↓

COLLECT DATA

↓

ANALYZE CHANGE

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

REFINE APPROACH


---

## Principle

Evaluation transforms operational experience into measurable knowledge.
