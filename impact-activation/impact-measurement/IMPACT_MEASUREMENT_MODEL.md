# Impact Measurement Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH measures outcomes generated through applied architectures and operational deployments.

---

## Impact Principle

Impact measurement connects architectural activity to observable outcomes through structured evaluation.

---

## Measurement Domains

Includes:

- technical impact;
- operational impact;
- institutional impact;
- ecosystem impact;
- research impact.

---

## Measurement Lifecycle


DEFINE OBJECTIVE

↓

SELECT INDICATORS

↓

COLLECT EVIDENCE

↓

ANALYZE RESULTS

↓

DOCUMENT IMPACT


---

## Principle

Impact measurement requires evidence-based assessment rather than assumption.
