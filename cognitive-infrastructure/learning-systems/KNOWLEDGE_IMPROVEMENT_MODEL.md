# Knowledge Improvement Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH knowledge structures are reviewed, refined, and enhanced.

---

## Improvement Principle

Knowledge improvement increases accuracy, usefulness, and applicability through structured evaluation.

---

## Improvement Areas

Includes:

- accuracy refinement;
- conceptual expansion;
- relationship improvement;
- documentation enhancement;
- application optimization.

---

## Knowledge Improvement Lifecycle


REVIEW KNOWLEDGE

↓

IDENTIFY LIMITATIONS

↓

DEFINE IMPROVEMENT

↓

UPDATE STRUCTURE

↓

VALIDATE CHANGE

↓

PRESERVE VERSION


---

## Principle

Continuous knowledge improvement preserves relevance as systems and environments evolve.
