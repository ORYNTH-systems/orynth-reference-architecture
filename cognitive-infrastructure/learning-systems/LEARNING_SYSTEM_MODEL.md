# Learning System Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH systems acquire, evaluate, refine, and apply knowledge improvements over time.

---

## Learning Principle

Learning transforms experience, evidence, and feedback into improved capability while preserving continuity.

---

## Learning Domains

Includes:

- experience learning;
- research learning;
- operational learning;
- feedback learning;
- capability learning.

---

## Learning Lifecycle


COLLECT EXPERIENCE

↓

ANALYZE INFORMATION

↓

IDENTIFY IMPROVEMENT

↓

UPDATE KNOWLEDGE

↓

APPLY LEARNING

↓

EVALUATE RESULT


---

## Principle

Learning systems enable continuous improvement through structured knowledge evolution.
