# Context Retention Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH maintains contextual relationships required for accurate interpretation and decision-making.

---

## Context Retention Principle

Meaningful information requires preservation of surrounding conditions, relationships, and historical state.

---

## Context Elements

Includes:

- origin;
- relationships;
- dependencies;
- timeline;
- interpretation conditions.

---

## Context Retention Lifecycle


IDENTIFY CONTEXT

↓

CAPTURE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

PRESERVE CONDITIONS

↓

MAINTAIN ASSOCIATIONS

↓

RECALL WHEN REQUIRED

↓

VERIFY RELEVANCE


---

## Principle

Context retention prevents information loss by preserving the conditions that give knowledge meaning.
