# Knowledge Recall Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH retrieves preserved knowledge while maintaining relevance, lineage, and context.

---

## Recall Principle

Knowledge recall connects present requirements with previously preserved information and understanding.

---

## Recall Factors

Includes:

- relevance;
- historical relationship;
- confidence;
- context compatibility;
- source lineage.

---

## Recall Lifecycle


IDENTIFY NEED

↓

SEARCH MEMORY

↓

EVALUATE RELEVANCE

↓

RESTORE CONTEXT

↓

APPLY KNOWLEDGE

↓

RECORD USAGE


---

## Principle

Structured recall enables continuity between accumulated knowledge and future decisions.
