# Logical Alignment Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH maintains consistency between assumptions, evidence, reasoning, and conclusions.

---

## Alignment Principle

Logical alignment ensures that conclusions remain connected to valid premises and available evidence.

---

## Alignment Domains

Includes:

- premise alignment;
- evidence alignment;
- constraint alignment;
- objective alignment;
- outcome alignment.

---

## Alignment Lifecycle


DEFINE PREMISES

↓

VERIFY ASSUMPTIONS

↓

CONNECT EVIDENCE

↓

EVALUATE LOGIC

↓

CHECK CONSISTENCY

↓

PRESERVE RESULT


---

## Principle

Logical alignment protects reasoning integrity across complex systems and decisions.
