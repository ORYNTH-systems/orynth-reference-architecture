# Decision Reasoning Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates decisions through evidence, objectives, constraints, and potential outcomes.

---

## Decision Reasoning Principle

Decisions should emerge from structured evaluation rather than isolated conclusions.

---

## Decision Factors

Includes:

- objective alignment;
- available evidence;
- system conditions;
- constraints;
- potential consequences;
- alternative pathways.

---

## Decision Reasoning Lifecycle


DEFINE DECISION

↓

UNDERSTAND CONTEXT

↓

EVALUATE OPTIONS

↓

ASSESS CONSEQUENCES

↓

SELECT PATHWAY

↓

DOCUMENT RATIONALE


---

## Principle

Decision reasoning preserves the connection between information, evaluation, and resulting action.
