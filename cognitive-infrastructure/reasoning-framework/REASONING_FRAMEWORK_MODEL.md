# Reasoning Framework Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH structures reasoning processes to evaluate information, relationships, conditions, and possible outcomes.

---

## Reasoning Principle

Reasoning transforms structured information into evaluated understanding through explicit analytical pathways.

---

## Reasoning Domains

Includes:

- analytical reasoning;
- systems reasoning;
- contextual reasoning;
- causal reasoning;
- strategic reasoning.

---

## Reasoning Lifecycle


IDENTIFY QUESTION

↓

COLLECT CONTEXT

↓

ANALYZE INFORMATION

↓

EVALUATE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

FORM DETERMINATION

↓

PRESERVE REASONING PATH


---

## Principle

Explicit reasoning pathways preserve transparency, consistency, and reconstructability.
