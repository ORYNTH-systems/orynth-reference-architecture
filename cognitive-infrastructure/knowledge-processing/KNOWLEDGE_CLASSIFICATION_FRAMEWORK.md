# Knowledge Classification Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH categorizes knowledge assets for organization, retrieval, and analysis.

---

## Classification Principle

Knowledge classification creates structured access while preserving relationships between concepts.

---

## Classification Domains

Includes:

- foundational knowledge;
- operational knowledge;
- research knowledge;
- implementation knowledge;
- historical knowledge.

---

## Classification Lifecycle


IDENTIFY KNOWLEDGE

↓

ASSIGN CATEGORY

↓

CONNECT RELATIONSHIPS

↓

VALIDATE CLASSIFICATION

↓

STORE STRUCTURE

↓

ENABLE RETRIEVAL


---

## Principle

Knowledge classification transforms accumulated information into a navigable intelligence structure.
