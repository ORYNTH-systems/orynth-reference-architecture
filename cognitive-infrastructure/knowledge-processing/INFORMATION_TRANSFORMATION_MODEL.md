# Information Transformation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how information changes form while preserving meaning, context, and lineage.

---

## Transformation Principle

Information transformation preserves essential meaning while enabling different forms of analysis, representation, and application.

---

## Transformation Stages

Includes:

- acquisition;
- normalization;
- interpretation;
- synthesis;
- application.

---

## Transformation Lifecycle


INPUT INFORMATION

↓

NORMALIZE STRUCTURE

↓

EXTRACT MEANING

↓

CONNECT CONTEXT

↓

GENERATE KNOWLEDGE

↓

ENABLE APPLICATION


---

## Principle

Controlled transformation allows information to become actionable knowledge without losing origin or context.
