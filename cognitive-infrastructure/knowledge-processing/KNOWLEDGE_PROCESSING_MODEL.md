# Knowledge Processing Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH receives, structures, evaluates, and transforms knowledge into usable system understanding.

---

## Knowledge Processing Principle

Knowledge processing converts raw information into organized, contextualized, and traceable understanding.

---

## Processing Domains

Includes:

- information intake;
- classification;
- contextual analysis;
- relationship mapping;
- knowledge refinement.

---

## Knowledge Processing Lifecycle


COLLECT INFORMATION

↓

CLASSIFY INPUT

↓

ANALYZE CONTEXT

↓

STRUCTURE KNOWLEDGE

↓

VALIDATE UNDERSTANDING

↓

PRESERVE OUTPUT


---

## Principle

Structured knowledge processing enables reliable interpretation while maintaining traceability.
