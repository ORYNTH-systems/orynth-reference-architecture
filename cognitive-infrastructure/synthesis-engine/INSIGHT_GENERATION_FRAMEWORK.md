# Insight Generation Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH produces, evaluates, and preserves insights derived from structured synthesis.

---

## Insight Principle

Insights emerge from validated relationships, patterns, and contextual understanding.

---

## Insight Factors

Includes:

- observed patterns;
- relationship structures;
- evidence connections;
- contextual relevance;
- potential applications.

---

## Insight Lifecycle


OBSERVE INFORMATION

↓

IDENTIFY PATTERN

↓

CONNECT CONTEXT

↓

GENERATE INSIGHT

↓

EVALUATE SIGNIFICANCE

↓

PRESERVE KNOWLEDGE


---

## Principle

Structured insight generation converts complex relationships into usable understanding.
