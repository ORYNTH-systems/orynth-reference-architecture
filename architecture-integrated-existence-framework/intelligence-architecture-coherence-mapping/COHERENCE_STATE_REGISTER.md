# Coherence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track intelligence–architecture coherence states, relationships, validation conditions, and evolutionary lineage.

---

## State Principle

Coherence states preserve relationships between intelligence capabilities, architecture structures, governance conditions, and resulting integrated states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- coherence identity;
- intelligence source;
- architecture context;
- relationship mapping;
- alignment conditions;
- validation status;
- resulting coherence state;
- historical lineage.

---

↓

↓

ANALYZE ALIGNMENT

↓

VALIDATE COHERENCE

↓

REGISTER STATE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY

A coherence state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence structures and their aligned architecture representations.
