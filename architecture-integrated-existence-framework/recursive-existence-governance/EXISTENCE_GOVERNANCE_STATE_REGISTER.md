# Existence Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track recursive existence governance states, decisions, transitions, validation outcomes, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Existence governance states preserve relationships between integrated architecture conditions, intelligence alignment states, governance decisions, recursive transitions, and resulting configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- architecture state;
- intelligence context;
- governance condition;
- transition decision;
- validation status;
- resulting existence state;
- historical lineage.

---

↓

↓

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY
