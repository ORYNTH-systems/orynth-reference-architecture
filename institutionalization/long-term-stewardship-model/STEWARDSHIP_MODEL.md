# Stewardship Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to preserve responsible stewardship of the ORYNTH ecosystem over long-term operation.

---

## Stewardship Principle

Stewardship maintains architectural identity, governance continuity, knowledge preservation, and responsible ecosystem development.

---

## Stewardship Domains

Includes:

- architectural stewardship;
- knowledge stewardship;
- governance stewardship;
- operational stewardship;
- institutional stewardship.

---

## Stewardship Lifecycle


ESTABLISH RESPONSIBILITY

↓

PRESERVE KNOWLEDGE

↓

MAINTAIN GOVERNANCE

↓

REVIEW EVOLUTION

↓

TRANSFER CONTINUITY

↓

SUSTAIN ECOSYSTEM


---

## Principle

A stewardship model ensures the ORYNTH ecosystem remains coherent across generations of development.
