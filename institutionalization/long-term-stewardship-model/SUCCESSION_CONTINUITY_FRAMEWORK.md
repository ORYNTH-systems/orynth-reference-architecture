# Succession Continuity Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define mechanisms for preserving ecosystem continuity through future leadership, organizational, and operational transitions.

---

## Succession Principle

Succession preserves system identity, institutional knowledge, and governance responsibilities during transitions.

---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- leadership continuity;
- knowledge continuity;
- architectural continuity;
- operational continuity;
- governance continuity.

---

A succession continuity framework protects the ecosystem from loss of institutional memory.
