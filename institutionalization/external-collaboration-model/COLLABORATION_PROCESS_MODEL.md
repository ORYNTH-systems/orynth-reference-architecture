# Collaboration Process Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the operational process for initiating, managing, and evaluating collaborative relationships.

---

## Process Principle

Collaboration processes preserve clarity of purpose, responsibilities, expectations, and resulting contributions.

---

## Process Domains

Includes:

- engagement initiation;
- role definition;
- information exchange;
- review process;
- outcome documentation.

---

## Process Lifecycle


INITIATE CONTACT

↓

DEFINE OBJECTIVES

↓

ESTABLISH WORKFLOW

↓

EXECUTE COLLABORATION

↓

EVALUATE RESULT

↓

DOCUMENT OUTCOME


---

## Principle

A defined collaboration process reduces ambiguity and supports sustainable institutional relationships.
