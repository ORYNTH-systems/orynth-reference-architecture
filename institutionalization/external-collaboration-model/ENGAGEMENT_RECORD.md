# Engagement Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for tracking external interactions, collaborations, and institutional engagements.

---

## Engagement Principle

Engagement records preserve relationship history, decisions, contributions, and future opportunities.

---

## Engagement Domains

Includes:

- organization;
- purpose;
- interaction history;
- outcomes;
- next actions.

---

## Engagement Lifecycle


CREATE RECORD

↓

DOCUMENT INTERACTION

↓

TRACK CONTRIBUTION

↓

REVIEW STATUS

↓

UPDATE RELATIONSHIP

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

An engagement record maintains continuity across evolving external relationships.
