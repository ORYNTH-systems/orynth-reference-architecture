# Collaboration Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to establish, manage, and preserve external collaborations involving ORYNTH.

---

## Collaboration Principle

External collaboration requires shared understanding, defined roles, preserved context, and alignment between participating entities.

---

## Collaboration Domains

Includes:

- research collaboration;
- technical collaboration;
- institutional collaboration;
- review collaboration;
- ecosystem collaboration.

---

## Collaboration Lifecycle


IDENTIFY COLLABORATION OPPORTUNITY

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

DEFINE RELATIONSHIP

↓

ESTABLISH ENGAGEMENT

↓

REVIEW OUTCOMES

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A structured collaboration framework enables meaningful partnerships while preserving ORYNTH architectural continuity.
