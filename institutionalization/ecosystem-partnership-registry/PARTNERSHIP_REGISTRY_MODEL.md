# Partnership Registry Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure used to register, organize, and maintain ORYNTH ecosystem partnerships.

---

## Partnership Principle

Partnership records preserve identity, purpose, relationship context, contributions, and continuity across institutional interactions.

---

## Partnership Domains

Includes:

- research partnerships;
- technical partnerships;
- institutional partnerships;
- review partnerships;
- ecosystem partnerships.

---

## Registry Lifecycle


IDENTIFY PARTNER

↓

CLASSIFY RELATIONSHIP

↓

DOCUMENT PURPOSE

↓

TRACK CONTRIBUTION

↓

REVIEW STATUS

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A partnership registry provides a durable reference system for ecosystem relationships.
