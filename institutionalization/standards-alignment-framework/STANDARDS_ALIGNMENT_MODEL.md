# Standards Alignment Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to evaluate and document alignment between ORYNTH architecture and applicable external standards, principles, and reference models.

---

## Alignment Principle

Standards alignment preserves compatibility, clarity, and interoperability while maintaining the unique identity of the ORYNTH architecture.

---

## Alignment Domains

Includes:

- technical standards;
- governance standards;
- documentation standards;
- validation standards;
- interoperability standards.

---

## Alignment Lifecycle


IDENTIFY STANDARD

↓

ANALYZE REQUIREMENTS

↓

MAP ARCHITECTURE

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

DOCUMENT RELATIONSHIP

↓

MAINTAIN RECORD


---

## Principle

Standards alignment enables ORYNTH to communicate effectively with established institutional frameworks while preserving architectural integrity.
