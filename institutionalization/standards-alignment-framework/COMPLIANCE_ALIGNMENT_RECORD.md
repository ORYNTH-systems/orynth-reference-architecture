# Compliance Alignment Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how alignment decisions, evaluations, and supporting evidence are recorded.

---

## Compliance Principle

Compliance alignment records preserve the reasoning, evidence, and conditions associated with institutional requirements.

---

## Record Domains

Includes:

- requirement analysis;
- evidence references;
- alignment status;
- identified gaps;
- corrective actions.

---

## Record Lifecycle


IDENTIFY REQUIREMENT

↓

EVALUATE CONDITION

↓

COLLECT EVIDENCE

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

DOCUMENT STATUS

↓

REVIEW PERIODICALLY


---

## Principle

Compliance alignment records create a durable foundation for institutional review and collaboration.
