# Intelligence State Representation

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the intelligence state representation framework for the ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Intelligence Principle

Intelligence requires structured representation of states, relationships, context, evidence, and interpretation.

An intelligence state is not merely stored information. It is a governed representation of information and its validated relationships.

---

## Intelligence State Domains

Includes:

- observed information;
- contextual relationships;
- interpreted meaning;
- validated knowledge;
- governed intelligence state;
- historical intelligence lineage.

---

## Intelligence State Lifecycle


OBSERVATION

↓

INFORMATION REPRESENTATION

↓

CONTEXTUAL ASSOCIATION

↓

INTERPRETATION

↓

VALIDATION

↓

GOVERNED INTELLIGENCE STATE


---

## Representation Requirements

Intelligence states must preserve:

- source relationships;
- context;
- uncertainty;
- evidence lineage;
- validation status;
- transformation history.

---

## Principle

Intelligence State Representation provides the foundation for governed reasoning and intelligence continuity.
