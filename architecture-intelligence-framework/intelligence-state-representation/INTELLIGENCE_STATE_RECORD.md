# Intelligence State Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for intelligence state tracking.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- intelligence identifier;
- source information;
- observed condition;
- contextual relationships;
- interpretation state;
- validation evidence;
- governance status;
- historical changes.

---

## Intelligence Record Lifecycle


INTELLIGENCE CREATED

↓

CONTEXT ATTACHED

↓

STATE EVALUATED

↓

VALIDATION PERFORMED

↓

GOVERNANCE APPLIED

↓

STATE PRESERVED


---

## Intelligence States

Possible states:

- OBSERVED;
- CONTEXTUALIZED;
- EVALUATED;
- VALIDATED;
- GOVERNED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Intelligence State Records preserve the evolution of intelligence from observation into governed architectural knowledge.
