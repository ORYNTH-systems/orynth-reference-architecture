# Reasoning Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for contextual reasoning operations.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- reasoning identifier;
- intelligence state input;
- contextual factors;
- relationships analyzed;
- reasoning output;
- validation status;
- governance status.

---

## Reasoning Lifecycle


REASONING INITIATED

↓

CONTEXT ASSEMBLED

↓

RELATIONSHIPS ANALYZED

↓

CONCLUSION GENERATED

↓

VALIDATION PERFORMED

↓

REASONING PRESERVED


---

## Reasoning States

Possible states:

- INITIATED;
- CONTEXTUALIZING;
- ANALYZING;
- GENERATED;
- VALIDATED;
- PRESERVED.

---

## Principle

Reasoning Status Records maintain traceability between intelligence inputs, contextual analysis, and resulting understanding.
