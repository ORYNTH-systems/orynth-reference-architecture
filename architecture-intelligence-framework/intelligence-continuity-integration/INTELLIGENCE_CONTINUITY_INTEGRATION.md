# Intelligence Continuity Integration

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the continuity framework for intelligence states within the ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Continuity Principle

Intelligence remains valid through time when its origin, transformation, reasoning, governance, and application relationships remain preserved.

Intelligence continuity prevents isolated conclusions from becoming disconnected architectural knowledge.

---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- source continuity;
- reasoning continuity;
- governance continuity;
- evidence continuity;
- temporal continuity;
- knowledge continuity.

---

## Intelligence Continuity Lifecycle


INTELLIGENCE OBSERVED

↓

↓

↓

GOVERNANCE ALIGNMENT

↓

INTELLIGENCE APPLICATION

↓

↓

CONTINUOUS INTELLIGENCE LINEAGE


---

## Preservation Requirements

Intelligence continuity must preserve:

- origin;
- context;
- transformations;
- validation history;
- governance decisions;
- uncertainty conditions.

---

## Principle

Intelligence Continuity Integration ensures intelligence remains connected across architectural states and future evolution.
