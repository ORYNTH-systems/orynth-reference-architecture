# Intelligence Lineage Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the lineage structure connecting intelligence states across time.

---

## Lineage Principle

Every intelligence state must maintain a traceable relationship to its source conditions, transformations, and resulting applications.

---

## Lineage Model


SOURCE OBSERVATION

↓

INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

CONTEXTUAL INTERPRETATION

↓

GOVERNANCE ALIGNMENT

↓

APPLIED INTELLIGENCE

↓

DERIVED INTELLIGENCE STATES

↓

HISTORICAL LINEAGE


---

## Lineage Components

Includes:

- origin reference;
- transformation record;
- reasoning history;
- governance history;
- validation evidence;
- derived relationships.

---

## Lineage States

Possible states:

- ORIGINATED;
- TRANSFORMED;
- VALIDATED;
- APPLIED;
- DERIVED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

The Intelligence Lineage Model preserves the memory structure of intelligence evolution.
