# Intelligence Continuity Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for intelligence continuity tracking.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- intelligence identifier;
- originating observation;
- intelligence states;
- reasoning transitions;
- governance evaluations;
- application history;
- continuity status.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


CONTINUITY CHECK INITIATED

↓

INTELLIGENCE HISTORY REVIEWED

↓

LINEAGE VERIFIED

↓

RELATIONSHIPS CONFIRMED

↓

CONTINUITY STATUS ASSIGNED

↓

RECORD PRESERVED


---

## Continuity States

Possible states:

- PRESERVED;
- VERIFIED;
- CONDITIONAL;
- REQUIRES REVIEW;
- DISCONTINUOUS.

---

## Principle

Intelligence Continuity Records maintain evidence that intelligence remains coherent throughout transformation and application.
