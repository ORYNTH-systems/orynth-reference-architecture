# Intelligence Alignment Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for intelligence governance alignment.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- intelligence identifier;
- source state;
- reasoning context;
- governance criteria;
- policy evaluation;
- alignment result;
- evidence references;
- historical record.

---

## Alignment Lifecycle


ALIGNMENT INITIATED

↓

INTELLIGENCE REVIEWED

↓

GOVERNANCE CONDITIONS CHECKED

↓

POLICY APPLIED

↓

ALIGNMENT DECISION GENERATED

↓

RECORD PRESERVED


---

## Alignment States

Possible states:

- UNASSESSED;
- REVIEWING;
- ALIGNED;
- CONDITIONALLY ALIGNED;
- RESTRICTED;
- MISALIGNED.

---

## Principle

Intelligence Alignment Records preserve the relationship between intelligence capability and architectural governance.
