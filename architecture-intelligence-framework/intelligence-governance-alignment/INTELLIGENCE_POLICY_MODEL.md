# Intelligence Policy Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define policy requirements governing intelligence states and derived outputs.

---

## Policy Principle

Intelligence policies establish conditions under which intelligence may be trusted, applied, modified, or restricted.

## Policy Categories

### Evidence Policy

Defines acceptable evidence conditions.

### Interpretation Policy

Defines reasoning boundaries.

### Authority Policy

Defines permitted application scope.

### Continuity Policy

Defines preservation requirements.

### Uncertainty Policy

Defines unknown and incomplete states.

---

## Principle

The Intelligence Policy Model provides the governance structure required for responsible intelligence operation.
