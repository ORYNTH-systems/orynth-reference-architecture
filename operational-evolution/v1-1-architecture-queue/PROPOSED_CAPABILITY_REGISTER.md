# Proposed Capability Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure for recording future ORYNTH capabilities under consideration.

---

## Capability Principle

Future capabilities must demonstrate architectural alignment, defined purpose, validation requirements, and ecosystem value.

---

## Capability Categories

Includes:

- research capabilities;
- operational capabilities;
- intelligence capabilities;
- governance capabilities;
- knowledge capabilities.

---

## Capability Lifecycle


IDENTIFY CAPABILITY

↓

DEFINE PURPOSE

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURAL FIT

↓

EVALUATE REQUIREMENTS

↓

SCHEDULE DEVELOPMENT

↓

INTEGRATE CAPABILITY


---

## Principle

A capability register preserves future possibilities while maintaining disciplined evolution.
