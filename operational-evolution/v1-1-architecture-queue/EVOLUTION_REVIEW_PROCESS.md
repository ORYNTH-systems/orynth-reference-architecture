# Evolution Review Process

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the review process for proposed architectural changes and future ecosystem evolution.

---

## Review Principle

Evolution review ensures changes preserve integrity, continuity, and alignment with ORYNTH architecture.

---

## Review Domains

Includes:

- technical review;
- architectural review;
- governance review;
- validation review;
- operational review.

---

## Review Lifecycle


SUBMIT CHANGE

↓

ANALYZE IMPACT

↓

REVIEW ARCHITECTURE

↓

VERIFY REQUIREMENTS

↓

APPROVE OR DEFER

↓

UPDATE EVOLUTION RECORD


---

## Principle

A formal evolution review process enables growth without uncontrolled architectural drift.
