# Review Evidence Index

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evidence artifacts are indexed for external examination.

---

## Evidence Principle

Evidence indexing preserves traceability between claims, documentation, validation results, and supporting records.

---

## Evidence Domains

Includes:

- architecture evidence;
- implementation evidence;
- validation evidence;
- governance evidence;
- release evidence.

---

## Evidence Lifecycle


IDENTIFY CLAIM

↓

MAP SUPPORTING RECORD

↓

VERIFY SOURCE

↓

CONNECT EVIDENCE

↓

ENABLE REVIEW

↓

UPDATE INDEX


---

## Principle

An evidence index provides reviewers with a structured path from assertion to supporting material.
