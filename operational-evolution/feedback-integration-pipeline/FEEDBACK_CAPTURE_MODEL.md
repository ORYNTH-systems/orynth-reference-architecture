# Feedback Capture Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH captures observations, recommendations, reviews, and improvement signals.

---

## Feedback Principle

Feedback capture preserves external observations and internal findings as structured inputs for ecosystem evolution.

---

## Feedback Domains

Includes:

- technical feedback;
- research feedback;
- governance feedback;
- operational feedback;
- user observations.

---

## Feedback Lifecycle


RECEIVE INPUT

↓

CLASSIFY FEEDBACK

↓

RECORD CONTEXT

↓

PRESERVE SOURCE

↓

FORWARD EVALUATION

↓

MAINTAIN HISTORY


---

## Principle

Structured feedback capture converts observations into traceable improvement opportunities.
