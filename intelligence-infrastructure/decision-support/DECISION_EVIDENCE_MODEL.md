# Decision Evidence Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how evidence supporting decisions is collected, evaluated, and preserved.

---

## Evidence Principle

Decisions should maintain traceable connections between information, reasoning, and resulting actions.

---

## Evidence Categories

Includes:

- supporting evidence;
- comparative evidence;
- validation evidence;
- historical evidence;
- outcome evidence.

---

## Evidence Lifecycle


COLLECT

↓

CLASSIFY

↓

EVALUATE

↓

CONNECT TO DECISION

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Decision evidence enables reconstruction of why actions were selected and how outcomes were evaluated.
