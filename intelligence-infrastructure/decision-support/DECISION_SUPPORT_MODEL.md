# Decision Support Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH supports structured decision-making through evidence, analysis, and traceable evaluation.

---

## Decision Support Principle

Decision support connects available knowledge, evidence, and reasoning pathways to informed action.

---

## Decision Support Areas

Includes:

- strategic decisions;
- operational decisions;
- research decisions;
- architectural decisions;
- governance decisions.

---

## Decision Support Lifecycle


IDENTIFY DECISION

↓

COLLECT INFORMATION

↓

ANALYZE OPTIONS

↓

EVALUATE IMPACT

↓

SELECT ACTION

↓

REVIEW OUTCOME


---

## Principle

Decision support improves clarity while preserving decision context and rationale.
