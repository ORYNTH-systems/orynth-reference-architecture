# Insight Management Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH insights are captured, organized, evaluated, and preserved.

---

## Insight Principle

Insights transform analysis and observations into reusable understanding that can guide future research and decisions.

---

## Insight Categories

Includes:

- research insights;
- operational insights;
- architectural insights;
- ecosystem insights;
- strategic insights.
