# Insight Evolution Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how insights mature through continued analysis, application, and refinement.

---

## Evolution Principle

Insights evolve as additional evidence, perspectives, and applications become available.

---

## Evolution Stages

### Initial Observation

A potential insight is identified.

---

### Analysis

Relationships and implications are examined.

---

### Validation

Evidence and application are reviewed.

---

### Integration

Insight becomes part of reusable knowledge.

---

### Advancement

Insight contributes to future development.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


OBSERVE

↓

ANALYZE

↓

VALIDATE

↓

INTEGRATE

↓

EXTEND


---

## Principle

Insight evolution transforms discoveries into continuously improving knowledge.
