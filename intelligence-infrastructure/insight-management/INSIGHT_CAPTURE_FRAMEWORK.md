# Insight Capture Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how meaningful observations and discoveries are recorded.

---

## Capture Principle

Insights should preserve context, origin, evidence, and relationship to existing knowledge.

---

## Capture Record

Includes:

- insight identifier;
- source;
- context;
- observation;
- analysis;
- relevance;
- related systems.

---

## Capture Lifecycle


OBSERVE

↓

RECORD

↓

VALIDATE

↓

CONNECT

↓

STORE


---

## Principle

Structured capture prevents valuable knowledge from being lost or disconnected from its origin.
