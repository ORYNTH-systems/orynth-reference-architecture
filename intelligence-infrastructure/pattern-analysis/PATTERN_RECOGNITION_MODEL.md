# Pattern Recognition Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how recurring structures are identified across ORYNTH knowledge and operational domains.

---

## Recognition Principle

Pattern recognition requires distinguishing meaningful relationships from isolated observations.

---

## Recognition Factors

Includes:

- repetition;
- correlation;
- structural similarity;
- temporal relationship;
- contextual relevance.

---

## Recognition Lifecycle


COLLECT OBSERVATIONS

↓

COMPARE STRUCTURES

↓

IDENTIFY SIMILARITIES

↓

ASSESS PATTERN

↓

VALIDATE FINDING


---

## Principle

Recognized patterns become useful when connected to evidence and context.
