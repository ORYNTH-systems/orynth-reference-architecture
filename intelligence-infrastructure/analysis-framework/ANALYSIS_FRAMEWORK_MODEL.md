# Analysis Framework Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure used to analyze ORYNTH systems, research outputs, evidence, and operational information.

---

## Analysis Principle

Analysis transforms information into structured understanding through defined methods, evidence, and evaluation criteria.

---

## Analysis Domains

Includes:

- technical analysis;
- operational analysis;
- research analysis;
- ecosystem analysis;
- impact analysis.

---

## Analysis Lifecycle


DEFINE QUESTION

↓

COLLECT INFORMATION

↓

APPLY METHOD

↓

EVALUATE RESULTS

↓

DOCUMENT FINDINGS

↓

REFINE UNDERSTANDING


---

## Principle

Structured analysis enables reliable interpretation while preserving evidence and context.
