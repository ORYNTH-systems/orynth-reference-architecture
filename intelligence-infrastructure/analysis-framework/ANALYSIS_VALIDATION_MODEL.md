# Analysis Validation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how analytical outputs are reviewed for accuracy, consistency, and usefulness.

---

## Validation Principle

Analysis must remain connected to evidence, methodology, and defined evaluation boundaries.

---

## Validation Areas

Includes:

- source quality;
- methodological consistency;
- reasoning clarity;
- result interpretation;
- uncertainty identification.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


PRODUCE ANALYSIS

↓

REVIEW METHOD

↓

VERIFY EVIDENCE

↓

ASSESS RESULT

↓

RECORD VALIDATION


---

## Principle

Validated analysis provides a trustworthy foundation for decisions and future research.
