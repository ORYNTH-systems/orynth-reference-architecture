# Analytical Methods Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define methods used to evaluate information and generate analytical findings.

---

## Method Categories

Includes:

- comparative analysis;
- systems analysis;
- evidence analysis;
- structural analysis;
- temporal analysis.

---

## Method Lifecycle


SELECT METHOD

↓

DEFINE CRITERIA

↓

APPLY ANALYSIS

↓

INTERPRET RESULTS

↓

VERIFY CONCLUSIONS


---

## Method Requirements

Maintain:

- assumptions;
- inputs;
- procedures;
- evidence;
- limitations.

---

## Principle

Transparent methods improve reproducibility and analytical reliability.
