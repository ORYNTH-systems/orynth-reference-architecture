# Inference Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how conclusions are derived from available information and evidence.

---

## Inference Principle

Inference should preserve connection between observations, assumptions, evidence, and resulting conclusions.

---

## Inference Components

Includes:

- observations;
- premises;
- relationships;
- reasoning path;
- conclusion.

---

## Inference Lifecycle


OBSERVE

↓

IDENTIFY PATTERNS

↓

EVALUATE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

GENERATE INFERENCE

↓

VERIFY CONSISTENCY


---

## Principle

Traceable inference allows conclusions to be examined and reconstructed.
