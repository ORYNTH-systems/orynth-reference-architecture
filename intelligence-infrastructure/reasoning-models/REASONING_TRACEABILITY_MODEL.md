# Reasoning Traceability Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how reasoning pathways are preserved for review and validation.

---

## Traceability Principle

Reasoning should maintain a visible connection between information inputs and derived conclusions.

---

## Traceability Elements

Includes:

- source information;
- assumptions;
- transformations;
- decision points;
- conclusions.

---

## Traceability Lifecycle


CAPTURE INPUT

↓

DOCUMENT PROCESS

↓

RECORD TRANSITIONS

↓

REVIEW REASONING

↓

PRESERVE PATHWAY


---

## Principle

Reasoning traceability supports accountability, review, and continuous improvement.
