# Reasoning Model Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define structures for reasoning processes used to interpret information, evaluate relationships, and support analysis.

---

## Reasoning Principle

Reasoning converts information into structured conclusions through defined assumptions, relationships, and evaluation pathways.

---

## Reasoning Domains

Includes:

- analytical reasoning;
- systems reasoning;
- causal reasoning;
- comparative reasoning;
- adaptive reasoning.

---

## Reasoning Lifecycle


IDENTIFY INPUT

↓

DEFINE CONTEXT

↓

ESTABLISH RELATIONSHIPS

↓

EVALUATE OPTIONS

↓

FORM CONCLUSION

↓

REVIEW RESULT


---

## Principle

Transparent reasoning preserves understanding between inputs, processes, and conclusions.
