# Intelligence Synthesis State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track synthesized intelligence states, capability relationships, validation conditions, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Synthesis states preserve relationships between intelligence capabilities, architectural context, governance conditions, and resulting unified intelligence configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- synthesis identity;
- intelligence capability layers;
- transition state;
- integration condition;
- governance status;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE SYNTHESIS RECORD

↓

CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE LAYERS

↓

MAP CAPABILITY RELATIONSHIPS

↓

VALIDATE INTEGRATION

↓

REGISTER SYNTHESIS STATE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

An intelligence synthesis state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence layers and their unified architectural representation.
