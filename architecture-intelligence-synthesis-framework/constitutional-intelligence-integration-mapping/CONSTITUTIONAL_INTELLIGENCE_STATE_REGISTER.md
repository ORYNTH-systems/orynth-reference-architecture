# Constitutional Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track constitutional intelligence states, governance conditions, capability transitions, and validation status.

---

## State Principle

Constitutional intelligence states preserve relationships between intelligence capability, constitutional conditions, governance decisions, and resulting validated system states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- constitutional identity;
- intelligence capability state;
- authority condition;
- integrity condition;
- governance status;
- transition eligibility;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.
