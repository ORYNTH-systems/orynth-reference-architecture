# Intelligence Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track governed intelligence states, transitions, validation conditions, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Governance states preserve relationships between intelligence capability, constitutional conditions, transition decisions, and resulting validated architecture states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- intelligence capability state;
- constitutional condition;
- transition condition;
- validation status;
- authorization status;
- integrity status;
- historical lineage.

---
