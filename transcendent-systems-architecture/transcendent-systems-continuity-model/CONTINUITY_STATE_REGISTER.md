# Continuity State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track continuity states, invariant relationships, transformation conditions, and evolutionary transitions.

---

## State Principle

Continuity states preserve relationships between prior architecture states, invariant structures, transformation events, and resulting system identities.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- continuity identity;
- preserved invariant;
- source architecture state;
- transformation condition;
- resulting architecture state;
- validation status;
- lineage record;
- future readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE CONTINUITY STATE

↓

IDENTIFY PRESERVED RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ASSESS TRANSFORMATION CONDITION

↓

VALIDATE IDENTITY PRESERVATION

↓

UPDATE CONTINUITY MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A continuity state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH architecture evolution events and the preserved structures connecting them.
