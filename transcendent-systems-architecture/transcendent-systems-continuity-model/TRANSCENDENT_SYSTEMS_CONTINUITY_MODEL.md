# Transcendent Systems Continuity Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the continuity framework used to preserve ORYNTH architectural identity, knowledge lineage, governance relationships, and evolutionary states across successive system transformations.

---

## Continuity Principle

Transcendent systems continuity preserves the invariant relationships that allow an architecture to evolve while remaining traceable to its foundational structures.

Continuity enables transformation without loss of identity.

---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- architectural identity;
- knowledge preservation;
- governance continuity;
- intelligence lineage;
- system relationships;
- evolutionary transitions;
- historical records;
- future adaptation pathways.
