# Meta-Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track integrated intelligence states, relationships, synthesis conditions, and evolutionary transitions.

---

## State Principle

Meta-intelligence states preserve relationships between intelligence components, knowledge structures, coordination processes, validation conditions, and resulting higher-order states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- intelligence identity;
- component relationship;
- synthesis condition;
- reasoning state;
- coordination status;
- validation state;
- continuity condition;
- evolution readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

CLASSIFY COMPONENT RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ASSESS SYNTHESIS CONDITION

↓

UPDATE META-INTELLIGENCE MODEL

↓

VALIDATE INTEGRATION RESULT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A meta-intelligence state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence structures and their integrated evolutionary states.
