## Purpose

## Meta-Intelligence Principle

Meta-intelligence integration represents the synthesis of multiple intelligence capabilities into a coordinated architecture while preserving the identity, boundaries, and operational integrity of each component.

Integration creates higher-order coordination without eliminating individual function.
