# Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track governance states, authority relationships, decision conditions, validation results, and evolutionary transitions.

---

## State Principle

Governance states preserve relationships between governing principles, system authorities, operational conditions, adaptation requirements, and resulting governance states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- authority structure;
- decision condition;
- validation status;
- accountability state;
- continuity condition;
- evolution readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

CLASSIFY AUTHORITY RELATIONSHIP

↓

ASSESS DECISION CONDITION

↓

UPDATE GOVERNANCE MODEL

↓

VALIDATE GOVERNANCE RESULT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A governance state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH governance structures and their evolving systemic applications.
