## Purpose

Define the universal governance synthesis framework used to integrate governance principles, decision structures, validation mechanisms, and adaptive controls across ORYNTH architecture domains.

---

## Governance Synthesis Principle

Universal governance synthesis establishes coordinated governance relationships between systems while preserving autonomy, accountability, traceability, and architectural continuity.

Governance enables alignment without eliminating system function.

- validation mechanisms;
- accountability structures;
- adaptive governance;
- continuity preservation;
- evolutionary management.
