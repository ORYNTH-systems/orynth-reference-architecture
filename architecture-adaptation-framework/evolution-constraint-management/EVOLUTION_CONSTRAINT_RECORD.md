# Evolution Constraint Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for architectural evolution constraints.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- constraint identifier;
- affected architecture layer;
- protected property;
- proposed adaptation;
- evaluation result;
- validation evidence;
- final decision.

---

## Constraint Lifecycle


CONSTRAINT IDENTIFIED

↓

ADAPTATION REVIEWED

↓

IMPACT ASSESSED

↓

DECISION RECORDED

↓

CONSTRAINT HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Constraint States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- UNDER REVIEW;
- SATISFIED;
- CONDITIONALLY SATISFIED;
- VIOLATED.

---

## Principle

Evolution Constraint Records preserve the boundaries that allow Morning Star to evolve without losing coherence.
