# Evolution Constraint Management

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the constraint management framework governing evolution of the ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Constraint Principle

Evolution must increase capability without violating preserved architectural properties.

Constraints define what may change, what must remain stable, and what requires additional validation.

---

## Constraint Domains

Includes:

- identity constraints;
- semantic constraints;
- dependency constraints;
- governance constraints;
- evidence constraints;
- continuity constraints.

---

## Evolution Constraint Lifecycle


ADAPTIVE STATE PROPOSED

↓

CONSTRAINT IDENTIFICATION

↓

CONSTRAINT EVALUATION

↓

COMPATIBILITY REVIEW

↓

GOVERNANCE DECISION

↓

VALIDATED EVOLUTION PATH


---

## Protected Properties

Evolution must preserve:

- architecture identity;
- constitutional purpose;
- semantic integrity;
- dependency order;
- governance authority;
- evidence lineage;
- continuity relationships.

---

## Principle

Evolution Constraint Management enables growth while preventing architectural identity loss.
