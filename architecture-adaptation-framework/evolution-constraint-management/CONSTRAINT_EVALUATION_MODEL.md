# Constraint Evaluation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the evaluation model for determining whether proposed evolution remains within architectural constraints.

---

## Evaluation Principle

A proposed adaptation is evaluated by its relationship to preserved properties, not by change magnitude alone.

---

## Evaluation Model


PROPOSED ADAPTATION

↓

IDENTIFY AFFECTED PROPERTIES

↓

EVALUATE CONSTRAINT IMPACT

↓

DETERMINE COMPATIBILITY

↓

APPROVE OR REJECT TRANSITION


---

## Evaluation Categories

### Compatible Evolution

Change preserves required architecture properties.

### Conditional Evolution

Change requires additional validation.

### Restricted Evolution

Change conflicts with defined constraints.

### Invalid Evolution

Change violates architectural identity.

---

## Evaluation Factors

Includes:

- identity preservation;
- semantic consistency;
- dependency stability;
- governance alignment;
- continuity impact.

---

## Principle

The Constraint Evaluation Model provides the decision structure for controlled architectural evolution.
