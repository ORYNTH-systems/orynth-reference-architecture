# Adaptation Approval Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the approval model for proposed architectural adaptations.

---

## Approval Principle

Approval determines whether an adaptive transition may proceed while preserving architectural coherence.

---

## Approval Model


ADAPTATION REQUEST

↓

IMPACT ASSESSMENT

↓

CONSTRAINT EVALUATION

↓

GOVERNANCE REVIEW

↓

APPROVAL DECISION

↓

AUTHORIZED TRANSITION


---

## Approval Outcomes

### Approved

Adaptation satisfies governance requirements.

### Approved With Conditions

Adaptation requires additional controls.

### Deferred

Additional information required.

### Rejected

Adaptation violates defined requirements.

---

## Approval Factors

Includes:

- necessity;
- architectural benefit;
- constraint compatibility;
- evidence availability;
- continuity impact.

---

## Principle

The Adaptation Approval Model provides controlled authorization for architectural evolution.
