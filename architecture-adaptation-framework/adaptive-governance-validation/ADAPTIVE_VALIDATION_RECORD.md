# Adaptive Validation Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the validation record structure for adaptive transitions.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- adaptation identifier;
- originating state;
- target state;
- governance review;
- constraint assessment;
- evidence references;
- approval result;
- implementation status.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


ADAPTATION SUBMITTED

↓

GOVERNANCE ASSESSED

↓

VALIDATION PERFORMED

↓

DECISION RECORDED

↓

TRANSITION TRACKED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Validation States

Possible states:

- SUBMITTED;
- REVIEWING;
- APPROVED;
- IMPLEMENTED;
- VERIFIED;
- REJECTED.

---

## Principle

Adaptive Validation Records maintain traceability between architectural evolution decisions and their outcomes.
