# Continuity Evolution Validation

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define validation requirements for confirming that architectural evolution maintains continuity.

---

## Validation Principle

An evolved architecture remains continuous when preserved properties and historical relationships remain demonstrable.

---

## Validation Model


PREVIOUS REFERENCE STATE

EVOLVED ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

COMPARE PRESERVED PROPERTIES

↓

VERIFY GOVERNANCE ALIGNMENT

↓

VERIFY EVIDENCE LINEAGE

↓

CONFIRM CONTINUITY


---

## Validation Domains

Includes:

- identity preservation;
- semantic preservation;
- governance preservation;
- evidence preservation;
- dependency preservation.

---

## Validation Results

Possible states:

- CONTINUOUS;
- CONTINUOUS WITH CONDITIONS;
- REQUIRES REVIEW;
- DISCONTINUOUS.

---

## Principle

Continuity Evolution Validation ensures future architecture states remain legitimate descendants of the original Morning Star reference architecture.
