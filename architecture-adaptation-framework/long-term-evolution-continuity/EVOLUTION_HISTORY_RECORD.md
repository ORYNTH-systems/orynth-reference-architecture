# Evolution History Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the historical record structure for architectural evolution.

---

## History Principle

Every accepted adaptation becomes part of a traceable evolutionary lineage connecting previous and current architecture states.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- evolution identifier;
- originating state;
- target state;
- adaptation purpose;
- governance approval;
- constraint evaluation;
- implementation evidence;
- continuity result.

---

## Evolution History Lifecycle


CHANGE IDENTIFIED

↓

ADAPTATION PROPOSED

↓

VALIDATION COMPLETED

↓

EVOLUTION IMPLEMENTED

↓

REFERENCE UPDATED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Evolution States

Possible states:

- PROPOSED;
- REVIEWED;
- APPROVED;
- IMPLEMENTED;
- VALIDATED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Evolution History Records preserve the memory and reasoning behind architectural transformation.
