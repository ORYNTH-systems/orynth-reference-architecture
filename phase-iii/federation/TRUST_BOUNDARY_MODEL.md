# Trust Boundary Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase III Ecosystem Expansion Layer

---

## Purpose

Define boundaries governing trust relationships between connected systems.

---

## Trust Principle

Trust is established through verification, not assumption.

---

## Boundary Domains

### Identity Boundary

Controls:

- who participates;
- system ownership;
- identity continuity.

---

### Authority Boundary

Controls:

- permitted actions;
- delegated rights;
- execution scope.

---

### Data Boundary

Controls:

- information exchange;
- access limitations;
- preservation.

---

### Runtime Boundary

Controls:

- execution interaction;
- failure isolation;
- recovery.

---
