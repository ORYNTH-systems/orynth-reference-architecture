## Purpose

Define mechanisms for discovering relationships, opportunities, and architectural improvements.

---

## Discovery Principle

Discovery expands understanding through structured exploration.

---

## Discovery Domains

### Structural Discovery

Identifies:

- architecture relationships;
- system patterns;
- dependencies.

---

### Operational Discovery

Identifies:

- execution improvements;
- efficiency patterns;
- failure conditions.

---

### Ecosystem Discovery

Identifies:

- new integrations;
- emerging capabilities;
- system relationships.

---

- architectural compatibility.
