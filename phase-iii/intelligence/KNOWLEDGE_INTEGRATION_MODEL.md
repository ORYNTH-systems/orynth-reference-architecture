# Knowledge Integration Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase III Ecosystem Expansion Layer

---

## Purpose

Define how knowledge sources are integrated into the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Knowledge Principle

Knowledge integration requires structure, provenance, and validation.

---

## Knowledge Domains

### Internal Knowledge

Includes:

- architecture records;
- runtime evidence;
- governance history.

---

### External Knowledge

Includes:

- external systems;
- research;
- validated information sources.

---

### Emergent Knowledge

Includes:

- discovered patterns;
- generated insights;
- ecosystem observations.

---

Integrated knowledge must preserve:

- origin;
- context;
- reliability;
- relationship.
