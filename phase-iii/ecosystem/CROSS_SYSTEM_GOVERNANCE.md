# Cross System Governance

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase III Ecosystem Expansion Layer

---

## Purpose

Define governance principles for coordinated operation between multiple systems.

---

## Governance Principle

Ecosystem coordination requires shared rules without eliminating system autonomy.

---

## Governance Domains

### Authority Governance

Defines:

- ownership;
- delegation;
- boundaries.

---

### Evolution Governance

Defines:

- change approval;
- compatibility;
- continuity.

---

### Operational Governance

Defines:

- execution expectations;
- accountability;
- validation.

---

## Governance Flow


SYSTEM REQUEST

↓

AUTHORITY EVALUATION

↓

CROSS-SYSTEM REVIEW

↓

EXECUTION

↓

EVIDENCE RECORD


---

## Governance Failure Conditions

Cross-system governance fails when:

- responsibility is unclear;
- authority cannot be verified;
- changes bypass review.

---

## Governance Invariant

The ecosystem evolves through coordinated autonomy.
