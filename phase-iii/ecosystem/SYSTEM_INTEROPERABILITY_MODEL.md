# System Interoperability Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase III Ecosystem Expansion Layer

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH systems communicate and operate together.

---

## Interoperability Principle

Systems must exchange capability without violating internal governance boundaries.

---

## Interoperability Layers

### Data Interoperability

Defines:

- information exchange;
- representation;
- preservation.

---

### Capability Interoperability

Defines:

- service availability;
- capability invocation;
- dependency relationships.

---

### Governance Interoperability

Defines:

- authority transfer;
- permission boundaries;
- accountability.

---

## Interaction Model


SYSTEM A

↓

INTERFACE CONTRACT

↓

GOVERNANCE CHECK

↓

SYSTEM B

↓

VERIFICATION


---

## Interoperability Requirements

Every connection requires:

- defined interface;
- known authority;
- observable exchange;
- recoverable state.

---

## Interoperability Invariant

Connected systems remain independently coherent while participating in shared operation.
