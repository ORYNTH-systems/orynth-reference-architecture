## Purpose

Define the framework used to evaluate, refine, and govern ORYNTH governance structures through recursive intelligence processes.

---

## Meta-Governance Intelligence Principle

Recursive meta-governance intelligence enables the governance system to evaluate its own conditions, relationships, assumptions, and evolutionary pathways while preserving authority boundaries, constitutional alignment, and traceability.

Governance becomes self-reflective without becoming self-contradictory.

- systemic constraint analysis;
- decision pathway evaluation;
- evolutionary governance modeling;
- continuity preservation.
