# Meta-Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track meta-governance states, governance evaluations, recursive assessments, and evolutionary transitions.

---

## State Principle

Meta-governance states preserve relationships between governance structures, evaluation processes, authority conditions, and resulting governance states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- evaluated governance layer;
- authority relationship;
- assessment condition;
- transition state;
- validation status;
- continuity record.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

EVALUATE GOVERNANCE CONDITION

↓

ASSESS SYSTEMIC EFFECT

↓

DEFINE IMPROVEMENT PATH

↓

VALIDATE GOVERNANCE TRANSITION

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A meta-governance state register maintains traceability between governance evaluation and governance evolution.
