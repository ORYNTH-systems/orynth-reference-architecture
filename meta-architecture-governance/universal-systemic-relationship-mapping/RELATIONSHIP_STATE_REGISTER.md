# Relationship State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track systemic relationships, dependency conditions, interaction states, and architectural continuity.

---

## State Principle

Relationship states preserve connections between entities, relationship types, dependency conditions, operational interactions, and evolutionary transitions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- relationship identity;
- source entity;
- target entity;
- relationship classification;
- dependency condition;
- interaction state;
- validation status;
- continuity record.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE RELATIONSHIP

↓

CLASSIFY CONNECTION TYPE

↓

ASSESS SYSTEM IMPACT

↓

VALIDATE RELATIONSHIP CONDITION

↓

UPDATE RELATIONSHIP MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A relationship state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH components and the systemic structures connecting them.
