# Universal Systemic Relationship Mapping

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to identify, represent, and preserve relationships between all ORYNTH architectural entities, systems, capabilities, and governance structures.

---

## Relationship Mapping Principle

Universal systemic relationship mapping maintains visibility into how architecture components interact, depend upon, influence, and evolve with one another.

Relationships preserve ecosystem coherence by making structural connections explicit.

---

## Relationship Domains

Includes:

- architecture dependencies;
- system interactions;
- capability relationships;
- governance relationships;
- knowledge relationships;
- intelligence relationships;
- evolutionary pathways;
- continuity relationships.
