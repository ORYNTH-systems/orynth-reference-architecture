# Ontology Evolution Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH ontology evolves while preserving architectural identity and conceptual continuity.

---

## Evolution Principle

Ontology evolution records preserve refinement of definitions, expansion of relationships, discovery of new classifications, and controlled conceptual advancement.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- terminology refinement;
- relationship expansion;
- entity discovery;
- classification improvement;
- semantic validation;
- future ontology development.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


CAPTURE ONTOLOGY CHANGE

↓

DOCUMENT SEMANTIC TRANSITION

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURAL IMPACT

↓

REFINE ONTOLOGY MODEL

↓

VALIDATE CONTINUITY

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY

↓

ENABLE FUTURE EVOLUTION


---

## Principle

An ontology evolution record ensures ORYNTH can expand conceptually while preserving the integrity of its foundational architecture.
