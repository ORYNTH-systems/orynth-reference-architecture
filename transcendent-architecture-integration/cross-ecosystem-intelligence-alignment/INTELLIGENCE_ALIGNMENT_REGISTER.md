Version:

v1.0.0

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track intelligence relationships, alignment conditions, and integration states.

---

## Alignment Principle

Intelligence alignment records preserve relationships between intelligence sources, system states, knowledge pathways, and architectural objectives.

---

## Alignment Domains

Includes:

- intelligence source;
- relationship mapping;
- alignment condition;
- integration status;
- validation evidence.
