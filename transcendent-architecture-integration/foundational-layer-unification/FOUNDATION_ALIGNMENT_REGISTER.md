## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track foundational elements, relationships, and alignment conditions.

---

## Alignment Principle

Foundation alignment records preserve relationships between architectural components, governing principles, and system dependencies.

---

## Alignment Domains

Includes:

- foundational component;
- architectural relationship;
- alignment status;
- dependency condition;
- validation evidence.
