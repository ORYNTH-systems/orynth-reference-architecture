# Relationship State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track intelligence relationships, interaction states, validation conditions, and resulting relationship configurations.

---

## State Principle

Relationship states preserve relationships between intelligence domains, architecture layers, governance structures, interaction patterns, and resulting intelligence configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- relationship identity;
- source intelligence domains;
- relationship type;
- contextual conditions;
- interaction pathways;
- validation status;
- resulting relationship state;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE RELATIONSHIP RECORD

↓

CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE DOMAINS

↓

MAP RELATIONSHIP CONDITIONS

↓

ANALYZE INTERACTIONS

↓

VALIDATE RELATIONSHIP STATE

↓

REGISTER RELATIONSHIP CONFIGURATION

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A relationship state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence domains and their connected relationship structures.
