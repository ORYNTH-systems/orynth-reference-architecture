# Global Intelligence Relationship Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to model relationships between ORYNTH intelligence domains, architecture structures, governance systems, and evolving intelligence states.

---

## Relationship Principle

The Global Intelligence Relationship Model represents intelligence capability as a network of connected relationships rather than isolated components.

Intelligence coherence emerges through preserved relationships.

---

## Relationship Domains

Includes:

- intelligence domain relationships;
- architecture relationships;
- governance relationships;
- knowledge relationships;
- continuity relationships;
- dependency relationships;
- evolutionary relationships;
- synthesis relationships.
