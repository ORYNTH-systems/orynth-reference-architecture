# Deployment Governance

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the governance framework for deploying ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture implementations.

---

## Deployment Principle

Deployment converts architectural intent into operational reality while preserving reference architecture requirements.

Deployment must remain controlled, observable, and validated.

---

## Deployment Domains

Includes:

- deployment authorization;
- environment preparation;
- implementation controls;
- operational monitoring;
- validation procedures;
- deployment continuity.

---

## Deployment Lifecycle


IMPLEMENTATION APPROVED

↓

DEPLOYMENT AUTHORIZED

↓

ENVIRONMENT PREPARED

↓

DEPLOYMENT EXECUTED

↓

OPERATION VALIDATED

↓

ADOPTION CONFIRMED


---

## Governance Requirements

Deployment must preserve:

- architecture identity;
- implementation boundaries;
- evidence traceability;
- validation requirements;
- operational accountability.

---

## Principle

Deployment Governance ensures adoption occurs through controlled execution rather than uncontrolled implementation.
