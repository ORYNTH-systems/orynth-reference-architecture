# Stakeholder Integration Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the stakeholder integration framework for adoption of the ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Integration Principle

Successful adoption requires clear participation models, defined responsibilities, and preserved governance relationships among all participants.

Stakeholder integration connects people and organizations to the architecture without altering the architecture's constitutional identity.

---

## Stakeholder Domains

Includes:

- architecture stewards;
- implementation partners;
- researchers;
- operational users;
- ecosystem participants;
- validation authorities.

---

## Integration Lifecycle


STAKEHOLDER IDENTIFIED

↓

ROLE DEFINED

↓

RESPONSIBILITY ASSIGNED

↓

PARTICIPATION ENABLED

↓

CONTRIBUTION VALIDATED

↓

RELATIONSHIP MAINTAINED


---

## Integration Requirements

Stakeholder participation must preserve:

- role clarity;
- authority boundaries;
- governance requirements;
- evidence responsibilities;
- architectural continuity.

---

## Principle

The Stakeholder Integration Model enables coordinated participation while preserving system integrity.
