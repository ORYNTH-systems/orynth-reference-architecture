# Participation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define participation roles within the ORYNTH / Morning Star adoption ecosystem.

---

## Participation Principle

Participants interact with the architecture through defined roles, responsibilities, and permissions.

---

## Participation Structure


ARCHITECTURE STEWARDS

    ↓

IMPLEMENTATION PARTICIPANTS

    ↓

OPERATIONAL USERS

    ↓

ECOSYSTEM CONTRIBUTORS

    ↓

VALIDATION PARTICIPANTS


---

## Participation Roles

### Architecture Steward

Maintains reference integrity and governance.

### Implementation Participant

Applies architecture within an operational environment.

### Research Participant

Extends knowledge and evaluation.

### Operational Participant

Uses implemented capabilities.

### Validation Participant

Reviews alignment and evidence.

---

## Participation Factors

Includes:

- authority;
- responsibility;
- contribution;
- evidence ownership;
- governance obligations.

---

## Principle

The Participation Model ensures every stakeholder relationship has a defined purpose and boundary.
