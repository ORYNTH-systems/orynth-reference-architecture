# Stakeholder Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for stakeholder participation.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- stakeholder identifier;
- organization or role;
- participation category;
- assigned responsibilities;
- governance relationship;
- contribution status;
- historical record.

---

## Stakeholder Lifecycle


STAKEHOLDER IDENTIFIED

↓

ROLE ASSESSED

↓

PARTICIPATION APPROVED

↓

ACTIVITY TRACKED

↓

STATUS REVIEWED

↓

RELATIONSHIP PRESERVED


---

## Stakeholder States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- REVIEWING;
- APPROVED;
- ACTIVE;
- REVIEW REQUIRED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Stakeholder Status Records preserve accountability throughout the adoption lifecycle.
