# Adoption Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for global adoption continuity.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- adoption identifier;
- participating entities;
- implementation status;
- governance status;
- certification condition;
- continuity assessment;
- historical record.

---

## Adoption Lifecycle


ADOPTION INITIATED

↓

IMPLEMENTATION VERIFIED

↓

PARTICIPATION EXPANDS

↓

CONTINUITY ASSESSED

↓

STATUS UPDATED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Adoption States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- IMPLEMENTING;
- ACTIVE;
- SCALING;
- ESTABLISHED;
- REVIEW REQUIRED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Adoption Status Records preserve accountability as architecture participation expands over time.
