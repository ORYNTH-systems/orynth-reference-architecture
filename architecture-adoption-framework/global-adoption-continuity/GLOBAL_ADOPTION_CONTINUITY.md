# Global Adoption Continuity

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the continuity framework for maintaining ORYNTH / Morning Star adoption across expanding operational ecosystems.

---

## Continuity Principle

Adoption at scale requires preservation of architecture identity, governance requirements, implementation consistency, and certification relationships.

Growth must expand participation without fragmenting the reference architecture.

---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- adoption lifecycle management;
- regional implementation continuity;
- governance preservation;
- certification maintenance;
- ecosystem coordination;
- long-term stewardship.

---

## Continuity Requirements

Global adoption must preserve:

- reference architecture identity;
- implementation lineage;
- stakeholder relationships;
- governance boundaries;
- validation evidence.

---

## Principle

Global Adoption Continuity ensures expansion remains connected to the certified architectural foundation.
