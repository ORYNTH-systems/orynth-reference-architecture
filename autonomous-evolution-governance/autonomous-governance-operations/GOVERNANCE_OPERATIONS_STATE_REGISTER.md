# Governance Operations State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track autonomous governance operation states, decisions, transitions, and validation conditions.

---

## State Principle

Governance operation states preserve relationships between authority conditions, governance decisions, operational execution, and resulting system states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- authority state;
- decision state;
- operational state;
- validation state;
- transition history.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

ASSESS OPERATIONAL CONDITION

↓

VERIFY AUTHORITY ALIGNMENT

↓

EXECUTE GOVERNANCE TRANSITION

↓

VALIDATE RESULT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A governance operations state register maintains traceability between autonomous governance activity and its governing conditions.
