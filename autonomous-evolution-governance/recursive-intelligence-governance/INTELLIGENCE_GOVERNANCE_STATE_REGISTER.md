# Intelligence Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track recursive intelligence states, governance conditions, reasoning transitions, and validation states.

---

## State Principle

Intelligence governance states preserve relationships between intelligence conditions, knowledge structures, reasoning processes, governance constraints, and resulting system states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- intelligence identity;
- reasoning condition;
- governance state;
- knowledge condition;
- adaptation state;
- historical continuity.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

MAP REASONING RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ASSESS GOVERNANCE CONDITION

↓

VERIFY INTELLIGENCE ALIGNMENT

↓

UPDATE GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An intelligence governance state register maintains traceability between recursive intelligence activity and its governing conditions.
