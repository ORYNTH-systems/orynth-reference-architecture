# Self-Adaptive Architecture Control

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to enable architectural systems to adapt through controlled transitions, environmental awareness, governance alignment, and preserved architectural continuity.

---

## Architecture Control Principle

Self-adaptive architecture control enables systems to evaluate changing conditions, determine appropriate structural adjustments, and maintain architectural integrity throughout evolution.

---

## Control Domains

Includes:

- architecture monitoring;
- adaptive transitions;
- constraint preservation;
- structural alignment;
- capability adjustment;
- continuity management.

---

## Architecture Control Lifecycle


OBSERVE ARCHITECTURE CONDITION

↓

ANALYZE SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

↓

EVALUATE ADAPTIVE TRANSITION

↓

APPLY CONTROLLED MODIFICATION

↓

VERIFY ARCHITECTURAL ALIGNMENT

↓

PRESERVE CONTROL HISTORY


---

## Principle

Self-adaptive architecture control provides a structured mechanism for continuous architectural adjustment while preserving identity, governance, and operational coherence.
