# Cross-Domain Architecture Mapping

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to map architectural relationships across multiple domains while preserving domain-specific characteristics and contextual meaning.

---

## Cross-Domain Mapping Principle

Cross-domain architecture mapping identifies structural relationships between different domains by comparing principles, patterns, functions, and interactions while maintaining the integrity of each domain.

Mapping reveals correspondence without creating false equivalence.

---

## Domain Categories

Includes:

- technological systems;
- organizational systems;
- governance systems;
- knowledge systems;
- intelligence systems;
- biological systems;
- social systems;
- conceptual frameworks;
- evolutionary structures.
