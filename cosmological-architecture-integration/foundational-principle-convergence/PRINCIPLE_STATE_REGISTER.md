# Principle State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track foundational principles, their applications, relationships, validation states, and evolutionary conditions.

---

## State Principle

Principle states preserve relationships between foundational concepts, architectural implementations, domain applications, and future evolutionary pathways.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- principle identity;
- canonical meaning;
- architectural relationship;
- domain application;
- validation condition;
- continuity state;
- evolution status.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE PRINCIPLE

↓

CLASSIFY PRINCIPLE ROLE

↓

MAP ARCHITECTURAL RELATIONSHIPS

↓

VALIDATE APPLICATION

↓

UPDATE PRINCIPLE MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD

## Principle

A principle state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH foundational concepts and their expression throughout the architecture ecosystem.
