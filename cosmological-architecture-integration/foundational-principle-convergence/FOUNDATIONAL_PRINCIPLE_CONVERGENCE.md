# Foundational Principle Convergence

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to identify, preserve, and integrate foundational principles across the ORYNTH architecture ecosystem.

---

## Convergence Principle

Foundational principle convergence identifies underlying principles that maintain coherence across multiple architectural layers while preserving the distinct purpose and context of each domain.

Convergence reveals structural relationships without eliminating necessary differences.

---

## Principle Domains

Includes:

- identity preservation;
- continuity;
- traceability;
- governance;
- transformation;
- emergence;
- relationship integrity;
- evolutionary capability.
