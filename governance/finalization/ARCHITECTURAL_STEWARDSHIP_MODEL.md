## Purpose

Define the long-term responsibility model for preserving ORYNTH architectural integrity.

---

## Stewardship Principle

Architectures that persist over time require active preservation of identity, evidence, and intent.

---

## Stewardship Responsibilities

### Foundation Stewardship

Maintains:

- Morning Star constitutional principles;
- architectural invariants;
- historical lineage.

---

### Architecture Stewardship

Maintains:

- applied system boundaries;
- implementation continuity;
- technical evolution.

---

### Ecosystem Stewardship

Maintains:

- relationships;
- adoption pathways;
- collaborative structures.

---

## Stewardship Model


PRESERVE

↓

REVIEW

↓

GUIDE

↓

VALIDATE

↓

EVOLVE


---

## Invariant

Stewardship protects continuity while allowing responsible evolution.
