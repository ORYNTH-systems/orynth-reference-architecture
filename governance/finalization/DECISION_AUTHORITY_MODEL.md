# Decision Authority Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Governance Layer

Domain:

Decision Governance

---

## Purpose

Define how architectural decisions are authorized.

---

## Authority Principle

Decision authority follows responsibility and architectural scope.

---

## Authority Layers

### Constitutional Authority

Responsible for:

- foundational principles;
- invariant changes;
- constitutional amendments.

---

### Architecture Authority

Responsible for:

- system design;
- implementation decisions;
- technical evolution.

---

### Operational Authority

Responsible for:

- deployment;
- maintenance;
- execution.

---

## Decision Structure


CONSTITUTION

↓

ARCHITECTURE

↓

IMPLEMENTATION

↓

OPERATION


---

## Invariant

Authority must remain aligned with impact.
