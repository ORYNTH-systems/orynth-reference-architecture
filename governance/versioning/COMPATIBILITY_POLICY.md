# Compatibility Policy

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Governance Layer

Domain:

Version Management

---

## Purpose

Define compatibility requirements between ORYNTH repositories.

---

## Compatibility Principle

Independent repositories may evolve while preserving defined relationships.

---

## Compatibility Domains

### Architectural Compatibility

Ensures:

- principles remain aligned;
- dependencies remain valid.

---

### Technical Compatibility

Ensures:

- implementations continue functioning.

---

### Ecosystem Compatibility

Ensures:

- systems continue interacting.

---

## Compatibility Rules

Changes must identify:

- affected systems;
- migration requirements;
- validation needs.

---

## Invariant

Evolution requires awareness of dependency impact.
