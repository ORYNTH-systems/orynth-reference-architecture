# Deprecation Policy

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Governance Layer

Domain:

Version Management

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH retires outdated components.

---

## Deprecation Principle

Systems should evolve through controlled transition rather than abrupt disappearance.

---

## Deprecation Lifecycle


ACTIVE

↓

NOTICE

↓

MIGRATION

↓

ARCHIVE

↓

RETIREMENT


---

## Deprecation Requirements

Document:

- reason;
- affected systems;
- replacement path;
- historical preservation.

---

## Invariant

Deprecated systems remain part of architectural history.
