Classification: Constitutional Governance

Status: Draft

---

## Purpose

The Stewardship Model governs the preservation, continuity, succession, and constitutional care of the Morning Star project.

Stewardship exists to preserve constitutional identity across time.

It does not independently create, redefine, or supersede canonical meaning.

---

## Stewardship Principles

Every Constitutional Steward shall preserve:

- constitutional identity;
- provenance;
- dependency integrity;
- semantic continuity;
- historical traceability;
- uncertainty;
- revision transparency;
- succession continuity.

---

## Stewardship Responsibilities

Constitutional Stewardship includes responsibility for:

- preserving canonical artifacts;
- maintaining governance records;
- supervising amendment procedures;
- protecting framework identity;
- preserving historical revisions;
- maintaining constitutional archives;
- coordinating constitutional succession;
- ensuring reconstruction remains possible.

---

## Stewardship Limitations

A Steward shall not:

- redefine canonical meaning;
- erase constitutional history;
- conceal uncertainty;
- bypass amendment governance;
- exercise authority beyond documented constitutional scope.

Stewardship is preservational rather than legislative.

---

## Stewardship Appointment

Every Stewardship appointment shall preserve:

- Steward Identifier;
- appointment authority;
- constitutional scope;
- effective date;
- review interval;
- succession conditions;
- revocation conditions.

---

## Stewardship Succession

Succession shall preserve:

- continuity of constitutional identity;
- historical stewardship records;
- transfer evidence;
- authority continuity;
- unresolved constitutional matters;
- active governance obligations.

No succession event shall invalidate prior constitutional history.

---

## Stewardship Review

Stewardship shall remain periodically reviewable.

Review shall evaluate:

- constitutional compliance;
- preservation quality;
- governance completeness;
- amendment adherence;
- provenance preservation;
- succession readiness.

Review outcomes shall remain permanently archived.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

The purpose of stewardship is the preservation of constitutional continuity, never the replacement of constitutional identity.
