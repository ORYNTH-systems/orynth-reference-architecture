# Accountability Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define accountability structures that preserve responsibility, transparency, and traceability throughout ORYNTH operations.

---

## Accountability Principle

Every governed action should have identifiable ownership, rationale, evidence, and review capability.

---

## Accountability Areas

Includes:

- decision accountability;
- operational accountability;
- technical accountability;
- governance accountability;
- stewardship accountability.

---

## Accountability Lifecycle


ASSIGN RESPONSIBILITY

↓

EXECUTE ACTION

↓

RECORD EVIDENCE

↓

REVIEW OUTCOME

↓

MAINTAIN ACCOUNTABILITY


---

## Principle

Accountability preserves trust by connecting decisions to responsible actions.
