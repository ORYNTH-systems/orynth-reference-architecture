# Audit Continuity Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how auditability is preserved across ORYNTH lifecycle stages.

---

## Audit Principle

Audit continuity ensures decisions, changes, and outcomes remain reconstructable over time.

---

## Audit Continuity Areas

Includes:

- decision history;
- change records;
- validation evidence;
- deployment records;
- governance activity.

---

## Audit Lifecycle


CAPTURE

↓

VERIFY

↓

STORE

↓

REVIEW

↓

REFERENCE


---

## Continuity Requirements

Preserve:

- historical records;
- evidence chains;
- version relationships;
- review outcomes.

---

## Principle

Continuous auditability maintains institutional memory and operational trust.
