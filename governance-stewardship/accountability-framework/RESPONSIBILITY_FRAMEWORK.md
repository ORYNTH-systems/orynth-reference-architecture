# Responsibility Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how responsibilities are assigned, maintained, and reviewed across ORYNTH activities.

---

## Responsibility Principle

Clear responsibility boundaries prevent ambiguity during system operation and evolution.

---

## Responsibility Categories

Includes:

- architectural responsibility;
- operational responsibility;
- review responsibility;
- implementation responsibility;
- stewardship responsibility.

---

## Responsibility Lifecycle


DEFINE ROLE

↓

ASSIGN AUTHORITY

↓

EXECUTE RESPONSIBILITY

↓

REVIEW PERFORMANCE

↓

UPDATE ROLE


---

## Responsibility Records

Maintain:

- role definition;
- assigned authority;
- scope;
- obligations;
- review history.

---

## Principle

Defined responsibility enables coordinated action while preserving governance clarity.
