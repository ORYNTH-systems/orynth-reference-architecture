# Steward Role Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define steward responsibilities for maintaining ORYNTH continuity, governance, and knowledge preservation.

---

## Stewardship Principle

Stewards preserve system identity while enabling responsible evolution.

---

## Steward Responsibilities

Includes:

- protecting foundational principles;
- maintaining knowledge continuity;
- supporting governance processes;
- preserving historical records;
- enabling future transitions.

---

## Stewardship Lifecycle


IDENTIFY RESPONSIBILITY

↓

ASSUME STEWARD ROLE

↓

MAINTAIN CONTINUITY

↓

SUPPORT EVOLUTION

↓

TRANSFER KNOWLEDGE


---

## Principle

Stewardship maintains the connection between past architecture and future development.
