# Knowledge Continuity Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH knowledge is preserved, transferred, and maintained over time.

---

## Knowledge Principle

Knowledge continuity ensures future participants can reconstruct, understand, and extend prior work.

---

## Knowledge Preservation Areas

Includes:

- architectural documentation;
- research records;
- governance decisions;
- implementation history;
- validation evidence.

---

## Knowledge Lifecycle


CREATE

↓

DOCUMENT

↓

ORGANIZE

↓

TRANSFER

↓

PRESERVE

↓

REFERENCE


---

## Continuity Requirements

Maintain:

- historical context;
- version relationships;
- decision rationale;
- source evidence.

---

## Principle

Preserved knowledge enables future evolution without loss of institutional memory.
