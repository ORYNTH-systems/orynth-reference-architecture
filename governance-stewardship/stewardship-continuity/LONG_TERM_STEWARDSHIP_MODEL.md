# Long-Term Stewardship Model

Version:

v1.0.0

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH maintains long-term stewardship across future operational eras.

---

## Stewardship Principle

Long-term continuity requires preservation of identity, governance, knowledge, and purpose.

---

## Stewardship Domains

Includes:

- architectural continuity;
- institutional continuity;
- knowledge continuity;
- ecosystem continuity.

↓

↓

## Continuity Boundary

Future changes should extend validated principles while maintaining traceable lineage.

## Principle

Long-term stewardship allows systems to evolve without losing their origin.
