# Policy Management Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH policies are created, maintained, reviewed, and retired.

---

## Policy Principle

Policies provide governed operational guidance while preserving alignment with architectural principles.

---

## Policy Lifecycle


IDENTIFY NEED

↓

DRAFT POLICY

↓

REVIEW POLICY

↓

APPROVE POLICY

↓

IMPLEMENT POLICY

↓

MONITOR POLICY

↓

UPDATE OR RETIRE


---

## Policy Management Areas

Includes:

- policy creation;
- policy ownership;
- review cycles;
- version tracking;
- retirement processes.

---

## Principle

Policy evolution must remain traceable and controlled.
