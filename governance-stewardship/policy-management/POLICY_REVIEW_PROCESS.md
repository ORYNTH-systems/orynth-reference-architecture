# Policy Review Process

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the review process used to evaluate policy changes.

---

## Review Criteria

Evaluate:

- architectural alignment;
- operational impact;
- stakeholder impact;
- implementation feasibility;
- continuity implications.

---

## Review Lifecycle


SUBMIT REVIEW REQUEST

↓

ANALYZE CHANGE

↓

COLLECT FEEDBACK

↓

DETERMINE ACTION

↓

DOCUMENT DECISION


---

## Review Outcomes

Includes:

- approved;
- approved with revision;
- deferred;
- rejected.

---

## Principle

Review processes preserve policy quality and prevent uncontrolled change.
