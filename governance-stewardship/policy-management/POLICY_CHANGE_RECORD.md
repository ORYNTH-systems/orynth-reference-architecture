# Policy Change Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Preserve historical records of policy modifications.

---

## Change Record Structure

Includes:

- policy identifier;
- version;
- change description;
- reason;
- reviewer;
- approval status;
- effective date.

---

## Change Lifecycle


PROPOSE

↓

REVIEW

↓

APPROVE

↓

IMPLEMENT

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Principle

Policy history maintains continuity and enables future review.
