# Amendment Governance Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how constitutional amendments are proposed, evaluated, and integrated.

---

## Amendment Principle

Changes to foundational structures require deliberate review and traceable justification.

---

## Amendment Lifecycle


PROPOSE AMENDMENT

↓

DOCUMENT RATIONALE

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

REVIEW COMPATIBILITY

↓

APPROVE OR REJECT

↓

ARCHIVE DECISION


---

## Amendment Record

Includes:

- amendment identifier;
- affected components;
- rationale;
- review findings;
- decision;
- effective version.

---

## Principle

Amendment governance enables evolution without uncontrolled architectural drift.
