# Invariant Preservation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH identifies and protects elements that must remain stable across evolution.

---

## Invariant Principle

Certain architectural properties must remain preserved to maintain system identity.

---

## Preservation Areas

Includes:

- core principles;
- governance logic;
- terminology integrity;
- evidence continuity;
- architectural boundaries.

---

## Preservation Lifecycle


IDENTIFY INVARIANT

↓

DOCUMENT REQUIREMENT

↓

EVALUATE CHANGE

↓

VERIFY PRESERVATION

↓

RECORD RESULT


---

## Change Constraint

A modification must demonstrate whether it:

- preserves identity;
- extends capability;
- creates a successor architecture.

---

## Principle

Preserved invariants provide continuity across generations of systems.
