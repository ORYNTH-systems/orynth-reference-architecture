# Governance Activity Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Preserve records of governance activities and decisions.

---

## Record Structure

Includes:

- activity identifier;
- date;
- participants;
- decision context;
- action taken;
- supporting evidence;
- outcome.

---

## Record Lifecycle


CREATE

↓

REVIEW

↓

APPROVE

↓

STORE

↓

REFERENCE


---

## Principle

Governance records create continuity across future operations.
