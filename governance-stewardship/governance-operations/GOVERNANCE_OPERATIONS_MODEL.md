## Purpose

Define operational structures for governing ORYNTH activities, decisions, and ecosystem processes.

---

## Governance Principle

Governance ensures that growth, change, and execution remain aligned with established architectural principles.

---

## Governance Areas

Includes:

- decision management;
- operational oversight;
- change review;
- record preservation;
- responsibility assignment.
