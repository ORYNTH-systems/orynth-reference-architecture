# Decision Process Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how governance decisions are evaluated and executed.

---

## Decision Requirements

Each decision should preserve:

- context;
- rationale;
- authority;
- evidence;
- outcome.

---

## Decision Lifecycle


SUBMIT

↓

ASSESS

↓

DELIBERATE

↓

DECIDE

↓

IMPLEMENT

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Decision Categories

Includes:

- architectural decisions;
- operational decisions;
- policy decisions;
- ecosystem decisions.

---

## Principle

Traceable decisions maintain accountability and institutional memory.
