## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track continuous governance states, evolutionary decisions, transformations, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Continuous governance states preserve relationships between intelligence architectures, transformation events, governance decisions, and resulting persistent configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- continuous intelligence state;
- architecture state;
- evolutionary condition;
- transition eligibility;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.
