# Ecosystem Structure Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structural layers that compose the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Structure Principle

An ecosystem structure organizes components into understandable layers while preserving relationships between them.

---

## Structural Layers

Includes:

- foundational layer;
- research layer;
- implementation layer;
- collaboration layer;
- stewardship layer.

---

## Structure Lifecycle


DEFINE COMPONENTS

↓

ORGANIZE LAYERS

↓

CONNECT SYSTEMS

↓

EVALUATE COHERENCE

↓

UPDATE STRUCTURE

↓

PRESERVE EVOLUTION


---

## Principle

A clear ecosystem structure supports expansion without loss of identity.
