# Ecosystem Modeling Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH ecosystems are represented, analyzed, and understood as interconnected systems.

---

## Ecosystem Modeling Principle

Ecosystem models preserve relationships between participants, systems, knowledge, resources, and outcomes.

---

## Modeling Domains

Includes:

- organizational structures;
- research networks;
- technology systems;
- collaboration pathways;
- impact relationships.

---

## Ecosystem Modeling Lifecycle


IDENTIFY COMPONENTS

↓

MAP RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ANALYZE STRUCTURE

↓

EVALUATE INTERACTIONS

↓

REFINE MODEL

↓

PRESERVE CONTEXT


---

## Principle

Ecosystem modeling enables understanding of complex relationships while maintaining structural clarity.
