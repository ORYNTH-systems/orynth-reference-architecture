# System Relationship Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how relationships between ORYNTH ecosystem components are represented and evaluated.

---

## Relationship Principle

Systems gain meaning through their connections, dependencies, interactions, and shared outcomes.

---

## Relationship Types

Includes:

- dependency relationships;
- collaboration relationships;
- inheritance relationships;
- influence relationships;
- knowledge relationships.

---

## Relationship Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ENTITY

↓

DEFINE CONNECTION

↓

EVALUATE INTERACTION

↓

MAP DEPENDENCY

↓

MAINTAIN RELATIONSHIP


---

## Principle

Explicit relationship modeling preserves ecosystem coherence.
