# Collective Knowledge Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how knowledge generated across the ORYNTH ecosystem is collected, organized, and preserved.

---

## Collective Knowledge Principle

Collective knowledge emerges from connected research, implementation, collaboration, and operational experience.

---

## Knowledge Categories

Includes:

- research findings;
- implementation lessons;
- institutional knowledge;
- collaboration insights;
- ecosystem observations.

---

## Knowledge Lifecycle


GENERATE KNOWLEDGE

↓

VALIDATE INFORMATION

↓

CONNECT CONTEXT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD

↓

SHARE UNDERSTANDING

↓

ENABLE FUTURE LEARNING


---

## Principle

Collective knowledge transforms individual contributions into persistent ecosystem capability.
