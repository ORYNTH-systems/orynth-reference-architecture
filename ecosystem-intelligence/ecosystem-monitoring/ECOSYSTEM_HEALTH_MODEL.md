# Ecosystem Health Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates the overall condition and sustainability of its ecosystem.

---

## Ecosystem Health Principle

Ecosystem health represents the alignment, activity, resilience, and continuity of connected participants and systems.

---

## Health Indicators

Includes:

- collaboration activity;
- knowledge exchange;
- institutional alignment;
- operational continuity;
- growth capacity.

---

## Ecosystem Health Lifecycle


ESTABLISH BASELINE

↓

MEASURE CONDITIONS

↓

IDENTIFY VARIANCE

↓

EVALUATE HEALTH

↓

APPLY IMPROVEMENT

↓

REASSESS STATE


---

## Principle

Ecosystem health measurement supports sustainable growth and long-term continuity.
