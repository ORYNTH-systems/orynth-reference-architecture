# Change Signal Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how changes affecting the ORYNTH ecosystem are identified, classified, and evaluated.

---

## Change Signal Principle

Change signals provide early awareness of shifts in relationships, technology, capability, and external conditions.

---

## Signal Categories

Includes:

- growth signals;
- risk signals;
- partnership signals;
- technology signals;
- governance signals.

---

## Change Signal Lifecycle


DETECT SIGNAL

↓

CLASSIFY CHANGE

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

DETERMINE RESPONSE

↓

TRACK EVOLUTION

↓

ARCHIVE RESULT


---

## Principle

Change signal analysis enables proactive ecosystem management while preserving continuity.
