# Collaboration Signal Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how collaboration activity and relationship signals are identified and evaluated.

---

## Collaboration Signal Principle

Collaboration signals reveal patterns of engagement, contribution, and ecosystem development.

---

## Signal Categories

Includes:

- participation signals;
- communication signals;
- contribution signals;
- alignment signals;
- growth signals.

---

## Collaboration Signal Lifecycle


OBSERVE ACTIVITY

↓

CAPTURE SIGNAL

↓

CLASSIFY CONDITION

↓

ANALYZE PATTERN

↓

RESPOND OR ADAPT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Collaboration signals provide visibility into ecosystem relationship health and evolution.
