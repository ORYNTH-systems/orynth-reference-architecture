# Relationship Intelligence Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH analyzes, preserves, and understands relationships across its ecosystem.

---

## Relationship Intelligence Principle

Relationship intelligence identifies meaningful connections between participants, organizations, systems, and shared objectives.

---

## Relationship Domains

Includes:

- institutional relationships;
- research relationships;
- collaboration relationships;
- partnership relationships;
- knowledge relationships.

---

IDENTIFY RELATIONSHIP

↓

CAPTURE CONTEXT

↓

ANALYZE INTERACTION

↓

EVALUATE VALUE

↓

MAINTAIN CONNECTION

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY

Relationship intelligence transforms interactions into structured ecosystem knowledge.
