# Partner Analysis Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates ecosystem partners and collaboration opportunities.

---

## Partner Analysis Principle

Partner analysis evaluates alignment, contribution potential, relationship context, and long-term compatibility.

---

## Analysis Factors

Includes:

- shared objectives;
- technical alignment;
- research contribution;
- resource compatibility;
- governance compatibility.

---

## Partner Analysis Lifecycle


IDENTIFY PARTNER

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

EVALUATE CONTRIBUTION

↓

DEFINE RELATIONSHIP PATH

↓

TRACK DEVELOPMENT

↓

REVIEW OUTCOME


---

## Principle

Structured partner analysis supports sustainable ecosystem growth.
