# Future Scenario Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates possible future states and development pathways.

---

## Scenario Principle

Future scenarios represent structured possibilities used to evaluate decisions, risks, and opportunities.

---

## Scenario Factors

Includes:

- technological change;
- ecosystem growth;
- adoption pathways;
- resource conditions;
- governance requirements.

---

## Scenario Lifecycle


DEFINE FUTURE QUESTION

↓

IDENTIFY VARIABLES

↓

MODEL POSSIBILITIES

↓

EVALUATE IMPACT

↓

SELECT STRATEGIC RESPONSE

↓

PRESERVE ANALYSIS


---

## Principle

Scenario modeling supports preparation without assuming a single future outcome.
