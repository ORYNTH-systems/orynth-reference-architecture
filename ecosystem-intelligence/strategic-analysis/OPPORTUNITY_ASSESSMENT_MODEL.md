# Opportunity Assessment Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates potential opportunities for alignment, expansion, and collaboration.

---

## Opportunity Principle

Opportunities are evaluated through alignment, feasibility, impact potential, and continuity requirements.

---

## Assessment Factors

Includes:

- strategic alignment;
- technical compatibility;
- ecosystem value;
- resource requirements;
- governance implications.

---

## Opportunity Lifecycle


IDENTIFY OPPORTUNITY

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

EVALUATE VALUE

↓

REVIEW RISKS

↓

DETERMINE PATH

↓

TRACK RESULT


---

## Principle

Structured opportunity assessment enables growth while preserving system integrity.
