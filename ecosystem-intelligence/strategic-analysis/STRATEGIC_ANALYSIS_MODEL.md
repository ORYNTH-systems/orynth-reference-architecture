# Strategic Analysis Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates strategic conditions, opportunities, and long-term direction.

---

## Strategic Analysis Principle

Strategic analysis connects ecosystem information, capabilities, constraints, and future conditions to support informed planning.

---

## Analysis Domains

Includes:

- ecosystem growth;
- technology alignment;
- institutional opportunities;
- capability development;
- future planning.

---

## Strategic Analysis Lifecycle


IDENTIFY OBJECTIVE

↓

COLLECT CONTEXT

↓

ANALYZE CONDITIONS

↓

EVALUATE OPTIONS

↓

DEFINE STRATEGY

↓

REVIEW OUTCOME


---

## Principle

Strategic analysis enables deliberate expansion while preserving architectural alignment.
