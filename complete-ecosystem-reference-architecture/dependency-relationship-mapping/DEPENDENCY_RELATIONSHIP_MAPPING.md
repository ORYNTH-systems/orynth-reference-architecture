# Dependency Relationship Mapping

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the dependency mapping framework used to identify, classify, and preserve relationships between ORYNTH architecture components.

---

## Dependency Principle

Dependency relationship mapping establishes the directional relationships between architecture layers, preserving prerequisite order, integration requirements, and system continuity.

---

## Dependency Domains

Includes:

- foundational dependencies;
- architectural dependencies;
- operational dependencies;
- knowledge dependencies;
- governance dependencies;
- intelligence dependencies;
- integration dependencies.
