# Unified Autonomous Intelligence Modeling

Version:

v1.0.0

## Purpose

Define the framework used to model autonomous intelligence capabilities within ORYNTH architecture systems, including autonomy conditions, operational relationships, governance constraints, and continuity requirements.

---

## Autonomy Principle

Unified Autonomous Intelligence Modeling represents autonomy as governed capability: an intelligence structure capable of adaptive operation while remaining bounded by architecture rules, validation conditions, provenance requirements, and continuity preservation.

Autonomy operates through maintained alignment between capability and governance.
