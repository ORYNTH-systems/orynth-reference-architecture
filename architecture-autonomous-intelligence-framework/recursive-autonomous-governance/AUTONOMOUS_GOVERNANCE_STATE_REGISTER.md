# Autonomous Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track autonomous governance states, recursive decisions, transitions, validation conditions, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Governance states preserve relationships between autonomous intelligence conditions, architecture constraints, authority states, and resulting autonomous configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- autonomous state;
- architecture context;
- authority condition;
- constraint evaluation;
- transition decision;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE GOVERNANCE RECORD

↓

CAPTURE AUTONOMOUS CONDITION

↓

EVALUATE GOVERNANCE CONTEXT

↓

ANALYZE RECURSIVE EFFECTS

↓

VALIDATE TRANSITION

↓

REGISTER GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY

## Principle

An autonomous governance state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH autonomous intelligence operations and their recursive governance processes.
