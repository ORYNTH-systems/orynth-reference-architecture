# Recursive Autonomous Governance

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to govern autonomous intelligence states across ORYNTH architecture systems, authority structures, and continuity models.

---

## Governance Principle

Recursive Autonomous Governance enables autonomous systems to continuously evaluate, adapt, and preserve operational integrity through recursive governance cycles.

Autonomy remains bounded by validated authority, architecture constraints, and continuity requirements.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- autonomous decision governance;
- authority preservation;
- adaptive behavior governance;
- architecture constraint governance;
- recursive evaluation;
- transition management;
- continuity preservation;
- validation governance.
