# Intelligence Ecosystem Integration

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to integrate ORYNTH intelligence domains into a coordinated ecosystem while preserving identity, relationships, governance conditions, and continuity.

---

## Integration Principle

Intelligence Ecosystem Integration establishes a coordinated intelligence environment where distinct intelligence capabilities maintain structured relationships while contributing to a unified architecture.

Integration creates cooperation without removing specialization.

---

## Integration Domains

Includes:

- intelligence capability integration;
- architecture intelligence relationships;
- knowledge exchange structures;
- governance coordination;
- continuity relationships;
- ecosystem dependency management;
- intelligence evolution tracking.
