## Dependency Principle

Each intelligence layer exists within a defined architectural relationship. Higher-order layers depend upon the preservation and validation of lower-order layers.

Dependency order prevents semantic drift and architectural conflation.

## Dependency Rules

### Rule 1 — Foundation Precedence

All intelligence layers require constitutional identity and integrity constraints.

### Rule 2 — Capability Before Synthesis

Intelligence synthesis requires defined intelligence capabilities.

### Rule 3 — Governance Before Transformation

Evolutionary transitions require governance validation.

### Rule 4 — Continuity Before Evolution

Evolution requires preservation of prior architectural identity.

### Rule 5 — Synthesis Requires Traceability

Integrated architecture requires complete dependency lineage.

---

## Resolution Principle

Intelligence layers are not independent modules. They form a dependency-preserving architectural continuum.
