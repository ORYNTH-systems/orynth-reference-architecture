# Architecture Ordering Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the canonical ordering model for ORYNTH architecture execution and interpretation.

---

## Ordering Principle

Architectural layers must be interpreted according to dependency order rather than surface similarity.

---

## Canonical Ordering

Constitutional Identity
Authority and Integrity
Execution Assurance
Intelligence Formation
Intelligence Synthesis
Intelligence Expansion
Intelligence Integration
Intelligence Governance
Intelligence Continuity
Intelligence Evolution
Architecture Synthesis
Constitutional Closure

---

## Ordering Constraints

The architecture preserves:

- dependency sequence;
- semantic ownership;
- transition eligibility;
- validation authority;
- historical continuity.

---

## Principle

The Architecture Ordering Model ensures ORYNTH remains a coherent system rather than a collection of disconnected intelligence concepts.
