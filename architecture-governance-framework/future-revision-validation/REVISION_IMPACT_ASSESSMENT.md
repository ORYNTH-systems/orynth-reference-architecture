# Revision Impact Assessment

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the impact analysis process for proposed architectural revisions.

---

## Assessment Principle

Every revision must be evaluated across all affected architectural relationships before acceptance.

---

## Impact Categories

Includes:

### Structural Impact

Changes to architecture organization or dependencies.

### Semantic Impact

Changes to definitions, meanings, or relationships.

### Governance Impact

Changes affecting validation or authority models.

### Continuity Impact

Changes affecting historical or identity preservation.

### Operational Impact

Changes affecting implementation or execution.

---

## Assessment Model


PROPOSED CHANGE

↓

AFFECTED COMPONENTS

↓

IMPACT CATEGORIES

↓

RISK EVALUATION

↓

MITIGATION REQUIREMENTS

↓

VALIDATION DECISION


---

## Principle

Revision impact assessment prevents unintended architectural divergence.
