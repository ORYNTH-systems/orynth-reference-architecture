# Revision Certification Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the certification record maintained for approved architectural revisions.

---

## Certification Principle

Each accepted revision receives a documented certification record preserving the transition from previous state to updated state.

---

## Certification Record Fields

Includes:

- Revision ID;
- Previous Reference State;
- Updated Reference State;
- Change Description;
- Validation Evidence;
- Identity Preservation Result;
- Approval Status;
- Release Version.

---

## Certification Lifecycle


REVISION SUBMITTED

↓

VALIDATION COMPLETED

↓

CERTIFICATION DECISION

↓

VERSION UPDATE

↓

REFERENCE STATE PRESERVED

↓

HISTORY RECORDED


---

## Principle

Revision certification maintains traceability between Morning Star versions and preserves long-term architectural continuity.
