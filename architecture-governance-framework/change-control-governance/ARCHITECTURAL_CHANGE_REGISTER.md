# Architectural Change Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the historical record structure for architectural changes.

---

## Register Principle

Every accepted or rejected change maintains a permanent governance record.

---

## Change Record Fields

Includes:

- Change ID;
- Version;
- Date;
- Description;
- Affected Architecture Layer;
- Impact Assessment;
- Validation Result;
- Approval Status;
- Final Disposition.

---

## Change Lifecycle


IDENTIFY CHANGE

↓

REGISTER REQUEST

↓

EVALUATE IMPACT

↓

VALIDATE TRANSITION

↓

RECORD DECISION

↓

UPDATE HISTORY


---

## Principle

The Architectural Change Register preserves complete lineage between reference states and future revisions.
