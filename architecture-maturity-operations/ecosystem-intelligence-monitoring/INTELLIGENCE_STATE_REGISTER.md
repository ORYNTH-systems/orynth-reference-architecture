# Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track ecosystem intelligence states, observations, interpretations, and maturity implications.

---

## State Principle

Intelligence states preserve relationships between observed signals, analytical findings, architectural relevance, and resulting operational awareness.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- intelligence identity;
- observation source;
- signal classification;
- analysis state;
- relevance assessment;
- continuity record.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE SIGNAL

↓

CLASSIFY OBSERVATION

↓

ANALYZE RELATIONSHIP

↓

ASSESS MATURITY IMPACT

↓

UPDATE INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An intelligence state register maintains traceability between ecosystem observations and ORYNTH maturity decisions.
