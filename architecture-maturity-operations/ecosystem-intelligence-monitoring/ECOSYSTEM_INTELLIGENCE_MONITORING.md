## Purpose

Define the intelligence monitoring framework used to observe, interpret, and organize ecosystem-level signals across the ORYNTH architecture.

---

## Intelligence Monitoring Principle

Ecosystem intelligence monitoring identifies meaningful patterns, relationships, opportunities, and emerging conditions while preserving traceability between observations and architectural decisions.

---

## Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- ecosystem signals;
- architecture interactions;
- technology developments;
- research indicators;
- external environment;
- capability opportunities;
- emerging constraints.
