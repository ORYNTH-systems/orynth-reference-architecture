# Alignment State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track cross-domain intelligence alignment states, relationships, validation conditions, and evolutionary lineage.

---

## State Principle

Alignment states preserve relationships between intelligence domains, architecture contexts, governance structures, coherence requirements, and resulting aligned configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- alignment identity;
- intelligence source domains;
- architecture context;
- relationship mappings;
- alignment conditions;
- validation status;
- resulting aligned state;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE ALIGNMENT RECORD

↓

CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE STATES

↓

MAP DOMAIN RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT CONDITIONS

↓

VALIDATE ALIGNMENT

↓

REGISTER STATE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

An alignment state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence domains and their coherent architectural relationships.
