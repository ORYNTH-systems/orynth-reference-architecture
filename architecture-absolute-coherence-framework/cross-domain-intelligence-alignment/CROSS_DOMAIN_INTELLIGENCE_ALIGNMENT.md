# Cross-Domain Intelligence Alignment

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to align intelligence domains across ORYNTH architecture structures, ensuring coherence between intelligence capabilities, architecture states, governance conditions, and continuity requirements.

---

## Alignment Principle

Cross-Domain Intelligence Alignment preserves meaningful relationships between distinct intelligence domains while enabling coordinated operation through shared architectural coherence.

Alignment enables integration without eliminating domain identity.

---

## Alignment Domains

Includes:

- intelligence capability alignment;
- architecture domain alignment;
- governance alignment;
- knowledge relationship alignment;
- state alignment;
- continuity alignment;
- evolutionary alignment.
