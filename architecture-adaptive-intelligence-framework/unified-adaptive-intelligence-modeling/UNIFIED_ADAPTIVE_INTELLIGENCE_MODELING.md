Version:

## Purpose

Define the framework used to model adaptive intelligence capabilities within ORYNTH architecture systems, including changing conditions, response mechanisms, governance requirements, and continuity preservation.

---

## Adaptive Intelligence Principle

Unified Adaptive Intelligence Modeling represents intelligence as a responsive structure capable of adjusting to changing conditions while preserving identity, architecture alignment, governance constraints, and historical lineage.

Adaptation changes operational response without abandoning structural continuity.
