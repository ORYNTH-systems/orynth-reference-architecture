# Recursive Adaptive Governance

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to govern adaptive intelligence states across ORYNTH architecture systems, changing conditions, and continuity models.

---

## Governance Principle

Recursive Adaptive Governance enables intelligence systems to observe conditions, evaluate adaptation requirements, and apply controlled responses through recursive governance cycles.

Adaptation remains bounded by architecture integrity, validation requirements, and continuity preservation.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- adaptive decision governance;
- condition evaluation;
- response validation;
- architecture preservation;
- constraint management;
- transition control;
- continuity governance;
- recursive assessment.
