# Evolution Framework Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH systems evolve through controlled, traceable, and governed progression.

---

## Evolution Principle

Evolution expands capability while preserving foundational identity, governance, and continuity.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- architectural evolution;
- technical evolution;
- research evolution;
- ecosystem evolution;
- capability evolution.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


IDENTIFY OPPORTUNITY

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

DESIGN EVOLUTION

↓

VALIDATE TRANSITION

↓

IMPLEMENT CHANGE

↓

PRESERVE LINEAGE


---

## Principle

Controlled evolution enables advancement without loss of system coherence.
