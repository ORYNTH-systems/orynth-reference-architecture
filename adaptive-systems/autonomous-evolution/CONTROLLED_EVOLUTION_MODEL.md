# Controlled Evolution Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define boundaries and controls for future ORYNTH system evolution.

---

## Evolution Control Principle

Changes must be evaluated before integration to preserve stability, identity, and governance.

---

## Control Areas

Includes:

- change classification;
- impact evaluation;
- validation requirements;
- approval pathways;
- historical recording.

---

## Controlled Evolution Lifecycle


PROPOSE CHANGE

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

REVIEW COMPATIBILITY

↓

AUTHORIZE TRANSITION

↓

IMPLEMENT

↓

VERIFY RESULT

↓

ARCHIVE CHANGE


---

## Principle

Governed evolution allows systems to grow while maintaining continuity.
