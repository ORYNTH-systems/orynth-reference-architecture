# Successor Adaptation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how successor systems may emerge while maintaining traceable inheritance from previous architectures.

---

## Successor Principle

A successor extends validated capabilities while preserving essential principles and historical lineage.

---

## Successor Evaluation Areas

Includes:

- inherited foundations;
- architectural differences;
- capability expansion;
- governance compatibility;
- continuity requirements.

---

## Successor Lifecycle


IDENTIFY SUCCESSOR NEED

↓

DEFINE SUCCESSOR PURPOSE

↓

MAP INHERITED PRINCIPLES

↓

EVALUATE DIFFERENCES

↓

VALIDATE SUCCESSOR

↓

ESTABLISH LINEAGE


---

## Principle

Successor systems evolve from preserved knowledge rather than uncontrolled replacement.
