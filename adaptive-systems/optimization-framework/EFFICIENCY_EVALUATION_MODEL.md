# Efficiency Evaluation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates efficiency across systems and processes.

---

## Efficiency Principle

Efficiency measures how effectively resources, processes, and capabilities produce desired outcomes.

---

## Evaluation Areas

Includes:

- resource usage;
- process effectiveness;
- operational cost;
- time efficiency;
- outcome quality.

---

## Evaluation Lifecycle


DEFINE METRICS

↓

COLLECT DATA

↓

ANALYZE PERFORMANCE

↓

COMPARE CONDITIONS

↓

IDENTIFY IMPROVEMENTS

↓

REASSESS


---

## Principle

Efficiency evaluation ensures improvement efforts remain aligned with meaningful outcomes.
