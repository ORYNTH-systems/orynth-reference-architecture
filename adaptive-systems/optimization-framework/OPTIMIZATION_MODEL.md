# Optimization Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH systems identify improvement opportunities and enhance performance while preserving architectural integrity.

---

## Optimization Principle

Optimization improves system capability through measured refinement, not uncontrolled modification.

---

## Optimization Domains

Includes:

- performance;
- efficiency;
- reliability;
- scalability;
- resource utilization.

---

## Optimization Lifecycle


IDENTIFY BASELINE

↓

MEASURE PERFORMANCE

↓

ANALYZE LIMITATIONS

↓

DEFINE IMPROVEMENT

↓

IMPLEMENT CHANGE

↓

VERIFY RESULT


---

## Principle

Optimization strengthens systems through evidence-based improvement while maintaining continuity.
