# System Resilience Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH systems maintain stability, recover from disruption, and preserve essential functions.

---

## Resilience Principle

Resilience enables systems to withstand changing conditions while maintaining continuity of purpose and operation.

---

## Resilience Domains

Includes:

- structural resilience;
- operational resilience;
- knowledge resilience;
- governance resilience;
- ecosystem resilience.

---

## Resilience Lifecycle


IDENTIFY RISK

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

PREPARE RESPONSE

↓

MAINTAIN FUNCTION

↓

RECOVER STATE

↓

IMPROVE CAPABILITY


---

## Principle

Resilient systems preserve continuity while adapting to disruption.
