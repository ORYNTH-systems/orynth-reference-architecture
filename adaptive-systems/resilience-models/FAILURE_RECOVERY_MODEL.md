# Failure Recovery Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define recovery pathways for maintaining ORYNTH system continuity following disruption or degraded conditions.

---

## Recovery Principle

Failure states should be identifiable, recoverable, and documented.

---

## Recovery Areas

Includes:

- operational recovery;
- data recovery;
- knowledge recovery;
- architectural recovery;
- process recovery.

---

## Recovery Lifecycle


DETECT FAILURE

↓

CLASSIFY CONDITION

↓

INITIATE RESPONSE

↓

RESTORE FUNCTION

↓

VERIFY RECOVERY

↓

DOCUMENT LESSONS


---

## Principle

Recovery processes transform disruption into improved future resilience.
