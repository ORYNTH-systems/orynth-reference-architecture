# Continuity Resilience Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH maintains continuity across evolving operational environments.

---

## Continuity Principle

Continuity preserves essential identity, knowledge, and function through transitions.

---

## Continuity Requirements

Maintain:

- architectural lineage;
- operational records;
- governance structures;
- knowledge assets;
- recovery pathways.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


ESTABLISH BASELINE

↓

MONITOR STATE

↓

IDENTIFY CHANGE

↓

PRESERVE ESSENTIALS

↓

TRANSITION SAFELY

↓

VERIFY CONTINUITY


---

## Principle

Continuity resilience ensures evolution occurs without loss of foundational integrity.
