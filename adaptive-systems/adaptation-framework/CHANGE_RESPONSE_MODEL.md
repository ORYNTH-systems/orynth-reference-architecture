# Change Response Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates and responds to internal and external changes.

---

## Change Principle

Changes require classification, evaluation, and controlled response.

---

## Change Categories

Includes:

- corrective changes;
- evolutionary changes;
- expansion changes;
- successor changes.

---

## Change Response Lifecycle


IDENTIFY CHANGE

↓

CLASSIFY IMPACT

↓

SELECT RESPONSE

↓

EXECUTE TRANSITION

↓

VALIDATE OUTCOME

↓

ARCHIVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Governed change preserves system continuity while enabling advancement.
