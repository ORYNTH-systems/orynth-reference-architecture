# Adaptation Framework Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH systems identify, evaluate, and incorporate adaptation while maintaining architectural alignment.

---

## Adaptation Principle

Adaptation enables systems to respond to changing conditions while preserving essential identity and governance constraints.

---

## Adaptation Domains

Includes:

- environmental change;
- operational change;
- technological change;
- ecosystem change;
- capability evolution.

---

## Adaptation Lifecycle


DETECT CHANGE

↓

ASSESS CONDITION

↓

DEFINE RESPONSE

↓

IMPLEMENT ADAPTATION

↓

VERIFY ALIGNMENT

↓

RECORD EVOLUTION


---

## Principle

Controlled adaptation allows growth without uncontrolled architectural drift.
