# Integrated Evolutionary Architecture

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to integrate evolutionary processes across ORYNTH architecture domains while preserving identity, coherence, and continuity.

---

## Evolutionary Architecture Principle

Integrated evolutionary architecture enables coordinated advancement across intelligence, governance, knowledge, capability, and ecosystem layers through governed transitions.

Evolution is treated as a continuous architectural process rather than isolated change events.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- architecture evolution;
- intelligence evolution;
- governance evolution;
- capability evolution;
- ecosystem evolution;
- knowledge evolution;
- continuity preservation.

Integrated evolutionary architecture enables ORYNTH to advance as a unified system while maintaining traceability between previous, current, and future states.
