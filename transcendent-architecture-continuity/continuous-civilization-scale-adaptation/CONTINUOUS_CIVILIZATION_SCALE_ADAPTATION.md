# Continuous Civilization-Scale Adaptation

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to enable continuous adaptation across future ORYNTH civilization-scale architecture states.

---

## Adaptation Principle

Continuous civilization-scale adaptation enables ORYNTH to respond to evolving environmental, technological, intellectual, governance, and ecosystem conditions while preserving architectural identity, continuity, and traceability.

Adaptation occurs through governed transformation rather than uncontrolled change.

---

## Adaptation Domains

Includes:

- civilization-scale architecture;
- ecosystem response;
- intelligence adaptation;
- governance evolution;
- capability transformation;
- knowledge advancement;
- continuity preservation.

---

## Principle

Continuous civilization-scale adaptation provides the mechanism through which ORYNTH remains capable of long-term evolution while preserving the principles that define its architectural identity.
