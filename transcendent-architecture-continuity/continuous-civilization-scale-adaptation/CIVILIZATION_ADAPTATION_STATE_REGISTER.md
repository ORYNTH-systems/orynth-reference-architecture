# Civilization Adaptation State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track civilization-scale adaptation states, transition conditions, adaptive responses, and continuity relationships.

---

## State Principle

Adaptation states preserve relationships between observed conditions, required transformations, applied responses, and resulting future states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- adaptation identity;
- environmental condition;
- transformation requirement;
- response state;
- continuity condition;
- future readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE ADAPTATION CONDITION

↓

CLASSIFY SYSTEM REQUIREMENT

↓

DESIGN ADAPTIVE RESPONSE

↓

VALIDATE TRANSFORMATION

↓

UPDATE ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A civilization adaptation state register maintains traceability between large-scale ecosystem conditions and ORYNTH evolutionary responses.
