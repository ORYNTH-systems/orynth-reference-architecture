## Governance Intelligence Principle

Recursive governance intelligence enables governance systems to continuously evaluate conditions, refine decision structures, and adapt operational responses while preserving authority boundaries, accountability, and architectural continuity.

- decision intelligence;

- adaptive oversight;

- evolutionary alignment.
