# Governance Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track governance intelligence states, authority conditions, adaptive decisions, and continuity relationships.

---

## State Principle

Governance intelligence states preserve relationships between governance observations, authority structures, decision processes, and resulting evolutionary conditions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- authority condition;
- intelligence assessment;
- decision state;
- adaptation status;
- continuity record.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE GOVERNANCE SIGNAL

↓

CLASSIFY GOVERNANCE CONDITION

↓

ANALYZE AUTHORITY RELATIONSHIP

↓

GENERATE INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

VALIDATE GOVERNANCE RESPONSE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A governance intelligence state register maintains traceability between recursive governance evaluation and ORYNTH continuity operations.
