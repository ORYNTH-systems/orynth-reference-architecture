## Purpose

Define the awareness framework used to observe, contextualize, and understand ecosystem-scale conditions across the ORYNTH architecture.

## Awareness Principle

Universal ecosystem awareness enables recognition of relationships, patterns, conditions, and transitions across interconnected systems while preserving governance boundaries, architectural identity, and traceability.

- intelligence signals;
- knowledge structures;
- governance states;
- capability interactions;
- evolutionary patterns.
