# Funding Decision Archive

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Preserve decisions and outcomes related to funding activity.

---

## Decision Record

Includes:

- funding opportunity;
- decision date;
- decision outcome;
- reasoning;
- lessons learned;
- future action.

---

## Decision Categories

Includes:

- accepted;
- declined;
- withdrawn;
- deferred;
- pending.

---

## Archive Principle

Funding decisions provide historical context for future strategy.

---

## Invariant

Past decisions inform future funding alignment without constraining future opportunities.
