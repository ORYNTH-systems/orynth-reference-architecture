# Funding History Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Preserve the historical record of ORYNTH funding activity.

---

## Funding Record Structure

Each record includes:

- opportunity;
- organization;
- submission date;
- funding category;
- requested support;
- outcome;
- follow-up actions.

---

## Historical Categories

Includes:

- grants;
- foundations;
- sponsorships;
- research support;
- strategic contributions.

---

## Principle

Funding history preserves institutional memory and strategic continuity.
