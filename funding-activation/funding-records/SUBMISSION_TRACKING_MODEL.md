# Submission Tracking Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how funding submissions are tracked from preparation through resolution.

---

## Submission Lifecycle


IDENTIFY

↓

QUALIFY

↓

PREPARE

↓

SUBMIT

↓

REVIEW

↓

RESOLVE

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Submission Data

Tracks:

- opportunity source;
- submission materials;
- deadlines;
- status;
- communications;
- decisions.

---

## Status Categories

Includes:

- researching;
- preparing;
- submitted;
- under review;
- accepted;
- declined;
- closed.

---

## Principle

Every funding action maintains traceable history.
