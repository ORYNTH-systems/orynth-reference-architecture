# Sponsorship Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define structured sponsorship pathways supporting ORYNTH research activities.

---

## Sponsorship Principle

Support relationships should enable research continuity while preserving architectural independence.

---

## Sponsorship Categories

Includes:

- research sponsorship;
- technology sponsorship;
- institutional support;
- ecosystem contribution.

---

## Sponsorship Lifecycle


IDENTIFY SUPPORTER

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

DEFINE CONTRIBUTION

↓

ESTABLISH RELATIONSHIP

↓

REVIEW CONTINUITY


---

## Requirements

Sponsorship relationships should define:

- purpose;
- contribution scope;
- expectations;
- documentation.

---

## Principle

Sponsorship supports advancement without altering governance authority.
