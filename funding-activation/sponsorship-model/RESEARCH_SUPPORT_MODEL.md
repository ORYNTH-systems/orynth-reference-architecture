# Research Support Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how external resources support ORYNTH research operations.

---

## Support Areas

Includes:

- research development;
- infrastructure;
- documentation;
- validation activities;
- operational requirements.

---

## Support Evaluation

Consider:

- strategic relevance;
- sustainability value;
- resource efficiency;
- governance compatibility.

---

## Support Lifecycle


IDENTIFY NEED

↓

MATCH RESOURCE

↓

ALLOCATE SUPPORT

↓

MEASURE VALUE

↓

RECORD OUTCOME


---

## Principle

Resources should amplify research capability while maintaining traceability.
