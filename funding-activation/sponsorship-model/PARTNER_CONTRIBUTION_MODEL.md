# Partner Contribution Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how partners contribute resources, expertise, or capability.

---

## Contribution Types

Includes:

- financial contribution;
- technical contribution;
- research contribution;
- infrastructure contribution;
- knowledge contribution.

---

## Contribution Record

Tracks:

- contributor;
- contribution type;
- purpose;
- duration;
- outcome.

---

## Principle

Contributions are preserved as part of ecosystem lineage.
