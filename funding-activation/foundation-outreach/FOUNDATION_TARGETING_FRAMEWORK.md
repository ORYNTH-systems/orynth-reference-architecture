# Foundation Targeting Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how potential foundations are evaluated.

---

## Evaluation Areas

Includes:

- mission alignment;
- funding priorities;
- research interests;
- strategic compatibility.

---

## Target Categories

Includes:

- science foundations;
- technology foundations;
- public-interest organizations;
- research initiatives.

---

## Principle

Target selection should maximize alignment and long-term value.
