# Foundation Engagement Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH engages with research foundations and philanthropic organizations.

---

## Engagement Principles

Maintain:

- research clarity;
- transparent communication;
- defined objectives;
- documented outcomes.

INTRODUCE ORYNTH

DISCUSS OPPORTUNITY

↓

TRACK OUTCOME

Foundation relationships are built through shared research objectives.
