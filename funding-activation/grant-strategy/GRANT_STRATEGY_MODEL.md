# Grant Strategy Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the strategic framework for securing research funding aligned with ORYNTH objectives.

---

## Funding Principle

Funding supports research continuity while preserving architectural independence and governance.

---

## Funding Categories

Includes:

- research grants;
- technology initiatives;
- institutional programs;
- foundation support;
- strategic research partnerships.

---

## Grant Lifecycle


IDENTIFY OPPORTUNITY

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

PREPARE APPLICATION

↓

SUBMIT

↓

TRACK OUTCOME

↓

RECORD RESULT


---

## Evaluation Criteria

Consider:

- mission alignment;
- research relevance;
- sustainability contribution;
- governance compatibility.

---

## Principle

Funding pathways should strengthen research capability without compromising architectural integrity.
