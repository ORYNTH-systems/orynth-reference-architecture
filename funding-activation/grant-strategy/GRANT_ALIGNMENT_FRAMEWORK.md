# Grant Alignment Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Evaluate whether funding opportunities align with ORYNTH research goals.

---

## Alignment Dimensions

### Research Alignment

Does the opportunity support meaningful research advancement?

---

### Architectural Alignment

Does the opportunity respect ORYNTH principles and boundaries?

---

### Sustainability Alignment

Does the opportunity support long-term continuity?

---

## Decision Outcomes

- aligned;
- requires adaptation;
- monitor;
- decline.

---

## Principle

Strategic alignment is prioritized over funding volume.
