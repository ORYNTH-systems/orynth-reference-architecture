# Funding Application Pipeline

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the workflow for funding applications.

---

## Pipeline Stages

### Discovery

Identify relevant opportunities.

---

### Qualification

Evaluate:

- eligibility;
- alignment;
- strategic value.

---

### Preparation

Develop:

- proposal materials;
- research descriptions;
- supporting documentation.

---

### Submission

Record:

- application date;
- opportunity;
- submitted materials.

---

### Review

Track:

- responses;
- decisions;
- follow-up actions.

---

## Principle

Funding activity requires structured tracking and historical preservation.
