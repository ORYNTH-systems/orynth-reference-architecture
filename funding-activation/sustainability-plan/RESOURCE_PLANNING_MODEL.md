# Resource Planning Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH resources are planned and allocated.

---

## Resource Categories

Includes:

- financial resources;
- technical resources;
- human expertise;
- infrastructure.

---

## Planning Factors

Evaluate:

- current needs;
- future requirements;
- strategic priorities;
- continuity risks.

---

## Principle

Resource planning converts strategic goals into sustainable execution capacity.
