## Purpose

Define the framework used to model synthesis relationships between ORYNTH systems, architecture components, intelligence structures, governance mechanisms, and continuity models.

---

## Synthesis Principle

Unified System Synthesis Modeling represents ORYNTH architecture as an integrated synthesis of distinct systems where combined capability emerges through structured relationships while preserving component identity.

Synthesis creates unity without eliminating structure.
