# Universal Knowledge Graph Architecture

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the architecture used to represent, connect, navigate, and evolve knowledge relationships across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Knowledge Graph Principle

A universal knowledge graph provides a relational structure that connects knowledge entities, concepts, domains, evidence, relationships, and transformations while preserving context and traceability.

---

## Graph Domains

Includes:

- knowledge entities;
- semantic relationships;
- domain connections;
- provenance relationships;
- conceptual mappings;
- evolutionary pathways.

---

## Knowledge Graph Lifecycle


IDENTIFY KNOWLEDGE ENTITIES

↓

MAP RELATIONSHIPS

↓

CONNECT KNOWLEDGE DOMAINS

↓

ANALYZE STRUCTURAL RELATIONSHIPS

↓

REFINE KNOWLEDGE GRAPH

↓

PRESERVE GRAPH HISTORY


---

## Principle

A universal knowledge graph architecture provides the relational foundation for interconnected knowledge discovery, synthesis, and evolution across ORYNTH.
