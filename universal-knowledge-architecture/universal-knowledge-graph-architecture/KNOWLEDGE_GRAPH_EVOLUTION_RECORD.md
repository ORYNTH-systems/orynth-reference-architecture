## Purpose

Define how the universal knowledge graph evolves over time.

## Evolution Principle

Knowledge graph evolution records preserve new relationships, expanded domains, semantic refinement, and future knowledge synthesis pathways.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- graph expansion;
- relationship refinement;
- semantic development;
- domain integration;
- knowledge discovery.
