# Knowledge Operating State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track knowledge operating states, access conditions, processing states, and transitions.

---

## State Principle

Knowledge operating states preserve relationships between knowledge structures, operational contexts, synthesis processes, governance conditions, and resulting knowledge states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- knowledge context;
- operational state;
- access condition;
- synthesis state;
- governance status;
- continuity record.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE STATE

↓

ASSESS OPERATING CONDITION

↓

PROCESS KNOWLEDGE ACTIVITY

↓

VERIFY GOVERNANCE ALIGNMENT

↓

UPDATE KNOWLEDGE MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A knowledge operating state register maintains traceability between knowledge operations and their operational environment.
