Version:

## Purpose

Define the operational knowledge layer used to manage knowledge access, synthesis, reasoning support, adaptive learning, and knowledge-driven operations across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Knowledge Operating Principle

A universal knowledge operating layer provides the environment through which knowledge structures are accessed, processed, coordinated, refined, and evolved while preserving provenance, governance alignment, and continuity.

---

## Operating Domains

Includes:

- knowledge access;
- knowledge processing;
- knowledge synthesis;
- knowledge coordination;
- knowledge governance;
- knowledge evolution.
