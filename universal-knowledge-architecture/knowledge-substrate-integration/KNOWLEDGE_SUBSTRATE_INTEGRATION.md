# Knowledge Substrate Integration

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the foundational framework used to integrate knowledge structures, information relationships, semantic representations, and knowledge continuity across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Knowledge Substrate Principle

A knowledge substrate provides the foundational structure through which information is represented, connected, validated, and evolved while preserving provenance, relationships, and continuity.

---

## Substrate Domains

Includes:

- knowledge representation;
- information relationships;
- semantic structures;
- provenance tracking;
- knowledge classification;
- continuity preservation.

---

## Integration Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE SOURCES

↓

CLASSIFY KNOWLEDGE STRUCTURES

↓

MAP SEMANTIC RELATIONSHIPS

↓

INTEGRATE KNOWLEDGE REPRESENTATIONS

↓

VALIDATE KNOWLEDGE CONTINUITY

↓

PRESERVE KNOWLEDGE HISTORY

## Principle

Knowledge substrate integration provides the foundational layer for organized, traceable, and continuously evolving knowledge across the ORYNTH architecture.
