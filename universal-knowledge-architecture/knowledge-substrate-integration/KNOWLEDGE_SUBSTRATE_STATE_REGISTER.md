# Knowledge Substrate State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track knowledge substrate states, relationships, transformations, and continuity conditions.

---

## State Principle

Knowledge substrate states preserve relationships between information entities, semantic structures, provenance conditions, validation states, and knowledge evolution.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- knowledge identity;
- source relationship;
- semantic state;
- validation condition;
- integration status;
- historical continuity.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE STATE

↓

MAP KNOWLEDGE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ASSESS KNOWLEDGE CONDITION

↓

UPDATE SUBSTRATE MODEL

↓

VERIFY CONTINUITY

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A knowledge substrate state register maintains traceability between knowledge structures and their evolutionary conditions.
