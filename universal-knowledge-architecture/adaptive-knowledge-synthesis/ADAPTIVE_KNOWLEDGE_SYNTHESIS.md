# Adaptive Knowledge Synthesis

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to synthesize knowledge structures through integration, comparison, refinement, and adaptive development across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Knowledge Synthesis Principle

Adaptive knowledge synthesis enables knowledge systems to combine existing information structures, identify relationships, generate refined models, and preserve knowledge continuity through controlled evolution.

---

## Synthesis Domains

Includes:

- knowledge integration;
- pattern identification;
- conceptual synthesis;
- model refinement;
- inference pathways;
- knowledge expansion.

---

## Synthesis Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE STRUCTURES

↓

IDENTIFY RELATIONSHIPS

↓

COMPARE KNOWLEDGE PATTERNS

↓

SYNTHESIZE NEW STRUCTURES

↓

VALIDATE KNOWLEDGE COHERENCE

↓

PRESERVE SYNTHESIS HISTORY


---

## Principle

Adaptive knowledge synthesis provides the mechanism for transforming connected knowledge structures into higher-order understanding while maintaining traceability and continuity.
