# Knowledge Synthesis State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track synthesis states, knowledge transformations, relationships, and validation conditions.

---

## State Principle

Knowledge synthesis states preserve relationships between source knowledge, synthesis processes, resulting structures, and evolutionary conditions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- source knowledge;
- synthesis condition;
- relationship mapping;
- generated structure;
- validation state;
- continuity record.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE STATE

↓

ANALYZE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

PERFORM SYNTHESIS PROCESS

↓

ASSESS RESULTING STRUCTURE

↓

VERIFY KNOWLEDGE ALIGNMENT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A knowledge synthesis state register maintains traceability between knowledge inputs, transformations, and resulting synthesized structures.
