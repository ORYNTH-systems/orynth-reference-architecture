Version:

v1.0.0

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track unified architecture continuity states, synthesis transitions, validation outcomes, and preserved lineage.

---

## State Principle

Unified continuity states preserve relationships between intelligence convergence, architecture integration, system harmonization, validated transitions, and resulting unified architecture configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- continuity identity;
- unified architecture state;
- convergence relationship;
- integration relationship;
- harmonization relationship;
- validation status;
- resulting architecture state;
- historical lineage.
