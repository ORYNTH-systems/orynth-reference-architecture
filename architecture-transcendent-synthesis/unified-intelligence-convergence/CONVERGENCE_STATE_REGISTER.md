# Convergence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track unified intelligence convergence states, domain relationships, validation conditions, and synthesis outcomes.

---

## State Principle

Convergence states preserve relationships between intelligence domains, architecture layers, governance conditions, synthesis processes, and resulting unified states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- convergence identity;
- source intelligence domains;
- architecture relationships;
- governance context;
- synthesis method;
- validation status;
- unified intelligence state;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE CONVERGENCE RECORD

↓

CAPTURE SOURCE INTELLIGENCE STATES

↓

MAP CROSS-DOMAIN RELATIONSHIPS

↓

GENERATE CONVERGENCE MODEL

↓

VALIDATE UNIFIED STATE

↓

REGISTER CONVERGENCE RESULT

↓

PRESERVE SYNTHESIS HISTORY


---

## Principle

A convergence state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence domains and their unified synthesis states.
