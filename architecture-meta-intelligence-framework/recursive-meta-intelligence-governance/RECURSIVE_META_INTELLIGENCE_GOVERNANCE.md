# Recursive Meta-Intelligence Governance

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to govern meta-intelligence processes across ORYNTH architecture systems.

---

## Governance Principle

Recursive Meta-Intelligence Governance enables intelligence architectures to observe, evaluate, and refine their own organizational structures while remaining bounded by constitutional integrity, governance authority, and continuity preservation.

The governance layer evaluates intelligence evolution without becoming indistinguishable from the intelligence itself.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- intelligence architecture observation;
- capability relationship evaluation;
- recursive analysis;
- governance validation;
- constitutional alignment;
- transition management;
- integrity preservation;
- continuity governance.

Recursive Meta-Intelligence Governance preserves:

- intelligence architecture identity;

- observation boundaries;
- governance authority;

- provenance;
- validation evidence;
