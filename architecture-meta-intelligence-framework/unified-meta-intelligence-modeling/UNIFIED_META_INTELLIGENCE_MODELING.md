# Unified Meta-Intelligence Modeling

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to model intelligence architectures as objects of observation, organization, governance, and continuous evolution within ORYNTH.

---

## Meta-Intelligence Principle

Unified Meta-Intelligence Modeling represents the capability to analyze, coordinate, and govern intelligence structures themselves.

Meta-intelligence does not replace intelligence capability; it provides the architectural layer through which intelligence systems are understood and evolved.

---

## Meta-Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- intelligence observation;
- intelligence classification;
- intelligence relationship modeling;
- capability coordination;
- self-analysis;
- governance alignment;
- architecture preservation;
- evolutionary continuity.
