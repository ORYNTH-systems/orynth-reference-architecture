Version:

## Purpose

Define the framework used to preserve ORYNTH architecture continuity across constitutional foundations, intelligence architectures, meta-intelligence governance, and future architectural evolution.

---

## Continuum Principle

The Meta-Architecture Continuum represents the capacity of ORYNTH to preserve, evaluate, and evolve architectures as governed structures.

The architecture itself becomes an object of continuity management.

Evolution occurs through preserved relationships rather than structural replacement.
