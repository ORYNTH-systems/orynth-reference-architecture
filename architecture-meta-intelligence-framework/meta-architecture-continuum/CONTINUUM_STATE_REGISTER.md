# Meta-Architecture State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track meta-architecture states, architectural relationships, governance transitions, validation conditions, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Meta-architecture states preserve relationships between constitutional structures, intelligence architectures, meta-intelligence processes, governance mechanisms, and resulting architectural configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- meta-architecture identity;
- constitutional state;
- intelligence architecture state;
- governance state;
- evolution condition;
- validation status;
- integrity status;
- historical lineage.
