# Verification Relationship Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define relationships between verification activities and architecture layers.

---

## Relationship Principle

Verification establishes whether architecture components remain consistent with their intended roles and relationships.

---

## Relationship Model


FOUNDATION

↓

GOVERNANCE VALIDATION

↓

OPERATIONAL VALIDATION

↓

EVOLUTION VALIDATION

↓

INTELLIGENCE VALIDATION

↓

SYNTHESIS VALIDATION

↓

CERTIFIED STATE


---

## Relationship Types

### Dependency Verification

Confirms required relationships exist.

### Boundary Verification

Confirms responsibilities remain separated.

### Evidence Verification

Confirms supporting records exist.

### Continuity Verification

Confirms state transitions preserve identity.

---

## Principle

The Verification Relationship Model ensures certification evaluates the architecture as a connected system.
