# Stewardship Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for long-term architecture stewardship.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- stewardship identifier;
- reference architecture version;
- maintained artifacts;
- revision history;
- validation requirements;
- public status;
- continuity assessment.

---

## Stewardship Lifecycle


REFERENCE STATE ACTIVE

↓

REVISION PROPOSED

↓

IMPACT ASSESSED

↓

VALIDATION COMPLETED

↓

PUBLIC STATE UPDATED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Stewardship States

Possible states:

- ACTIVE;
- UNDER REVIEW;
- UPDATED;
- CERTIFIED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Stewardship Status Records preserve accountability for the long-term maintenance of the architecture reference state.
