# Certification Decision Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the decision process for architecture reference certification.

---

## Decision Principle

Certification decisions require alignment between evidence, verification outcomes, governance requirements, and continuity conditions.

---

## Decision Model


EVIDENCE STATE

VERIFICATION STATE

GOVERNANCE STATE

CONTINUITY STATE

↓

CERTIFICATION EVALUATION

↓

REFERENCE STATE DECISION


---

## Decision Categories

### Certified

All required conditions satisfied.

### Conditionally Certified

Minor unresolved conditions remain.

### Deferred

Additional evidence or validation required.

### Rejected

Certification requirements not satisfied.

---

## Decision Factors

Includes:

- completeness;
- consistency;
- traceability;
- reproducibility;
- continuity.

---

## Principle

The Certification Decision Model provides a structured method for determining authoritative architecture states.
