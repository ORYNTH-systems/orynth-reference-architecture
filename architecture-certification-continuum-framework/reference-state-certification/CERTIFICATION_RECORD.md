# Certification Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for reference state certification.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- certification identifier;
- architecture version;
- evidence set;
- verification results;
- governance assessment;
- continuity assessment;
- certification decision;
- historical record.

---

## Certification Lifecycle


CERTIFICATION REQUESTED

↓

EVIDENCE REVIEWED

↓

VERIFICATION COMPLETED

↓

DECISION GENERATED

↓

REFERENCE STATE ESTABLISHED

↓

CERTIFICATION PRESERVED


---

## Certification States

Possible states:

- PROPOSED;
- REVIEWING;
- VERIFIED;
- CERTIFIED;
- REFERENCE;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Certification Records preserve the authority and history of validated architecture reference states.
