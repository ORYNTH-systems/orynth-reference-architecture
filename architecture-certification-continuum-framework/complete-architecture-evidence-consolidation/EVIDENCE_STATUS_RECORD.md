# Evidence Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for consolidated architecture evidence.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- evidence identifier;
- source artifact;
- related framework;
- validation reference;
- certification purpose;
- status;
- historical record.

---

## Evidence Lifecycle


EVIDENCE IDENTIFIED

↓

SOURCE VERIFIED

↓

RELATIONSHIP MAPPED

↓

VALIDATION COMPLETED

↓

CERTIFICATION STATUS ASSIGNED

↓

RECORD PRESERVED


---

## Evidence States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- MAPPED;
- VERIFIED;
- VALIDATED;
- CERTIFIED;
- ARCHIVED.

---

## Principle

Evidence Status Records maintain accountability for every artifact contributing to architecture certification.
