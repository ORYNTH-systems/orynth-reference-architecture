# Complete Architecture Evidence Consolidation

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the evidence consolidation framework for the ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Evidence Principle

Certification requires a complete relationship between architectural claims, implementation artifacts, validation records, and historical evidence.

Evidence is not a collection of isolated artifacts. It is a traceable system of proof relationships.

---

## Evidence Domains

Includes:

- architectural definitions;
- framework specifications;
- validation records;
- implementation evidence;
- governance records;
- continuity records;
- publication history.

---

## Evidence Consolidation Lifecycle


ARCHITECTURE ARTIFACTS

↓

EVIDENCE IDENTIFICATION

↓

PROVENANCE MAPPING

↓

VALIDATION ASSOCIATION

↓

CERTIFICATION EVIDENCE SET

↓

REFERENCE ARCHITECTURE RECORD


---

## Consolidation Requirements

Evidence must preserve:

- source identity;
- artifact relationship;
- creation history;
- validation status;
- dependency context;
- continuity linkage.

---

## Principle

Complete Architecture Evidence Consolidation establishes the evidence foundation required for trustworthy certification.
