# Evidence Lineage Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the lineage structure connecting evidence artifacts to architecture states.

---

## Lineage Principle

Every certification artifact must maintain a traceable relationship to its origin, transformation, validation, and final reference state.

---

## Evidence Lineage Model


ARCHITECTURE ORIGIN

↓

FRAMEWORK ARTIFACT

↓

IMPLEMENTATION OR SPECIFICATION

↓

VALIDATION EVENT

↓

CERTIFICATION EVIDENCE

↓

PUBLIC REFERENCE STATE


---

## Lineage Components

Includes:

- originating framework;
- artifact identifier;
- evidence source;
- validation relationship;
- certification relevance;
- historical status.

---

## Lineage States

Possible states:

- CREATED;
- ASSOCIATED;
- VALIDATED;
- CERTIFIED;
- PRESERVED.

---

## Principle

The Evidence Lineage Model preserves the history and authority of certification evidence.
