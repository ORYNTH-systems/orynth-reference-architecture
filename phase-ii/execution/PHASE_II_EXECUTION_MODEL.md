Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase II Architecture Integration Layer

## Purpose

Define the execution model for operating integrated Morning Star architecture.

---

## Execution Principle

Phase II execution coordinates existing constitutional capabilities through governed architecture pathways.

---

## Execution Architecture


REQUEST

↓

ARCHITECTURE CONTEXT

↓

CAPABILITY RESOLUTION

↓

GOVERNANCE REVIEW

↓

EXECUTION

↓

VERIFICATION

↓

STATE RECORD


---

## Execution Stages

## Stage 1 — Context

Identify:

- environment;
- applicable architecture;
- required capabilities.

---

## Stage 2 — Resolution

Determine:

- capability pathway;
- interface requirements;
- authority conditions.

---

## Stage 3 — Governance

Validate:

- permission;
- constraints;
- decision authority.

---

## Stage 4 — Execution

Perform:

- coordinated operation;
- state transition;
- evidence generation.

---

## Stage 5 — Verification

Confirm:

- expected outcome;
- preserved identity;
- complete evidence.

---

## Stage 6 — Recording

Store:

- execution history;
- state;
- verification;
- reconstruction data.

---

## Execution Invariant

Execution operationalizes architecture.

Execution does not redefine constitutional structure.
