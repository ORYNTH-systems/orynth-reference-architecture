Status:

## Purpose

Define standard operating procedures for runtime execution.

---

## Protocol Principle

Runtime operations follow governed and observable pathways.

---

## Operation Sequence


INITIALIZE

↓

LOAD CONTEXT

↓

RESOLVE AUTHORITY

↓

EXECUTE

↓

VERIFY

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Operational Requirements

Every runtime operation requires:

- valid context;
- authorized execution;
- observable state;
- verification pathway.

---

## Operational Controls

The protocol maintains:

- execution order;
- evidence collection;
- state consistency;
- recovery readiness.

---

## Operational Invariant

Every operation remains traceable from initiation to final state.
