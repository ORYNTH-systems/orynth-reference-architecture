# Continuous Validation Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase II Assurance Hardening Layer

---

## Purpose

Define ongoing validation processes for evolving architecture.

---

## Validation Principle

A mature architecture remains valid through continuous examination.

---

## Validation Cycle


CHANGE

↓

ANALYZE

↓

VALIDATE

↓

COMPARE

↓

ACCEPT OR REVISE


---

## Validation Targets

Continuous validation evaluates:

- architecture changes;
- runtime behavior;
- governance effects;
- evidence continuity.

---

## Validation Requirements

Every significant change requires:

- impact analysis;
- authority review;
- verification;
- recorded outcome.

---

## Validation Invariant

Evolution preserves identity through controlled validation.
