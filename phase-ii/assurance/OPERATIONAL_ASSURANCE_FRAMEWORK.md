# Operational Assurance Framework

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase II Assurance Hardening Layer

---

## Purpose

Define assurance controls preserving runtime integrity during continued operation.

---

## Assurance Principle

Operational systems require continuous confidence through observable evidence.

---

## Assurance Domains

### Execution Assurance

Evaluates:

- runtime behavior;
- transition validity;
- operational outcomes.

---

### Governance Assurance

Evaluates:

- authority;
- decisions;
- constraints.

---

### Evidence Assurance

Evaluates:

- completeness;
- provenance;
- traceability.

---

### Recovery Assurance

Evaluates:

- restoration capability;
- resilience;
- continuity.

---

## Assurance Cycle


OBSERVE

↓

VALIDATE

↓

ASSESS

↓

CORRECT

↓

REVERIFY


---

## Assurance Outputs

Produces:

- assurance records;
- identified risks;
- validation results;
- improvement pathways.

---

## Assurance Invariant

Operational confidence is maintained through continuous verification.
