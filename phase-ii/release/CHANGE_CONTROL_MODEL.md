# Change Control Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase II Assurance Hardening Layer

---

## Purpose

Define controlled modification pathways for evolving architecture.

---

## Change Principle

Every change must preserve traceability and governance.

---

## Change Flow


CHANGE REQUEST

↓

IMPACT REVIEW

↓

AUTHORITY APPROVAL

↓

IMPLEMENTATION

↓

VALIDATION

↓

RECORD


---

## Change Categories

### Structural Change

Modifies architecture.

### Operational Change

Modifies runtime behavior.

### Corrective Change

Resolves identified issues.

---

## Change Failure Conditions

Change control fails when:

- modifications are undocumented;
- authority is absent;
- impact is unknown.

---

## Change Invariant

Controlled change enables evolution without loss of identity.
