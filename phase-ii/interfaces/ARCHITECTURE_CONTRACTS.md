# Architecture Contracts

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase II Architecture Integration Layer

---

## Purpose

Define contractual requirements governing relationships between integrated architecture components.

---

## Contract Principle

Architecture contracts establish predictable relationships between components while preserving constitutional constraints.

---

## Contract Components

Every architecture contract contains:

### Identity Contract

Defines:

- component identity;
- ownership;
- purpose.

---

### Interaction Contract

Defines:

- permitted communication;
- dependency relationships;
- expected behavior.

---

### Constraint Contract

Defines:

- limitations;
- prohibited actions;
- authority boundaries.

---

### Verification Contract

Defines:

- evidence requirements;
- validation process;
- reconstruction criteria.

---

## Contract Lifecycle


PROPOSED

↓

REVIEWED

↓

ACCEPTED

↓

ACTIVE

↓

VERIFIED

↓

RETIRED


---

## Contract Failure Conditions

A contract fails when:

- terms are ambiguous;
- authority is undefined;
- dependencies are hidden;
- verification is impossible.

---

## Contract Invariant

A contract preserves architectural trust through explicit relationships.
