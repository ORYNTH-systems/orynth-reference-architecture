# Maturity Metrics

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase II Operationalization Layer

---

## Purpose

Define measurements for evaluating architectural maturity.

---

## Metrics Categories

### Execution Metrics

Measure:

- completion;
- latency;
- transitions.

---

### Integrity Metrics

Measure:

- evidence completeness;
- reconstruction success.

---

### Governance Metrics

Measure:

- authority resolution;
- constraint outcomes.

---

## Maturity Principle

Progress requires measurable improvement.

---

## Metric Invariant

Maturity is demonstrated through observable system behavior.
