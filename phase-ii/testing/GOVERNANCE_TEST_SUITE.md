# Governance Test Suite

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase II Operationalization Layer

---

## Purpose

Validate authority, decisions, and constraint enforcement.

---

## Governance Testing Principle

Governance pathways must produce deterministic and reviewable outcomes.

---

## Test Areas

### Authority Tests

Validate:

- authority resolution;
- scope;
- expiration.

---

### Decision Tests

Validate:

- decision pathway;
- execution eligibility;
- records.

---

### Constraint Tests

Validate:

- enforcement;
- blocking;
- escalation.

---

## Governance Invariant

Every governed action must have an explainable decision pathway.
