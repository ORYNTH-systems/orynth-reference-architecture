# Evidence Pipeline

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase II Architecture Integration Layer

---

## Purpose

Define the evidence lifecycle supporting integrated architectural operations.

---

## Evidence Principle

Every architectural transition must produce a traceable evidence pathway.

---

## Evidence Flow


INPUT

↓

CONTEXT RECORD

↓

AUTHORITY RECORD

↓

EXECUTION RECORD

↓

STATE RECORD

↓

VERIFICATION RECORD

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Evidence Components

### Context Evidence

Records:

- initial conditions;
- operating environment;
- applicable constraints.

---

### Authority Evidence

Records:

- governing source;
- decision authority;
- permission state.

---

### Execution Evidence

Records:

- actions performed;
- sequence;
- transitions.

---

### Verification Evidence

Records:

- validation results;
- checks performed;
- final assessment.

---

### Reconstruction Evidence

Records:

- historical state;
- transition pathway;
- reproducibility conditions.

---

## Evidence Requirements

Evidence must be:

- attributable;
- ordered;
- preserved;
- independently reviewable.

---

## Evidence Failure Conditions

Evidence integrity fails when:

- source is unknown;
- sequence is incomplete;
- transitions cannot be reproduced;
- records are modified without trace.

---

## Evidence Invariant

Evidence preserves architectural memory.

Without evidence, integrated execution cannot become reconstructable.
