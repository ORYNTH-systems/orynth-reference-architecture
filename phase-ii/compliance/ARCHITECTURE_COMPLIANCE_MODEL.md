# Architecture Compliance Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase II Assurance Hardening Layer

---

## Purpose

Define compliance requirements ensuring integrated architecture remains aligned with approved structural principles.

---

## Compliance Principle

Architecture compliance preserves intended system relationships through measurable requirements.

---

## Compliance Domains

### Structural Compliance

Validates:

- component relationships;
- interface definitions;
- architectural boundaries.

---

### Integration Compliance

Validates:

- capability coordination;
- dependency management;
- contract adherence.

---

### Runtime Compliance

Validates:

- execution behavior;
- state transitions;
- operational controls.

---

## Failure Conditions

Architecture compliance fails when:

- boundaries are violated;
- contracts are ignored;
- identity is altered without governance.

---

## Compliance Invariant

Architecture evolves through controlled change while preserving governing structure.
