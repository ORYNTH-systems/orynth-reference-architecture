# Governance Compliance Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase II Assurance Hardening Layer

---

## Purpose

Define compliance requirements for authority, decisions, and governance execution.

---

## Governance Compliance Principle

Every governed operation must remain traceable to valid authority.

---

## Compliance Areas

### Authority Compliance

Validates:

- ownership;
- scope;
- permission state.

---

### Decision Compliance

Validates:

- decision pathway;
- review process;
- execution record.

---

### Constraint Compliance

Validates:

- enforcement;
- exception handling;
- escalation.

---

## Governance Evaluation


REQUEST

↓

AUTHORITY REVIEW

↓

DECISION REVIEW

↓

CONSTRAINT REVIEW

↓

COMPLIANCE RESULT


---

## Governance Failure Conditions

Failure occurs when:

- authority is ambiguous;
- decisions cannot be reconstructed;
- constraints are bypassed.

---

## Governance Invariant

Compliance preserves authority clarity throughout execution.
