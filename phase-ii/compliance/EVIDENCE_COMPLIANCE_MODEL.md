# Evidence Compliance Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase II Assurance Hardening Layer

---

## Purpose

Define evidence requirements required for compliant architectural operation.

---

## Evidence Compliance Principle

Operational claims require preserved evidence.

---

## Evidence Requirements

Evidence must maintain:

- provenance;
- sequence;
- attribution;
- verification status.

---

COMPLETE

---

## Failure Conditions

Evidence compliance fails when:

- source is unknown;
- records are incomplete;
- reconstruction is impossible.

---

## Evidence Invariant

Evidence compliance preserves system memory and accountability.
