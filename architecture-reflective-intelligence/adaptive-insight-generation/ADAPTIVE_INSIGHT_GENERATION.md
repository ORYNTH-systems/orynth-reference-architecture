Version:

## Purpose

Define the framework used to generate adaptive architectural insights from reflection, governance learning, and validated intelligence processes.

---

## Insight Generation Principle

Adaptive insight generation transforms analyzed architecture conditions, recursive learning outcomes, and reflective observations into structured insights that support future ORYNTH evolution.

Insights emerge through validated understanding.

## Insight Domains

Includes:

- reflective observations;
- governance learning outcomes;
- architecture relationships;
- pattern recognition;
- improvement opportunities;
- predictive understanding;
- decision support;
- evolutionary guidance.
