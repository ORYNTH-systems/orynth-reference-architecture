# Reflective Intelligence Continuity Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to preserve ORYNTH reflective intelligence continuity across reflection processes, governance learning, insight generation, and future architectural evolution.

---

## Continuity Principle

Reflective intelligence continuity ensures that accumulated observations, learned relationships, generated insights, and validated improvements remain connected across changing architecture states.

Continuity preserves intelligence lineage through transformation.

---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- reflection history;
- governance learning states;
- insight lineage;
- knowledge relationships;
- intelligence validation;
- architecture identity;
- evolutionary continuity;
- future intelligence readiness.
