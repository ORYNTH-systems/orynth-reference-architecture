# Intelligence Continuity State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track reflective intelligence states, lineage relationships, transitions, and continuity conditions.

---

## State Principle

Intelligence continuity states preserve relationships between reflection events, learning processes, generated insights, validation results, and future intelligence conditions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- continuity identity;
- source intelligence state;
- reflection lineage;
- learning relationship;
- insight connection;
- validation condition;
- resulting intelligence state;
- historical preservation.
