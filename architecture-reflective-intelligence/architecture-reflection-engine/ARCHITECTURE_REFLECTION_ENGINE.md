# Architecture Reflection Engine

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to enable structured reflection across ORYNTH architecture states, governance decisions, transformations, and outcomes.

---

## Reflection Engine Principle

The Architecture Reflection Engine evaluates architecture conditions through observation, comparison, analysis, and validated learning while preserving provenance, context, and structural identity.

Reflection transforms experience into architectural understanding.

---

## Reflection Domains

Includes:

- architecture state observation;
- governance decision review;
- transition analysis;
- outcome evaluation;
- pattern recognition;
- improvement identification;
- knowledge refinement;
- continuity preservation.
