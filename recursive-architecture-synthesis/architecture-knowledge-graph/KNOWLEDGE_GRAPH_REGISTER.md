# Knowledge Graph Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track graph nodes, relationships, and architectural connections.

---

## Registry Principle

Knowledge graph records preserve node identity, relationship type, source context, and validation status.

---

## Graph Elements

Includes:

- node identity;
- relationship classification;
- dependency mapping;
- evidence source;
- evolution state.

---

## Registry Lifecycle


REGISTER NODE

↓

DEFINE RELATIONSHIP

↓

VALIDATE CONNECTION

↓

MAP DEPENDENCY

↓

UPDATE GRAPH

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A knowledge graph register maintains traceability between architectural concepts and their relationships.
