## Purpose

Define the framework used to represent relationships, dependencies, and conceptual connections across the ORYNTH architecture ecosystem.

---

## Graph Principle

An architecture knowledge graph preserves relationships between systems, components, concepts, evidence, and evolutionary states.

---

## Graph Domains

Includes:

- system relationships;
- architectural dependencies;
- conceptual mappings;
- governance relationships;
- evolution pathways.
