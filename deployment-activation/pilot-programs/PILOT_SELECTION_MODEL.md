# Pilot Selection Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define criteria for selecting appropriate pilot opportunities.

---

## Selection Criteria

### Alignment

Does the pilot support ORYNTH objectives?

---

### Feasibility

Are required resources and conditions available?

---

### Validation Value

Can the pilot produce meaningful evidence?

---

### Risk Management

Can deployment boundaries be maintained?

---

## Selection Lifecycle


IDENTIFY

↓

ASSESS

↓

APPROVE

↓

INITIATE

↓

REVIEW


---

## Principle

Pilot selection prioritizes learning value and controlled validation.
