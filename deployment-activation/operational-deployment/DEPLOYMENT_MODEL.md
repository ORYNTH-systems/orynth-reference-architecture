# Deployment Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the controlled deployment model for ORYNTH-based systems.

---

## Deployment Principle

Deployment transforms validated implementations into operational environments through governed execution pathways.

---

## Deployment Lifecycle


PREPARE

↓

CONFIGURE

↓

VALIDATE

↓

DEPLOY

↓

MONITOR

↓

REVIEW


---

## Deployment Requirements

Each deployment defines:

- target environment;
- implementation scope;
- dependencies;
- validation requirements;
- operational ownership.

---

## Deployment Boundary

Deployment activity must preserve separation between:

- foundational architecture;
- applied implementation;
- operational environment.

---

## Principle

Deployment expands operational capability while maintaining architectural integrity.
