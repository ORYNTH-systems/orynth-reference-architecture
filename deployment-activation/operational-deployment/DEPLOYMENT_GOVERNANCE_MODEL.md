# Deployment Governance Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define governance controls for ORYNTH deployments.

---

## Governance Requirements

Maintain:

- deployment authorization;
- change tracking;
- evidence records;
- rollback considerations;
- accountability.

---

## Deployment Control Flow


REQUEST

↓

REVIEW

↓

AUTHORIZE

↓

EXECUTE

↓

VERIFY

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Governance Records

Include:

- deployment identifier;
- responsible parties;
- affected systems;
- validation results;
- final outcome.

---

## Principle

Deployment decisions remain traceable throughout the operational lifecycle.
