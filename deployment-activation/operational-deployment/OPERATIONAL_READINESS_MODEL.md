# Operational Readiness Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define readiness requirements before operational deployment.

---

## Readiness Areas

### Technical Readiness

Verify:

- system functionality;
- dependencies;
- integration stability.

---

### Operational Readiness

Verify:

- procedures;
- ownership;
- support capability.

---

### Governance Readiness

Verify:

- documentation;
- validation evidence;
- decision authority.

---

## Readiness Lifecycle


ASSESS

↓

VERIFY

↓

APPROVE

↓

ACTIVATE

↓

MONITOR


---

## Principle

Operational deployment requires demonstrated readiness, not assumption.
