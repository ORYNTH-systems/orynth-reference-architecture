# Deployment History Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Preserve the historical record of ORYNTH deployment activity.

---

## Deployment Record Structure

Each deployment record includes:

- deployment identifier;
- system or architecture applied;
- deployment date;
- environment;
- responsible parties;
- validation status;
- outcome.

---

## Deployment Categories

Includes:

- pilot deployments;
- operational deployments;
- domain validation deployments;
- future expansion deployments.

---

## Principle

Deployment history preserves operational memory and architectural lineage.
