# Governance Network Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase IV External Application & Civilization Layer

---

## Purpose

Define governance relationships across institutions participating within the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Governance Principle

Complex systems require coordinated governance networks rather than isolated decision structures.

---

## Governance Network Domains

### Authority Network

Defines:

- decision ownership;
- delegation;
- responsibility.

---

### Collaboration Network

Defines:

- shared objectives;
- communication pathways;
- coordination.

---

### Assurance Network

Defines:

- verification;
- evidence exchange;
- accountability.

---

## Governance Network Flow


INSTITUTIONAL ACTOR

↓

AUTHORITY REVIEW

↓

COLLABORATION PATHWAY

↓

EXECUTION

↓

ASSURANCE RECORD


---

## Network Requirements

Governance networks require:

- clear authority boundaries;
- interoperable processes;
- traceable decisions.

---

## Governance Invariant

Distributed participation requires preserved accountability.
