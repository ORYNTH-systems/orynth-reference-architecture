# Institutional Adoption Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase IV External Application & Civilization Layer

---

## Purpose

Define pathways for institutions to adopt ORYNTH-based architectures while preserving institutional identity, governance, and operational continuity.

---

## Adoption Principle

Institutional adoption is a governed transition from external architecture evaluation to integrated operational capability.

---

## Adoption Domains

### Institutional Readiness

Evaluates:

- organizational capability;
- operational maturity;
- governance alignment.

---

### Architectural Alignment

Evaluates:

- system compatibility;
- requirement mapping;
- integration boundaries.

---

### Adoption Execution

Defines:

- implementation sequence;
- responsibility allocation;
- validation procedures.

---

## Adoption Lifecycle


ASSESSMENT

↓

ALIGNMENT

↓

IMPLEMENTATION

↓

VALIDATION

↓

INSTITUTIONAL OPERATION


---

## Adoption Requirements

Institutional adoption requires:

- defined authority;
- measurable objectives;
- evidence preservation;
- change governance.

---

## Adoption Invariant

Institutional integration expands capability without compromising constitutional continuity.
