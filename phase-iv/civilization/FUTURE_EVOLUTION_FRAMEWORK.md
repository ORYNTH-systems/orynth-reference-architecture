## Purpose

Define principles for future evolution of ORYNTH and associated civilization-scale systems.

## Evolution Principle

Future development requires controlled adaptation across technical, social, and knowledge domains.

---

## Evolution Domains

### Technological Evolution

Includes:

- emerging capabilities;
- new architectures;
- advanced systems.

---

### Social Evolution

Includes:

- organizational change;
- collaboration;
- collective adaptation.

---

### Knowledge Evolution

Includes:

- discovery;
- synthesis;
- refinement.

---

### Governance Evolution

Includes:

- new decision structures;
- accountability models;
- adaptive policies.

---

## Evolution Controls

Future changes require:

- evidence;
- impact assessment;
- governance alignment;
- continuity preservation.

---

## Evolution Invariant

Future expansion must remain connected to foundational principles while enabling transformation.
