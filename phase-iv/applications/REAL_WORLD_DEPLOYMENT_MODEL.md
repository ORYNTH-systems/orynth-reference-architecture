# Real World Deployment Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase IV External Application & Civilization Layer

---

## Purpose

Define deployment methods for applying ORYNTH systems outside the research environment.

---

## Deployment Principle

Real-world deployment requires controlled transition from validated architecture to operational environment.

---

## Deployment Domains

### Pilot Deployment

Purpose:

- evaluate implementation;
- collect evidence;
- identify constraints.

---

### Operational Deployment

Purpose:

- establish active usage;
- measure performance;
- maintain governance.

---

### Expanded Deployment

Purpose:

- scale adoption;
- coordinate systems;
- support long-term operation.

---

## Deployment Lifecycle


RESEARCH

↓

PILOT

↓

VALIDATION

↓

DEPLOYMENT

↓

EVOLUTION


---

## Deployment Assurance

Deployment requires:

- evidence collection;
- performance evaluation;
- recovery pathways;
- governance oversight.

---

## Deployment Invariant

Real-world application must remain traceable to validated architectural foundations.
