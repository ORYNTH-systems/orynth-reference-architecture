# Systemic Impact Assurance

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase IV External Application & Civilization Layer

---

## Purpose

Define safeguards for evaluating broad systemic effects resulting from ORYNTH integration.

---

## Impact Principle

Large-scale systems must consider direct, indirect, and emergent consequences.

---

## Impact Domains

### Technical Impact

Evaluates:

- system performance;
- reliability;
- interaction effects.

---

### Institutional Impact

Evaluates:

- organizational change;
- governance effects;
- operational consequences.

---

### Human Impact

Evaluates:

- agency;
- accessibility;
- societal outcomes.

---

### Ecological Impact

Evaluates:

- resource relationships;
- environmental considerations;
- long-term sustainability.

---

## Impact Assessment Flow


CHANGE

↓

IMPACT IDENTIFICATION

↓

RISK EVALUATION

↓

MITIGATION

↓

MONITORING


---

## Assurance Requirements

Impact assurance requires:

- transparency;
- accountability;
- evidence;
- continuous review.

---

## Impact Invariant

System expansion must increase capability while maintaining responsible integration.
