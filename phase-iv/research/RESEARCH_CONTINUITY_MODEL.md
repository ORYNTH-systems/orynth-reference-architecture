# Research Continuity Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase IV External Application & Civilization Layer

---

## Purpose

Define mechanisms that preserve research identity, lineage, and continuity across long-term ORYNTH development.

---

## Research Continuity Principle

Research evolution requires preservation of origin, evidence, context, and architectural relationships.

---

## Continuity Domains

### Intellectual Continuity

Maintains:

- foundational concepts;
- theoretical lineage;
- conceptual evolution.

---

### Technical Continuity

Maintains:

- implementations;
- experiments;
- validation records.

---

### Historical Continuity

Maintains:

- development sequence;
- decisions;
- architectural transitions.

---

## Research Lifecycle


DISCOVERY

↓

DOCUMENTATION

↓

VALIDATION

↓

INTEGRATION

↓

CONTINUATION


---

## Continuity Requirements

Research artifacts must preserve:

- provenance;
- version history;
- relationships;
- evidence.

---

## Research Continuity Invariant

New discoveries extend existing knowledge without severing architectural lineage.
