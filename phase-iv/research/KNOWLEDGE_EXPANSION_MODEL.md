# Knowledge Expansion Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase IV External Application & Civilization Layer

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH expands its knowledge base through research, integration, and validated discovery.

---

## Knowledge Expansion Principle

Knowledge growth requires structured accumulation rather than isolated information collection.

---

## Expansion Domains

### Internal Expansion

Includes:

- architecture refinement;
- runtime observations;
- system improvements.

---

### External Expansion

Includes:

- scientific research;
- institutional knowledge;
- domain expertise.

---

### Synthetic Expansion

Includes:

- cross-domain relationships;
- derived models;
- new frameworks.

---

## Knowledge Expansion Pipeline


SOURCE

↓

ANALYSIS

↓

RELATIONSHIP MAPPING

↓

VALIDATION

↓

INTEGRATION

↓

APPLICATION


---

## Expansion Controls

Knowledge integration requires:

- source traceability;
- evaluation;
- compatibility assessment;
- governance review.

---

## Knowledge Expansion Invariant

Expanded knowledge increases capability while maintaining coherence.
