## Purpose

Define assurance considerations for clinical environments.

---

## Assurance Principles

Clinical systems should preserve:

- safety;
- traceability;
- evidence;
- decision accountability.

---

## Assurance Domains

### Decision Assurance

Evaluates:

- reasoning transparency;
- evidence availability;
- human review pathways.

---

### Workflow Assurance

Evaluates:

- process integrity;
- operational consistency;
- failure handling.

---

### Data Assurance

Evaluates:

- provenance;
- accuracy;
- integrity.

---

## Invariant

Clinical assurance requires preserved evidence throughout system operation.
