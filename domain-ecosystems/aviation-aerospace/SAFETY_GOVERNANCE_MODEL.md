Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

## Purpose

Define governance considerations for safety-critical systems.

---

## Governance Principles

Maintain:

- accountability;
- traceability;
- review processes;
- controlled change.

---

## Governance Areas

- operational authority;
- safety review;
- system modification;
- incident analysis.

---

## Invariant

Safety-critical systems require preserved governance visibility.
