# Research Institution Pathway

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define pathways for research institution interaction.

---

## Pathway Stages

### Initial Contact

Provide:

- architecture overview;
- research materials;
- engagement purpose.

---

### Evaluation

Assess:

- relevance;
- expertise alignment;
- collaboration opportunity.

---

### Engagement

Develop:

- research discussions;
- reviews;
- joint exploration.

---

## Principle

Institutional engagement begins with examination and shared understanding.
