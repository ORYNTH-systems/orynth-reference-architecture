# Feedback Classification Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how external feedback is categorized.

---

## Classification Categories

### Informational

Provides awareness or context.

---

### Clarification

Requests additional explanation.

---

### Corrective

Identifies possible errors or inconsistencies.

---

### Improvement

Suggests enhancement opportunities.

---

### Strategic

Introduces new opportunities or directions.

---

## Classification Principle

Feedback classification determines appropriate evaluation pathways.
