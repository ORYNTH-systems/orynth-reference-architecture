# Engagement Response Tracking

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Track responses to external engagement feedback.

---

## Response Record

Includes:

- feedback source;
- response owner;
- evaluation status;
- decision;
- follow-up action.

---

## Response Lifecycle


FEEDBACK RECEIVED

↓

REVIEWED

↓

DECISION MADE

↓

ACTION TAKEN

↓

OUTCOME RECORDED


---

## Response Categories

Includes:

- accepted;
- incorporated;
- deferred;
- requires discussion;
- declined with rationale.

---

## Principle

Responses preserve trust through documented reasoning.
