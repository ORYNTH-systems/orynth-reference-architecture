# Feedback Intake Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how external feedback is collected and recorded.

---

## Feedback Sources

Includes:

- reviewers;
- researchers;
- institutions;
- collaborators;
- stakeholders.

---

## Intake Lifecycle


RECEIVE

↓

REGISTER

↓

CLASSIFY

↓

ASSESS

↓

RESPOND

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Feedback Record

Includes:

- source;
- date;
- category;
- observation;
- supporting evidence;
- requested action.

---

## Principle

Every external input should become traceable knowledge.
