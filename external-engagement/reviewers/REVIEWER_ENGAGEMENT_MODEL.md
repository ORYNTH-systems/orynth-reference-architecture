# Reviewer Engagement Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how external reviewers engage with ORYNTH architecture.

---

## Reviewer Role

Reviewers provide:

- independent examination;
- technical assessment;
- methodological feedback;
- architectural critique.

---

## Reviewer Categories

Includes:

- academic reviewers;
- technical specialists;
- domain experts;
- systems researchers.

---

## Engagement Lifecycle


IDENTIFY

↓

INVITE

↓

REVIEW

↓

RESPOND

↓

RECORD


---

## Principle

Independent review strengthens architectural credibility through examination.
