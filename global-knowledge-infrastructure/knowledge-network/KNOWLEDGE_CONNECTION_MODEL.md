# Knowledge Connection Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH establishes and maintains connections between knowledge domains.

---

## Connection Principle

Knowledge connections enable discovery by linking related concepts, evidence, and capabilities.

---

## Connection Domains

Includes:

- domain connections;
- evidence connections;
- system connections;
- contributor connections;
- historical connections.

---

## Connection Lifecycle


DISCOVER CONNECTION

↓

EVALUATE RELEVANCE

↓

ESTABLISH LINK

↓

TRACK EVOLUTION

↓

REVIEW VALUE

↓

PRESERVE CONNECTION


---

## Principle

Knowledge connections enable continuous expansion while maintaining structural awareness.
