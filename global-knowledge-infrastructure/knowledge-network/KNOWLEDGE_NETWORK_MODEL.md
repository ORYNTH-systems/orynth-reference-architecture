# Knowledge Network Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH connects knowledge assets, research outputs, systems, and relationships into an integrated knowledge network.

---

## Knowledge Network Principle

A knowledge network preserves relationships between information assets so that understanding can expand beyond isolated records.

---

## Network Domains

Includes:

- research knowledge;
- architectural knowledge;
- institutional knowledge;
- operational knowledge;
- historical knowledge.

---

## Knowledge Network Lifecycle


IDENTIFY KNOWLEDGE

↓

MAP RELATIONSHIPS

↓

CONNECT INFORMATION

↓

EVALUATE STRUCTURE

↓

ENABLE DISCOVERY

↓

PRESERVE NETWORK


---

## Principle

Knowledge networks transform distributed information into connected intelligence.
