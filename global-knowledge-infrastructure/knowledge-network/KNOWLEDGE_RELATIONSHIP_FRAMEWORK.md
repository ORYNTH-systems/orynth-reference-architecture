# Knowledge Relationship Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how relationships between knowledge assets are identified, represented, and maintained.

---

## Relationship Principle

Knowledge value increases when connections, dependencies, and contextual relationships are preserved.

---

## Relationship Domains

Includes:

- conceptual relationships;
- causal relationships;
- dependency relationships;
- historical relationships;
- operational relationships.

---

## Relationship Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ASSET

↓

MAP CONNECTION

↓

CLASSIFY RELATIONSHIP

↓

VERIFY CONTEXT

↓

MAINTAIN LINK

↓

UPDATE NETWORK


---

## Principle

Structured relationships preserve meaning across expanding knowledge systems.
