# Organizational Knowledge Archive

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH organizational knowledge assets are stored, maintained, and preserved.

---

## Archive Principle

Organizational archives preserve decisions, processes, milestones, and knowledge required for future reconstruction.

---

## Archive Domains

Includes:

- decisions;
- processes;
- milestones;
- research outputs;
- operational records.

---

## Archive Lifecycle


IDENTIFY RECORD

↓

CLASSIFY INFORMATION

↓

STORE KNOWLEDGE

↓

TRACK VERSION

↓

ENABLE REVIEW

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A structured archive ensures organizational knowledge remains accessible and reconstructable.
