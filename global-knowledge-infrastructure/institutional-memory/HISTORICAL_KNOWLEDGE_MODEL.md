## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH preserves historical context surrounding research, architecture, and organizational evolution.

---

## Historical Knowledge Principle

Historical knowledge connects current conditions with prior states, decisions, and developmental pathways.

---

## Historical Domains

Includes:

- origin history;
- architecture evolution;
- research milestones;
- governance changes;
- ecosystem development.

Historical knowledge enables informed evolution by preserving the path through which systems develop.
