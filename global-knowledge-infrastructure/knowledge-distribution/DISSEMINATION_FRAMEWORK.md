# Dissemination Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the methods through which ORYNTH research outputs are communicated and disseminated.

---

## Dissemination Principle

Effective dissemination connects knowledge creation with appropriate recipients, contexts, and applications.

---

## Dissemination Domains

Includes:

- publications;
- documentation;
- presentations;
- research communication;
- collaborative exchange.

---

## Dissemination Lifecycle


PREPARE KNOWLEDGE

↓

SELECT FORMAT

↓

COMMUNICATE OUTPUT

↓

COLLECT RESPONSE

↓

REFINE MESSAGE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A structured dissemination framework enables knowledge to move from creation into meaningful application.
