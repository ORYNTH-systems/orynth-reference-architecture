# Knowledge Distribution Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH knowledge assets are communicated, shared, and made available across research, institutional, and ecosystem environments.

---

## Distribution Principle

Knowledge distribution transforms preserved knowledge into accessible understanding while maintaining context, provenance, and integrity.

---

## Distribution Domains

Includes:

- research dissemination;
- institutional sharing;
- technical communication;
- ecosystem access;
- public knowledge availability.

---
