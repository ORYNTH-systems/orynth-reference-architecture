# Access Management Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH manages access to knowledge resources while preserving appropriate controls and context.

---

## Access Principle

Knowledge accessibility requires balancing availability, integrity, and responsible use.

---

## Access Domains

Includes:

- public access;
- research access;
- institutional access;
- restricted resources;
- archival access.

---

## Access Lifecycle


IDENTIFY RESOURCE

↓

DEFINE ACCESS LEVEL

↓

AUTHORIZE AVAILABILITY

↓

PROVIDE ACCESS

↓

MONITOR USE

↓

REVIEW CONDITIONS


---

## Principle

Controlled access enables broad knowledge availability while preserving responsible stewardship.
