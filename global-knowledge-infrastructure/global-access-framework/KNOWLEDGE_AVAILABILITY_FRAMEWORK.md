# Knowledge Availability Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH ensures knowledge resources remain discoverable, available, and maintainable.

---

## Availability Principle

Knowledge availability requires preservation, accessibility, discoverability, and continuity.

---

## Availability Domains

Includes:

- resource preservation;
- discovery mechanisms;
- access pathways;
- maintenance processes;
- continuity planning.

---

## Availability Lifecycle


CREATE RESOURCE

↓

PRESERVE INFORMATION

↓

INDEX KNOWLEDGE

↓

ENABLE ACCESS

↓

MAINTAIN AVAILABILITY

↓

REVIEW CONDITION


---

## Principle

Knowledge availability ensures valuable information remains usable across time and changing environments.
