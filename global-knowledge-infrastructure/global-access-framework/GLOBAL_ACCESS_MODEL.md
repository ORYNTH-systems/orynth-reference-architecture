# Global Access Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH knowledge resources become accessible across research, institutional, and ecosystem environments.

---

## Access Principle

Global access expands knowledge availability while preserving integrity, context, and responsible stewardship.

---

## Access Domains

Includes:

- public knowledge access;
- research collaboration access;
- institutional access;
- educational access;
- archival access.

---

## Global Access Lifecycle


IDENTIFY RESOURCE

↓

DEFINE ACCESS PATHWAY

↓

ENABLE AVAILABILITY

↓

SUPPORT DISCOVERY

↓

MEASURE UTILIZATION

↓

IMPROVE ACCESS

Accessible knowledge increases ecosystem participation while preserving knowledge integrity.
