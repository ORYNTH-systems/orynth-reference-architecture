# Open Research Connectivity Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH connects research outputs with broader research communities and knowledge ecosystems.

---

## Connectivity Principle

Research connectivity enables collaboration, exchange, and collective advancement while preserving source attribution and context.

---

## Connectivity Domains

Includes:

- researcher networks;
- institutional collaboration;
- publication pathways;
- knowledge exchange;
- discovery systems.

---

## Connectivity Lifecycle


IDENTIFY COMMUNITY

↓

SHARE KNOWLEDGE

↓

ESTABLISH CONNECTION

↓

EXCHANGE INFORMATION

↓

CREATE COLLABORATION

↓

PRESERVE RELATIONSHIP


---

## Principle

Open research connectivity transforms isolated knowledge into collaborative ecosystem capability.
