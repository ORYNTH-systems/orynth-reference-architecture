# Research Synthesis Pipeline

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the pathway through which aggregated research becomes integrated understanding.

---

## Synthesis Principle

Research synthesis transforms collected materials into coherent models, insights, and reusable knowledge.

---

## Pipeline Stages

Includes:

- collection;
- normalization;
- comparison;
- synthesis;
- validation;
- publication.

---

## Synthesis Lifecycle


COLLECT RESEARCH

↓

STRUCTURE INFORMATION

↓

COMPARE FINDINGS

↓

GENERATE SYNTHESIS

↓

VALIDATE INTERPRETATION

↓

DISTRIBUTE KNOWLEDGE


---

## Principle

A structured synthesis pipeline enables cross-domain understanding while preserving research lineage.
