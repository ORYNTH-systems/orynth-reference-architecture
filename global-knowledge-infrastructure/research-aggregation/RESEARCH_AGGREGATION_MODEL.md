# Research Aggregation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH collects, organizes, and combines research materials into structured knowledge resources.

---

## Research Aggregation Principle

Research aggregation connects distributed research outputs while preserving source identity, context, and analytical value.

---

## Aggregation Domains

Includes:

- internal research;
- external research;
- technical documentation;
- experimental findings;
- historical records.

---

## Aggregation Lifecycle


IDENTIFY SOURCES

↓

COLLECT MATERIAL

↓

CLASSIFY INFORMATION

↓

CONNECT RESEARCH

↓

EVALUATE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

PRESERVE AGGREGATION


---

## Principle

Research aggregation enables broader understanding through structured combination of knowledge sources.
