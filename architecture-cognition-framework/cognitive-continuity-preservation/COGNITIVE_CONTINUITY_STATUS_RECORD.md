# Cognitive Continuity Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for cognitive continuity preservation.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- cognitive identifier;
- originating knowledge states;
- discovery inputs;
- reasoning history;
- synthesis records;
- governance records;
- continuity status.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


COGNITIVE RECORD CREATED

↓

SOURCE STATES LINKED

↓

PROCESS HISTORY ATTACHED

↓

VALIDATION PRESERVED

↓

CONTINUITY REVIEWED

↓

STATUS MAINTAINED


---

## Continuity States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- PRESERVED;
- VERIFIED;
- INTEGRATED;
- CONTINUING;
- EVOLVING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Cognitive Continuity Status Records preserve accountability between historical cognitive states and future architectural intelligence.
