# Synthesis Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for unified architecture synthesis.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- synthesis identifier;
- contributing architecture states;
- relationship mappings;
- synthesis method;
- validation evidence;
- resulting cognitive state;
- current status.

---

## Synthesis Lifecycle


SYNTHESIS INITIATED

↓

SOURCE STATES LINKED

↓

RELATIONSHIPS ANALYZED

↓

UNIFIED STATE GENERATED

↓

VALIDATION COMPLETED

↓

SYNTHESIS PRESERVED


---

## Synthesis States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- COLLECTING;
- INTEGRATING;
- SYNTHESIZING;
- VALIDATING;
- ESTABLISHED;
- EVOLVING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Synthesis Status Records preserve accountability between individual architectural states and unified cognitive understanding.
