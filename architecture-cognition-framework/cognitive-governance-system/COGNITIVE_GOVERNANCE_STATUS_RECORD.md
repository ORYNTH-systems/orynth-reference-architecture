# Cognitive Governance Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for cognitive governance activities.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- cognitive identifier;
- source states;
- synthesis output;
- governing constraints;
- validation evidence;
- authorization status;
- final disposition.

---

## Governance Lifecycle


COGNITIVE REVIEW INITIATED

↓

INPUT STATES ANALYZED

↓

CONSTRAINTS APPLIED

↓

SYNTHESIS REVIEWED

↓

GOVERNANCE DECISION RECORDED

↓

COGNITIVE HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Governance States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- REVIEWING;
- CONSTRAINED;
- VALIDATING;
- APPROVED;
- ACTIVE;
- REVISED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Cognitive Governance Status Records preserve accountability between integrated intelligence and governed architectural action.
