# Successor Criteria

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Successor Architecture Layer

---

## Purpose

Define when a future architecture qualifies as a successor rather than an extension of Morning Star.

---

## Successor Principle

A successor architecture inherits foundational principles while introducing a new architectural identity.

---

## Extension vs Successor

### Extension

Characteristics:

- preserves Morning Star identity;
- remains compatible;
- adds capability.

---

### Successor

Characteristics:

- introduces new organizing principles;
- creates a distinct architectural identity;
- requires independent governance.

---

## Successor Evaluation

Criteria:

- architectural novelty;
- identity distinction;
- independent purpose;
- validated necessity;
- inheritance traceability.

---

## Invariant

Successor architectures inherit lineage but do not replace history.
