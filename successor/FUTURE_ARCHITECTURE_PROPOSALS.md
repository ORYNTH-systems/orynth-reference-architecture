Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

## Purpose

Define a structured process for proposing future ORYNTH architectures.

---

## Proposal Requirements

Each proposal includes:

- purpose;
- architectural identity;
- relationship to Morning Star;
- expected capabilities;
- validation approach.

## Proposal Principle

Future architectures should emerge from demonstrated need rather than uncontrolled expansion.
