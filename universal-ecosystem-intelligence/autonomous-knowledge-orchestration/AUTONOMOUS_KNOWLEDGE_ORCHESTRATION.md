# Autonomous Knowledge Orchestration

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to coordinate, organize, and evolve knowledge structures across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Knowledge Orchestration Principle

Autonomous knowledge orchestration enables information structures to be organized, related, and refined while preserving context, provenance, and architectural integrity.

---

## Knowledge Domains

Includes:

- architectural knowledge;
- operational knowledge;
- research knowledge;
- governance knowledge;
- evolutionary knowledge.

---

## Orchestration Lifecycle


IDENTIFY KNOWLEDGE SOURCES

↓

ASSESS KNOWLEDGE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ORGANIZE INFORMATION STRUCTURE

↓

INTEGRATE KNOWLEDGE CONTEXT

↓

REFINE UNDERSTANDING

↓

PRESERVE KNOWLEDGE STATE


---

## Principle

An autonomous knowledge orchestration layer enables continuous organization and refinement of ecosystem knowledge.
