# Knowledge Orchestration Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track knowledge sources, relationships, orchestration states, and integration outcomes.

---

## Registry Principle

Knowledge orchestration records preserve source identity, relationship context, processing state, and evolution history.

---

## Registry Domains

Includes:

- knowledge source;
- relationship mapping;
- integration state;
- refinement status;
- validation condition.

---

## Registry Lifecycle


REGISTER KNOWLEDGE SOURCE

↓

MAP RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ASSESS INTEGRATION STATE

↓

ORCHESTRATE CONNECTION

↓

VALIDATE RESULT

↓

UPDATE REGISTER


---

## Principle

A knowledge orchestration register maintains traceability between information sources and integrated ecosystem understanding.
