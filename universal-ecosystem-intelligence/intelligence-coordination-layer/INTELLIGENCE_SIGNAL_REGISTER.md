# Intelligence Signal Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to capture intelligence signals across ORYNTH systems.

---

## Signal Principle

Intelligence signals preserve observations, conditions, relationships, and contextual information required for ecosystem awareness.

---

## Signal Domains

Includes:

- operational signals;
- architectural signals;
- governance signals;
- knowledge signals;
- evolutionary signals.

---

## Signal Lifecycle


IDENTIFY SIGNAL

↓

CAPTURE CONTEXT

↓

CLASSIFY INFORMATION

↓

ANALYZE RELATIONSHIP

↓

APPLY INTELLIGENCE

↓

UPDATE REGISTER


---

## Principle

An intelligence signal register maintains traceability between observed conditions and coordinated ecosystem intelligence.
