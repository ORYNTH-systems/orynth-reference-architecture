# Ecosystem Partner Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the role of partners within the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Partner Roles

Includes:

- research contributors;
- implementation partners;
- validation partners;
- institutional participants;
- strategic supporters.

---

## Partner Alignment Areas

Evaluate:

- mission compatibility;
- technical relevance;
- contribution value;
- governance compatibility.

---

## Partner Engagement Model


DISCOVER

↓

EVALUATE

↓

ALIGN

↓

CONTRIBUTE

↓

REVIEW


---

## Principle

Partners participate through defined roles and preserved contribution records.
