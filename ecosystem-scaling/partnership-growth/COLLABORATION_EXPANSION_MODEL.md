# Collaboration Expansion Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how collaboration networks expand over time.

---

## Collaboration Growth Areas

Includes:

- shared research;
- technical development;
- domain exploration;
- validation activity;
- knowledge exchange.

---

## Expansion Lifecycle


CONNECT

↓

COLLABORATE

↓

CREATE VALUE

↓

DOCUMENT

↓

EXTEND


---

## Continuity Requirements

Maintain:

- collaboration history;
- contribution attribution;
- relationship records;
- outcome documentation.

---

## Principle

Collaboration expands through preserved knowledge and mutual contribution.
