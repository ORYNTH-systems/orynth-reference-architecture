# Domain Priority Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how future application domains are evaluated and prioritized.

---

## Priority Factors

### Strategic Alignment

Does the domain support ORYNTH objectives?

---

### Applicability

Can ORYNTH principles provide meaningful value?

---

### Validation Opportunity

Can outcomes be measured and reviewed?

---

### Ecosystem Contribution

Does expansion strengthen broader ecosystem capability?

---

## Priority Classification

Includes:

- high priority;
- emerging opportunity;
- exploratory;
- deferred.

---

## Principle

Domain priority is determined by alignment and value, not expansion speed.
