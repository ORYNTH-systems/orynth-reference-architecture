# Application Growth Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH applications mature across different domains.

---

## Growth Dimensions

### Capability Growth

Measures:

- increased functionality;
- improved validation;
- expanded application range.

---

### Domain Growth

Measures:

- new industries;
- new use cases;
- new operational environments.

---

### Ecosystem Growth

Measures:

- adoption;
- collaboration;
- knowledge exchange.

---

## Growth Lifecycle


DISCOVER

↓

ADAPT

↓

VALIDATE

↓

DEPLOY

↓

EXPAND


---

## Principle

Application growth must preserve system coherence while increasing practical utility.
