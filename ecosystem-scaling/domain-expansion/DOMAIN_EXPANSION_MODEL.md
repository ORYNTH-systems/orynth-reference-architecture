# Domain Expansion Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH expands into additional application domains while preserving architectural integrity.

---

## Expansion Principle

New domains must demonstrate alignment with ORYNTH principles, defined boundaries, and validation requirements.

---

## Domain Expansion Lifecycle


IDENTIFY DOMAIN

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

DEFINE APPLICATION SCOPE

↓

VALIDATE REQUIREMENTS

↓

IMPLEMENT

↓

REVIEW


---

## Expansion Criteria

Includes:

- architectural compatibility;
- domain relevance;
- measurable value;
- validation pathway;
- governance alignment.

---

## Boundary

Domain expansion extends application, not constitutional identity.

---

## Principle

Growth occurs through governed applicability rather than uncontrolled scope expansion.
