# Ecosystem Maturity Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH ecosystem maturity is measured across architecture, adoption, operations, and institutional participation.

---

## Maturity Principle

Ecosystem maturity reflects increasing capability, stability, and coordinated participation while preserving governance continuity.

---

## Maturity Dimensions

Includes:

- architectural maturity;
- operational maturity;
- institutional maturity;
- partnership maturity;
- research maturity.

---

## Maturity Lifecycle


INITIAL

↓

DEFINED

↓

VALIDATED

↓

OPERATIONAL

↓

EXPANDING

↓

MATURE ECOSYSTEM


---

## Assessment Areas

Evaluate:

- system stability;
- adoption capability;
- collaboration strength;
- knowledge preservation;
- future readiness.

---

## Principle

Maturity is measured through capability development, not size alone.
