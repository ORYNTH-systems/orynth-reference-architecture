# Maturity Progression Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define progression pathways for ORYNTH ecosystem development.

---

## Progression Stages

### Stage 1 — Foundation

Core architecture and governance established.

---

### Stage 2 — Validation

Systems reviewed, tested, and documented.

---

### Stage 3 — Application

Architectures deployed into practical environments.

---

### Stage 4 — Expansion

Domains, institutions, and partnerships grow.

---

### Stage 5 — Ecosystem

Sustained operation, evolution, and continuity achieved.

---

## Progression Principle

Each stage builds on validated capability from the previous stage.
