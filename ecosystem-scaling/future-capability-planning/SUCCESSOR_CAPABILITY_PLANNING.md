# Successor Capability Planning

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how future successor systems may emerge from ORYNTH principles.

---

## Successor Principle

Successor systems inherit validated principles while allowing appropriate architectural evolution.

---

## Continuity Requirements

Preserve:

- foundational concepts;
- governance logic;
- evidence history;
- architectural identity.

---

## Successor Lifecycle


IDENTIFY NEED

↓

DEFINE SUCCESSOR ROLE

↓

ESTABLISH INHERITANCE

↓

VALIDATE DIFFERENCES

↓

RELEASE SUCCESSOR


---

## Evolution Boundary

A successor system may evolve architecture while maintaining traceable lineage to originating principles.

---

## Principle

Future systems should extend continuity rather than replace history.
