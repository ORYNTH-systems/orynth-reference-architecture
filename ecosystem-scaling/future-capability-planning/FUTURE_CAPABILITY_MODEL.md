# Future Capability Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates and develops future capabilities while maintaining architectural continuity.

---

## Capability Principle

Future capabilities must extend validated foundations without compromising governance, identity, or system coherence.

---

## Capability Domains

Includes:

- research capability;
- technical capability;
- operational capability;
- ecosystem capability;
- adaptive capability.

---

## Capability Lifecycle


IDENTIFY POSSIBILITY

↓

INTEGRATE

↓

EVOLVE


---

## Principle

Future capability development is governed expansion, not uncontrolled growth.
