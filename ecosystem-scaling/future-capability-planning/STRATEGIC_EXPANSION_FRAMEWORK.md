# Strategic Expansion Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define strategic criteria for future ORYNTH ecosystem expansion.

---

## Expansion Considerations

Evaluate:

- architectural alignment;
- ecosystem value;
- resource requirements;
- validation opportunity;
- long-term sustainability.

---

## Strategic Expansion Process


IDENTIFY OPPORTUNITY

↓

ANALYZE IMPACT

↓

ASSESS FEASIBILITY

↓

DEFINE IMPLEMENTATION

↓

REVIEW OUTCOME


---

## Expansion Boundaries

Expansion must preserve:

- constitutional principles;
- governance structures;
- system identity;
- historical continuity.

---

## Principle

Strategic expansion increases capability while preserving the original architecture.
