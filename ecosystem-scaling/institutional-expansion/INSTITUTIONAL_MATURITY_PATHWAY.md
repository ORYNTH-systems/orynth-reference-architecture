# Institutional Maturity Pathway

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define stages of institutional maturity within the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Maturity Stages

### Awareness

Institution understands ORYNTH principles.

---

### Engagement

Institution participates in discussions or evaluation.

---

### Adoption

Institution applies ORYNTH frameworks.

---

### Integration

Institution incorporates ORYNTH into operational processes.

---

### Contribution

Institution contributes research, validation, or ecosystem growth.

---

## Principle

Maturity progression is measured through capability and alignment, not scale alone.
