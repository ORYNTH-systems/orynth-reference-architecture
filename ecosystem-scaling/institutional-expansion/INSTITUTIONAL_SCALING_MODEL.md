# Institutional Scaling Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH expands from individual research activity into broader institutional participation.

---

## Scaling Principle

Institutional growth must preserve architectural identity, governance continuity, and research integrity.

---

## Scaling Dimensions

Includes:

- organizational participation;
- institutional adoption;
- research collaboration;
- operational integration.

---

## Institutional Scaling Lifecycle


IDENTIFY OPPORTUNITY

↓

ESTABLISH RELATIONSHIP

↓

ALIGN OBJECTIVES

↓

ENABLE PARTICIPATION

↓

ASSESS MATURITY

↓

EXPAND CAPABILITY


---

## Principle

Institutional scale is achieved through structured participation, not uncontrolled expansion.
