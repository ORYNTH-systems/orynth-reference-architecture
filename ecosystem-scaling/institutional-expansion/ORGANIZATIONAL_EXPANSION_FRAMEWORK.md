# Organizational Expansion Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how organizations engage with and contribute to the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Expansion Areas

Includes:

- research participation;
- implementation capability;
- operational adoption;
- knowledge contribution.

---

## Expansion Requirements

Organizations should define:

- engagement scope;
- responsibilities;
- integration requirements;
- evaluation criteria.

---

## Expansion Lifecycle


ENGAGE

↓

ASSESS

↓

INTEGRATE

↓

CONTRIBUTE

↓

EVOLVE


---

## Principle

Organizational expansion increases ecosystem capability while preserving system boundaries.
