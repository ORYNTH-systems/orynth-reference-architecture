# Continuum Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for final architecture continuum status.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- continuum identifier;
- architecture state;
- integrated layers;
- synthesis results;
- continuity validation;
- reference status.

---

## Continuum Lifecycle


CONTINUUM INITIALIZED

↓

LAYERS INTEGRATED

↓

RELATIONSHIPS VERIFIED

↓

SYNTHESIS VALIDATED

↓

CONTINUITY CONFIRMED

↓

REFERENCE STATE ESTABLISHED


---

## Continuum States

Possible states:

- INITIALIZED;
- INTEGRATED;
- VALIDATED;
- CONTINUOUS;
- REFERENCE.

---

## Principle

Continuum Status Records preserve the final condition of the integrated architecture.
