# Architecture Continuity Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the continuity structure preserving the complete Morning Star architecture.

---

## Continuity Principle

Continuity is maintained through preserved relationships between architectural states rather than static preservation of individual configurations.

---

## Continuity Model


ORIGIN ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

FRAMEWORK EVOLUTION

↓

LAYER INTEGRATION

↓

SYNTHESIS STATE

↓

FUTURE ARCHITECTURE STATES


---

## Preservation Domains

Includes:

- structural continuity;
- semantic continuity;
- governance continuity;
- intelligence continuity;
- evolutionary continuity.

---

## Continuity Requirements

Future architecture states must preserve:

- origin traceability;
- constitutional relationships;
- evidence history;
- validated transitions;
- architectural identity.

---

## Principle

The Architecture Continuity Model ensures future evolution remains connected to the original Morning Star reference architecture.
