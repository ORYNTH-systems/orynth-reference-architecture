# Architecture Layer Relationship Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define relationships between Morning Star architectural layers.

---

## Relationship Principle

Architecture layers form a coordinated system where each layer provides capability required by higher-order operation.

---

## Layer Relationship Model


GOVERNANCE

↓

OBSERVABILITY

↓

ASSURANCE

↓

RESILIENCE

↓

ADAPTATION

↓

AUTONOMY

↓

INTELLIGENCE

↓

SYNTHESIS


---

## Relationship Types

### Dependency Relationship

One layer requires another layer's output.

### Validation Relationship

One layer verifies another layer's condition.

### Continuity Relationship

One layer preserves another layer's historical state.

### Evolution Relationship

One layer enables controlled future change.

---

## Principle

The Architecture Layer Relationship Model defines how capabilities cooperate while remaining structurally distinct.
