# Unified State Relationship Modeling

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the unified state relationship model for the ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Relationship Principle

The complete architecture exists as a network of related states rather than isolated components.

Unified State Relationship Modeling represents how states depend on, validate, preserve, and evolve with one another.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance state;
- observability state;
- assurance state;
- resilience state;
- adaptation state;
- autonomy state;
- intelligence state;
- synthesis state.

---

## Unified State Lifecycle


ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

STATE RELATIONSHIP IDENTIFICATION

↓

DEPENDENCY MAPPING

↓

TRANSITION ANALYSIS

↓

RELATIONSHIP VALIDATION

↓

UNIFIED ARCHITECTURE STATE


---

## Relationship Requirements

States must preserve:

- identity;
- dependency order;
- transition history;
- validation evidence;
- governance alignment;
- continuity.

---

## Principle

Unified State Relationship Modeling provides the structural map connecting all architectural conditions into one coherent system.
