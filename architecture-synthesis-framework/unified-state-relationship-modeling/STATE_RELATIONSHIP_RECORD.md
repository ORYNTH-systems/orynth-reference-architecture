# State Relationship Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for unified state relationships.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- relationship identifier;
- source state;
- target state;
- relationship type;
- dependency conditions;
- validation evidence;
- continuity status.

---

## Relationship Lifecycle


RELATIONSHIP IDENTIFIED

↓

RELATIONSHIP MAPPED

↓

DEPENDENCY ANALYZED

↓

VALIDATION PERFORMED

↓

RELATIONSHIP VERIFIED

↓

RECORD PRESERVED


---

## Relationship States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- MAPPED;
- ANALYZED;
- VALIDATED;
- ACTIVE;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

State Relationship Records preserve the structural memory of how architecture states interact.
