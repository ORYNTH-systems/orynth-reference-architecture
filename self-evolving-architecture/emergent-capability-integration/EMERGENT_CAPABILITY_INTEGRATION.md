# Emergent Capability Integration

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to identify, evaluate, integrate, and govern emergent capabilities within the ORYNTH architecture ecosystem.

---

## Capability Integration Principle

Emergent capability integration enables newly available functions, behaviors, knowledge structures, and architectural possibilities to become part of ORYNTH through controlled evaluation and governed incorporation.

Emergence expands capability while preserving architectural identity.

---

## Capability Domains

Includes:

- newly discovered capabilities;
- architectural extensions;
- intelligence improvements;
- operational enhancements;
- knowledge expansions;
- adaptive functions;
- ecosystem improvements;
- future capability pathways.

---

## Capability Integration Lifecycle


IDENTIFY EMERGENT CAPABILITY

↓

ANALYZE ARCHITECTURAL RELEVANCE

↓

EVALUATE COMPATIBILITY

↓

CLASSIFY INTEGRATION PATHWAY

↓

INCORPORATE GOVERNED CAPABILITY

↓

VALIDATE ARCHITECTURAL EFFECT

↓

PRESERVE CAPABILITY LINEAGE

↓

ENABLE FUTURE EXPANSION


---

## Principle

Emergent capability integration ensures ORYNTH can incorporate new capabilities while maintaining coherence, governance, traceability, and evolutionary continuity.
