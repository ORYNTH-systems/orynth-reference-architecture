# Continuum State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track continuous evolution states, architectural transitions, capability changes, and ecosystem conditions.

---

## State Principle

Continuum states preserve relationships between previous architecture states, evolutionary events, integrated capabilities, governance conditions, and future development pathways.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- continuum identity;
- architecture state;
- learning condition;
- evolution transition;
- capability integration;
- governance state;
- continuity condition;
- future readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE ECOSYSTEM CONDITION

↓

IDENTIFY EVOLUTION EVENT

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURAL TRANSITION

↓

UPDATE CONTINUUM STATE

↓

VALIDATE EVOLUTION RESULT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A continuum state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH evolutionary processes and their long-term architectural progression.
