# Evolution State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track evolutionary states, transition conditions, governance decisions, and resulting architecture states.

---

## State Principle

Evolution states preserve relationships between prior architecture conditions, proposed changes, governance evaluations, transition outcomes, and future evolution pathways.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- evolution identity;
- originating architecture state;
- transition condition;
- governance classification;
- approval status;
- resulting architecture state;
- continuity status;
- future readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE EVOLUTION EVENT

↓

CLASSIFY TRANSITION TYPE

↓

EVALUATE GOVERNANCE CONDITION

↓

RECORD EVOLUTION DECISION

↓

VALIDATE NEW ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An evolution state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH architectural transitions and their governed evolutionary progression.
