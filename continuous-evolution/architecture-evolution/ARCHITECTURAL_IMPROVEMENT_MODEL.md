# Architectural Improvement Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define improvement pathways for existing ORYNTH systems.

---

## Improvement Domains

Includes:

- clarity;
- efficiency;
- validation;
- maintainability;
- interoperability.

---

## Improvement Requirements

Each improvement should define:

- motivation;
- affected components;
- expected benefit;
- validation method;
- compatibility impact.

---

## Improvement Process


IDENTIFY GAP

↓

PROPOSE CHANGE

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

IMPLEMENT

↓

VERIFY RESULT


---

## Principle

Improvement strengthens architecture without weakening its foundations.
