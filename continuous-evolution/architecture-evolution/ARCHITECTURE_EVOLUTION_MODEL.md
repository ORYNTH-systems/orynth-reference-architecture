# Architecture Evolution Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH architecture evolves while preserving system identity.

---

## Evolution Principle

Architecture evolves through governed extension, not uncontrolled replacement.

---

## Evolution Categories

### Incremental Evolution

Changes:

- improve capability;
- refine components;
- preserve existing relationships.

---

### Structural Evolution

Changes:

- modify architecture relationships;
- introduce new layers;
- revise integration patterns.

---

### Successor Evolution

Changes:

- create independent architectures;
- inherit principles;
- establish new identity.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle
PROPOSE

↓

EVALUATE

↓

REVIEW

↓

INTEGRATE

↓

VERSION

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Invariant

Evolution must preserve the continuity required to identify ORYNTH across versions.
