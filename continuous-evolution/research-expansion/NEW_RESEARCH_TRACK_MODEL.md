# New Research Track Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how new research directions are evaluated and incorporated.

---

## Research Track Requirements

Each research track requires:

- defined purpose;
- relationship to existing architecture;
- validation pathway;
- documentation record.

---

## Research Categories

### Foundational Research

Expands principles and theories.

---

### Applied Research

Explores practical implementations.

---

### Exploratory Research

Investigates future possibilities.

---

## Principle

New research tracks inherit governance requirements.
