# Successor Architecture Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how future ORYNTH architectures emerge from the completed foundation.

---

## Successor Principle

Future architectures inherit principles without requiring expansion of the original constitutional layer.

---

## Successor Criteria

A successor architecture requires:

- distinct purpose;
- defined scope;
- independent identity;
- documented relationship to ORYNTH.

---

## Relationship Model
Morning Star

↓

Constitutional Inheritance

↓

Successor Architecture

↓

Independent Evolution


---

## Boundary

Successor architectures extend ORYNTH capability while preserving Morning Star as the governing foundation.

---

## Invariant

Inheritance does not require replacement.
