# Experimental Architecture Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how experimental architectures are explored without affecting stable systems.

---

## Experimental Principle

Innovation requires protected exploration environments.

---

## Experimental Stages

### Exploration

Investigate concepts and possibilities.

---

### Prototype

Develop limited experimental structures.

---

### Evaluation

Assess alignment, usefulness, and risks.

---

### Transition

Determine whether advancement is appropriate.

---

## Isolation Principle

Experimental systems remain separate until validated.

---

## Invariant

Experimentation enables evolution without compromising architectural stability.
