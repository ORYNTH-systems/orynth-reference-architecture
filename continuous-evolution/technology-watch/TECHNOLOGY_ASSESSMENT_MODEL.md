# Technology Assessment Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how emerging technologies are evaluated for potential relevance to ORYNTH.

---

## Assessment Principle

New technologies are evaluated through alignment, impact, and compatibility rather than adoption pressure.

---

## Assessment Domains

### Capability Assessment

Evaluates:

- technical capability;
- maturity;
- potential application.

---

### Architectural Assessment

Evaluates:

- compatibility;
- dependency impact;
- governance implications.

---

### Strategic Assessment

Evaluates:

- relevance;
- opportunity;
- long-term value.

---

## Assessment Lifecycle


IDENTIFY TECHNOLOGY

↓

ANALYZE CAPABILITY

↓

EVALUATE ALIGNMENT

↓

DOCUMENT FINDINGS

↓

DECIDE ACTION


---

## Invariant

Technology adoption must preserve architectural integrity.
