# Technology Alignment Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how external technologies are evaluated against ORYNTH principles.

---

## Alignment Criteria

### Architectural Alignment

Evaluates:

- compatibility with existing structures;
- preservation of system identity.

---

### Governance Alignment

Evaluates:

- accountability;
- transparency;
- controlled change.

---

### Operational Alignment

Evaluates:

- maintainability;
- reliability;
- integration requirements.

---

## Alignment Decision

Possible outcomes:

- aligned;
- requires adaptation;
- requires further research;
- not suitable.

---

## Invariant

Technology alignment protects ORYNTH from uncontrolled architectural drift.
