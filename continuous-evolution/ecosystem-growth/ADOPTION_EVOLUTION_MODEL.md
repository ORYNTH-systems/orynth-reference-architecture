# Adoption Evolution Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH adoption develops over time.

---

## Adoption Principle

Adoption progresses through understanding, evaluation, validation, and responsible implementation.

---

## Adoption Stages

### Awareness

Stakeholders discover ORYNTH architecture.

---

### Evaluation

Stakeholders assess relevance and applicability.

---

### Validation

Stakeholders examine evidence and requirements.

---

### Implementation

Systems are adapted through governed pathways.

---

### Evolution

Adoption feedback contributes to future improvement.

---

## Adoption Lifecycle


AWARENESS

↓

EVALUATION

↓

VALIDATION

↓

IMPLEMENTATION

↓

CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT


---

## Invariant

Adoption expands through demonstrated value and preserved governance.
