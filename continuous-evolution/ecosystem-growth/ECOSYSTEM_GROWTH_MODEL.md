# Ecosystem Growth Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how the ORYNTH ecosystem expands while preserving architectural continuity.

---

## Growth Principle

Ecosystem expansion occurs through aligned participation, validated relationships, and governed adoption.

---

## Growth Domains

Includes:

- research participation;
- institutional relationships;
- implementation pathways;
- community development;
- domain expansion.

---

## Growth Lifecycle


IDENTIFY OPPORTUNITY

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

ESTABLISH RELATIONSHIP

↓

ENABLE PARTICIPATION

↓

MEASURE OUTCOME

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Growth Boundary

Expansion must increase capability without weakening architectural governance.

---

## Invariant

ORYNTH growth preserves identity while enabling broader participation.
