# Partnership Expansion Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH develops and maintains external partnerships.

---

## Partnership Principle

Partnerships are formed through alignment of purpose, capability, and governance expectations.

---

## Partnership Categories

### Research Partnerships

Focus:

- discovery;
- validation;
- knowledge exchange.

---

### Institutional Partnerships

Focus:

- evaluation;
- adoption pathways;
- organizational alignment.

---

### Implementation Partnerships

Focus:

- deployment;
- integration;
- operational support.

---

## Partnership Lifecycle


IDENTIFY

↓

DISCUSS

↓

EVALUATE

↓

COLLABORATE

↓

REVIEW

↓

CONTINUE


---

## Invariant

Partnership expansion must preserve mutual accountability and architectural integrity.
