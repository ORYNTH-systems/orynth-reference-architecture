## Purpose

Define the framework used to govern integrated intelligence architectures through recursive evaluation, coordination, and continuity preservation.

## Governance Principle

Recursive Integrated Intelligence Governance enables unified intelligence architectures to observe operational conditions, evaluate relationships, and refine coordinated behavior while preserving constitutional boundaries and component identity.

Integration requires continuous governance.
