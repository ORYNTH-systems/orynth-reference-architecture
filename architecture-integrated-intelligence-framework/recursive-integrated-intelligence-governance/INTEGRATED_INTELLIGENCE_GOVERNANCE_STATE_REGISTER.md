# Integrated Intelligence Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track recursive integrated intelligence governance states, decisions, transitions, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Integrated governance states preserve relationships between intelligence components, operational architecture, governance decisions, and resulting system configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- integrated intelligence state;
- architecture state;
- coordination condition;
- constitutional condition;
- transition eligibility;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE GOVERNANCE RECORD

↓

CAPTURE INTEGRATED CONDITIONS

↓

ANALYZE SYSTEM RELATIONSHIPS

↓

EVALUATE GOVERNANCE PATH

↓

VALIDATE DECISION

↓

REGISTER GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY

An integrated intelligence governance state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH unified intelligence operation and recursive governance processes.
