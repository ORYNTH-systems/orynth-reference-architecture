# Integrated Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track integrated intelligence states, relationships, governance conditions, and architectural lineage.

---

## State Principle

Integrated intelligence states preserve relationships between intelligence layers, architecture structures, governance mechanisms, and resulting unified operational configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- integration identity;
- intelligence component states;
- architectural state;
- coordination condition;
- governance state;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.

---

↓

EVALUATE INTEGRATION CONDITIONS

↓

VALIDATE UNIFIED STATE

↓

REGISTER STATE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY

## Principle

An integrated intelligence state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence architectures and their unified operational state.
