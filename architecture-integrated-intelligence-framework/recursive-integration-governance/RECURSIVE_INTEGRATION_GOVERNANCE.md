# Recursive Integration Governance

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to govern integrated intelligence and architecture states across ORYNTH systems, relationships, and continuity models.

---

## Governance Principle

Recursive Integration Governance enables integrated states to continuously evaluate, refine, and preserve themselves through recursive governance cycles.

Governance maintains integration through structured observation, evaluation, validation, and controlled transformation.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- intelligence integration governance;
- architecture integration governance;
- cross-domain relationship governance;
- recursive evaluation;
- transition management;
- continuity preservation;
- validation governance;
- evolutionary governance.
