# Adaptive Knowledge Evolution

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to enable continuous evolution of ORYNTH knowledge structures through governed adaptation, refinement, and synthesis.

---

## Knowledge Evolution Principle

Adaptive knowledge evolution enables architecture knowledge to transform as new relationships, validated discoveries, and synthesized structures emerge while preserving origin, context, and integrity.

Knowledge evolves through controlled transformation.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- knowledge refinement;
- semantic expansion;
- relationship discovery;
- architecture integration;
- validation feedback;
- historical preservation;
- adaptive learning;
- future knowledge readiness.
