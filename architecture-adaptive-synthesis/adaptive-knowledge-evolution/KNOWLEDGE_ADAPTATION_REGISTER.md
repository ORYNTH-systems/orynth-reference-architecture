# Knowledge Adaptation Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track knowledge adaptation states, transformation events, validation conditions, and resulting knowledge structures.

---

## State Principle

Knowledge adaptation states preserve relationships between original knowledge conditions, transformation processes, validation outcomes, and evolved knowledge states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- adaptation identity;
- source knowledge state;
- transformation event;
- relationship changes;
- validation condition;
- resulting knowledge state;
- reference alignment;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE ADAPTATION RECORD

↓

CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE CONDITION

↓

IDENTIFY EVOLUTION REQUIREMENT

↓

APPLY KNOWLEDGE TRANSFORMATION

↓

VALIDATE UPDATED STATE

↓

REGISTER ADAPTATION

↓

PRESERVE EVOLUTION HISTORY


---

## Principle

A knowledge adaptation register maintains traceability between ORYNTH knowledge states and their controlled evolutionary progression.
