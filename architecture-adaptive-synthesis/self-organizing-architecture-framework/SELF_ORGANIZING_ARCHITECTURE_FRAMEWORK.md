# Self-Organizing Architecture Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to enable adaptive organization, coordination, and structural evolution across the ORYNTH architecture ecosystem.

---

## Self-Organization Principle

Self-organizing architecture enables system components, knowledge structures, and governance relationships to adjust through validated interactions while preserving architectural identity and reference integrity.

Organization emerges through governed relationships.

---

## Organization Domains

Includes:

- architecture coordination;
- component relationships;
- adaptive configuration;
- governance alignment;
- knowledge arrangement;
- capability formation;
- structural optimization;
- continuity preservation.

---

## Organization Constraints

Self-organization preserves:

- architecture identity;
- semantic integrity;
- provenance;
- governance boundaries;
- dependency relationships;
- reference continuity.

---

## Principle

The Self-Organizing Architecture Framework enables ORYNTH to adapt its structural relationships while maintaining coherence, traceability, and controlled evolution.
