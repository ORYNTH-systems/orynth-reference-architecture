# Dynamic Architecture Synthesis

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to dynamically synthesize ORYNTH architecture structures through governed combination, transformation, and validation.

---

## Synthesis Principle

Dynamic architecture synthesis enables existing architecture components, knowledge structures, and governance models to form new configurations while preserving identity, provenance, and structural coherence.

Synthesis creates relationships without losing origin.

---

## Synthesis Domains

Includes:

- architecture composition;
- framework relationships;
- knowledge integration;
- governance alignment;
- dependency synthesis;
- capability formation;
- reference preservation;
- evolutionary transition.
