# Knowledge Transfer State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track knowledge assets, transfer states, comprehension conditions, and continuity relationships.

---

## State Principle

Knowledge transfer states preserve relationships between architectural knowledge sources, transmission methods, retained understanding, and future application states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- knowledge identity;
- source architecture;
- transfer method;
- comprehension state;
- preservation status;
- lineage reference;
- future applicability.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE ASSET

↓

CLASSIFY TRANSFER REQUIREMENT

↓

DOCUMENT CONTEXT

↓

TRANSFER ARCHITECTURAL UNDERSTANDING

↓

VALIDATE RETENTION

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A knowledge transfer state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH knowledge assets and their movement across generations.
