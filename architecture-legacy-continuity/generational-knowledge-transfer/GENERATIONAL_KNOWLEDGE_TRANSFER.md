# Generational Knowledge Transfer

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to transfer ORYNTH architectural knowledge, principles, methodologies, historical context, and operational understanding across future generations.

---

## Knowledge Transfer Principle

Generational knowledge transfer preserves the reasoning, relationships, and lessons behind architectural structures so future generations can understand, maintain, and responsibly evolve the system.

Knowledge continuity requires both preservation and comprehension.

---

## Transfer Domains

Includes:

- architectural principles;
- foundational reasoning;
- technical documentation;
- governance knowledge;
- historical context;
- implementation lessons;
- evolutionary decisions;
- stewardship practices.
