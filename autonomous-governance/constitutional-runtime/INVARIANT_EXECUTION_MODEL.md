# Invariant Execution Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH invariants are evaluated during execution and system transitions.

---

## Invariant Principle

Invariants represent conditions that must remain preserved for the system to maintain identity and coherence.

---

## Invariant Categories

Includes:

- architectural invariants;
- governance invariants;
- operational invariants;
- continuity invariants.

---

## Invariant Lifecycle


DEFINE INVARIANT

↓

EVALUATE CONDITION

↓

COMPARE SYSTEM STATE

↓

PRESERVE OR BLOCK CHANGE

↓

RECORD DETERMINATION


---

## Principle

Invariant execution prevents changes that would compromise foundational system identity.
