# Policy Validation Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH policies are evaluated for correctness, alignment, and operational applicability.

---

## Validation Principle

Policies require validation before adoption to ensure they remain consistent with constitutional requirements and system objectives.

---

## Validation Areas

Includes:

- policy intent;
- constitutional alignment;
- operational impact;
- dependency effects;
- historical consistency.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


PROPOSE POLICY

↓

REVIEW REQUIREMENTS

↓

EVALUATE ALIGNMENT

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

APPROVE OR REVISE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Policy validation prevents uncontrolled governance changes and preserves system integrity.
