# Policy Change Engine Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how policy changes are introduced, evaluated, approved, and recorded.

---

## Change Principle

Policy changes must follow controlled pathways that preserve continuity and prevent unintended governance drift.

---

## Change Categories

Includes:

- corrective changes;
- evolutionary changes;
- expansion changes;
- retirement changes.

---

## Change Lifecycle


IDENTIFY CHANGE

↓

CLASSIFY IMPACT

↓

EVALUATE COMPATIBILITY

↓

AUTHORIZE TRANSITION

↓

IMPLEMENT CHANGE

↓

ARCHIVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

Controlled policy evolution enables adaptation while preserving governance identity.
