# Policy Automation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH policy processes are structured, evaluated, and maintained through governed automation.

---

## Policy Automation Principle

Policy automation supports consistent application of governance rules while preserving review, traceability, and controlled change.

---

## Policy Automation Domains

Includes:

- policy evaluation;
- rule application;
- lifecycle management;
- change tracking;
- compliance verification.

---

## Policy Automation Lifecycle


DEFINE POLICY

↓

EVALUATE CONDITION

↓

APPLY GOVERNANCE RULE

↓

VERIFY RESULT

↓

RECORD ACTION

↓

MAINTAIN HISTORY


---

## Principle

Automated policy processes must preserve human-readable reasoning, evidence, and governance continuity.
