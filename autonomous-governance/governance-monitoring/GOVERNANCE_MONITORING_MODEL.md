## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH governance conditions are observed, evaluated, and maintained over time.

## Governance Monitoring Principle

Governance monitoring provides continuous awareness of system alignment, policy conditions, and constitutional continuity.

## Monitoring Domains

- governance state;
- policy alignment;
- constitutional integrity;
- decision continuity;
- operational accountability.
