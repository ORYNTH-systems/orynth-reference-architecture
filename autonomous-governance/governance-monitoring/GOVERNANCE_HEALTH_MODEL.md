# Governance Health Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how overall governance condition is evaluated and maintained.

---

## Governance Health Principle

Governance health represents the continuing alignment between principles, policies, operations, and system evolution.

---

## Health Indicators

Includes:

- constitutional alignment;
- policy consistency;
- accountability continuity;
- decision traceability;
- stewardship readiness.

---

## Governance Health Lifecycle


ESTABLISH BASELINE

↓

MEASURE CONDITIONS

↓

IDENTIFY VARIANCE

↓

EVALUATE IMPACT

↓

APPLY CORRECTION

↓

REASSESS HEALTH


---

## Principle

Governance health measurement preserves long-term system integrity and continuity.
