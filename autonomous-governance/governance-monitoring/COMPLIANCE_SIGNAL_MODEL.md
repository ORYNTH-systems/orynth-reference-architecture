# Compliance Signal Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how signals indicating governance alignment or deviation are identified and evaluated.

---

## Compliance Signal Principle

Compliance signals provide evidence regarding whether systems remain within defined governance requirements.

---

## Signal Categories

Includes:

- policy signals;
- constitutional signals;
- operational signals;
- integrity signals;
- transition signals.

---

## Signal Lifecycle


GENERATE SIGNAL

↓

CLASSIFY CONDITION

↓

EVALUATE RELEVANCE

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

RESPOND IF REQUIRED

↓

ARCHIVE RESULT


---

## Principle

Clear compliance signals improve governance visibility and response capability.
