# Knowledge Stewardship Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH knowledge assets are maintained, transferred, and preserved across future transitions.

---

## Knowledge Stewardship Principle

Knowledge stewardship ensures that critical information remains understandable, accessible, and connected to its origin.

---

## Stewardship Activities

Includes:

- archive maintenance;
- lineage preservation;
- documentation review;
- knowledge transfer;
- historical reconstruction.

---

## Knowledge Stewardship Lifecycle


IDENTIFY KNOWLEDGE

↓

CLASSIFY IMPORTANCE

↓

PRESERVE CONTEXT

↓

TRANSFER UNDERSTANDING

↓

MAINTAIN ACCESS

↓

REVIEW CONTINUITY


---

## Principle

Knowledge stewardship protects accumulated understanding across generations of development.
