# Continuity Automation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define automated processes that support long-term continuity of ORYNTH governance and knowledge systems.

---

## Continuity Principle

Continuity automation preserves essential structures during change, transition, and system evolution.

---

## Continuity Areas

Includes:

- governance continuity;
- knowledge continuity;
- architectural continuity;
- operational continuity;
- historical continuity.
