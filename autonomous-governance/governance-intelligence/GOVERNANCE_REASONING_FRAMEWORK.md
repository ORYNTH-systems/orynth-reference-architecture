# Governance Reasoning Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the reasoning structures used to evaluate governance questions and system evolution.

---

## Reasoning Principle

Governance reasoning connects principles, evidence, constraints, and outcomes through traceable evaluation pathways.

---

## Reasoning Components

Includes:

- governing principles;
- system state;
- evidence;
- alternatives;
- consequences;
- final determination.

---

## Reasoning Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ISSUE

↓

GATHER CONTEXT

↓

APPLY PRINCIPLES

↓

EVALUATE CONDITIONS

↓

FORM CONCLUSION

↓

RECORD REASONING


---

## Principle

Traceable governance reasoning preserves institutional memory and decision integrity.
