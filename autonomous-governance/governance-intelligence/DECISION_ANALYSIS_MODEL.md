# Decision Analysis Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how governance decisions are evaluated through structured analysis.

---

## Decision Principle

Governance decisions require alignment between objectives, constraints, evidence, and long-term consequences.

---

## Decision Factors

Includes:

- purpose;
- available evidence;
- affected systems;
- risks;
- alternatives;
- expected outcomes.

---

## Decision Lifecycle


IDENTIFY DECISION

↓

DEFINE CONTEXT

↓

ANALYZE OPTIONS

↓

EVALUATE IMPACT

↓

SELECT PATH

↓

DOCUMENT RATIONALE


---

## Principle

Structured decision analysis preserves reasoning continuity across governance processes.
