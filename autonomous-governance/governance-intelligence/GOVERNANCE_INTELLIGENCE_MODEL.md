# Governance Intelligence Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how governance information is evaluated, interpreted, and transformed into structured decisions.

---

## Governance Intelligence Principle

Governance intelligence combines evidence, principles, constraints, and context to support accountable decision-making.

---

## Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- governance analysis;
- policy interpretation;
- risk evaluation;
- decision support;
- continuity assessment.

---

## Governance Intelligence Lifecycle


COLLECT INFORMATION

↓

ANALYZE CONTEXT

↓

EVALUATE CONDITIONS

↓

FORM GOVERNANCE ASSESSMENT

↓

SUPPORT DECISION

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Governance intelligence improves decision quality while preserving transparency and accountability.
