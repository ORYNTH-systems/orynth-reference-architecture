# Agency Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structural model for architectural agency.

---

## Agency Principle

Agency emerges when a validated cognitive state possesses the capability and eligibility to produce governed interaction with an environment.

---

## Agency Model


COGNITION

CAPABILITY

AUTHORIZATION

ENVIRONMENT

GOVERNANCE

↓

AGENCY STATE

↓

EXECUTION PATHWAY


---

## Agency Categories

### Reactive Agency

Responds to environmental conditions.

### Adaptive Agency

Adjusts behavior based on changing states.

### Coordinated Agency

Manages multiple capability interactions.

### Autonomous Agency

Executes governed actions independently.

### Evolutionary Agency

Improves through accumulated experience.

---

## Agency Factors

Includes:

- capability readiness;
- action eligibility;
- environmental awareness;
- governance alignment;
- outcome traceability.

---

## Principle

The Agency Model ensures action emerges from governed conditions rather than unrestricted execution.
