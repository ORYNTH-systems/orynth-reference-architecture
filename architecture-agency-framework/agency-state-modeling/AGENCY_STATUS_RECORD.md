# Agency Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for agency states.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- agency identifier;
- originating cognitive state;
- available capabilities;
- environmental conditions;
- governing constraints;
- action pathway;
- outcome status.

---

## Agency Lifecycle


AGENCY STATE CREATED

↓

CAPABILITIES ASSESSED

↓

ELIGIBILITY VERIFIED

↓

ACTION EXECUTED

↓

OUTCOME EVALUATED

↓

AGENCY HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Agency States

Possible states:

- INITIALIZED;
- ASSESSING;
- ELIGIBLE;
- READY;
- EXECUTING;
- ADAPTING;
- COMPLETED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Agency Status Records preserve accountability between cognitive understanding and autonomous execution.
