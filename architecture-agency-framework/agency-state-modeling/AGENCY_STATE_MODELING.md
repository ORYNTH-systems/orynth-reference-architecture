# Agency State Modeling

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework for representing agency states within ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Agency Principle

Agency represents the governed capacity to transition from understanding into intentional interaction with an environment.

An agency state preserves:

- cognitive readiness;
- capability availability;
- authorization conditions;
- execution boundaries;
- environmental context.

---

## Agency Domains

Includes:

- action eligibility;
- capability activation;
- environmental interaction;
- adaptive response;
- execution coordination;
- outcome evaluation.

---

## Agency Lifecycle
COGNITIVE STATE AVAILABLE

↓

AGENCY CONDITIONS EVALUATED

↓

CAPABILITY STATE IDENTIFIED

↓

ACTION PATHWAY FORMED

↓

GOVERNED EXECUTION OCCURS

↓

OUTCOME STATE RECORDED


---

## Agency Requirements

Agency states must preserve:

- originating cognitive state;
- available capabilities;
- governing constraints;
- execution conditions;
- environmental context;
- outcome history.

---

## Principle

Agency State Modeling ensures autonomous capability remains connected to cognition, governance, and execution integrity.
