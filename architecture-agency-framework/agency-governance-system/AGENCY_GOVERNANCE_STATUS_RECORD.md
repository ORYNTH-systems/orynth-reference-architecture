# Agency Governance Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for agency governance activities.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- agency identifier;
- cognitive source state;
- capability state;
- authorization conditions;
- governance evaluation;
- execution decision;
- outcome status.

---

## Governance Lifecycle


AGENCY REVIEW INITIATED

↓

STATE CONDITIONS ANALYZED

↓

CAPABILITY REVIEWED

↓

ELIGIBILITY DETERMINED

↓

ACTION DECISION RECORDED

↓

AGENCY HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Governance States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- REVIEWING;
- EVALUATING;
- ELIGIBLE;
- AUTHORIZED;
- EXECUTING;
- COMPLETED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Agency Governance Status Records preserve accountability between autonomous capability and governed execution.
