# Agency Governance System

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the governance framework for maintaining integrity within ORYNTH / Morning Star agency operations.

---

## Agency Governance Principle

Agency must enable adaptive interaction while preserving:

- authorization boundaries;
- execution eligibility;
- constitutional constraints;
- evidence relationships;
- environmental awareness;
- outcome accountability.

Agency capability does not independently establish permission to act.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- agency eligibility;
- action authorization;
- capability constraints;
- environmental conditions;
- execution oversight;
- outcome review.

---

AGENCY STATE GENERATED

↓

↓

OUTCOME EVALUATED

## Governance Requirements

Agency governance must preserve:

- originating cognitive state;
- capability lineage;
- authorization conditions;
- execution context;
- uncertainty;
- outcome history.

---

## Principle

The Agency Governance System ensures autonomous action remains governed, traceable, and aligned with architectural purpose.
