# Agency Control Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define controls governing autonomous agency behavior.

---

## Control Principle

Agency controls maintain alignment between capability, authorization, execution, and environmental interaction.

---

## Control Model


COGNITION

AGENCY STATE

CAPABILITY CONDITIONS

GOVERNANCE RULES

ENVIRONMENTAL CONTEXT

↓

CONTROLLED ACTION STATE

↓

EXECUTION OUTCOME


---

## Control Categories

### Identity Control

Preserves agency identity and continuity.

### Authorization Control

Determines permitted action scope.

### Eligibility Control

Determines whether execution conditions are satisfied.

### Environmental Control

Evaluates external conditions.

### Outcome Control

Records and evaluates results.

---

## Control Factors

Includes:

- authority;
- capability readiness;
- dependency state;
- environmental conditions;
- execution constraints.

---

## Principle

The Agency Control Model prevents autonomous capability from exceeding governed execution boundaries.
