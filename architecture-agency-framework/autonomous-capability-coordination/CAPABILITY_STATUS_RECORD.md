# Capability Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for autonomous capability coordination.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- capability identifier;
- agency state;
- objective relationship;
- dependency requirements;
- coordination pathway;
- governance status;
- execution outcome.

---

## Capability Lifecycle


CAPABILITY IDENTIFIED

↓

CAPABILITY ASSESSED

↓

COORDINATION PLANNED

↓

EXECUTION AUTHORIZED

↓

CAPABILITY ACTIVE

↓

RESULT EVALUATED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Capability States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- AVAILABLE;
- EVALUATING;
- COORDINATING;
- AUTHORIZED;
- ACTIVE;
- COMPLETED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Capability Status Records preserve accountability between autonomous capability availability and governed agency execution.
