## Continuity Categories

### Identity Continuity

Preserves agency identity across states.

### Capability Continuity

Preserves capability development and history.

### Governance Continuity

Preserves authorization and constraint history.

### Execution Continuity

Preserves action pathways and outcomes.

### Evolution Continuity

Preserves adaptive development over time.

## Continuity Factors

Includes:

- traceability;
- accountability;
- reproducibility;
- historical preservation;
- adaptive consistency.

## Principle

The Agency Continuity Model ensures autonomous systems evolve while preserving their operational identity.
