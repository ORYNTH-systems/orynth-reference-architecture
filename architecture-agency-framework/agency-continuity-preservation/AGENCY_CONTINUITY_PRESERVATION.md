# Agency Continuity Preservation

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework for preserving agency lineage across future ORYNTH / Morning Star architectural states.

---

## Continuity Principle

An agency state remains meaningful when its relationship to cognition, capabilities, governance conditions, execution history, and environmental interactions is preserved.

Agency continuity maintains:

- agency identity;
- capability lineage;
- authorization history;
- execution records;
- environmental context;
- adaptive evolution.

---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- agency history;
- capability evolution;
- governance continuity;
- execution continuity;
- environmental relationship continuity;
- adaptive behavior continuity.

## Continuity Requirements

Agency continuity must preserve:

- originating cognitive state;
- capability relationships;
- authorization conditions;
- execution pathway;
- outcome records;
- revision history.

---

## Principle

Agency Continuity Preservation ensures autonomous capability evolves without losing identity, accountability, or connection to prior execution states.
