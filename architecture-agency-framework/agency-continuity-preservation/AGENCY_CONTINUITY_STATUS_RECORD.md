# Agency Continuity Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for agency continuity preservation.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- agency identifier;
- originating cognitive state;
- capability lineage;
- governance history;
- execution history;
- environmental interactions;
- continuity status.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


AGENCY RECORD CREATED

↓

SOURCE STATES LINKED

↓

CAPABILITY HISTORY ATTACHED

↓

EXECUTION RECORDS PRESERVED

↓

CONTINUITY REVIEWED

↓

STATUS MAINTAINED


---

## Continuity States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- PRESERVED;
- VERIFIED;
- ACTIVE;
- EVOLVING;
- CONTINUING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Agency Continuity Status Records preserve accountability between autonomous actions, historical conditions, and future agency states.
