# Knowledge Governance Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for knowledge governance activities.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- knowledge governance identifier;
- affected knowledge state;
- governance authority;
- review outcome;
- validation evidence;
- revision action;
- final status.

---

## Governance Lifecycle

KNOWLEDGE REVIEW INITIATED

↓

EVIDENCE ANALYZED

↓

DECISION RECORDED

↓

KNOWLEDGE UPDATED

↓

VALIDATION COMPLETED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED

---

## Governance States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED
- REVIEWING
- ASSESSING
- APPROVED
- UPDATED
- REJECTED
- HISTORICAL

---

## Principle

Knowledge Governance Status Records preserve accountability between knowledge evolution and architectural stewardship.
