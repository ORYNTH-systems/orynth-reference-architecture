# Knowledge Governance Alignment

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the governance framework for maintaining alignment between architectural knowledge states and ORYNTH / Morning Star governance principles.

---

## Governance Principle

Knowledge evolution requires controlled governance to ensure:

- accuracy;
- traceability;
- contextual integrity;
- validation alignment;
- preservation of architectural meaning.

Knowledge may evolve while governed relationships remain visible.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- knowledge review;
- validation authority;
- interpretation governance;
- revision management;
- reference state maintenance;
- knowledge lifecycle control.

---

## Governance Lifecycle

KNOWLEDGE CHANGE IDENTIFIED

↓

CONTEXT REVIEWED

↓

EVIDENCE ASSESSED

↓

GOVERNANCE DECISION

↓

KNOWLEDGE STATE UPDATED

↓

REFERENCE HISTORY PRESERVED

---

## Principle

Knowledge Governance Alignment ensures knowledge evolves without losing architectural continuity.
