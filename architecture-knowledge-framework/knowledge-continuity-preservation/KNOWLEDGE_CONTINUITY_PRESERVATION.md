# Knowledge Continuity Preservation

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework for preserving ORYNTH / Morning Star architectural knowledge across future states, revisions, and custodial transitions.

---

## Continuity Principle

Knowledge continuity preserves not only information, but the relationships that give information meaning.

Continuity requires preservation of:

- origin;
- context;
- evidence;
- interpretation;
- validation;
- historical evolution.

---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- knowledge lineage;
- version continuity;
- semantic preservation;
- evidence preservation;
- governance history;
- future accessibility.

---

---

## Continuity Requirements

Knowledge continuity must preserve:

- provenance;
- dependency relationships;
- revision history;
- validation records;
- architectural meaning.

---

## Principle

Knowledge Continuity Preservation ensures future architectural states remain connected to their originating knowledge foundation.
