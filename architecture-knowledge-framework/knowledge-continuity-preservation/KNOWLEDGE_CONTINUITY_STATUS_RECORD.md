# Knowledge Continuity Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for knowledge continuity preservation.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- continuity identifier;
- knowledge state;
- origin reference;
- validation history;
- revision lineage;
- preservation status;
- future review requirements.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle

CONTINUITY RECORD CREATED

↓

KNOWLEDGE STATE LINKED

↓

HISTORY VERIFIED

↓

REFERENCE PRESERVED

↓

CONTINUITY REVIEWED

↓

STATUS MAINTAINED

---

## Continuity States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- PRESERVED;
- VERIFIED;
- MAINTAINING;
- EVOLVING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Knowledge Continuity Status Records preserve accountability between past knowledge states and future architectural understanding.
