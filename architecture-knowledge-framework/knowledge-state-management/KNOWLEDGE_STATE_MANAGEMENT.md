# Knowledge State Management

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework for managing knowledge states within the ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Knowledge Principle

Knowledge is preserved through controlled transitions between:

- observed information;
- interpreted understanding;
- validated knowledge;
- governed reference knowledge.

Knowledge state must remain traceable across time.

---

## Knowledge Domains

Includes:

- architectural knowledge;
- operational knowledge;
- validation knowledge;
- historical knowledge;
- evolutionary knowledge;
- institutional knowledge.

---

## Knowledge Lifecycle


INFORMATION OBSERVED

↓

CONTEXT ESTABLISHED

↓

KNOWLEDGE FORMULATED

↓

KNOWLEDGE VALIDATED

↓

REFERENCE KNOWLEDGE ESTABLISHED

↓

KNOWLEDGE PRESERVED


---

## Knowledge Requirements

Knowledge management must preserve:

- origin;
- context;
- evidence;
- interpretation boundaries;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.

---

## Principle

The Knowledge State Management framework ensures knowledge remains accurate, traceable, and continuously connected to its originating evidence.
