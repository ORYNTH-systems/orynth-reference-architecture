# Knowledge Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for knowledge state management.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- knowledge identifier;
- source origin;
- knowledge category;
- validation state;
- relationship mapping;
- revision history;
- current status.

---

## Knowledge Lifecycle


KNOWLEDGE IDENTIFIED

↓

CONTEXT REVIEWED

↓

VALIDATION PERFORMED

↓

REFERENCE STATUS ASSIGNED

↓

KNOWLEDGE UPDATED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Knowledge States

Possible states:

- OBSERVED;
- CONTEXTUALIZED;
- ANALYZING;
- VALIDATED;
- REFERENCED;
- EVOLVING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Knowledge Status Records preserve accountability between information origin and established architectural knowledge.
