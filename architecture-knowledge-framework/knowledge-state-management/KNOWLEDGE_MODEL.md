## Purpose

Define the structural model for architectural knowledge representation.

---

## Knowledge Principle

Knowledge requires relationship between information, context, evidence, and validation.

---

---

## Knowledge Categories

### Raw Knowledge

Unprocessed observations and inputs.

### Contextual Knowledge

Information connected to a defined environment.

### Validated Knowledge

Knowledge supported through verification.

### Reference Knowledge

Knowledge accepted as a stable architectural reference.

### Historical Knowledge

Knowledge preserved through evolution.

---

## Knowledge Factors

Includes:

- provenance;
- confidence conditions;
- validation state;
- dependency relationships;
- revision history.

---

## Principle

The Knowledge Model ensures architectural understanding remains structured rather than fragmented.
