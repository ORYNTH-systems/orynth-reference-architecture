# Adoption Validation Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Morning Star Adoption & Implementation Layer

---

## Purpose

Define validation criteria for external adoption.

---

## Validation Principle

Adoption success requires evidence that implementation preserves intended properties.

---

## Validation Domains

### Structural Validation

Checks:

- architecture alignment;
- component relationships.

---

### Operational Validation

Checks:

- execution;
- reliability;
- outcomes.

---

### Governance Validation

Checks:

- accountability;
- authority;
- reviewability.

---

## Validation Cycle


IMPLEMENTATION

↓

MEASUREMENT

↓

ASSESSMENT

↓

CORRECTION

↓

ACCEPTANCE


---

## Validation Invariant

Adoption is successful when capability expands without loss of integrity.
