# Architectural Integrity Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase V Regenerative Continuity Layer

## Purpose

Define mechanisms preserving Morning Star architectural integrity through continuous evolution.

---

## Integrity Principle

A living architecture requires both adaptability and preservation of its defining relationships.

---

## Integrity Domains

### Structural Integrity

Maintains:

- architecture relationships;
- dependency chains;
- system boundaries.

---

### Semantic Integrity

Maintains:

- terminology;
- meaning;
- conceptual continuity.

---

### Operational Integrity

Maintains:

- execution validity;
- assurance;
- verification.

---

### Evolutionary Integrity

Maintains:

- controlled change;
- historical lineage;
- future compatibility.

---

## Integrity Requirements

Requires:

- evidence;
- traceability;
- governance;

## Integrity Invariant

A system remains itself through preservation of the relationships that define it.
