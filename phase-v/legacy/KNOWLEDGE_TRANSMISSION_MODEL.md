# Knowledge Transmission Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase V Regenerative Continuity Layer

---

## Purpose

Define mechanisms for transferring accumulated knowledge across generations, systems, and institutions.

---

## Transmission Principle

Knowledge persistence requires more than storage; it requires understanding, context, and reconstruction capability.

---

## Transmission Domains

### Human Transmission

Supports:

- education;
- mentorship;
- interpretation.

---

### Technical Transmission

Supports:

- documentation;
- specifications;
- reproducible systems.

---

### Institutional Transmission

Supports:

- organizational memory;
- governance continuity;
- operational learning.

---

## Knowledge Transmission Cycle


CREATION

↓

DOCUMENTATION

↓

VERIFICATION

↓

TEACHING

↓

APPLICATION

↓

REFINEMENT


---

## Transmission Requirements

Knowledge transfer requires:

- provenance;
- clarity;
- accessibility;
- validation.

---

## Transmission Invariant

Transferred knowledge must preserve meaning while allowing continued discovery.
