# Regenerative System Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase V Regenerative Continuity Layer

---

## Purpose

Define mechanisms through which Morning Star maintains, restores, and improves itself through validated evolution.

---

## Regenerative Principle

A persistent architecture must not only resist degradation; it must convert experience into improved future capability.

---

## Regeneration Domains

### Structural Regeneration

Maintains:

- architectural coherence;
- system relationships;
- constitutional identity.

---

### Operational Regeneration

Maintains:

- execution capability;
- recovery pathways;
- performance improvement.

---

### Knowledge Regeneration

Maintains:

- accumulated knowledge;
- research continuity;
- discovery integration.

---

## Regeneration Cycle


EXPERIENCE

↓

EVIDENCE

↓

ANALYSIS

↓

ADAPTATION

↓

VALIDATION

↓

INTEGRATION


---

## Regenerative Requirements

Regeneration requires:

- preserved lineage;
- measurable change;
- validation;
- reversible transitions where necessary.

---

## Regenerative Invariant

Improvement must increase capability without destroying foundational identity.
