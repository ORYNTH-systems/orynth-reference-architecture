# Self Improvement Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase V Regenerative Continuity Layer

---

## Purpose

Define controlled mechanisms for system improvement.

---

## Self Improvement Principle

Self-improvement requires governed modification rather than uncontrolled alteration.

---

## Improvement Domains

### Capability Improvement

Evaluates:

- new functions;
- expanded abilities;
- performance gains.

---

### Efficiency Improvement

Evaluates:

- resource usage;
- operational processes;
- optimization.

---

### Reliability Improvement

Evaluates:

- failure reduction;
- resilience;
- recovery.

---

## Improvement Pipeline


OBSERVE

↓

IDENTIFY LIMITATION

↓

PROPOSE CHANGE

↓

VALIDATE CHANGE

↓

IMPLEMENT

↓

MEASURE RESULT


---

## Improvement Controls

Every improvement requires:

- documented rationale;
- evidence;
- compatibility review;
- impact assessment.

---

## Self Improvement Invariant

The system may improve its capabilities while preserving its governing principles.
