# Adaptive Continuity Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase V Regenerative Continuity Layer

---

## Purpose

Define how Morning Star adapts while maintaining continuity of identity.

---

## Continuity Principle

Adaptation without continuity creates fragmentation. Continuity without adaptation creates stagnation.

---

## Continuity Domains

### Identity Continuity

Preserves:

- foundational principles;
- architectural lineage;
- system purpose.

---

### Functional Continuity

Preserves:

- operational capability;
- interfaces;
- relationships.

---

### Evolution Continuity

Preserves:

- change history;
- transition logic;
- future pathways.

---

## Adaptive Transition Model


CURRENT STATE

↓

CHANGE PRESSURE

↓

↓

NEW STABLE STATE


---

## Continuity Assurance

Adaptive changes require:

- provenance;
- validation;
- traceability;
- recovery options.

---

## Adaptive Continuity Invariant

The architecture evolves through change while remaining recognizable as the same system.
