# Autonomous Governance Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase V Regenerative Continuity Layer

---

## Purpose

Define governance mechanisms for autonomous coordination while preserving constitutional authority and system integrity.

---

## Autonomous Governance Principle

Autonomy requires structured decision capability, bounded authority, and continuous accountability.

---

## Governance Domains

### Decision Governance

Defines:

- decision pathways;
- authority boundaries;
- approval conditions.

---

### Evolution Governance

Defines:

- modification rules;
- transition approval;
- continuity preservation.

---

### Resource Governance

Defines:

- allocation;
- prioritization;
- operational balance.

---

## Autonomous Governance Cycle


OBSERVE

↓

EVALUATE

↓

DECIDE

↓

EXECUTE

↓

VERIFY

↓

ADAPT


---

## Governance Constraints

Autonomous governance requires:

- defined limits;
- evidence generation;
- review mechanisms;
- recoverability.

---

## Governance Invariant

Autonomy expands execution capability without removing accountability.
