# Self Management Framework

Status:

INITIALIZATION

## Purpose

Define internal coordination mechanisms for maintaining system operation and evolution.

---

## Self Management Principle

A persistent system requires mechanisms for maintaining stability, allocating resources, and responding to changing conditions.

---

## Management Domains

### Operational Management

Maintains:

- execution processes;
- resource utilization;
- system health.

---

### Knowledge Management

Maintains:

- information organization;
- learning pathways;
- knowledge availability.

---

### Change Management

Maintains:

- controlled transitions;
- compatibility;
- historical continuity.

## Management Requirements

Self-management requires:

- observability;
- measurable states;
- defined objectives;
- corrective pathways.
