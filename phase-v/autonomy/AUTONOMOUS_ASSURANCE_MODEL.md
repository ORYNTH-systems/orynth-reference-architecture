# Autonomous Assurance Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Phase V Regenerative Continuity Layer

---

## Purpose

Define assurance mechanisms for autonomous operations.

---

## Assurance Principle

Autonomous systems require stronger verification because decision authority is internally exercised.

---

## Assurance Domains

### Decision Assurance

Validates:

- reasoning pathways;
- decision criteria;
- authority compliance.

---

### Execution Assurance

Validates:

- actions;
- outcomes;
- state transitions.

---

### Continuity Assurance

Validates:

- identity preservation;
- historical alignment;
- evolutionary consistency.

---

## Assurance Pipeline


DECISION

↓

VALIDATION

↓

EXECUTION

↓

EVIDENCE

↓

REVIEW

↓

CONTINUATION


---

## Assurance Requirements

Autonomous operation requires:

- transparency;
- traceability;
- evidence;
- correction mechanisms.

---

## Assurance Invariant

Autonomy remains trustworthy through continuous verification.
