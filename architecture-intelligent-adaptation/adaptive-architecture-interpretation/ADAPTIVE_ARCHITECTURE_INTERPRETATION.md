# Adaptive Architecture Interpretation

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to interpret ORYNTH architecture states, relationships, and conditions through adaptive contextual intelligence.

---

## Interpretation Principle

Adaptive architecture interpretation enables ORYNTH to understand changing architecture conditions while preserving semantic integrity, structural relationships, governance constraints, and reference continuity.

Interpretation enables adaptation without identity loss.

---

## Interpretation Domains

Includes:

- architecture state understanding;
- contextual relationship analysis;
- dependency interpretation;
- environmental awareness;
- governance context;
- semantic preservation;
- adaptation readiness;
- continuity assessment.
