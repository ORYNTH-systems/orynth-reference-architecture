# Intelligence Continuity State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track intelligent architecture continuity states, adaptive transitions, and preserved lineage.

---

## State Principle

Continuity states preserve relationships between original architecture conditions, interpreted states, optimized states, evolutionary transitions, and resulting intelligent configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- continuity identity;
- source architecture state;
- adaptive interpretation state;
- optimization state;
- evolution condition;
- validation status;
- resulting architecture state;
- historical lineage.
