# Contextual Evolution Management

Version:

v1.0.0

## Purpose

Define the framework used to manage ORYNTH architecture evolution through contextual analysis, adaptive transitions, and validated evolutionary processes.

---

## Evolution Principle

Contextual evolution management enables ORYNTH to adapt architecture states according to changing conditions while preserving identity, governance constraints, semantic relationships, and historical continuity.

Evolution occurs through contextual understanding.

## Evolution Domains

- environmental context analysis;
- architecture transition management;
- adaptive state progression;
- dependency evolution;
- governance alignment;
- historical continuity;
- future capability readiness;
- evolutionary validation.
