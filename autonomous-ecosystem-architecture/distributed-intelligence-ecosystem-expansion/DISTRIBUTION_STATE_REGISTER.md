# Distribution State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track distributed intelligence states, ecosystem relationships, coordination conditions, and expansion transitions.

---

## State Principle

Distribution states preserve relationships between intelligence nodes, autonomous components, communication pathways, governance conditions, and resulting ecosystem states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- ecosystem identity;
- distributed component;
- intelligence capability;
- relationship structure;
- synchronization condition;
- governance status;
- continuity state;
- expansion readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE DISTRIBUTION STATE

↓

CLASSIFY ECOSYSTEM COMPONENT

↓

MAP DISTRIBUTED RELATIONSHIPS

↓

EVALUATE SYNCHRONIZATION

↓

UPDATE EXPANSION MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A distribution state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH distributed intelligence components and their evolving ecosystem relationships.
