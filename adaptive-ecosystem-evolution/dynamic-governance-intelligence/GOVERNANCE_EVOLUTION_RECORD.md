# Governance Evolution Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how governance intelligence capabilities evolve over time.

---

## Evolution Principle

Governance evolution records preserve structural changes, decision history, lessons learned, and future governance improvements.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- governance refinement;
- decision outcomes;
- participation changes;
- intelligence improvements;
- future governance models.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


CAPTURE GOVERNANCE EVENT

↓

DOCUMENT DECISION

↓

ASSESS EFFECT

↓

REFINE GOVERNANCE MODEL

↓

VALIDATE CHANGE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A governance evolution record ensures adaptive governance remains deliberate, traceable, and continuously improving.
