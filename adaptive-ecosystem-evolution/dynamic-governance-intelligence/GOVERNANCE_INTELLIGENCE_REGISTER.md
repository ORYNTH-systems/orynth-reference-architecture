# Governance Intelligence Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to capture governance intelligence states, signals, and decision inputs.

---

## Registry Principle

Governance intelligence records preserve context, evidence, alignment conditions, and resulting governance actions.

---

## Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- governance observations;
- decision factors;
- alignment conditions;
- policy evolution;
- response actions.

---

## Registry Lifecycle


IDENTIFY GOVERNANCE SIGNAL

↓

CAPTURE CONTEXT

↓

CLASSIFY INFORMATION

↓

EVALUATE IMPACT

↓

RECORD DECISION

↓

UPDATE REGISTER


---

## Principle

A governance intelligence register provides traceability between ecosystem conditions and governance responses.
