# Dynamic Governance Intelligence

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to support adaptive governance decisions across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Governance Intelligence Principle

Dynamic governance intelligence combines system conditions, operational evidence, and architectural knowledge to support informed governance evolution.

---

## Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- governance signals;
- decision context;
- policy alignment;
- ecosystem conditions;
- future governance requirements.

---

## Governance Intelligence Lifecycle


OBSERVE GOVERNANCE CONDITION

↓

COLLECT RELEVANT SIGNALS

↓

ANALYZE SYSTEM CONTEXT

↓

EVALUATE GOVERNANCE OPTIONS

↓

APPLY GOVERNANCE DECISION

↓

PRESERVE DECISION HISTORY


---

## Principle

Dynamic governance intelligence enables governance structures to adapt while maintaining architectural continuity.
