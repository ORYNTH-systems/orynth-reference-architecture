# Learning Signal Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to capture learning signals throughout the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Signal Principle

Learning signals preserve observations, feedback, discoveries, and contextual information required for adaptive improvement.

---

## Signal Domains

Includes:

- system observations;
- user feedback;
- operational patterns;
- research findings;
- evolutionary indicators.

---

## Signal Lifecycle


IDENTIFY LEARNING SIGNAL

↓

CAPTURE CONTEXT

↓

CLASSIFY INFORMATION

↓

ANALYZE PATTERN

↓

APPLY KNOWLEDGE

↓

UPDATE REGISTER


---

## Principle

A learning signal register provides structured awareness of information that contributes to ecosystem adaptation.
