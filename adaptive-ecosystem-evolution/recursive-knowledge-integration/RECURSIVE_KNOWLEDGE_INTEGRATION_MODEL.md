# Recursive Knowledge Integration Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to integrate, relate, and preserve knowledge structures across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Knowledge Principle

Recursive knowledge integration enables accumulated information, discoveries, and architectural relationships to continuously inform future understanding.

---

## Knowledge Domains

Includes:

- architectural knowledge;
- operational knowledge;
- research knowledge;
- governance knowledge;
- ecosystem knowledge.

---

## Knowledge Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE SOURCE

↓

IDENTIFY RELATIONSHIPS

↓

INTEGRATE INFORMATION

↓

REFINE KNOWLEDGE MODEL

↓

APPLY UNDERSTANDING

↓

PRESERVE KNOWLEDGE STATE


---

## Principle

A recursive knowledge integration model maintains continuity between accumulated knowledge and future ecosystem evolution.
