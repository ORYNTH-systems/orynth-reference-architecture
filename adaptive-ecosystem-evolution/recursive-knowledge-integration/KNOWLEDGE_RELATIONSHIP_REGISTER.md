# Knowledge Relationship Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track relationships between knowledge elements across ORYNTH systems.

---

## Relationship Principle

Knowledge relationships preserve context, dependencies, references, and connections between evolving information structures.

---

## Relationship Domains

Includes:

- knowledge source;
- relationship type;
- dependency mapping;
- contextual meaning;
- evolution state.

---

## Relationship Lifecycle


IDENTIFY KNOWLEDGE ELEMENT

↓

MAP RELATIONSHIP

↓

ASSESS CONNECTION

↓

INTEGRATE CONTEXT

↓

UPDATE KNOWLEDGE GRAPH

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A knowledge relationship register provides visibility into the interconnected structure of ecosystem knowledge.
