# Knowledge Evolution Record

Version:

v1.0.0

## Purpose

Define how integrated knowledge structures evolve over time.

---

## Evolution Principle

Knowledge evolution records preserve discoveries, refinements, relationships, and changes in ecosystem understanding.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- knowledge expansion;
- conceptual refinement;
- relationship discovery;
- research integration;
- future knowledge pathways.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE EVENT

↓

DOCUMENT DISCOVERY

↓

ANALYZE RELATIONSHIP

↓

REFINE KNOWLEDGE MODEL

↓

VALIDATE INTEGRATION

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A knowledge evolution record ensures recursive learning remains connected to traceable development and validated understanding.
