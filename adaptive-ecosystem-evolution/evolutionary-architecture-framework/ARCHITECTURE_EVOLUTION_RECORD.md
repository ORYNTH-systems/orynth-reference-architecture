# Architecture Evolution Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH architectural evolution events are documented and preserved.

---

## Evolution Principle

Architecture evolution records maintain historical continuity between foundational decisions, system changes, and future development pathways.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- architectural milestones;
- system revisions;
- governance changes;
- research integration;
- future evolution pathways.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


IDENTIFY EVOLUTION EVENT

↓

DOCUMENT CHANGE

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURAL IMPACT

↓

VERIFY CONTINUITY

↓

RECORD OUTCOME

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

An architecture evolution record provides a permanent reference for understanding how ORYNTH progresses through time.
