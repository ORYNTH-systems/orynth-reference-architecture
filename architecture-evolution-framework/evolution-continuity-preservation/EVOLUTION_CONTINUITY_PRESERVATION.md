## Purpose

Define the framework for preserving architectural evolution lineage across future ORYNTH / Morning Star states.

---

## Continuity Principle

An evolved architecture remains coherent when transformation preserves its relationship to origin, identity, governance, implementation history, and future direction.

Evolution continuity maintains:

- originating architecture;
- transformation lineage;
- state transitions;
- governance history;
- validation records;
- future development pathways.

---
