## Continuity Principle

Evolution progresses through changing states while preserving relationships between previous and future architectural forms.

## Continuity Categories

### Identity Continuity

Preserves what remains invariant through change.

### Transformation Continuity

Preserves how change occurred.

### Governance Continuity

Preserves why changes were accepted.

### Capability Continuity

Preserves developed capabilities.

### Evolutionary Continuity

Preserves future adaptation pathways.

## Continuity Factors

Includes:

- traceability;
- historical accuracy;
- reversibility;
- accountability;
- adaptability;
- future interpretation.

The Evolution Continuity Model ensures transformation remains connected to architectural origin.
