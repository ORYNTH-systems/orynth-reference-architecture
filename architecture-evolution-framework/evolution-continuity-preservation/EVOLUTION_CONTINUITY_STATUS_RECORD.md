# Evolution Continuity Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for evolution continuity preservation.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- evolution identifier;
- originating state;
- transition history;
- transformation records;
- governance decisions;
- resulting state;
- continuity status.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


EVOLUTION RECORD CREATED

↓

ORIGIN STATE LINKED

↓

TRANSFORMATION HISTORY ATTACHED

↓

STATE RELATIONSHIPS VERIFIED

↓

CONTINUITY CONFIRMED

↓

EVOLUTION RECORD PRESERVED


---

## Continuity States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- TRACKING;
- PRESERVED;
- VERIFIED;
- ACTIVE;
- EVOLVING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Evolution Continuity Status Records preserve accountability between architectural transformation and future evolutionary states.
