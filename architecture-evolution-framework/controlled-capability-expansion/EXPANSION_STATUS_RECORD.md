# Expansion Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for controlled capability expansion.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- expansion identifier;
- capability description;
- originating discovery;
- affected architecture layers;
- evaluation result;
- implementation status;
- validation evidence.

---

## Expansion Lifecycle


EXPANSION PROPOSED

↓

CAPABILITY REVIEWED

↓

GOVERNANCE APPROVED

↓

IMPLEMENTATION TRACKED

↓

VALIDATION COMPLETED

↓

CAPABILITY PRESERVED


---

## Expansion States

Possible states:

- PROPOSED;
- EVALUATING;
- APPROVED;
- IMPLEMENTING;
- VALIDATED;
- INTEGRATED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Expansion Status Records preserve the relationship between architectural growth and evolutionary continuity.
