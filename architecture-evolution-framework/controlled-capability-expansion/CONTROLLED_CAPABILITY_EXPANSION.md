# Controlled Capability Expansion

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the controlled process for expanding ORYNTH / Morning Star architectural capabilities.

---

## Expansion Principle

Capability growth must occur through governed progression from validated discovery into integrated architecture.

Expansion increases capability while preserving architectural identity.

---

## Expansion Domains

Includes:

- capability extensions;
- framework additions;
- functional improvements;
- research-derived enhancements;
- integration opportunities;
- operational improvements.

---

## Expansion Lifecycle


DISCOVERY ACCEPTED

↓

CAPABILITY DEFINED

↓

COMPATIBILITY ASSESSED

↓

EXPANSION AUTHORIZED

↓

IMPLEMENTATION PERFORMED

↓

VALIDATION COMPLETED

↓

CAPABILITY INTEGRATED


---

## Expansion Requirements

New capabilities must preserve:

- reference architecture identity;
- constitutional constraints;
- evidence lineage;
- governance relationships;
- certification continuity.

---

## Principle

Controlled Capability Expansion enables growth without architectural fragmentation.
