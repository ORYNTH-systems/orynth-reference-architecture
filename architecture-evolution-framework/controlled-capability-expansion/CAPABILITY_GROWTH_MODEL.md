# Capability Growth Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the model for evaluating and integrating new architectural capabilities.

---

## Growth Principle

Capability growth is measured not only by addition of functionality but by preservation of system coherence.

---

## Growth Model


EXISTING REFERENCE STATE

NEW CAPABILITY PROPOSAL

COMPATIBILITY ANALYSIS

GOVERNANCE REVIEW

↓

CAPABILITY GROWTH DECISION

↓

INTEGRATED ARCHITECTURAL CAPABILITY


---

## Growth Categories

### Incremental Growth

Improves existing capability.

### Extension Growth

Adds a new bounded capability.

### Transformational Growth

Creates a significant architectural evolution.

### Restricted Growth

Requires additional validation.

---

## Growth Factors

Includes:

- architectural alignment;
- dependency impact;
- evidence requirements;
- governance requirements;
- continuity impact.

---

## Principle

The Capability Growth Model ensures expansion strengthens rather than destabilizes the architecture.
