# Evolution Control Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define controls governing architectural evolution.

---

## Control Principle

Evolution controls maintain alignment between transformation capability and preservation of architectural identity.

---

## Control Model


CURRENT ARCHITECTURE

↓

---

## Control Categories

### Identity Control

Ensures transformation preserves architectural identity.

### Transition Control

Governs movement between states.

### Dependency Control

Evaluates affected relationships.

### Validation Control

Confirms resulting state integrity.

### Continuity Control

Preserves historical lineage.

---

## Control Factors

Includes:

- necessity;
- compatibility;
- impact;
- reversibility;
- validation;
- continuity.

---

## Principle

The Evolution Control Model prevents uncontrolled change from becoming architectural divergence.
