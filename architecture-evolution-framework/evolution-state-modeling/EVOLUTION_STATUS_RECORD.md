# Evolution Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for architectural evolution states.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- evolution identifier;
- originating architecture state;
- transformation objective;
- change conditions;
- governance evaluation;
- resulting state;
- continuity status.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


EVOLUTION STATE CREATED

↓

ORIGIN STATE LINKED

↓

TRANSFORMATION REVIEWED

↓

GOVERNANCE APPLIED

↓

NEW STATE FORMED

↓

EVOLUTION HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Evolution States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- ANALYZING;
- PROPOSED;
- VALIDATING;
- TRANSITIONING;
- ESTABLISHED;
- EVOLVING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Evolution Status Records preserve accountability between previous architectural states and future transformed states.
