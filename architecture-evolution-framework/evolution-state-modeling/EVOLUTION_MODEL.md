# Evolution Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structural model for architectural evolution.

---

## Evolution Principle

An architecture evolves when it adapts to new conditions while maintaining continuity with its originating principles.

---

## Evolution Model


EXISTING ARCHITECTURE

NEW CONDITIONS

EVIDENCE

TRANSFORMATION REQUIREMENTS

GOVERNANCE CONSTRAINTS

↓

EVOLUTION STATE

↓

FUTURE ARCHITECTURAL FORM


---

## Evolution Categories

### Incremental Evolution

Improves existing capabilities while preserving structure.

### Adaptive Evolution

Responds to changing environments.

### Structural Evolution

Modifies architecture organization.

### Capability Evolution

Expands functional potential.

### Generational Evolution

Creates a new architectural state derived from previous lineage.

---

## Evolution Factors

Includes:

- identity preservation;
- improvement potential;
- compatibility;
- validation;
- adaptability;
- continuity.

---

## Principle

The Evolution Model ensures transformation expands capability without abandoning architectural identity.
