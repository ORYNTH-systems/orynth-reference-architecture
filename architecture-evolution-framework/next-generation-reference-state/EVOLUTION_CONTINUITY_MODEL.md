# Evolution Continuity Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the continuity model between current and future architecture generations.

---

## Continuity Principle

Evolution maintains identity through preserved relationships rather than static preservation of identical structures.

---

## Continuity Model


CURRENT REFERENCE STATE

GOVERNED EVOLUTION

VALIDATED CHANGE

INTEGRATED CAPABILITY

↓

SUCCESSOR ARCHITECTURE STATE


---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- identity continuity;
- semantic continuity;
- governance continuity;
- evidence continuity;
- certification continuity.

---

## Continuity Conditions

Future states must maintain:

- traceable origin;
- validated transitions;
- documented changes;
- preserved principles;
- measurable evolution.

---

## Principle

The Evolution Continuity Model ensures future architecture remains connected to its certified foundation.
