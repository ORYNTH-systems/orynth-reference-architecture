# Future Architecture Discovery

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the discovery framework for identifying future architectural capabilities within the ORYNTH / Morning Star ecosystem.

---

## Discovery Principle

Future architecture begins as a possibility state.

Discovery does not automatically become implementation. Potential capabilities must be observed, evaluated, and governed before integration.

---

## Discovery Domains

Includes:

- emerging concepts;
- new framework opportunities;
- capability extensions;
- research directions;
- architectural improvements;
- external knowledge alignment.

---

## Discovery Lifecycle


POSSIBILITY IDENTIFIED

↓

DISCOVERY RECORDED

↓

CONCEPT EVALUATED

↓

ARCHITECTURAL RELEVANCE ASSESSED

↓

INTEGRATION CANDIDATE CREATED

↓

FUTURE EVOLUTION PATH DEFINED


---

## Discovery Requirements

Future concepts must preserve:

- architectural identity;
- constitutional principles;
- semantic integrity;
- evidence requirements;
- governance boundaries.

---

## Principle

Future Architecture Discovery provides a controlled space for exploration without compromising the certified reference architecture.
