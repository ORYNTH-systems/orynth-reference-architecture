# Discovery Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for future architecture discovery.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- discovery identifier;
- originating concept;
- evaluation status;
- architectural relevance;
- integration potential;
- governance assessment;
- historical record.

---

## Discovery Lifecycle


DISCOVERY CREATED

↓

CONCEPT REVIEWED

↓

RELEVANCE ASSESSED

↓

STATUS ASSIGNED

↓

FUTURE PATH DEFINED

↓

RECORD PRESERVED


---

## Discovery States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- EXPLORING;
- EVALUATED;
- CANDIDATE;
- ACCEPTED;
- ARCHIVED.

---

## Principle

Discovery Status Records preserve the history of future architectural possibilities.
