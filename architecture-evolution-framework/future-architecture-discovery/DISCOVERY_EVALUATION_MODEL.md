# Discovery Evaluation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the evaluation process for discovered architectural possibilities.

---

## Evaluation Principle

Not every discovered possibility becomes architecture. Evaluation determines relevance, compatibility, and potential contribution.

---

## Evaluation Model


DISCOVERY OBJECT

ARCHITECTURAL CONTEXT

REFERENCE STATE REQUIREMENTS

POTENTIAL VALUE

↓

EVALUATION PROCESS

↓

EVOLUTION DECISION


---

## Evaluation Categories

### Compatible

Aligns with existing architecture.

### Extension Candidate

May expand capability.

### Research Candidate

Requires further investigation.

### Incompatible

Conflicts with preserved architecture conditions.

---

## Evaluation Factors

Includes:

- identity compatibility;
- governance compatibility;
- evidence requirements;
- integration complexity;
- continuity impact.

---

## Principle

The Discovery Evaluation Model ensures future possibilities are assessed before architectural adoption.
