# Knowledge State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track knowledge states, relationships, provenance, validation conditions, and evolutionary transitions.

---

## State Principle

Knowledge states preserve relationships between original knowledge sources, derived understanding, contextual meaning, validation conditions, and future evolution pathways.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- knowledge identity;
- source provenance;
- contextual relationship;
- validation condition;
- interpretation state;
- evolutionary status;
- continuity record.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE EVENT

↓

CLASSIFY KNOWLEDGE STATE

↓

PRESERVE CONTEXT

↓

VALIDATE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

UPDATE KNOWLEDGE MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A knowledge state register maintains traceability between accumulated knowledge and future ORYNTH stewardship decisions.
