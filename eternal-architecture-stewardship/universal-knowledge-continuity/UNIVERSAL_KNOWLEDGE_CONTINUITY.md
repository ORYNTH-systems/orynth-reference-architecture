# Universal Knowledge Continuity

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to preserve, organize, and evolve ORYNTH knowledge across continuous architecture stewardship cycles.

---

## Knowledge Continuity Principle

Universal knowledge continuity maintains the identity, provenance, relationships, and evolutionary history of accumulated knowledge while enabling future interpretation, expansion, and refinement.

Knowledge is preserved as a connected evolutionary record rather than isolated information.

---

## Knowledge Domains

Includes:

- architectural knowledge;
- constitutional principles;
- research discoveries;
- operational records;
- governance knowledge;
- intelligence models;
- evolutionary history;
- future knowledge pathways.
