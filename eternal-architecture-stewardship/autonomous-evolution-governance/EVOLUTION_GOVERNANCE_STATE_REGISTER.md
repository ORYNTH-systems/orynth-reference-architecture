# Evolution Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track evolutionary governance states, transition conditions, authority relationships, and validated outcomes.

---

## State Principle

Evolution governance states preserve relationships between evolutionary conditions, governance decisions, transition pathways, and resulting architecture states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- evolutionary condition;
- authority relationship;
- transition status;
- validation condition;
- continuity state.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE EVOLUTION EVENT

↓

CLASSIFY TRANSITION CONDITION

↓

ASSESS GOVERNANCE REQUIREMENT

↓

DEFINE EVOLUTION STATE

↓

VALIDATE AUTHORIZED CHANGE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An evolution governance state register maintains traceability between autonomous evolution processes and ORYNTH stewardship authority.
