# Ecosystem Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track ecosystem intelligence states, observations, relationships, analytical conditions, and stewardship implications.

---

## State Principle

Ecosystem intelligence states preserve relationships between observed conditions, intelligence processes, contextual relationships, and resulting stewardship actions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- intelligence identity;
- ecosystem condition;
- observation source;
- relationship mapping;
- analytical state;
- stewardship relevance;
- continuity status.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE EVENT

↓

CLASSIFY ECOSYSTEM CONDITION

↓

MAP KNOWLEDGE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ANALYZE SYSTEM PATTERNS

↓

UPDATE INTELLIGENCE MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An ecosystem intelligence state register maintains traceability between recursive intelligence processes and ORYNTH stewardship decisions.
