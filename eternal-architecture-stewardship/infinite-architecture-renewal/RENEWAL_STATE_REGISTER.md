# Renewal State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track renewal states, transformation conditions, continuity relationships, and future architecture pathways.

---

## State Principle

Renewal states preserve relationships between previous architecture conditions, renewal requirements, applied transformations, validated outcomes, and future evolution states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- renewal identity;
- architecture condition;
- transformation pathway;
- continuity validation;
- renewed state;
- future readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE RENEWAL CONDITION

↓

CLASSIFY TRANSFORMATION NEED

↓

MAP RENEWAL PATHWAY

↓

VALIDATE CONTINUITY REQUIREMENTS

↓

ESTABLISH RENEWED ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A renewal state register maintains traceability across all ORYNTH architectural renewal transitions.
