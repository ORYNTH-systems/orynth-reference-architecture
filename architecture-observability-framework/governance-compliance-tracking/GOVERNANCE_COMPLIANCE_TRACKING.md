# Governance Compliance Tracking

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework for observing and tracking governance compliance within the ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Compliance Principle

Governance Compliance Tracking verifies that architecture changes, operations, and transitions remain aligned with established governance requirements.

---

## Compliance Domains

Includes:

- reference state compliance;
- change control compliance;
- revision validation compliance;
- stewardship compliance;
- documentation compliance;
- provenance compliance.

---

---

## Compliance Requirements

The architecture must maintain:

- approved transition processes;
- documented changes;
- validation evidence;
- preserved lineage;
- constitutional alignment.

---

## Principle

Governance Compliance Tracking provides continuous visibility into whether Morning Star remains within its defined governance boundaries.
