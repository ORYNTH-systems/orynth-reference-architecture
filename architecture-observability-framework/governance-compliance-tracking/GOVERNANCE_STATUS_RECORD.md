# Governance Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for ongoing governance status monitoring.

---

## Status Record Fields

Includes:

- assessment identifier;
- governance domain;
- observed condition;
- evidence reference;
- compliance state;
- corrective action;
- validation history.

---

## Status Lifecycle


ASSESSMENT CREATED

↓

GOVERNANCE STATE OBSERVED

↓

EVIDENCE REVIEWED

↓

COMPLIANCE STATUS ASSIGNED

↓

ACTIONS TRACKED

↓

STATUS PRESERVED


---

## Status Values

Possible states:

- COMPLIANT;
- MONITORED;
- UNDER REVIEW;
- CORRECTIVE ACTION;
- VERIFIED.

---

## Principle

Governance Status Records provide a continuous record of architectural governance health.
