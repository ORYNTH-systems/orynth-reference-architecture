# Compliance Validation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the validation process used to determine governance compliance.

---

## Validation Principle

Compliance is established through evidence-based verification against defined governance requirements.

---

## Validation Model


GOVERNANCE CRITERIA

↓

EVIDENCE IDENTIFICATION

↓

CURRENT STATE REVIEW

↓

COMPLIANCE ANALYSIS

↓

VALIDATION RESULT

↓

COMPLIANCE RECORD


---

## Compliance States

Possible states:

### COMPLIANT

Requirements satisfied.

### CONDITIONAL

Requirements satisfied with identified limitations.

### REVIEW REQUIRED

Additional evidence required.

### NON-COMPLIANT

Governance requirements not satisfied.

---

## Principle

Compliance validation ensures governance remains measurable, traceable, and enforceable.
