# Drift Response Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for responses to identified integrity drift.

---

## Response Principle

Every detected drift condition maintains a traceable response history.

---

## Response Record Fields

Includes:

- Drift ID;
- detection date;
- affected framework;
- drift classification;
- impact assessment;
- response action;
- validation result;
- final disposition.

---

## Response Lifecycle


DRIFT IDENTIFIED

↓

RESPONSE REGISTERED

↓

ANALYSIS COMPLETED

↓

CORRECTIVE ACTION OR ACCEPTANCE

↓

VALIDATION

↓

HISTORICAL RECORD


---

## Response States

Possible states:

- OBSERVED;
- UNDER ANALYSIS;
- ACCEPTED EVOLUTION;
- CORRECTED;
- CLOSED.

---

## Principle

Drift Response Records preserve accountability and continuity throughout architectural evolution.
