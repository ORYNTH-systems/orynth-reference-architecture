# Assurance Metrics Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define measurable assurance indicators for the Morning Star architecture.

---

## Metrics Principle

Assurance metrics provide structured observations of architecture integrity, governance alignment, and continuity.

---

## Metric Domains

Includes:

### Architecture Integrity

Measures:

- identity preservation;
- dependency consistency;
- semantic alignment.

### Governance Health

Measures:

- change compliance;
- validation completion;
- stewardship status.

### Continuity Health

Measures:

- provenance;
- historical records;
- reference state alignment.

### Operational Assurance

Measures:

- observation completeness;
- reporting consistency;
- review status.
