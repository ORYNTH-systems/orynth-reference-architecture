# Assurance History Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the historical record for continuous assurance reporting.

---

## History Principle

Every assurance assessment contributes to the long-term understanding of architecture condition and evolution.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- report identifier;
- assessment period;
- architecture version;
- observed state;
- assurance metrics;
- findings;
- actions;
- resolution status.

---

## Assurance History Lifecycle


ASSURANCE REPORT CREATED

↓

ARCHITECTURE STATE CAPTURED

↓

METRICS EVALUATED

↓

FINDINGS RECORDED

↓

ACTIONS TRACKED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Principle

Assurance History Records ensure Morning Star maintains a continuous evidence trail of architectural health and evolution.
