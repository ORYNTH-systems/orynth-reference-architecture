# Continuous Assurance Reporting

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the reporting framework for continuous assurance of the ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture.

---

## Assurance Principle

Continuous Assurance Reporting transforms architecture observations, integrity assessments, and governance evaluations into a persistent assurance record.

---

## Assurance Domains

Includes:

- architecture state;
- integrity condition;
- governance compliance;
- change activity;
- validation status;
- continuity health.

---

## Assurance Reporting Lifecycle


ARCHITECTURE OBSERVATIONS

↓

STATE ANALYSIS

↓

DRIFT ASSESSMENT

↓

GOVERNANCE REVIEW

↓

ASSURANCE EVALUATION

↓

REPORT GENERATION

↓

HISTORICAL PRESERVATION


---

## Reporting Requirements

Reports preserve:

- current architecture condition;
- comparison against baseline;
- detected changes;
- compliance status;
- validation evidence;
- recommended actions.

---

## Principle

Continuous Assurance Reporting provides ongoing visibility into the health and integrity of the Morning Star architecture.
