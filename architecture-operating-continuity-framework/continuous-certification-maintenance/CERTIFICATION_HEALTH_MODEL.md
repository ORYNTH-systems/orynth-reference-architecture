## Purpose

Define the health model used to evaluate certification continuity.

---

## Health Principle

Certification health represents the condition of evidence, verification, governance, and continuity supporting the certified architecture.

---

## Health Categories

### Healthy

Certification conditions remain satisfied.

### Review Required

Changes require evaluation.

### At Risk

Certification conditions may be impacted.

### Invalidated

Reference state no longer satisfies requirements.

---

## Health Indicators

Includes:

- evidence completeness;
- verification currency;
- governance alignment;
- change impact status;
- continuity preservation.

---

## Principle

The Certification Health Model provides continuous awareness of reference state validity.
