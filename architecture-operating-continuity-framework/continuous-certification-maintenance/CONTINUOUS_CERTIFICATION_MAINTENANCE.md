# Continuous Certification Maintenance

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the maintenance framework required to preserve the certified ORYNTH / Morning Star reference architecture state.

---

## Maintenance Principle

Certification is not a static event. A certified architecture requires continuous validation that its evidence, relationships, governance, and identity remain intact.

---

## Maintenance Domains

Includes:

- certification health monitoring;
- evidence refresh;
- verification updates;
- reference state review;
- change impact tracking;
- stewardship continuity.

---

## Maintenance Lifecycle


CERTIFIED REFERENCE STATE

↓

HEALTH MONITORING

↓

EVIDENCE REVIEW

↓

VERIFICATION UPDATE

↓

CERTIFICATION ASSESSMENT

↓

REFERENCE STATE MAINTAINED


---

## Maintenance Requirements

Certification continuity must preserve:

- architecture identity;
- validation evidence;
- governance conditions;
- revision history;
- public reference integrity;
- continuity relationships.

---

## Principle

Continuous Certification Maintenance ensures the architecture remains a valid reference state throughout its operational lifetime.
