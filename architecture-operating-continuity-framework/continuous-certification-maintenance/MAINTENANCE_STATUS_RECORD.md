# Maintenance Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for continuous certification maintenance.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- maintenance identifier;
- certified version;
- health assessment;
- evidence condition;
- verification condition;
- change review status;
- certification outcome.

---

## Maintenance Lifecycle


MAINTENANCE INITIATED

↓

CERTIFICATION REVIEWED

↓

HEALTH ASSESSED

↓

UPDATES APPLIED

↓

STATUS CONFIRMED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Maintenance States

Possible states:

- ACTIVE;
- REVIEWING;
- HEALTHY;
- UPDATE REQUIRED;
- CERTIFICATION IMPACTED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Maintenance Status Records preserve the operational history of certification continuity.
