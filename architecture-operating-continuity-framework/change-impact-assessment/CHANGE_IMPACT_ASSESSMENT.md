# Change Impact Assessment

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the impact assessment framework for evaluating proposed changes to the ORYNTH / Morning Star certified reference architecture.

---

## Change Principle

A change is not evaluated only by its immediate modification. It must be evaluated by its effect on architectural identity, relationships, governance, evidence, and continuity.

---

## Assessment Domains

Includes:

- structural impact;
- semantic impact;
- dependency impact;
- governance impact;
- evidence impact;
- certification impact.

---

## Change Assessment Lifecycle


CHANGE PROPOSED

↓

CHANGE IDENTIFIED

↓

IMPACT ANALYZED

↓

DEPENDENCIES REVIEWED

↓

GOVERNANCE EVALUATED

↓

CHANGE DECISION GENERATED

↓

RECORD PRESERVED


---

## Assessment Requirements

Every change assessment must preserve:

- original reference state;
- affected components;
- relationship impact;
- evidence requirements;
- certification consequences.

---

## Principle

Change Impact Assessment ensures evolution occurs without uncontrolled architectural drift.
