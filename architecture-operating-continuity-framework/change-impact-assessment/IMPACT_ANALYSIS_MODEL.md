# Impact Analysis Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the model used to analyze architectural change effects.

---

## Impact Principle

A modification may affect multiple architectural layers even when the change appears isolated.

---

## Impact Model


PROPOSED CHANGE

AFFECTED ARCHITECTURE STATE

DEPENDENCY RELATIONSHIPS

CERTIFICATION CONDITIONS

↓

IMPACT ANALYSIS

↓

GOVERNED CHANGE DECISION


---

## Impact Categories

### Local Impact

Change affects a bounded component.

### Relational Impact

Change affects connected architecture relationships.

### Constitutional Impact

Change affects architectural identity or governing principles.

### Certification Impact

Change affects validated reference status.

---

## Impact Outcomes

Possible outcomes:

- NO IMPACT;
- REVIEW REQUIRED;
- CONTROLLED REVISION;
- CERTIFICATION UPDATE REQUIRED.

---

## Principle

The Impact Analysis Model ensures changes are evaluated through architectural relationships rather than isolated modifications.
