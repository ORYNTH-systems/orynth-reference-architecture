# Change Impact Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for architecture change impact evaluation.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- change identifier;
- proposed modification;
- affected frameworks;
- impact category;
- assessment result;
- governance decision;
- certification consequence;
- historical record.

---

## Change Lifecycle


CHANGE SUBMITTED

↓

IMPACT RECORDED

↓

RELATIONSHIPS ANALYZED

↓

DECISION ASSIGNED

↓

CHANGE TRACKED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Change States

Possible states:

- PROPOSED;
- ANALYZING;
- REVIEWED;
- APPROVED;
- IMPLEMENTED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Change Impact Records preserve the relationship between architectural evolution and reference state continuity.
