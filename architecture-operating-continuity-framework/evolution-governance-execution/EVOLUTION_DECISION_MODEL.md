# Evolution Decision Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the decision structure for governing architectural evolution.

---

## Decision Principle

Evolution decisions require evaluation of benefit, impact, compatibility, and continuity.

---

## Decision Model


PROPOSED EVOLUTION

IMPACT ASSESSMENT

REFERENCE STATE CONDITIONS

GOVERNANCE REQUIREMENTS

↓

EVOLUTION DECISION

↓

AUTHORIZED TRANSITION


---

## Decision Categories

### Approved Evolution

Maintains identity and satisfies requirements.

### Conditional Evolution

Requires additional validation.

### Deferred Evolution

Requires future review.

### Rejected Evolution

Conflicts with preserved architecture conditions.

---

## Decision Factors

Includes:

- identity preservation;
- architectural compatibility;
- evidence requirements;
- certification impact;
- future continuity.

---

## Principle

The Evolution Decision Model ensures architectural advancement remains governed and traceable.
