# Evolution Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for governed evolution execution.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- evolution identifier;
- originating change;
- affected architecture layers;
- approval status;
- implementation state;
- validation evidence;
- reference state impact.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


EVOLUTION PROPOSED

↓

GOVERNANCE REVIEWED

↓

EXECUTION AUTHORIZED

↓

TRANSITION COMPLETED

↓

VALIDATION CONFIRMED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Evolution States

Possible states:

- PROPOSED;
- REVIEWING;
- APPROVED;
- EXECUTING;
- VALIDATED;
- INTEGRATED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Evolution Status Records preserve accountability between architectural change and certified continuity.
