# Recursive Reflective Governance

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to govern reflective intelligence states across ORYNTH architecture systems, internal evaluation processes, and continuity models.

---

## Governance Principle

Recursive Reflective Governance enables intelligence systems to observe, evaluate, and refine their own states through controlled recursive cycles while preserving architecture identity, governance constraints, and historical lineage.

Reflection remains bounded by validated continuity.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- reflective decision governance;
- internal state evaluation;
- recursive observation;
- refinement validation;
- architecture preservation;
- constraint management;
- transition control;
- continuity governance.
