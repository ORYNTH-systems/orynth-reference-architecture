# Unified Reflective Intelligence Modeling

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to model reflective intelligence capabilities within ORYNTH architecture systems, including observation, evaluation, internal representation, refinement, and continuity preservation.

---

## Reflective Intelligence Principle

Unified Reflective Intelligence Modeling represents intelligence as a system capable of examining its own states, relationships, processes, and outcomes while preserving identity, architecture alignment, governance conditions, and historical lineage.

Reflection enables awareness of state without loss of structural continuity.

---

## Reflective Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- self-observation modeling;
- internal state representation;
- reasoning evaluation;
- outcome reflection;
- architecture awareness;
- governance alignment;
- continuity maintenance;
- reflective refinement.
