# Reflective State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track reflective intelligence states, observations, evaluations, refinement decisions, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Reflective states preserve relationships between intelligence capability, internal observations, architecture context, governance requirements, and resulting refined configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- reflective identity;
- intelligence state;
- observation context;
- internal representation;
- evaluation result;
- refinement condition;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE REFLECTIVE RECORD

↓

CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

OBSERVE INTERNAL CONDITION

↓

ANALYZE REFLECTIVE DATA

↓

VALIDATE REFINEMENT

↓

REGISTER REFLECTIVE STATE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A reflective state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH reflective intelligence processes and their governed architectural relationships.
