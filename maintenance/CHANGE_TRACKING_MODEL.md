# Change Tracking Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Morning Star Stewardship Layer

---

## Purpose

Define mechanisms for recording architectural changes.

---

## Change Principle

Every modification requires traceable origin, purpose, and impact.

---

## Change Record

Tracks:

- change source;
- rationale;
- affected components;
- validation;
- approval.

---

## Change Lifecycle


REQUEST

↓

ANALYSIS

↓

DECISION

↓

IMPLEMENTATION

↓

VERIFICATION


---

## Change Invariant

No significant change occurs without preserved lineage.
