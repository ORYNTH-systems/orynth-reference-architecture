# Architecture Health Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Morning Star Stewardship Layer

---

## Purpose

Define mechanisms for monitoring the health, coherence, and integrity of the Morning Star architecture after completion.

---

## Health Domains

### Structural Health

Monitors:

- architectural relationships;
- dependency integrity;
- subsystem alignment.

---

### Semantic Health

Monitors:

- terminology consistency;
- conceptual continuity;
- meaning preservation.

---

### Operational Health

Monitors:

- execution reliability;
- validation status;
- implementation alignment.

---

### Evolutionary Health

Monitors:

- change impact;
- adaptation quality;
- continuity preservation.

---

## Health Cycle


OBSERVE

## Health Invariant

Architectural evolution must preserve system identity.
