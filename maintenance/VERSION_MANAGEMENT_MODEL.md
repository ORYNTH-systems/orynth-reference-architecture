# Version Management Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

Morning Star Stewardship Layer

---

## Purpose

Define version evolution rules after Morning Star v1.0.0.

---

## Version Principle

Versions represent controlled evolution while preserving architectural lineage.

---

## Version Classes

### Patch Evolution

Changes:

- corrections;
- documentation;
- non-structural improvements.

---

### Minor Evolution

Changes:

- extensions;
- compatible capabilities;
- additional components.

---

### Major Evolution

Changes:

- foundational modifications;
- architectural restructuring.

---

## Version Lifecycle


PROPOSAL

↓

REVIEW

↓

VALIDATION

↓

RELEASE

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Version Invariant

Version changes preserve traceability to the original architecture.
