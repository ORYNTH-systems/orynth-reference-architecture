# Readiness State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track evolutionary readiness states, assessment conditions, transition eligibility, and future development pathways.

---

## State Principle

Readiness states preserve relationships between current architecture conditions, evaluation criteria, transition requirements, and approved evolutionary states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- readiness identity;
- architecture condition;
- integrity status;
- governance status;
- capability condition;
- transition requirement;
- readiness classification;
- future pathway.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE ARCHITECTURE CONDITION

↓

EVALUATE READINESS FACTORS

↓

CLASSIFY EVOLUTION STATE

↓

RECORD TRANSITION ELIGIBILITY

↓

VALIDATE READINESS RESULT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A readiness state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH architecture states and their capacity for future evolution.
