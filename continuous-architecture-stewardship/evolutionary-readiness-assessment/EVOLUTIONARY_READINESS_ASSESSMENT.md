# Evolutionary Readiness Assessment

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to evaluate ORYNTH architecture readiness for future evolutionary transitions, capability expansions, and systemic transformations.

---

## Readiness Principle

Evolutionary readiness assessment determines whether an architecture state possesses the required continuity, integrity, capability, and governance conditions necessary for controlled evolution.

Readiness precedes transformation.

---

## Readiness Domains

Includes:

- architecture stability;
- integrity condition;
- governance alignment;
- capability maturity;
- continuity preservation;
- transition capacity;
- dependency readiness;
- future expansion potential.

---

## Readiness Lifecycle


ASSESS CURRENT ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

EVALUATE INTEGRITY CONDITIONS

↓

REVIEW GOVERNANCE ALIGNMENT

↓

MEASURE EVOLUTION CAPABILITY

↓

IDENTIFY TRANSITION REQUIREMENTS

↓

DETERMINE READINESS STATUS

↓

APPROVE EVOLUTION PATHWAY

↓

PRESERVE ASSESSMENT RECORD


---

## Principle

Evolutionary readiness assessment ensures ORYNTH advances through validated transitions while preserving coherence, continuity, and architectural integrity.
