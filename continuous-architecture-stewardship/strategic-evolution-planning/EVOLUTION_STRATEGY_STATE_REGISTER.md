# Evolution Strategy State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track strategic evolution states, priorities, decisions, and future architecture pathways.

---

## State Principle

Evolution strategy states preserve relationships between ecosystem conditions, architecture assessments, strategic decisions, and future development states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- strategic objective;
- evolution priority;
- affected architecture domain;
- impact assessment;
- development state;
- continuity condition.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE STRATEGIC SIGNAL

↓

DEFINE EVOLUTION STATE

↓

ASSESS PRIORITY

↓

MAP DEVELOPMENT PATH

↓

VERIFY STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An evolution strategy state register maintains traceability between strategic intent and governed architecture advancement.
