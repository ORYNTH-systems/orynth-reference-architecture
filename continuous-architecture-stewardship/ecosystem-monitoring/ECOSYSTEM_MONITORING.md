# Ecosystem Monitoring

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the continuous monitoring framework used to observe ORYNTH ecosystem conditions after publication.

---

## Monitoring Principle

Ecosystem monitoring preserves awareness of architecture condition, external interaction, operational signals, emerging requirements, and potential evolution pathways while maintaining reference continuity.

---

## Monitoring Domains

Includes:

- architecture activity;
- ecosystem interaction;
- external validation signals;
- implementation observations;
- technology changes;
- governance conditions;
- future requirements.

---

## Monitoring Lifecycle


OBSERVE ECOSYSTEM CONDITIONS

↓

COLLECT RELEVANT SIGNALS

↓

CLASSIFY OBSERVATION TYPE

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURAL IMPACT

↓

UPDATE ECOSYSTEM STATE

↓

PRESERVE OBSERVATION HISTORY


---

## Principle

Ecosystem monitoring provides the continuous awareness layer required for responsible ORYNTH stewardship and future adaptation.
