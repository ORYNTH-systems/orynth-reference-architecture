## Evolution Principle

Stewardship evolution records preserve improvements in maintenance processes, governance practices, preservation methods, and long-term architecture management.

- stewardship methodology;
- architecture review;
- continuity management;
- governance refinement;
- knowledge preservation;
- future planning.
