# Stewardship State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track stewardship states, preservation actions, governance conditions, and long-term architectural transitions.

---

## State Principle

Stewardship states preserve relationships between architecture conditions, maintenance activities, evolutionary decisions, and future continuity requirements.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- stewardship identity;
- architecture condition;
- preservation activity;
- governance state;
- integrity condition;
- evolution status;
- continuity record;
- future readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE STEWARDSHIP CONDITION

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURE REQUIREMENTS

↓

RECORD MAINTENANCE ACTIONS

↓

EVALUATE EVOLUTION NEEDS

↓

VALIDATE CONTINUITY STATE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A stewardship state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH architecture maintenance activities and long-term evolutionary progression.
