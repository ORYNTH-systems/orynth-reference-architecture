# Assurance Report Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH assurance activities, findings, and execution evaluations are communicated and preserved.

---

## Assurance Reporting Principle

Assurance reports provide structured visibility into execution conditions, verification results, risks, and outcomes.

---

## Report Components

Includes:

- evaluation subject;
- execution context;
- evidence reviewed;
- assurance findings;
- identified conditions;
- recommended actions;
- final disposition.

---

## Reporting Lifecycle


INITIATE REPORT

↓

COLLECT ASSURANCE DATA

↓

ANALYZE FINDINGS

↓

DOCUMENT RESULTS

↓

REVIEW REPORT

↓

ARCHIVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Assurance reporting creates a durable record connecting execution activity, evaluation, and operational understanding.
