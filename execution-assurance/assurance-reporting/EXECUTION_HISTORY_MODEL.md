# Execution History Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how execution events and transitions are preserved over time.

---

## History Principle

Execution history maintains a reconstructable record of actions, decisions, states, and outcomes.

---

## History Elements

Includes:

- execution request;
- authorization context;
- verification results;
- state transitions;
- controls applied;
- completion status.

---

## History Lifecycle


CAPTURE EVENT

↓

RECORD CONTEXT

↓

TRACK TRANSITIONS

↓

DOCUMENT OUTCOME

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY

↓

ENABLE REVIEW


---

## Principle

Execution history supports accountability, learning, and future assurance activities.
