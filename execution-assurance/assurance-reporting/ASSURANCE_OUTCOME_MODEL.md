# Assurance Outcome Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how assurance conclusions and execution outcomes are classified and preserved.

---

## Outcome Principle

Assurance outcomes communicate whether execution conditions remained aligned with defined requirements.

---

## Outcome Categories

Includes:

- verified;
- conditionally verified;
- unresolved;
- restricted;
- failed;
- unknown.

---

## Outcome Lifecycle


EVALUATE RESULT

↓

CLASSIFY OUTCOME

↓

DOCUMENT EVIDENCE

↓

REVIEW DETERMINATION

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Clear outcome classification enables transparent assurance decisions and continuous improvement.
