# Execution Decision Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Preserve records of execution evaluations, decisions, and outcomes.

---

## Record Structure

Includes:

- execution identifier;
- requested action;
- authority source;
- evaluation conditions;
- decision result;
- evidence references;
- outcome status.

---

## Decision Lifecycle


CREATE RECORD

↓

CAPTURE CONTEXT

↓

DOCUMENT EVALUATION

↓

RECORD DECISION

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

Execution decision records provide reconstructable evidence of why execution was permitted, blocked, or modified.
