# Authority Execution Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the relationship between authorization state and execution eligibility.

---

## Authority Principle

Authorization establishes permission, while execution requires current eligibility under applicable conditions.

---

## Evaluation Factors

Includes:

- authority validity;
- identity continuity;
- policy state;
- dependency conditions;
- environmental conditions;
- temporal validity.

---

## Execution Evaluation Lifecycle


REQUEST ACTION

↓

VERIFY AUTHORITY

↓

RECONSTRUCT CONDITIONS

↓

ASSESS ELIGIBILITY

↓

PERMIT OR PREVENT EXECUTION

↓

RECORD DECISION


---

## Principle

Permission alone does not guarantee executable validity.
