# Execution Governance Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define governance structures controlling how execution decisions are evaluated, authorized, and preserved.

---

## Execution Governance Principle

Execution governance ensures that actions occur only through defined authority, evaluated conditions, and traceable decision pathways.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- authority evaluation;
- execution eligibility;
- decision review;
- responsibility assignment;
- execution history.

---

## Execution Governance Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ACTION

↓

EVALUATE AUTHORITY

↓

ASSESS CONDITIONS

↓

DETERMINE ELIGIBILITY

↓

AUTHORIZE EXECUTION

↓

RECORD OUTCOME


---

## Principle

Governed execution preserves alignment between intended action, current conditions, and accountable operation.
