# Validation Pathway Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define pathways through which execution decisions and system conditions are validated.

---

## Validation Principle

Validation confirms that evaluated conditions remain consistent with intended requirements and operational boundaries.

---

## Validation Stages

Includes:

- requirement validation;
- state validation;
- evidence validation;
- outcome validation.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


IDENTIFY SUBJECT

↓

DEFINE CRITERIA

↓

PERFORM VALIDATION

↓

ASSESS FINDINGS

↓

RECORD RESULT

↓

MAINTAIN HISTORY


---

## Principle

Validation pathways preserve confidence by connecting requirements, evidence, and outcomes.
