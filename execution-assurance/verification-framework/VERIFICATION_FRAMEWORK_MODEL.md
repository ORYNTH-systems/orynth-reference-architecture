# Verification Framework Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH execution conditions, system states, and evidence are verified before and during execution.

---

## Verification Principle

Verification establishes whether required conditions are satisfied through evidence, evaluation, and traceable assessment.

---

## Verification Domains

Includes:

- execution conditions;
- authority state;
- dependency state;
- policy alignment;
- evidence sufficiency.

---

## Verification Lifecycle


DEFINE REQUIREMENTS

↓

COLLECT EVIDENCE

↓

EVALUATE CONDITIONS

↓

VERIFY ALIGNMENT

↓

DETERMINE RESULT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Verification transforms assumptions into evaluated execution conditions.
