# Evidence Verification Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how execution evidence is collected, reviewed, and verified.

---

## Evidence Principle

Evidence must maintain traceable relationships between system conditions, evaluation activities, and execution outcomes.

---

## Evidence Categories

Includes:

- authority evidence;
- state evidence;
- dependency evidence;
- policy evidence;
- outcome evidence.

---

## Evidence Verification Lifecycle


COLLECT EVIDENCE

↓

CLASSIFY SOURCE

↓

VERIFY AUTHENTICITY

↓

ASSESS RELEVANCE

↓

CONNECT TO DECISION

↓

ARCHIVE RESULT


---

## Principle

Verified evidence provides the foundation for trustworthy execution assurance.
