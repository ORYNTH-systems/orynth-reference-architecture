# Reconstruction Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how execution conditions are reconstructed for review, validation, and assurance.

---

## Reconstruction Principle

Execution reconstruction recreates the relevant conditions, evidence, and decisions associated with an execution event.

---

## Reconstruction Elements

Includes:

- initial request;
- authority state;
- evaluated conditions;
- dependencies;
- constraints;
- execution outcome.

---

## Reconstruction Lifecycle


COLLECT RECORDS

↓

RESTORE CONTEXT

↓

MAP CONDITIONS

↓

REVIEW DECISION PATH

↓

VERIFY OUTCOME

↓

PRESERVE RECONSTRUCTION


---

## Principle

Reconstructable execution enables accountability, validation, and continuous improvement.
