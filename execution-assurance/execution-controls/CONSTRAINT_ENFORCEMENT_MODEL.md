# Constraint Enforcement Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how constraints are identified, evaluated, and enforced during execution.

---

## Constraint Principle

Constraints preserve system integrity by preventing actions that violate defined requirements.

---

## Constraint Categories

Includes:

- authority constraints;
- policy constraints;
- resource constraints;
- temporal constraints;
- dependency constraints.

---

## Constraint Lifecycle


DEFINE CONSTRAINT

↓

ASSESS CONDITION

↓

ENFORCE REQUIREMENT

↓

BLOCK OR PERMIT ACTION

↓

VERIFY RESULT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Constraint enforcement maintains alignment between intended execution and valid system conditions.
