# Execution Control Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define controls that govern execution behavior, enforce requirements, and preserve operational integrity.

---

## Execution Control Principle

Execution controls ensure actions remain within defined authority, constraints, and validated conditions.

---

## Control Domains

Includes:

- authorization controls;
- eligibility controls;
- policy controls;
- dependency controls;
- operational controls.

---

## Execution Control Lifecycle
IDENTIFY ACTION

↓

EVALUATE CONDITIONS

↓

APPLY CONTROLS

↓

ALLOW OR PREVENT EXECUTION

↓

MONITOR RESULT

↓

RECORD OUTCOME


---

## Principle

Execution controls transform governance requirements into enforceable operational boundaries.
