# Observations

Execution ID:

MS-010

Status:

ACTIVE

Classification:

Constitutional Execution

---

## Observation 1

Constitutional verification follows constitutional evaluation.

Evaluation establishes assessed conditions.

Verification establishes confirmed constitutional integrity.

---

## Observation 2

Verification is distinct from evaluation.

Evaluation determines assessed state conditions.

Verification confirms that required conditions, evidence, and constraints are satisfied.

---

## Observation 3

Verification requires complete evidence continuity.

Every verification event shall preserve:

- verification target;
- evidence sources;
- evaluation records;
- execution history;
- verification criteria;
- reconstruction pathway.

---

## Observation 4

Verification preserves constitutional identity.

Confirming the state of a framework does not modify the framework being verified.

---

## Observation 5

Verification preserves provenance.

Every verified condition shall remain attributable to:

- originating framework;
- originating authority;
- evidence source;
- execution artifact;
- verification record.

---

## Observation 6

Verification preserves semantic integrity.

Verification shall confirm established meaning without:

- redefining terminology;
- altering boundaries;
- introducing unsupported equivalence;
- modifying constitutional interpretation.

---

## Observation 7

Verification requires dependency continuity.

A constitutional verification shall only occur when all required prior states and execution dependencies have been satisfied.

---

## Observation 8

Verification shall remain deterministic.

Equivalent constitutional states, evidence chains, and verification criteria shall produce equivalent verification outcomes.

---

## Observation 9

Verification shall remain reconstructable.

An independent observer shall reproduce verification results using:

- admitted evidence;
- execution records;
- verification criteria;
- constitutional artifacts.

---

## Constitutional Finding

Constitutional Verification is an independent capability governing final confirmation of constitutional integrity while preserving identity, provenance, evidence integrity, semantic integrity, dependency ordering, framework independence, and reconstructability.
