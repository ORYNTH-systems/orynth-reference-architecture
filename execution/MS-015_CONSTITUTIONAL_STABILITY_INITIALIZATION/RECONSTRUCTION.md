# Reconstruction

Execution ID:

MS-015

Status:

ACTIVE

Classification:

Constitutional Execution

---

## Reconstruction Objective

Define how stable constitutional states are independently reproduced from operating conditions, transition records, and preserved constraints.

---

## Required Inputs

- stable state identifier;
- operating conditions;
- constitutional boundaries;
- transition history;
- provenance records.

---

## Reconstruction Sequence

1. Identify stable state.
2. Restore operating conditions.
3. Restore transition history.
4. Verify identity preservation.
5. Verify dynamic balance.
6. Generate reconstructed stability state.

---

## Reconstruction Equality

Rc(St₁)=Rc(St₂)

when equivalent stability inputs produce equivalent stability states.

---

## Failure Conditions

Reconstruction fails when:

- operating context is missing;
- stability conditions are undefined;
- provenance is incomplete;
- identity impact cannot be assessed.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

Stable states remain admissible only when their coherence can be reconstructed.
