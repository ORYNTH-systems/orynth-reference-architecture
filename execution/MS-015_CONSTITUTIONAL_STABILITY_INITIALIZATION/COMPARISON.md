## Stability Compared with Resilience

Resilience preserves integrity under disruption.

Stability preserves coherent operation across conditions.

---

## Stability Compared with Adaptation

Adaptation responds to changing conditions.

Stability maintains operational coherence while allowing adaptation.

---

## Stability Compared with Evolution

Evolution governs structured transformation.

Stability preserves coherence during and between transformations.

---

## Stability Compared with Continuity

Continuity preserves identity through time.

Stability preserves reliable state conditions through operation.

---

## Stability Compared with Stagnation

Stagnation prevents meaningful change.

Stability permits governed change while maintaining coherence.

---

## Constitutional Boundary

MS-015 governs coherent operation.

It does not prevent evolution, adaptation, or authorized transformation.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

A stable framework remains coherent without becoming static.
