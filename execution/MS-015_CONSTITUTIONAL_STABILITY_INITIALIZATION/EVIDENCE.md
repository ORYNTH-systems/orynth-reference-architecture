# Evidence

Execution ID:

MS-015

Status:

ACTIVE

Classification:

Constitutional Execution

---

## Evidence Objective

Demonstrate that constitutional stability preserves coherent operation while maintaining identity, continuity, provenance, semantic integrity, and reconstructability.

---

## Evidence Category 1 — Stability State Evidence

Evidence shall identify:

- operating state;
- stability conditions;
- contextual environment;
- observed deviations.

---

## Evidence Category 2 — Coherence Preservation

Evidence shall demonstrate preservation of:

- identity;
- invariant relationships;
- operational consistency;
- semantic boundaries.

---

## Evidence Category 3 — Dynamic Stability Evidence

Evidence shall demonstrate that stability permits:

- adaptation;
- evolution;
- resilience;
- controlled transitions.

---

## Evidence Category 4 — Instability Classification

Evidence shall distinguish:

- temporary fluctuation;
- recoverable instability;
- transitional state;
- constitutional failure.

---

## Evidence Category 5 — Provenance Continuity

Evidence shall preserve:

- prior stable state;
- transition conditions;
- stabilization pathway;
- resulting state.

---

## Evidence Category 6 — Reconstruction Capability

Evidence shall demonstrate stable states remain reproducible from:

- operating conditions;
- constitutional constraints;
- transition records;
- historical evidence.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

Stability preserves coherence without preventing legitimate transformation.
