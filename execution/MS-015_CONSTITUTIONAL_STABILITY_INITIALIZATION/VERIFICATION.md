## Verification Objective

Verify that Constitutional Stability preserves coherent operation while maintaining identity, continuity, adaptability, provenance, and reconstruction capability.

---

## Verification Conditions

### Stable State Recognition

PASS when:

- stable conditions are defined;
- operational context is preserved;
- state boundaries are explicit.

---

### Identity Preservation

PASS when:

- stability does not freeze identity;
- invariants remain preserved;
- evolution remains possible.

---

### Dynamic Balance

PASS when:

- stability supports responsiveness;
- adaptation remains available;
- transitions remain governed.

---

### Provenance Integrity

PASS when:

- stable states;
- transitions;
- stabilization actions

remain attributable.

---

### Reconstruction Capability

PASS when:

- operating conditions;
- stability rules;
- historical states

reproduce equivalent stability assessments.
