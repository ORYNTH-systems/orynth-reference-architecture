## Observation 1

Orientation precedes specialization.

An individual shall first understand the constitutional ecosystem before studying individual frameworks.

---

## Observation 2

Orientation establishes context rather than authority.

Morning Star introduces constitutional relationships without becoming the constitutional authority for those relationships.

---

## Observation 3

Orientation preserves ecosystem coherence.

Every introduced framework shall appear within its constitutional context rather than as an isolated artifact.

---

## Observation 4

Orientation preserves dependency awareness.

Individuals shall understand why a framework appears within the ecosystem before studying its internal scientific content.

---

## Observation 5

Orientation preserves provenance.

Every framework introduced through Morning Star shall remain attributable to its originating constitutional authority.

---

## Observation 6

Orientation preserves semantic integrity.

Frameworks shall not inherit meaning from neighboring frameworks merely because they appear adjacent within the orientation architecture.

---

## Observation 7

Orientation shall remain reconstructable.

Independent observers shall be capable of reproducing identical ecosystem orientation using only constitutionally admitted repository artifacts.

---

## Observation 8

Orientation reduces constitutional ambiguity.

A governed orientation architecture establishes a common constitutional understanding before framework-specific interpretation begins.

---

## Constitutional Finding

Ecosystem orientation constitutes an independent constitutional capability whose function is to establish coherent understanding of the ORYNTH ecosystem while preserving provenance, semantic integrity, dependency ordering, reconstructability, and complete framework independence.
