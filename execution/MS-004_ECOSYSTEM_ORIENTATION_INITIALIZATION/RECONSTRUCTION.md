## Purpose

This document defines the constitutional reconstruction procedure for the Morning Star Ecosystem Orientation Architecture.

Reconstruction demonstrates that ecosystem orientation can be reproduced exclusively from admitted constitutional evidence.

---

## Reconstruction Inputs

The orientation architecture shall be reconstructed using:

- constitution/CANONICAL_MODEL.md
- governance artifacts
- ontology artifacts
- execution/MS-001_CONSTITUTIONAL_INITIALIZATION/*
- execution/MS-002_ENTRY_ONTOLOGY_INITIALIZATION/*
- execution/MS-003_CONSTITUTIONAL_NAVIGATION_INITIALIZATION/*
- repository history
- admitted constitutional amendments
- version tags

No external interpretation shall be required.

---

## Reconstruction Procedure

A valid constitutional reconstruction shall proceed in the following order:

1. Restore repository state.
2. Restore Constitutional Model.
3. Restore governance.
4. Restore ontology.
5. Restore constitutional navigation.
6. Restore ecosystem orientation.
7. Restore dependency relationships.
8. Restore provenance.
9. Restore semantic boundaries.
10. Verify constitutional invariants.

---

## Reconstruction Outputs

A successful reconstruction shall reproduce:

- identical orientation ordering;
- identical dependency relationships;
- identical framework attribution;
- identical provenance;
- identical semantic boundaries;
- identical constitutional authority assignments.

---

## Reconstruction Requirements

Reconstruction shall remain:

- deterministic;
- reproducible;
- auditable;
- constitutionally governed;
- historically traceable.

---

## Reconstruction Failure Conditions

Reconstruction fails if:

- repository evidence is incomplete;
- orientation ordering changes;
- dependency relationships differ;
- provenance becomes ambiguous;
- semantic integrity cannot be reproduced;
- constitutional invariants are violated.

---

## Equivalence Criterion

A reconstructed orientation architecture is constitutionally equivalent when every orientation sequence, dependency relationship, provenance chain, semantic boundary, and constitutional authority exactly matches the admitted architecture.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

Reconstruction preserves constitutional orientation without altering constitutional identity, framework authority, scientific meaning, provenance, or governance.
