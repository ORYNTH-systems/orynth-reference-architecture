## Comparison Objective

Distinguish Constitutional Learning from the preceding Morning Star constitutional capabilities while preserving the role, authority, and boundaries of each execution stage.

---

## Capability Comparison

| Capability | Primary Function | Required Output |
|---|---|---|
| Constitutional Initialization | Establish Morning Star constitutional identity | Admitted constitutional foundation |
| Entry Ontology | Define admissible ecosystem entry concepts | Canonical entry ontology |
| Constitutional Navigation | Govern movement through admitted structures | Reconstructable navigation state |
| Ecosystem Orientation | Establish relational understanding of the ecosystem | Constitutional orientation state |

---

## Discovery Compared with Learning

Framework Discovery answers:

- What framework exists?
- What is its constitutional identity?
- Where is its authority located?
- What artifacts establish its existence?
- Is it admissible for further constitutional engagement?

Constitutional Learning answers:

- What does the framework constitutionally establish?
- Which concepts must be learned first?
- What dependencies govern comprehension?
- What provenance belongs to each learned concept?
- Has constitutional understanding been acquired admissibly?

Discovery identifies the framework.

Learning acquires governed understanding of the framework.

---

## Orientation Compared with Learning

Ecosystem Orientation establishes:

- framework placement;
- ecosystem relationships;
- constitutional boundaries;
- broad structural context.

Constitutional Learning establishes:

- concept-level understanding;
- dependency-aware comprehension;
- provenance-preserving knowledge acquisition;
- reconstructable learning state.

Orientation establishes where a framework belongs.

Learning establishes what may constitutionally be understood from it.

---

## Navigation Compared with Learning

Constitutional Navigation governs:

- admissible movement;
- route selection;
- constitutional access;
- traversal state.

Constitutional Learning governs:

- admissible comprehension;
- prerequisite satisfaction;
- semantic acquisition;
- learning state.

Navigation determines where the initiate may proceed.

Learning determines what the initiate may constitutionally acquire.

---

## Learning Compared with Interpretation

Constitutional Learning preserves admitted meaning.

Interpretation may produce contextual analysis, synthesis, or application.

Learning shall not be treated as authorization to:

- redefine canonical terminology;
- alter scientific conclusions;
- merge framework identities;
- create unsupported equivalence;
- supersede constitutional authority.

Interpretation is outside the constitutional scope of MS-006 unless separately governed.

---

## Learning Compared with Framework Modification

Constitutional Learning is observational and acquisitional.

Framework modification is authoritative and evolutionary.

Learning shall never:

- amend a framework;
- revise constitutional authority;
- change mathematical models;
- alter evidence;
- modify governance;
- change scientific conclusions;
- initiate framework evolution.

---

## Constitutional Boundary

MS-006 begins after constitutional framework discovery and ends when admissible constitutional understanding has been acquired and recorded.

It does not govern interpretation, application, implementation, modification, amendment, or evolution unless those capabilities are established through separate constitutional executions.

---

## Final Comparative Finding

Constitutional Learning is a distinct Morning Star capability.

It cannot be reduced to discovery, orientation, navigation, interpretation, or framework modification because it independently governs dependency-aware, provenance-preserving, semantically intact, reconstructable acquisition of constitutional understanding.
