## Purpose

This document defines the constitutional verification procedure for the Morning Star Framework Discovery Architecture.

Verification confirms that framework discovery preserves constitutional identity while providing admissible discovery throughout the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Verification Objectives

The verification process shall confirm that:

- framework discovery is deterministic;
- constitutional identity is preserved;
- framework independence is preserved;
- dependency ordering is maintained;
- provenance remains intact;
- semantic boundaries remain unchanged;
- reconstruction produces constitutionally equivalent discovery behavior.

---

## Verification Criteria

Execution shall PASS only when all of the following conditions are satisfied:

- every framework becomes discoverable only when constitutionally admissible;
- dependency ordering remains preserved;
- provenance remains explicit and reconstructable;
- semantic integrity is preserved;
- discovery remains deterministic;
- constitutional invariants remain satisfied.

---

## Verification Procedure

1. Verify the Constitutional Model.
2. Verify governance artifacts.
3. Verify ontology artifacts.
4. Verify navigation architecture.
5. Verify ecosystem orientation architecture.
6. Verify framework discovery architecture.
7. Verify dependency ordering.
8. Verify provenance preservation.
9. Verify semantic boundary preservation.
10. Verify reconstruction equivalence.
11. Verify constitutional invariants.

---

## Failure Conditions

Verification shall FAIL if:

- framework authority is modified;
- dependency ordering is violated;
- discoverability bypasses constitutional prerequisites;
- provenance becomes ambiguous;
- semantic drift is introduced;
- discovery cannot be reconstructed;
- constitutional identity changes.

---

## Expected Result

Successful verification demonstrates that Morning Star provides constitutionally admissible framework discovery while preserving complete framework independence, constitutional identity, and deterministic discovery behavior.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

Verification shall establish that framework discovery preserves constitutional identity, provenance, dependency ordering, semantic integrity, framework independence, and deterministic reconstructability.
