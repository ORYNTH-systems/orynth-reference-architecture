## Purpose

This document compares the reconstructed Morning Star Navigation Architecture against the constitutionally admitted architecture.

The purpose of comparison is to determine constitutional equivalence rather than functional similarity.

---

## Comparison Baseline

The canonical baseline consists of:

- Constitution
- Governance
- Ontology
- MS-001 Constitutional Initialization
- MS-002 Entry Ontology Initialization

---

## Comparison Criteria

The following properties shall remain equivalent:

- constitutional identity;
- navigation ordering;
- dependency relationships;
- framework independence;
- provenance;
- semantic boundaries;
- reconstruction behavior;
- constitutional authority.

---

## Identity Comparison

The reconstructed navigation architecture shall preserve the identical constitutional identity of the admitted architecture.

No framework shall gain or lose constitutional authority through reconstruction.

---

## Structural Comparison

Equivalent structures shall contain:

- identical navigation hierarchy;
- identical dependency graph;
- identical framework relationships;
- identical constitutional boundaries.

Structural variation constitutes constitutional divergence.

---

## Semantic Comparison

Equivalent navigation shall preserve:

- terminology;
- constitutional meaning;
- provenance;
- framework attribution.

Equivalent ordering shall not modify semantic identity.

---

## Behavioral Comparison

Equivalent navigation behavior shall:

- admit identical entry paths;
- reject identical inadmissible paths;
- preserve dependency constraints;
- produce identical reconstruction outcomes.

---

## Comparison Result

Constitutional equivalence is established only when every comparison criterion is satisfied without exception.

Functional resemblance alone is insufficient.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

Morning Star Navigation remains constitutionally equivalent only when its reconstructed architecture preserves identical identity, governance, provenance, dependency ordering, semantic integrity, and admissible navigation behavior.
