MS-003

---

## Purpose

This document defines the constitutional reconstruction procedure for the Morning Star Navigation Architecture.

Reconstruction demonstrates that constitutional navigation can be reproduced solely from admitted constitutional evidence.

---

## Reconstruction Inputs

The navigation architecture shall be reconstructed using:

- constitution/CANONICAL_MODEL.md
- governance artifacts
- ontology artifacts
- execution records
- admitted amendments
- repository history
- version tags

No external assumptions shall be required.

---

## Reconstruction Procedure

A constitutional reconstruction shall proceed in the following order:

1. Restore repository state.
2. Restore Constitutional Model.
3. Restore governance.
4. Restore ontology.
5. Restore framework relationships.
6. Restore dependency model.
7. Restore navigation architecture.
8. Restore semantic boundaries.
9. Restore provenance.
10. Verify constitutional invariants.

---

## Reconstruction Outputs

A successful reconstruction shall reproduce:

- identical navigation ordering;
- identical dependency relationships;
- identical provenance;
- identical semantic boundaries;
- identical constitutional authority assignments;
- identical framework independence.

---

## Reconstruction Requirements

Reconstruction shall be:

- deterministic;
- reproducible;
- auditable;
- constitutionally governed;
- historically traceable.

---

## Reconstruction Failure Conditions

Reconstruction fails if:

- repository evidence is incomplete;
- provenance cannot be established;
- dependency ordering differs;
- framework identities change;
- semantic boundaries cannot be reproduced;
- constitutional invariants are violated.

---

## Equivalence Criterion

A reconstructed navigation architecture is constitutionally equivalent when every admissible navigation path, dependency, provenance chain, and constitutional authority exactly matches the admitted architecture.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

Reconstruction preserves constitutional identity.

It reproduces the navigation architecture without introducing, removing, or modifying constitutional meaning.
