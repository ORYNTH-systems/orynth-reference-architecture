## Relationship Integrity

represent the relationship between constitutional concepts x and y.

- provenance(x) remains preserved;
- provenance(y) remains preserved;
- semantic boundaries remain intact.

S(c,t₁)=S(c,t₂)

---

## Identity Preservation

I(f,t₁)=I(f,t₂)

when identical admitted constitutional inputs are provided.
