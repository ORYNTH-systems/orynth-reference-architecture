# Evidence

Execution ID:

MS-009

Status:

ACTIVE

Classification:

Constitutional Execution

---

## Evidence Objective

This execution establishes the constitutional evidence required to demonstrate that evaluation within the Morning Star architecture remains evidence-based, admissible, provenance-preserving, semantically stable, and constitutionally governed.

---

## Evidence Category 1 — Evaluation Provenance

Every constitutional evaluation event shall preserve:

- evaluated state identifier;
- originating framework;
- originating constitutional authority;
- evidence sources;
- evaluation criteria;
- dependency conditions;
- execution identifier.

---

## Evidence Category 2 — Evidence Integrity

Evidence shall demonstrate:

- source attribution;
- evidence completeness;
- evidence context;
- evidence availability;
- evidence reconstruction capability.

Evaluation shall not exceed the evidence available.

---

## Evidence Category 3 — Assessment Integrity

Evidence shall demonstrate that evaluation preserves:

- defined evaluation criteria;
- constitutional boundaries;
- framework context;
- semantic constraints.

---

## Evidence Category 4 — Dependency Validation

Evidence shall demonstrate that:

- required prerequisites were satisfied;
- evaluation ordering was preserved;
- no evaluation occurred from invalid states.

---

## Evidence Category 5 — Deterministic Evaluation

Evidence shall demonstrate that equivalent constitutional states and evidence conditions reproduce:

- identical evaluation criteria;
- identical evaluation state;
- identical admissibility outcome.

---

## Evidence Category 6 — Authority Preservation

Evidence shall demonstrate that evaluation never modifies:

- framework authority;
- mathematical models;
- governance;
- evidence;
- scientific conclusions;
- constitutional evolution.

---

## Constitutional Evidence Requirement

An evaluation state is constitutionally admissible only when assessment remains attributable, evidence-supported, context-preserving, semantically intact, dependency-valid, and independently reconstructable.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

Evaluation may measure constitutional conditions.

Evaluation shall never become a substitute source of constitutional authority.
