## Application Compared with Evaluation

- governed utilization;
- preserved context;
- admissible expression of understanding.

Constitutional Evaluation establishes:

- assessment of resulting states;
- evidence-based measurement;
- integrity verification.

Application uses knowledge.

Evaluation assesses conditions resulting from use.

## Evaluation Compared with Authority

Constitutional Evaluation may determine:

- whether conditions are satisfied;
- whether evidence is sufficient;
- whether requirements are met.

- framework ownership;
- scientific truth beyond evidence;
- constitutional authority;
- governance control.

Constitutional Evaluation observes framework states.

Framework evolution modifies framework states.

Evaluation shall never:

- amend frameworks;
- revise mathematics;
- alter evidence;
- modify governance;
- change constitutional identity.

---

## Constitutional Boundary

MS-009 begins after constitutional application and governs admissible assessment of resulting states.

It does not govern:

- framework modification;
- governance evolution;
- scientific revision;
- authority transfer;
- constitutional amendment.

---

## Final Comparative Finding

Constitutional Evaluation is a distinct Morning Star capability.

It cannot be reduced to application, comprehension, learning, discovery, navigation, or judgment because it governs evidence-based assessment while preserving constitutional boundaries.
