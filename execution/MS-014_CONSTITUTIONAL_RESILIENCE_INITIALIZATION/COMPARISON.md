## Resilience Compared with Adaptation

Adaptation responds to changing conditions.

Resilience preserves integrity when conditions threaten stability.

---

## Resilience Compared with Evolution

Evolution governs structured transformation.

Resilience preserves identity during disruption.

---

## Resilience Compared with Recovery

Recovery restores operation.

Resilience preserves the conditions necessary for recovery without identity loss.

---

## Resilience Compared with Continuity

Continuity preserves lineage.

Resilience preserves integrity under adverse conditions.

---

## Constitutional Boundary

MS-014 governs preservation under stress.

It does not authorize redesign or replacement.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

A resilient framework remains itself despite disruption.
