## Verification Objective

Verify that Constitutional Resilience preserves framework integrity under stress, disruption, uncertainty, degradation, and recovery conditions while maintaining identity, continuity, provenance, semantic integrity, and reconstruction capability.

---

## Verification Conditions

### Stress Recognition

PASS when:

- stress conditions are identified;
- affected states are traceable;
- contextual factors are preserved.

---

### Identity Preservation

PASS when:

- framework identity remains intact;
- invariants remain preserved;
- resilience response does not become unauthorized transformation.

---

### Recovery Integrity

PASS when:

- restoration preserves lineage;
- recovery pathways are documented;
- resulting states remain reconstructable.

---

### Failure Boundary Recognition

PASS when resilience distinguishes:

- temporary stress;
- degraded operation;
- recoverable failure;
- constitutional collapse.

---

### Provenance Integrity

PASS when:

- prior states remain available;
- response history is preserved;
- recovery transitions remain attributable.

---

### Reconstruction Capability

PASS when:

- stress conditions;
- response pathways;
- recovery records;

reproduce equivalent resilience states.
