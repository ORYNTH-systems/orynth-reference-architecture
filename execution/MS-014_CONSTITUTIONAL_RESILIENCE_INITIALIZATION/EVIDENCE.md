# Evidence

Execution ID:

MS-014

Status:

ACTIVE

Classification:

Constitutional Execution

---

## Evidence Objective

Demonstrate that resilience preserves constitutional integrity during stress, degradation, uncertainty, and recovery conditions.

---

## Evidence Category 1 — Stress State Evidence

Evidence shall identify:

- stress condition;
- affected state;
- severity;
- contextual factors.

---

## Evidence Category 2 — Integrity Preservation

Evidence shall demonstrate preservation of:

- identity;
- invariants;
- provenance;
- semantic boundaries.

---

## Evidence Category 3 — Recovery Evidence

Evidence shall preserve:

- recovery pathway;
- restoration conditions;
- resulting state;
- reconstruction history.

---

## Evidence Category 4 — Failure Boundary Evidence

Evidence shall distinguish:

- degradation;
- recoverable failure;
- constitutional collapse.

---

## Evidence Category 5 — Reconstruction Evidence

Evidence shall demonstrate that resilience states remain reproducible from:

- stress conditions;
- response rules;
- historical records;
- constitutional constraints.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

Resilience preserves identity under stress without becoming a mechanism of unauthorized change.
