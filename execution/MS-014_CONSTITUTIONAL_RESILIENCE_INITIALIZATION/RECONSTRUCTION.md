# Reconstruction

Execution ID:

MS-014

Status:

ACTIVE

Classification:

Constitutional Execution

---

## Reconstruction Objective

Define how resilient states are independently reproduced from stress conditions, response pathways, recovery records, and constitutional constraints.

---

## Required Inputs

- stress condition;
- affected framework state;
- response pathway;
- recovery records;
- provenance chain;
- identity constraints.

---

## Reconstruction Sequence

1. Identify disruption condition.
2. Restore affected constitutional state.
3. Restore response pathway.
4. Verify identity preservation.
5. Verify provenance continuity.
6. Verify recovery relationship.
7. Generate reconstructed resilience state.

---

## Reconstruction Equality

Rc(R₁)=Rc(R₂)

when equivalent resilience inputs produce equivalent resilient states.

---

## Failure Conditions

Reconstruction fails when:

- stress conditions are unknown;
- response history is unavailable;
- provenance is broken;
- identity impact cannot be determined.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

Resilience remains admissible only when preservation and recovery remain reconstructable.
