---

## Comparison Objective

Distinguish Constitutional Continuity from prior Morning Star capabilities while preserving execution boundaries.

---

## Capability Comparison

| Capability | Function |
|---|---|
| Verification | Confirms integrity |
| Continuity | Preserves identity through change |

---

## Verification Compared with Continuity

Verification confirms that a state satisfies requirements.

Continuity preserves the relationship between states across time.

---

## Evolution Compared with Continuity

Evolution changes a framework.

Continuity determines whether change preserves framework identity.

---

## Preservation Compared with Continuity

Preservation maintains artifacts.

Continuity maintains:

- identity;
- lineage;
- invariants;
- provenance;
- reconstruction.

---

## Constitutional Boundary

MS-011 governs preservation of identity across authorized change.

It does not authorize change.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

A framework may evolve while remaining itself only when its constitutional identity remains reconstructable.
