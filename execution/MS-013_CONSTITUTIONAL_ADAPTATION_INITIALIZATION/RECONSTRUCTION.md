# Reconstruction

Execution ID:

MS-013

Status:

ACTIVE

Classification:

Constitutional Execution

---

## Reconstruction Objective

Define how adaptive states are independently reproduced from environmental conditions, adaptive rules, preserved constraints, and historical records.

---

## Required Inputs

- adaptive trigger;
- environmental context;
- adaptive constraints;
- framework identity;
- provenance records;
- resulting adaptive state.

---

## Reconstruction Sequence

1. Identify adaptive condition.
2. Restore environmental context.
3. Restore constitutional boundaries.
4. Restore adaptive rules.
5. Verify identity preservation.
6. Verify provenance continuity.
7. Generate reconstructed adaptive state.

---

## Reconstruction Equality

Rc(A₁)=Rc(A₂)

when equivalent adaptive inputs and constraints produce equivalent adaptive states.

---

## Failure Conditions

Reconstruction fails when:

- adaptive triggers are unknown;
- constraints are missing;
- provenance is incomplete;
- identity impact cannot be determined.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

Adaptation remains admissible only when response remains reconstructable.
