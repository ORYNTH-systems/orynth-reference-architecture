# Comparison

---

## Evolution Compared with Continuity

Continuity preserves identity through change.

Evolution governs structured transformation.

---

## Evolution Compared with Revision

Revision changes an artifact.

Evolution determines whether that change preserves framework identity.

---

## Evolution Compared with Verification

Verification confirms integrity.

Evolution governs classified transformation.

---

## Evolution Compared with Replacement

Evolution maintains lineage.

Replacement creates a separate constitutional identity.

---

## Constitutional Boundary

MS-012 governs transformation classification and preservation requirements.

It does not create authority to modify frameworks.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

A framework evolves only when the properties defining its identity remain preserved.
