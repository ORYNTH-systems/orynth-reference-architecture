# Sentient State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track sentient intelligence states, perception conditions, contextual relationships, validation status, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Sentient states preserve relationships between intelligence capability, perception conditions, environmental context, architecture constraints, governance requirements, and resulting coherent configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- sentient identity;
- intelligence state;
- perception condition;
- environmental context;
- internal state;
- response condition;
- coherence status;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.

---

A sentient state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH sentient intelligence processes and their governed architectural relationships.
