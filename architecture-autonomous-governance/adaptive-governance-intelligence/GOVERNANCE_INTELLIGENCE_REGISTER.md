# Governance Intelligence Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track governance intelligence states, assessments, decisions, and evolutionary conditions.

---

## State Principle

Governance intelligence states preserve relationships between observed conditions, analytical processes, governance decisions, and resulting architecture transitions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- intelligence identity;
- observed condition;
- governance assessment;
- decision classification;
- constraint evaluation;
- transition status;
- validation state;
- historical record.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE INTELLIGENCE RECORD

↓

CAPTURE GOVERNANCE CONDITION

↓

ANALYZE SYSTEM STATE

↓

EVALUATE TRANSITION OPTIONS

↓

VALIDATE GOVERNANCE RESPONSE

↓

REGISTER INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

PRESERVE DECISION LINEAGE


---

## Principle

A governance intelligence register maintains traceability between ORYNTH conditions, governance reasoning, and controlled architectural transitions.
