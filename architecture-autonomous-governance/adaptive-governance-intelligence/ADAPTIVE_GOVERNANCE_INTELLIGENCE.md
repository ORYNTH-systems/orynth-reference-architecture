# Adaptive Governance Intelligence

Version:

v1.0.0

## Purpose

Define the framework used to enable adaptive governance intelligence across the ORYNTH architecture system.

---

## Governance Intelligence Principle

Adaptive governance intelligence enables architecture governance to respond to changing conditions while preserving constitutional constraints, reference integrity, and validated continuity.

Adaptation occurs through governed awareness.

---

## Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- governance condition analysis;
- architecture state interpretation;
- reference alignment;
- transition evaluation;
- risk classification;
- continuity assessment;
- decision support;
- evolutionary readiness.
