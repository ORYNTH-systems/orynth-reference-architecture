Version:

## Purpose

Define the framework used to govern recursive intelligence evolution across ORYNTH architecture systems, transformation processes, and continuity models.

---

## Governance Principle

Recursive Self-Evolution Governance enables intelligence systems to evaluate, refine, and transform themselves through controlled recursive cycles while preserving architecture identity, governance constraints, and historical lineage.

Self-evolution remains bounded by validated continuity.

## Governance Domains

- recursive adaptation governance;
- intelligence refinement governance;
- transformation validation;
- architecture preservation;
- authority preservation;
- evolutionary constraint management;
- continuity governance;
- transition control.
