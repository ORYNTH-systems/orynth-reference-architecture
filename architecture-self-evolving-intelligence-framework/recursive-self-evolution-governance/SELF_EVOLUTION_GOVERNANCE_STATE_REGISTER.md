Define the registry structure used to track recursive self-evolution governance states, transformation decisions, validation conditions, and lineage.

Governance states preserve relationships between intelligence evolution conditions, architecture constraints, recursive decisions, and resulting evolved configurations.

- intelligence evolution state;
- architecture context;
- transformation condition;
- recursive evaluation;
- transition decision;
- validation status;
- historical lineage.
