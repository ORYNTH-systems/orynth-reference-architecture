# Succession Management Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Continuity Management Operations

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH maintains continuity across future maintainers, contributors, and architectural stewards.

---

## Succession Principle

Architectural continuity requires transfer of understanding, not only transfer of ownership.

---

## Succession Domains

### Stewardship Transfer

Defines:

- responsibilities;
- authority;
- decision processes.

---

### Knowledge Transfer

Defines:

- documentation;
- historical records;
- learning pathways.

---

### Governance Transfer

Defines:

- amendment authority;
- review processes;
- preservation rules.

---

### Technical Transfer

Defines:

- implementation knowledge;
- operational procedures;
- dependency awareness.

---

## Succession Lifecycle


IDENTIFY

↓

PREPARE

↓

TRANSFER

↓

VALIDATE

↓

CONTINUE


---

## Succession Invariant

A successor inherits responsibility for continuity, not merely access to artifacts.
