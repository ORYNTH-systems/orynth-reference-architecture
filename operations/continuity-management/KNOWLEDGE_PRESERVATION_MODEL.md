# Knowledge Preservation Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Continuity Management Operations

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH preserves accumulated knowledge and architectural understanding.

---

## Knowledge Principle

Knowledge must remain accessible, understandable, and reconstructable.

---

## Preservation Domains

### Technical Knowledge

Preserves:

- specifications;
- implementations;
- validation methods.

---

### Architectural Knowledge

Preserves:

- design rationale;
- relationships;
- governing principles.

---

### Historical Knowledge

Preserves:

- decisions;
- milestones;
- changes.

---

### Research Knowledge

Preserves:

- discoveries;
- publications;
- evidence.

---

## Knowledge Lifecycle


CREATE

↓

DOCUMENT

↓

VALIDATE

↓

ARCHIVE

↓

TRANSMIT


---

## Preservation Invariant

Loss of knowledge must not prevent reconstruction of architectural intent.
