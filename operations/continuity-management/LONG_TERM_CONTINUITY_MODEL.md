# Long Term Continuity Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Continuity Management Operations

---

## Purpose

Define the mechanisms that preserve ORYNTH architectural continuity over extended periods of time.

---

## Continuity Principle

A persistent architecture requires preservation of identity, knowledge, governance, and evidence.

---

## Continuity Domains

### Architectural Continuity

Preserves:

- foundational principles;
- architectural invariants;
- system relationships.

---

### Historical Continuity

Preserves:

- development lineage;
- decision history;
- evolution records.

---

### Operational Continuity

Preserves:

- maintenance capability;
- release processes;
- operational knowledge.

---

### Ecosystem Continuity

Preserves:

- relationships;
- integrations;
- adoption pathways.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


PRESERVE

↓

VERIFY

↓

TRANSFER

↓

MAINTAIN

↓

EVOLVE


---

## Continuity Invariant

Future evolution must remain traceable to prior architectural identity.
