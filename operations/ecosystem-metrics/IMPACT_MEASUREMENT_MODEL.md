# Impact Measurement Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Ecosystem Metrics Operations

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates long-term effects and outcomes.

---

## Impact Principle

Impact must be evaluated through meaningful changes in capability, understanding, and system performance.

---

## Impact Domains

### Technical Impact

Measures:

- improved system capability;
- reliability;
- execution quality.

---

### Research Impact

Measures:

- knowledge contribution;
- new discoveries;
- theoretical advancement.

---

### Institutional Impact

Measures:

- organizational improvement;
- governance effectiveness;
- adoption outcomes.

---

### Civilization Impact

Measures:

- broader system effects;
- resilience;
- long-term value.

---

## Impact Evaluation Lifecycle


DEFINE OBJECTIVE

↓

COLLECT EVIDENCE

↓

ANALYZE RESULT

↓

VALIDATE FINDING

↓

RECORD IMPACT


---

## Impact Invariant

Impact measurement must preserve context, evidence, and causal understanding.
