# Adoption Metrics Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Ecosystem Metrics Operations

---

## Purpose

Define how adoption of ORYNTH architectures is evaluated.

---

## Adoption Principle

Adoption represents meaningful integration, not simple usage volume.

---

## Adoption Domains

### Technical Adoption

Measures:

- implementations;
- integrations;
- system usage.

---

### Institutional Adoption

Measures:

- organizational engagement;
- partnerships;
- operational incorporation.

---

### Research Adoption

Measures:

- citations;
- publications;
- knowledge exchange.

---

### Community Adoption

Measures:

- contributors;
- participation;
- educational engagement.

---

## Adoption Lifecycle


AWARENESS

↓

EXPLORATION

↓

IMPLEMENTATION

↓

INTEGRATION

↓

SUSTAINED USE


---

## Adoption Invariant

Adoption must preserve the intended purpose and integrity of the architecture.
