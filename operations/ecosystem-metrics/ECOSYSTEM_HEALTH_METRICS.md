# Ecosystem Health Metrics

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Ecosystem Metrics Operations

---

## Purpose

Define measurable indicators for evaluating the health of the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Metrics Principle

Ecosystem growth must be evaluated through capability, stability, participation, and continuity.

---

## Health Metric Domains

### Architectural Health

Measures:

- system integrity;
- dependency stability;
- governance alignment.

---

### Ecosystem Connectivity

Measures:

- active relationships;
- integrations;
- collaboration pathways.

---

### Operational Health

Measures:

- maintenance activity;
- release consistency;
- validation practices.

---

### Knowledge Health

Measures:

- documentation quality;
- research continuity;
- information preservation.

---

## Health Evaluation Model


MEASURE

↓

COMPARE

↓

## Health Invariant

Metrics provide awareness without reducing architecture to single measurements.
