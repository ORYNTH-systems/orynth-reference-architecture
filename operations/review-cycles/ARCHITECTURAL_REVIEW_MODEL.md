# Architectural Review Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

---

## Purpose

Define the recurring review process used to preserve architectural quality and alignment.

---

## Review Principle

Architectures require deliberate examination to ensure continued alignment with their original purpose.

---

## Review Domains

### Structural Review

Evaluates:

- repository organization;
- component relationships;
- architecture boundaries.

---

### Governance Review

Evaluates:

- authority models;
- amendment records;
- decision processes.

---

### Operational Review

Evaluates:

- maintenance activities;
- release processes;
- system health.

---

### Evolution Review

Evaluates:

- proposed changes;
- successor relationships;
- long-term direction.

---

## Review Lifecycle


SCHEDULE

↓

↓

↓

---

## Review Invariant

Continuous review preserves architectural coherence over time.
