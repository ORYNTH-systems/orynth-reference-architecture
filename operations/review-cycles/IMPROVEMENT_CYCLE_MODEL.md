# Improvement Cycle Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Review Cycle Operations

---

## Purpose

Define the controlled process for improving ORYNTH systems.

---

## Improvement Principle

Evolution should be deliberate, evidence-based, and compatible with architectural identity.

---

## Improvement Lifecycle


OBSERVE

↓

IDENTIFY OPPORTUNITY

↓

PROPOSE CHANGE

↓

VALIDATE IMPACT

↓

IMPLEMENT

↓

REVIEW RESULT


---

## Improvement Types

### Corrective Improvement

Addresses:

- defects;
- inconsistencies;
- degradation.

---

### Adaptive Improvement

Addresses:

- new requirements;
- changing environments;
- ecosystem needs.

---

### Evolutionary Improvement

Addresses:

- new capabilities;
- future architecture pathways.

---

## Improvement Invariant

Improvement must increase capability while preserving continuity.
