# Periodic Assessment Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Review Cycle Operations

---

## Purpose

Define the cadence and structure for recurring architecture assessments.

---

## Assessment Principle

Regular evaluation enables early detection of degradation and opportunities for improvement.

---

## Assessment Categories

### Health Assessment

Measures:

- system condition;
- integrity;
- stability.

---

### Maturity Assessment

Measures:

- capability growth;
- governance maturity;
- operational readiness.

---

### Ecosystem Assessment

Measures:

- adoption;
- integration;
- external relationships.

---

## Assessment Cadence


PERIODIC REVIEW

↓

MEASURE STATE

↓

COMPARE BASELINE

↓

IDENTIFY CHANGE

↓

UPDATE RECORD


---

## Assessment Invariant

Assessment creates awareness without forcing unnecessary change.
