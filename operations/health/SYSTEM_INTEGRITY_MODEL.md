# System Integrity Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Architecture Health Operations

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH verifies that systems remain internally coherent.

---

## Integrity Principle

System integrity exists when architecture, governance, implementation, and evidence remain aligned.

---

## Integrity Domains

### Identity Integrity

Ensures:

- system purpose remains defined;
- architectural boundaries remain clear.

---

### Dependency Integrity

Ensures:

- relationships remain documented;
- changes do not create hidden coupling.

---

### Evidence Integrity

Ensures:

- decisions are traceable;
- history remains preserved.

---

### Governance Integrity

Ensures:

- authority remains aligned;
- amendments follow process.

---

## Integrity Validation


COMPARE

↓

VERIFY

↓

RECONSTRUCT

↓

CONFIRM


---

## Integrity Invariant

No system change should remove the ability to understand why the system exists.
