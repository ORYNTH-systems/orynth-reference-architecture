# Maturity Assessment Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Architecture Health Operations

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evaluates architectural maturity over time.

---

## Maturity Principle

Maturity is measured by capability, stability, governance, and continuity.

---

## Maturity Levels

### Level 1 — Defined

Characteristics:

- architecture documented;
- purpose established.

---

### Level 2 — Governed

Characteristics:

- decision processes exist;
- change is controlled.

---

### Level 3 — Operational

Characteristics:

- systems maintained;
- validation performed.

---

### Level 4 — Ecosystem

Characteristics:

- external adoption;
- integration pathways.

---

### Level 5 — Continuity

Characteristics:

- long-term preservation;
- successor readiness.

---

## Assessment Cycle


MEASURE

↓

REVIEW

↓

IMPROVE

↓

REASSESS


---

## Maturity Invariant

Growth must increase capability without reducing coherence.
