# Architecture Health Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Architecture Health Operations

---

## Purpose

Define the operational model for evaluating the health and continuity of ORYNTH architectures.

---

## Health Principle

A healthy architecture preserves:

- identity;
- integrity;
- dependency clarity;
- operational capability;
- evolutionary stability.

---

## Health Dimensions

### Structural Health

Evaluates:

- repository organization;
- component relationships;
- architectural boundaries.

---

### Governance Health

Evaluates:

- decision authority;
- amendment processes;
- stewardship alignment.

---

### Operational Health

Evaluates:

- execution capability;
- maintenance readiness;
- system reliability.

---

### Evolution Health

Evaluates:

- controlled change;
- compatibility;
- future adaptability.

---

OBSERVE

↓

## Health Invariant

Architectural evolution must preserve system identity.
