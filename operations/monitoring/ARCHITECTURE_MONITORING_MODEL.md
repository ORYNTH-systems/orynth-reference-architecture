# Architecture Monitoring Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Monitoring Operations

---

## Purpose

Define continuous observation mechanisms for maintaining architectural awareness.

---

## Monitoring Principle

Architectures require visibility into their current state to preserve continuity.

---

## Monitoring Domains

### Structural Monitoring

Observes:

- repository organization;
- component relationships;
- architecture boundaries.

---

### Governance Monitoring

Observes:

- decision records;
- amendments;
- authority alignment.

---

### Operational Monitoring

Observes:

- system activity;
- release processes;
- maintenance state.

---

### Evolution Monitoring

Observes:

- architectural changes;
- compatibility;
- successor relationships.

---

## Monitoring Cycle
OBSERVE

↓

COMPARE

↓

IDENTIFY CHANGE

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

RESPOND


---

## Monitoring Invariant

Unobserved change creates unmanaged risk.
