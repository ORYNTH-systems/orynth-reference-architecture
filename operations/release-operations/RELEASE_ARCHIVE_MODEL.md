# Release Archive Model

Status:

INITIALIZATION

Classification:

ORYNTH Operational Stewardship Layer

Domain:

Release Operations

---

## Purpose

Define preservation of ORYNTH release history.

---

## Archive Principle

Every release remains part of the permanent architectural record.

---

## Archive Components

### Version Records

Preserve:

- release identifiers;
- tags;
- milestones.

---

### Evidence Records

Preserve:

- validation results;
- decisions;
- supporting artifacts.

---

### Historical Records

Preserve:

- evolution path;
- previous states;
- architectural lineage.

---

## Archive Lifecycle


CREATE

↓

VERIFY

↓

STORE

↓

REFERENCE

↓

PRESERVE


---

## Archive Invariant

Past releases remain accessible as evidence of architectural evolution.
