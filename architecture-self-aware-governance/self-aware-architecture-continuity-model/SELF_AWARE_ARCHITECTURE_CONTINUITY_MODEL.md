# Self-Aware Architecture Continuity Model

Version:

## Purpose

Define the framework used to preserve ORYNTH architecture continuity through self-aware governance, reflective analysis, integrity assessment, and controlled evolution.

---

## Continuity Principle

Self-aware architecture continuity ensures that ORYNTH maintains recognition of its architectural identity across changing states, adaptations, and evolutionary transitions.

Continuity preserves identity through transformation.

---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- architecture identity preservation;
- historical state relationships;
- governance continuity;
- semantic continuity;
- provenance preservation;
- transition awareness;
- integrity maintenance;
- future evolution readiness.
