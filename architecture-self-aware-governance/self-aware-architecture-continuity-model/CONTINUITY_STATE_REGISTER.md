# Continuity State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track continuity states, identity conditions, evolution transitions, and validation outcomes.

---

## State Principle

Continuity states preserve relationships between historical architecture conditions, present states, transformation events, and future validated configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- continuity identity;
- historical state reference;
- current architecture state;
- transition record;
- integrity condition;
- validation status;
- future state relationship;
- lineage preservation.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE CONTINUITY RECORD

↓

CAPTURE REFERENCE STATE

↓

COMPARE ARCHITECTURE CONDITIONS

↓

ANALYZE TRANSITION

↓

VALIDATE CONTINUITY

↓

REGISTER CONTINUITY STATE

↓

PRESERVE ARCHITECTURE LINEAGE


---

## Principle

A continuity state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH architecture evolution and preserved identity across changing states.
