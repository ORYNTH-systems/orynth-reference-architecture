# Integrity Assessment Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track integrity assessments, evaluated conditions, validation results, and continuity states.

---

## State Principle

Integrity states preserve relationships between architecture conditions, assessment methods, detected conditions, validation outcomes, and resulting integrity classifications.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- assessment identity;
- architecture state;
- evaluation criteria;
- observed condition;
- integrity classification;
- validation evidence;
- corrective status;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE ASSESSMENT RECORD

↓

CAPTURE ARCHITECTURE CONDITION

↓

APPLY INTEGRITY EVALUATION

↓

RECORD OBSERVATIONS

↓

VALIDATE ASSESSMENT

↓

REGISTER INTEGRITY STATE

↓

PRESERVE ASSESSMENT HISTORY


---

## Principle

An integrity assessment register maintains traceability between ORYNTH architecture conditions and their verified integrity states.
