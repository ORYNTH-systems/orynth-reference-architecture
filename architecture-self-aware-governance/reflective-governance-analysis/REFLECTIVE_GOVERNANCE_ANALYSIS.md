# Reflective Governance Analysis

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to analyze ORYNTH governance conditions through reflective evaluation of architecture states, decisions, relationships, and outcomes.

---

## Reflective Governance Principle

Reflective governance analysis enables ORYNTH to evaluate its own governance processes by comparing intended architecture conditions, observed states, and resulting outcomes.

Reflection enables continuous governance refinement.

---

## Reflection Domains

Includes:

- governance decisions;
- architecture state changes;
- synthesis outcomes;
- transition effectiveness;
- constraint alignment;
- semantic consistency;
- validation results;
- evolutionary improvement.

---

ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

## Principle

Reflective Governance Analysis enables ORYNTH to improve governance capability through structured self-evaluation while preserving architectural coherence and continuity.
