## Purpose

Define how future ORYNTH civilization-scale architecture evolves through successive generations.

---

## Evolution Principle

Civilization evolution records preserve generational advancement, ecosystem transformation, architecture expansion, governance refinement, and long-term continuity.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- generational architecture;
- ecosystem expansion;
- intelligence advancement;
- capability convergence;
- governance evolution;
- future civilization models.

---

CAPTURE GENERATIONAL CHANGE

↓

↓

↓

REFINE FUTURE ARCHITECTURE

↓

VALIDATE CONTINUITY

↓

## Principle

A civilization evolution record ensures ORYNTH future generations remain connected, traceable, and governed by the foundational architecture principles established through prior evolution cycles.
