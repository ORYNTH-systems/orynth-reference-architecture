# Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track autonomous intelligence states, observations, correlations, and ecosystem conditions.

---

## State Principle

Intelligence states preserve relationships between ecosystem observations, analytical processes, architectural relevance, and resulting intelligence conditions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- intelligence identity;
- observation source;
- signal condition;
- correlation state;
- interpretation status;
- governance condition;
- continuity record.

---

↓

CLASSIFY SIGNAL STATE

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURAL IMPACT

↓

UPDATE INTELLIGENCE MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD

An intelligence state register maintains traceability between autonomous ecosystem awareness and ORYNTH evolutionary operations.
