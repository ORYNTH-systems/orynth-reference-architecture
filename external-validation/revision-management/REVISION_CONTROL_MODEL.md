# Revision Control Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH revisions are proposed, evaluated, approved, and recorded.

---

## Revision Principle

Change must preserve architectural continuity while allowing controlled improvement.

---

## Revision Categories

### Documentation Revision

Updates:

- clarification;
- formatting;
- explanation.

---

### Structural Revision

Updates:

- architecture relationships;
- component organization;
- system boundaries.

---

### Constitutional Revision

Updates:

- foundational principles;
- governing rules;
- architectural invariants.

---

## Revision Lifecycle


PROPOSE

↓

ASSESS

↓

REVIEW

↓

APPROVE

↓

IMPLEMENT

↓

RECORD


---

## Invariant

No revision bypasses governance and continuity requirements.
