# Change Evaluation Process

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how proposed changes are evaluated before integration.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

### Necessity

Does the change address a validated need?

---

### Impact

Does the change affect:

- dependencies;
- architecture;
- governance;
- external applications?

---

### Continuity

Does the change preserve system identity?

---

## Evaluation Lifecycle


SUBMIT CHANGE

↓

ANALYZE IMPACT

↓

REVIEW CONSEQUENCES

↓

DECIDE ACTION

↓

DOCUMENT RESULT


---

## Principle

Change is accepted through evidence and evaluation, not uncontrolled expansion.
