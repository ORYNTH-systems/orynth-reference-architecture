# Amendment Tracking Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how significant architectural amendments are recorded.

---

## Amendment Record

Includes:

- amendment identifier;
- proposal source;
- affected components;
- reasoning;
- decision;
- implementation record.

---

## Amendment Classes

### Minor Amendment

Limited impact.

---

### Major Amendment

Affects architecture relationships.

---

### Constitutional Amendment

Affects foundational identity.

---

## Amendment Lifecycle


PROPOSAL

↓

REVIEW

↓

DECISION

↓

IMPLEMENTATION

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Principle

Every significant change remains historically traceable.
