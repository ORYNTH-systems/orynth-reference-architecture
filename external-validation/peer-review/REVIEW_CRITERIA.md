# Review Criteria

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define evaluation criteria for external reviewers.

---

## Criteria

### Architecture

Evaluate:

- organization;
- relationships;
- consistency.

---

### Methodology

Evaluate:

- definitions;
- reasoning;
- validation approach.

---

### Evidence

Evaluate:

- documentation;
- traceability;
- reproducibility.

---

### Scope

Evaluate:

- appropriate claims;
- boundaries;
- limitations.

---

## Principle

Review quality depends on clear evaluation criteria.
