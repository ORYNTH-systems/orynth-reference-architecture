# Engagement Assessment Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Evaluate quality and relevance of external engagements.

---

## Assessment Areas

### Alignment

Measures:

- shared objectives;
- architectural compatibility.

---

### Capability

Measures:

- expertise;
- resources;
- contribution potential.

---

### Continuity

Measures:

- sustained relationship value;
- future collaboration pathways.

---

## Principle

Engagement quality is measured by alignment and contribution, not quantity.
