# Stakeholder Feedback Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Preserve structured feedback from external stakeholders.

---

## Record Structure

Includes:

- stakeholder;
- engagement date;
- discussion scope;
- observations;
- recommendations;
- disposition.

---

## Feedback Categories

- technical;
- institutional;
- strategic;
- operational.

---

## Principle

Stakeholder feedback becomes part of the documented evolution record.
