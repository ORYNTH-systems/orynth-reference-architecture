# Institutional Review Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the process for institutional evaluation of ORYNTH architecture.

---

## Review Participants

May include:

- research institutions;
- industry organizations;
- government entities;
- foundations;
- domain specialists.

---

## Review Areas

Includes:

- architectural relevance;
- research alignment;
- implementation feasibility;
- collaboration opportunities.

---

## Review Lifecycle


INTRODUCTION

↓

ASSESSMENT

↓

DISCUSSION

↓

FEEDBACK

↓

RECORD


---

## Principle

Institutional review evaluates alignment and opportunity without assuming adoption.
