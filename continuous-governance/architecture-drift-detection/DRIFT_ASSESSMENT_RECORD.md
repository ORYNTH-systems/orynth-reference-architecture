# Drift Assessment Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record used to evaluate identified architectural deviations.

---

## Assessment Principle

Drift assessment distinguishes acceptable evolution from unintended divergence.

---

## Assessment Domains

Includes:

- detected change;
- affected component;
- architectural impact;
- governance impact;
- required response.

---

## Assessment Lifecycle


IDENTIFY VARIANCE

↓

CLASSIFY DRIFT

↓

EVALUATE IMPACT

↓

DETERMINE RESPONSE

↓

DOCUMENT DECISION

↓

UPDATE RECORD


---

## Principle

A drift assessment record creates traceability between architectural change and governance decisions.
