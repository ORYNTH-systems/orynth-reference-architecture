# Architecture Drift Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to identify, evaluate, and manage architectural drift within ORYNTH.

---

## Drift Principle

Architecture drift occurs when system evolution creates divergence from defined architectural identity, requirements, governance constraints, or intended relationships.

---

## Drift Domains

Includes:

- structural drift;
- terminology drift;
- governance drift;
- dependency drift;
- capability drift.

---

## Drift Detection Lifecycle


ESTABLISH BASELINE

↓

OBSERVE CHANGE

↓

COMPARE CONDITIONS

↓

IDENTIFY VARIANCE

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

RECORD RESPONSE


---

## Principle

Architecture drift detection preserves continuity while allowing controlled evolution.
