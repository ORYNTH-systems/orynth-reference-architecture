# Long Term Operations Readiness

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define readiness conditions for sustained ORYNTH ecosystem operations.

---

## Readiness Principle

Long-term operations require maintained governance, current validation, preserved knowledge, and controlled architectural evolution.

---

## Readiness Domains

Includes:

- governance continuity;
- validation continuity;
- research continuity;
- documentation continuity;
- architecture continuity.

---

## Operations Lifecycle


OPERATE SYSTEM

↓

MONITOR CONDITION

↓

REVIEW CHANGE

↓

VALIDATE IMPACT

↓

UPDATE KNOWLEDGE

↓

EVOLVE ARCHITECTURE


---

## Principle

Long-term readiness ensures ORYNTH can continue development without losing architectural continuity.
