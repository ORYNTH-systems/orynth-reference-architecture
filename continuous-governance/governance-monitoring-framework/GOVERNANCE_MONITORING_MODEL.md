# Governance Monitoring Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the continuous monitoring framework used to observe ORYNTH governance conditions, responsibilities, and structural continuity.

---

## Governance Monitoring Principle

Continuous governance monitoring preserves awareness of authority structures, stewardship responsibilities, decision pathways, and ecosystem alignment.

---

## Monitoring Domains

Includes:

- governance state;
- stewardship continuity;
- responsibility alignment;
- decision traceability;
- constitutional consistency.

---

## Monitoring Lifecycle


IDENTIFY GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

OBSERVE CONDITIONS

↓

ASSESS ALIGNMENT

↓

RECORD STATUS

↓

REVIEW CHANGES

↓

MAINTAIN CONTINUITY


---

## Principle

Governance monitoring provides continuous visibility into the condition and evolution of ORYNTH governance structures.
