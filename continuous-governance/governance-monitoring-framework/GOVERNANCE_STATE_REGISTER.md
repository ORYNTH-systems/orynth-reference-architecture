# Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for tracking governance states across ORYNTH evolution.

---

## State Register Principle

Governance states preserve the relationship between current conditions, historical transitions, and future governance requirements.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- active governance state;
- transition state;
- review state;
- validated state;
- historical state.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE STATE RECORD

↓

DEFINE CONDITION

↓

ASSESS GOVERNANCE STATUS

↓

DOCUMENT TRANSITION

↓

VERIFY CONTINUITY

↓

ARCHIVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A governance state register maintains traceability across changing ecosystem conditions.
