# Revalidation Schedule Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure used to track recurring validation reviews and renewal activities.

---

## Schedule Principle

Revalidation schedules preserve awareness of when systems, evidence, and architectural assumptions require renewed examination.

---

## Schedule Domains

Includes:

- validation cycles;
- review intervals;
- evidence checks;
- architecture assessments;
- governance reviews.

---

## Schedule Lifecycle


DEFINE REVIEW TARGET

↓

ESTABLISH INTERVAL

↓

PERFORM ASSESSMENT

↓

RECORD OUTCOME

↓

SCHEDULE NEXT REVIEW

↓

MAINTAIN HISTORY


---

## Principle

A revalidation schedule provides continuity between completed validation and future assurance activities.
