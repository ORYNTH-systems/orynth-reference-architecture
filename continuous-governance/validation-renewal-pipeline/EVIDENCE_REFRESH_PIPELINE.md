# Evidence Refresh Pipeline

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how validation evidence remains current, traceable, and connected to system state.

---

## Evidence Refresh Principle

Evidence refresh maintains the relationship between system conditions, validation claims, and supporting records.

---

## Evidence Domains

Includes:

- technical evidence;
- operational evidence;
- governance evidence;
- documentation evidence;
- historical evidence.

---

## Evidence Refresh Lifecycle


IDENTIFY EVIDENCE

↓

CHECK CURRENT STATE

↓

VERIFY CONTINUITY

↓

REFRESH RECORDS

↓

LINK NEW EVIDENCE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

An evidence refresh pipeline prevents validation records from becoming disconnected from evolving systems.
