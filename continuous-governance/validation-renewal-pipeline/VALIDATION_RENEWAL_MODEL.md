# Validation Renewal Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the process used to renew validation confidence across ORYNTH systems, frameworks, and architectural records.

---

## Validation Renewal Principle

Validation renewal preserves assurance over time by periodically reviewing conditions, evidence, requirements, and system alignment.

---

## Renewal Domains

Includes:

- architecture validation;
- governance validation;
- operational validation;
- evidence validation;
- documentation validation.

---

## Renewal Lifecycle


IDENTIFY VALIDATION REQUIREMENT

↓

REVIEW CURRENT EVIDENCE

↓

ASSESS SYSTEM CONDITION

↓

PERFORM REVALIDATION

↓

UPDATE VALIDATION STATE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Validation renewal ensures that confidence remains connected to current system conditions.
