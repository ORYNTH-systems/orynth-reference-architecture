## Purpose

Define the record structure for tracking knowledge growth across ORYNTH research and architecture evolution.

---

## Knowledge Principle

Knowledge expansion preserves discoveries, relationships, references, and emerging understanding across ecosystem development.

---

## Knowledge Domains

Includes:

- research findings;
- conceptual models;
- technical discoveries;
- external references;
- architectural insights.

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY
