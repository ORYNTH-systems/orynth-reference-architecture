# Research Evaluation Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the evaluation structure used to assess proposed research directions.

---

## Evaluation Principle

Research evaluation determines relevance, compatibility, evidence requirements, and potential ecosystem impact.

---

## Evaluation Domains

Includes:

- research significance;
- architectural compatibility;
- validation requirements;
- implementation relevance;
- future potential.

---

## Evaluation Lifecycle


RECEIVE RESEARCH INPUT

↓

ANALYZE CONTEXT

↓

ASSESS VALUE

↓

REVIEW ALIGNMENT

↓

DOCUMENT DECISION

↓

UPDATE RESEARCH RECORD


---

## Principle

Research evaluation converts exploratory concepts into structured opportunities for ecosystem advancement.
