# Research Intake Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the process used to capture, organize, and evaluate future research opportunities within ORYNTH.

---

## Research Intake Principle

Research intake preserves emerging ideas, external discoveries, internal investigations, and potential extensions while maintaining alignment with ecosystem objectives.

---

## Intake Domains

Includes:

- theoretical research;
- technical research;
- architectural research;
- validation research;
- interdisciplinary exploration.

---

## Intake Lifecycle


IDENTIFY RESEARCH OPPORTUNITY

↓

CAPTURE CONTEXT

↓

CLASSIFY DOMAIN

↓

EVALUATE RELEVANCE

↓

DETERMINE PATHWAY

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A structured research intake model enables exploration while maintaining architectural coherence.
