Version:

## Purpose

Define the continuing assurance model for ORYNTH ecosystem maturity.

---

## Assurance Principle

Long-term assurance requires recurring review, evidence preservation, reproducibility, and institutional readiness.

---

## Assurance Domains

Includes:

- independent assessment;
- continuous verification;
- evidence continuity;
- operational scaling;
- ecosystem trust.
