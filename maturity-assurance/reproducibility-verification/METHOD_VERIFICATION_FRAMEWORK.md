# Method Verification Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework for verifying that ORYNTH methods maintain clarity, consistency, and repeatable application.

---

## Method Verification Principle

Verified methods require defined assumptions, documented procedures, measurable conditions, and reviewable outcomes.

---

## Verification Domains

Includes:

- methodology definition;
- assumptions;
- procedures;
- measurements;
- interpretation criteria.

---

## Verification Lifecycle


DEFINE METHOD

↓

DOCUMENT ASSUMPTIONS

↓

VERIFY PROCEDURE

↓

ASSESS RESULTS

↓

REVIEW CONSISTENCY

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Method verification maintains confidence that processes remain understandable and consistently applicable over time.
