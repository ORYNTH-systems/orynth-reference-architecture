# Reproducibility Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to ensure ORYNTH methods, records, and validation processes can be independently examined and reproduced.

---

## Reproducibility Principle

Reproducibility requires sufficient documentation, preserved inputs, defined procedures, and traceable outputs.

---

## Reproducibility Domains

Includes:

- methodology;
- system configuration;
- input conditions;
- execution procedures;
- validation evidence.

---

## Reproducibility Lifecycle


DEFINE METHOD

↓

PRESERVE CONDITIONS

↓

DOCUMENT PROCEDURE

↓

REPEAT EXECUTION

↓

COMPARE RESULTS

↓

RECORD VALIDATION


---

## Principle

A reproducible system enables independent verification of claims, processes, and outcomes.
