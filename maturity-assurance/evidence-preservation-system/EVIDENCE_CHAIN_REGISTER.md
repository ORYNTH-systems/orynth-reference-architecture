# Evidence Chain Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure used to maintain relationships between claims, artifacts, sources, and validation outcomes.

---

## Evidence Chain Principle

An evidence chain preserves the path from originating information through processing, validation, and final record.

---

## Evidence Chain Domains

Includes:

- source identification;
- artifact relationship;
- transformation history;
- validation status;
- ownership continuity.

---

## Evidence Chain Lifecycle


IDENTIFY SOURCE

↓

LINK ARTIFACT

↓

TRACE TRANSFORMATION

↓

VERIFY CONDITION

↓

RECORD RELATIONSHIP

↓

PRESERVE CHAIN


---

## Principle

Evidence chain registration creates traceability across the complete lifecycle of information.
