# Artifact Retention Record

Version:

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH artifacts remain preserved and accessible across future ecosystem evolution.

---

## Retention Principle

Artifact retention preserves important records beyond immediate use while maintaining accessibility and historical context.

---

## Retention Domains

Includes:

- documentation artifacts;
- software artifacts;
- validation artifacts;
- research artifacts;
- release artifacts.
