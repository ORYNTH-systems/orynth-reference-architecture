# Evidence Preservation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to preserve ORYNTH evidence artifacts, validation records, and historical documentation.

---

## Evidence Preservation Principle

Evidence preservation maintains the identity, origin, context, and integrity of records required for review, verification, and future analysis.

---

## Preservation Domains

Includes:

- technical evidence;
- validation evidence;
- governance evidence;
- research evidence;
- historical evidence.

---

## Preservation Lifecycle


IDENTIFY EVIDENCE

↓

CAPTURE SOURCE

↓

DOCUMENT CONTEXT

↓

VERIFY INTEGRITY

↓

STORE RECORD

↓

MAINTAIN HISTORY


---

## Principle

Evidence preservation ensures that ecosystem claims remain connected to traceable supporting records.
