# Audit Findings Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how independent audit observations, findings, and resolutions are recorded.

---

## Findings Principle

Audit findings preserve observations, evidence references, impact assessments, and resolution pathways.

---

## Finding Domains

Includes:

- identified condition;
- supporting evidence;
- severity assessment;
- corrective action;
- verification status.

---

## Findings Lifecycle


RECORD OBSERVATION

↓

ATTACH EVIDENCE

↓

CLASSIFY FINDING

↓

DEFINE RESPONSE

↓

VERIFY RESOLUTION

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

Audit findings records provide a durable connection between review activity and ecosystem improvement.
