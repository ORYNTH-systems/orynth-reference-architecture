# Independent Audit Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used for independent examination of ORYNTH architecture, systems, processes, and validation records.

---

## Audit Principle

Independent auditing provides external assessment of system condition, evidence quality, governance alignment, and architectural integrity.

---

## Audit Domains

Includes:

- architectural review;
- governance review;
- technical review;
- validation review;
- documentation review.

---

## Audit Lifecycle


DEFINE AUDIT SCOPE

↓

COLLECT EVIDENCE

↓

PERFORM INDEPENDENT REVIEW

↓

DOCUMENT FINDINGS

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

PRESERVE AUDIT RECORD


---

## Principle

An independent audit framework enables objective evaluation while preserving traceability and accountability.
