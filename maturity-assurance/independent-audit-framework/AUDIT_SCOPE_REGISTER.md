# Audit Scope Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the scope boundaries for independent audits performed against ORYNTH ecosystem components.

---

## Scope Principle

Audit scope establishes what is examined, what evidence is required, and what conditions determine review completeness.

---

## Scope Domains

Includes:

- architecture components;
- implementation records;
- validation artifacts;
- governance structures;
- operational processes.

---

## Scope Lifecycle


IDENTIFY AUDIT TARGET

↓

DEFINE REVIEW BOUNDARY

↓

SELECT EVIDENCE

↓

EXECUTE AUDIT

↓

DOCUMENT RESULT

↓

ARCHIVE SCOPE RECORD


---

## Principle

A defined audit scope ensures reviews remain focused, reproducible, and interpretable.
