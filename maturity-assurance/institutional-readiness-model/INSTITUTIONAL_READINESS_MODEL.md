# Institutional Readiness Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to evaluate ORYNTH readiness for institutional adoption, collaboration, and sustained operation.

---

## Readiness Principle

Institutional readiness requires demonstrated structure, documented processes, validated capabilities, governance clarity, and operational continuity.

---

## Readiness Domains

Includes:

- organizational readiness;
- technical readiness;
- governance readiness;
- documentation readiness;
- collaboration readiness.

---

## Readiness Lifecycle


ASSESS REQUIREMENTS

↓

REVIEW CAPABILITIES

↓

VERIFY STRUCTURE

↓

EVALUATE ADOPTION CONDITIONS

↓

DOCUMENT READINESS

↓

MAINTAIN STATE


---

## Principle

Institutional readiness establishes the conditions required for responsible ecosystem adoption.
