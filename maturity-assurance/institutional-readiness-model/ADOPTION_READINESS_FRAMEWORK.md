# Adoption Readiness Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH adoption readiness is evaluated across potential institutional environments.

---

## Adoption Principle

Adoption requires alignment between system capability, organizational needs, governance expectations, and operational requirements.

---

## Adoption Domains

Includes:

- stakeholder alignment;
- implementation readiness;
- resource requirements;
- governance compatibility;
- operational support.

---

## Adoption Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ADOPTION CONTEXT

↓

EVALUATE REQUIREMENTS

↓

ASSESS COMPATIBILITY

↓

DEFINE IMPLEMENTATION PATH

↓

VERIFY READINESS

↓

REVIEW OUTCOMES

## Principle

A structured adoption framework enables measured expansion without compromising system integrity.
