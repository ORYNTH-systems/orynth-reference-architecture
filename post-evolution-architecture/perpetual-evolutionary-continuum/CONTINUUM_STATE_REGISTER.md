# Continuum State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track continuous evolution states, transition conditions, historical relationships, and future architecture pathways.

---

## State Principle

Continuum states preserve relationships between previous architecture states, current operational conditions, future evolution states, and validated transitions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- architecture generation;
- evolutionary condition;
- transition pathway;
- continuity relationship;
- validation status;
- future readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE CURRENT STATE

↓

IDENTIFY EVOLUTION CONDITION

↓

MAP TRANSITION PATHWAY

↓

VALIDATE CONTINUITY

↓

ESTABLISH NEW STATE

↓

PRESERVE LINEAGE RECORD


---

## Principle

A continuum state register maintains traceability across all ORYNTH evolutionary transitions.
