## Purpose

Define the framework governing continuous ORYNTH evolution beyond individual architecture releases.

---

## Continuum Principle

The perpetual evolutionary continuum preserves architectural identity while enabling continuous adaptation, refinement, expansion, and generational advancement.

Evolution occurs through governed transitions rather than uncontrolled transformation.

- architecture continuity;
- evolutionary governance;
- intelligence advancement;
- capability development;
- ecosystem adaptation;
- historical preservation;
- future state preparation.
