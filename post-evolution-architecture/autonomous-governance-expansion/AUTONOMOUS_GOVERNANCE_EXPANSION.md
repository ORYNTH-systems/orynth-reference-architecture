# Autonomous Governance Expansion

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to expand ORYNTH governance capabilities across increasingly complex autonomous ecosystem environments.

---

## Governance Principle

Autonomous governance expansion enables distributed coordination, decision accountability, and controlled evolution while preserving constitutional constraints, authority relationships, and system integrity.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- authority management;
- decision coordination;
- autonomous oversight;
- policy evolution;
- accountability structures;
- governance interoperability;
- continuity preservation.
