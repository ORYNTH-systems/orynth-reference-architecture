# Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track governance states, authority conditions, decision processes, and evolutionary governance transitions.

---

## State Principle

Governance states preserve relationships between authority structures, operational conditions, decision outcomes, and future governance requirements.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance identity;
- authority condition;
- decision state;
- oversight condition;
- policy relationship;
- continuity state.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE GOVERNANCE EVENT

↓

CLASSIFY AUTHORITY STATE

↓

ASSESS DECISION CONDITION

↓

UPDATE GOVERNANCE MODEL

↓

VERIFY ACCOUNTABILITY

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A governance state register maintains traceability between autonomous governance operations and ORYNTH constitutional alignment.
