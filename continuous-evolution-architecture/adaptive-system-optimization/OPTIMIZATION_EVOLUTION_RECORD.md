## Purpose

Define how adaptive system optimization evolves over time.

---

## Evolution Principle

Optimization evolution records preserve refinement strategies, capability improvements, architectural adjustments, and future optimization pathways.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- performance improvement;
- system refinement;
- adaptive capability;
- operational efficiency;
- future optimization models.

---

CAPTURE OPTIMIZATION CHANGE

↓

DOCUMENT SYSTEM REFINEMENT

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURAL IMPACT

↓

REFINE OPTIMIZATION MODEL

↓

VALIDATE SYSTEM IMPROVEMENT

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY

## Principle

An optimization evolution record ensures ORYNTH adaptive improvement processes remain coherent, traceable, and continuously refinable.
