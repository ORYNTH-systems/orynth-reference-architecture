# Knowledge Evolution Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to manage knowledge growth, integration, refinement, preservation, and evolutionary development across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Knowledge Evolution Principle

Knowledge evolution enables systems to acquire, organize, evaluate, refine, and preserve knowledge structures while maintaining provenance, relationships, and continuity.

---

## Knowledge Domains

Includes:

- knowledge acquisition;
- knowledge integration;
- semantic relationships;
- knowledge validation;
- knowledge refinement;
- knowledge preservation.

---

## Knowledge Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE INPUT

↓

CLASSIFY KNOWLEDGE STRUCTURE

↓

INTEGRATE KNOWLEDGE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

EVALUATE KNOWLEDGE VALIDITY

↓

REFINE KNOWLEDGE MODEL

↓

PRESERVE KNOWLEDGE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A knowledge evolution framework provides a structured foundation for continuous knowledge development while preserving traceability and architectural coherence.
