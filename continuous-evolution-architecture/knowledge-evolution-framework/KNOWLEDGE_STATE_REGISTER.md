# Knowledge State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track knowledge states, relationships, transformations, and evolutionary conditions.

---

## State Principle

Knowledge states preserve relationships between knowledge entities, sources, classifications, validation conditions, and resulting knowledge structures.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- knowledge identity;
- knowledge source;
- relationship state;
- validation condition;
- refinement status;
- historical record.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE STATE

↓

MAP KNOWLEDGE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ASSESS KNOWLEDGE CONDITION

↓

UPDATE KNOWLEDGE MODEL

↓

VERIFY CONTINUITY

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A knowledge state register maintains traceability between knowledge evolution events and their resulting architectural impact.
