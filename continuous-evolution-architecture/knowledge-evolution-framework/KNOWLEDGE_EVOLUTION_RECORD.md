Version:

## Purpose

Define how knowledge structures evolve over time.

---

## Evolution Principle

Knowledge evolution records preserve discoveries, integrations, refinements, relationship changes, and future knowledge pathways.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- knowledge expansion;
- conceptual refinement;
- relationship development;
- validation improvement;
- future knowledge synthesis.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


CAPTURE KNOWLEDGE CHANGE

↓

DOCUMENT KNOWLEDGE TRANSITION

↓

ASSESS SYSTEM IMPACT

↓

REFINE KNOWLEDGE MODEL

↓

VALIDATE INTEGRATION

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY

## Principle

A knowledge evolution record ensures ORYNTH knowledge structures remain coherent, traceable, and continuously refinable.
