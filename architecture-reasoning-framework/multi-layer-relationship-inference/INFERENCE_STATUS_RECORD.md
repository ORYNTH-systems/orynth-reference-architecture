# Inference Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for multi-layer relationship inference.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- inference identifier;
- source states;
- related architecture layers;
- relationship type;
- evidence sources;
- reasoning pathway;
- validation status.

---

## Inference Lifecycle


INFERENCE IDENTIFIED

↓

RELATIONSHIPS CAPTURED

↓

ANALYSIS PERFORMED

↓

CONCLUSION GENERATED

↓

VALIDATION COMPLETED

↓

INFERENCE PRESERVED


---

## Inference States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- MAPPING;
- ANALYZING;
- INFERRING;
- VALIDATING;
- ACCEPTED;
- REVISED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Inference Status Records preserve accountability between architectural relationships and derived reasoning outcomes.
