# Reasoning Continuity Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for reasoning continuity preservation.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- reasoning identifier;
- originating knowledge state;
- inference history;
- governance record;
- validation history;
- current reasoning state;
- future review requirements.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


REASONING RECORD CREATED

↓

SOURCE STATES LINKED

↓

INFERENCE HISTORY ATTACHED

↓

VALIDATION PRESERVED

↓

CONTINUITY REVIEWED

↓

STATUS MAINTAINED


---

## Continuity States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- PRESERVED;
- VERIFIED;
- INTEGRATED;
- CONTINUING;
- EVOLVING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Reasoning Continuity Status Records preserve accountability between prior reasoning states and future architectural intelligence.
