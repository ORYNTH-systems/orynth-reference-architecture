# Reasoning Governance Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for constitutional reasoning governance.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- reasoning identifier;
- source states;
- governing constraints;
- inference process;
- validation evidence;
- governance decision;
- final disposition.

---

## Governance Lifecycle


REASONING REVIEW INITIATED

↓

CONTEXT ANALYZED

↓

CONSTRAINTS APPLIED

↓

INFERENCE REVIEWED

↓

DECISION RECORDED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Governance States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- REVIEWING;
- CONSTRAINED;
- VALIDATING;
- APPROVED;
- INTEGRATED;
- REJECTED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Reasoning Governance Status Records preserve accountability between reasoning processes and constitutional architectural integrity.
