# Reasoning Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for reasoning states.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- reasoning identifier;
- source knowledge states;
- contextual conditions;
- inference pathway;
- conclusion;
- validation status.

---

## Reasoning Lifecycle


REASONING INITIATED

↓

INPUTS CAPTURED

↓

RELATIONSHIPS ANALYZED

↓

INFERENCE GENERATED

↓

VALIDATION PERFORMED

↓

REASONING PRESERVED


---

## Reasoning States

Possible states:

- INITIALIZED;
- REPRESENTED;
- ANALYZING;
- INFERRING;
- VALIDATING;
- ACCEPTED;
- REVISED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Reasoning Status Records preserve accountability between architectural inputs and derived conclusions.
