# Reasoning Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structural model for architectural reasoning.

---

## Reasoning Principle

Reasoning emerges through relationships between knowledge states, context, constraints, and evidence.

---

## Reasoning Model


KNOWLEDGE STATE

CONTEXT

RELATIONSHIPS

CONSTRAINTS

EVIDENCE

↓

REASONING STATE

↓

CONCLUSION OR DECISION


---

## Reasoning Categories

### Interpretive Reasoning

Determines meaning from represented states.

### Relational Reasoning

Evaluates connections between components.

### Constraint Reasoning

Evaluates permissible states and boundaries.

### Predictive Reasoning

Evaluates possible future conditions.

### Validation Reasoning

Evaluates reliability of conclusions.

---

## Reasoning Factors

Includes:

- evidence quality;
- context accuracy;
- relationship integrity;
- uncertainty;
- validation requirements.

---

## Principle

The Reasoning Model ensures conclusions emerge through structured relationships rather than isolated assumptions.
