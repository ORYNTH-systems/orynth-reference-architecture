# Operational Reporting Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH operational information is compiled, communicated, and preserved.

---

## Reporting Principle

Operational reporting converts system observations, measurements, and events into structured records that support awareness and decision-making.

---

## Reporting Domains

Includes:

- system status;
- operational performance;
- assurance activity;
- risk conditions;
- historical outcomes.

---

## Reporting Lifecycle


COLLECT INFORMATION

↓

STRUCTURE REPORT

↓

REVIEW CONTENT

↓

DISTRIBUTE FINDINGS

↓

ARCHIVE RECORD

↓

REFERENCE HISTORY


---

## Principle

Operational reporting maintains visibility across evolving system conditions.
