# Status Report Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH system status is represented and communicated.

---

## Status Principle

Status reports provide a consistent view of current conditions, changes, and operational priorities.

---

## Status Components

Includes:

- current state;
- recent changes;
- active conditions;
- risks;
- required actions;
- future considerations.

---

## Status Lifecycle


CAPTURE STATE

↓

SUMMARIZE CONDITION

↓

IDENTIFY CHANGES

↓

COMMUNICATE STATUS

↓

UPDATE RECORD


---

## Principle

Clear status reporting enables coordinated awareness and response.
