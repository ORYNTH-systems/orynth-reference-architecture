# Operational History Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how operational events, decisions, and outcomes are preserved over time.

---

## History Principle

Operational history provides a reconstructable record of system evolution and activity.

---

## History Categories

Includes:

- operational events;
- system transitions;
- changes;
- decisions;
- outcomes.

---

## History Lifecycle


CAPTURE EVENT

↓

DOCUMENT CONTEXT

↓

STORE RECORD

↓

CONNECT TIMELINE

↓

REVIEW EVOLUTION

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

Historical records enable learning, accountability, and future improvement.
