# Risk Signal Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how potential risks are identified, classified, and evaluated.

---

## Risk Signal Principle

Risk signals provide early indicators of conditions that may affect system integrity or performance.

---

## Signal Categories

Includes:

- operational signals;
- integrity signals;
- dependency signals;
- continuity signals;
- change signals.

---

## Risk Signal Lifecycle


DETECT SIGNAL

↓

CLASSIFY CONDITION

↓

ASSESS SEVERITY

↓

DEFINE RESPONSE

↓

MONITOR CHANGE

↓

ARCHIVE RESULT


---

## Principle

Early risk identification enables preventative action and preserves system continuity.
