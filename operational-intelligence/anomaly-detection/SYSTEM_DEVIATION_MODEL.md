# System Deviation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how deviations from expected system behavior are represented and evaluated.

---

## Deviation Principle

A deviation is a measurable difference between observed conditions and established expectations.

---

## Deviation Categories

Includes:

- operational deviation;
- performance deviation;
- process deviation;
- architectural deviation;
- environmental deviation.

---

## Deviation Lifecycle


OBSERVE STATE

↓

COMPARE EXPECTATION

↓

IDENTIFY DIFFERENCE

↓

CLASSIFY IMPACT

↓

SELECT RESPONSE

↓

TRACK RESOLUTION


---

## Principle

Explicit deviation modeling enables controlled response rather than reactive correction.
