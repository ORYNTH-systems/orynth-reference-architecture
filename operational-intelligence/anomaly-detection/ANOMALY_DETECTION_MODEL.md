# Anomaly Detection Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH identifies unusual system conditions, evaluates deviations, and supports appropriate responses.

---

## Anomaly Principle

Anomalies represent conditions that differ from expected patterns and require contextual evaluation before action.

---

## Detection Domains

Includes:

- operational anomalies;
- performance deviations;
- integrity changes;
- behavioral shifts;
- environmental changes.

---

## Detection Lifecycle


ESTABLISH BASELINE

↓

OBSERVE CONDITION

↓

IDENTIFY DEVIATION

↓

ANALYZE CONTEXT

↓

EVALUATE RESPONSE

↓

RECORD OUTCOME


---

## Principle

Anomaly detection improves awareness by identifying conditions requiring examination.
