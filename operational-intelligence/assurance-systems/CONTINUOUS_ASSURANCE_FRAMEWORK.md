# Continuous Assurance Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how assurance activities operate continuously throughout ORYNTH system lifecycles.

---

## Continuous Assurance Principle

Assurance must remain active as systems evolve, operate, and adapt.

---

## Assurance Activities

Includes:

- monitoring;
- validation;
- review;
- evidence assessment;
- corrective action.

---

## Assurance Lifecycle


OBSERVE

↓

ASSESS

↓

VERIFY

↓

RESPOND

↓

REASSESS

↓

RECORD


---

## Principle

Continuous assurance prevents degradation by identifying changes before they become uncontrolled failures.
