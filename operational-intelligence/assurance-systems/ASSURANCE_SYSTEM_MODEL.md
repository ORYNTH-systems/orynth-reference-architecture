# Assurance System Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH systems maintain confidence through continuous evaluation, evidence preservation, and operational verification.

---

## Assurance Principle

Assurance provides ongoing confirmation that systems remain aligned with defined requirements, constraints, and operational expectations.

---

## Assurance Domains

Includes:

- operational integrity;
- architectural alignment;
- evidence validity;
- process compliance;
- system continuity.

---

## Assurance Lifecycle


DEFINE REQUIREMENTS

↓

COLLECT EVIDENCE

↓

EVALUATE CONDITION

↓

VERIFY ALIGNMENT

↓

IDENTIFY GAPS

↓

MAINTAIN ASSURANCE


---

## Principle

Assurance transforms system confidence into a continuously maintained operational property.
