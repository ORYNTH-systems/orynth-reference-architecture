# Assurance Evidence Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how evidence supporting operational assurance is collected, structured, and preserved.

---

## Evidence Principle

Assurance requires traceable evidence connecting system state, evaluation activity, and resulting conclusions.

---

## Evidence Categories

Includes:

- operational records;
- validation results;
- monitoring outputs;
- review findings;
- corrective actions.

---

## Evidence Lifecycle


COLLECT

↓

CLASSIFY

↓

VERIFY

↓

LINK TO ASSURANCE ACTIVITY

↓

STORE

↓

REFERENCE


---

## Principle

Evidence preservation enables reconstruction of assurance decisions and operational conditions.
