# Operational State Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH operational states are represented, tracked, and evaluated.

---

## State Principle

Operational states describe current conditions and support informed response.

---

## State Categories

Includes:

- normal operation;
- transitional state;
- degraded state;
- recovery state;
- unknown state.

---

## State Lifecycle


INITIALIZE

↓

OBSERVE

↓

CLASSIFY

↓

EVALUATE

↓

TRANSITION

↓

RECORD


---

## Principle

Explicit state modeling preserves awareness across operational transitions.
