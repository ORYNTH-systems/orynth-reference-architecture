# Monitoring Framework Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH systems are observed, monitored, and evaluated during operation.

---

## Monitoring Principle

Monitoring provides awareness of system conditions through structured observation, measurement, and state evaluation.

---

## Monitoring Domains

Includes:

- system activity;
- operational state;
- performance conditions;
- integrity conditions;
- environmental changes.

---

## Monitoring Lifecycle


DEFINE OBSERVATION TARGET

↓

COLLECT SIGNALS

↓

ANALYZE STATE

↓

IDENTIFY CONDITIONS

↓

RESPOND APPROPRIATELY

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

Effective monitoring creates operational awareness while maintaining traceability.
