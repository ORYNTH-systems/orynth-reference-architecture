# System Observability Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how internal system conditions become visible through observable signals.

---

## Observability Principle

A system is observable when relevant states, transitions, and outcomes can be understood through available evidence.

---

## Observability Areas

Includes:

- state visibility;
- event tracking;
- dependency awareness;
- performance visibility;
- historical reconstruction.

---

## Observability Lifecycle


IDENTIFY STATE

↓

CAPTURE SIGNAL

↓

INTERPRET CONDITION

↓

CONNECT EVIDENCE

↓

UNDERSTAND SYSTEM


---

## Principle

Observability enables informed operation by connecting visible signals to underlying system behavior.
