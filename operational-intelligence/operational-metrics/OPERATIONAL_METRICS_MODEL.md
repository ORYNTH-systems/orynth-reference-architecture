# Operational Metrics Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH operational performance and system conditions are measured.

---

## Metrics Principle

Operational metrics transform system activity into measurable indicators that support evaluation and improvement.

---

## Metric Domains

Includes:

- performance metrics;
- reliability metrics;
- efficiency metrics;
- integrity metrics;
- growth metrics.

---

## Metrics Lifecycle


DEFINE OBJECTIVE

↓

SELECT MEASUREMENT

↓

COLLECT DATA

↓

ANALYZE INDICATOR

↓

EVALUATE CONDITION

↓

IMPROVE SYSTEM


---

## Principle

Meaningful metrics provide visibility into system health and operational progress.
