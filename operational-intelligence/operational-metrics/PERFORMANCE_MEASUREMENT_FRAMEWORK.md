# Performance Measurement Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define methods for measuring operational performance across ORYNTH systems.

---

## Measurement Principle

Performance measurement requires defined criteria, consistent evaluation, and contextual interpretation.

---

## Measurement Areas

Includes:

- execution quality;
- resource utilization;
- process efficiency;
- response capability;
- outcome effectiveness.

---

## Measurement Lifecycle


DEFINE BASELINE

↓

COLLECT MEASUREMENTS

↓

COMPARE CONDITIONS

↓

IDENTIFY VARIANCE

↓

APPLY IMPROVEMENT

↓

REASSESS


---

## Principle

Performance measurement enables evidence-based operational improvement.
