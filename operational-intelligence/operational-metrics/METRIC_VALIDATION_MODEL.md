# Metric Validation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how operational metrics are reviewed for accuracy, relevance, and reliability.

---

## Validation Principle

Metrics must represent meaningful system conditions and remain connected to their intended purpose.

---

## Validation Criteria

Includes:

- measurement accuracy;
- data consistency;
- relevance;
- repeatability;
- interpretation boundaries.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


DEFINE METRIC

↓

VERIFY SOURCE

↓

TEST MEASUREMENT

↓

REVIEW RESULT

↓

APPROVE USAGE

↓

MONITOR CONTINUITY


---

## Principle

Validated metrics provide reliable foundations for operational decisions.
