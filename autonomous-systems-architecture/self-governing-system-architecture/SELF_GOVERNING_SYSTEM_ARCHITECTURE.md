# Self-Governing System Architecture

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to establish autonomous governance structures, decision processes, authority relationships, and continuity mechanisms across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Governance Principle

Self-governing system architecture enables autonomous systems to operate through defined constraints, governance models, validation mechanisms, and preserved decision integrity.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- authority management;
- policy alignment;
- autonomous decision structures;
- constraint enforcement;
- governance continuity;
- adaptive governance.

---

## Governance Lifecycle


ESTABLISH GOVERNANCE MODEL

↓

DEFINE AUTHORITY CONDITIONS

↓

EVALUATE DECISION CONTEXT

↓

APPLY GOVERNANCE CONSTRAINTS

↓

EXECUTE AUTHORIZED TRANSITION

↓

PRESERVE GOVERNANCE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A self-governing system architecture provides autonomous operation while maintaining alignment with defined governance structures and system identity.
