# Governance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track autonomous governance states, authority relationships, transitions, and conditions.

---

## State Principle

Governance states preserve relationships between authority, policy, decision conditions, execution constraints, and resulting system states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance condition;
- authority state;
- policy state;
- decision status;
- transition history;
- continuity state.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

ASSESS AUTHORITY CONDITIONS

↓

VERIFY GOVERNANCE ALIGNMENT

↓

UPDATE DECISION STATE

↓

RECORD TRANSITION

↓

PRESERVE GOVERNANCE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A governance state register maintains traceability between autonomous decisions and their governing conditions.
