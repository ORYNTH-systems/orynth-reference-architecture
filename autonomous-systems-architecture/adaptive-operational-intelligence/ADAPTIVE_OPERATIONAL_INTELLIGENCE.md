## Purpose

Define the framework used to provide adaptive operational awareness, intelligence coordination, environmental assessment, and continuous operational improvement across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

## Operational Intelligence Principle

Adaptive operational intelligence enables systems to observe conditions, interpret operational states, adapt responses, and preserve continuity through structured intelligence processes.

---

## Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- operational awareness;
- environmental analysis;
- system monitoring;
- adaptive response;
- performance optimization;
- operational learning.
