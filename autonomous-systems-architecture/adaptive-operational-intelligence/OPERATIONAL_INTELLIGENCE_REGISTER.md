# Operational Intelligence Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track operational intelligence states, signals, responses, and adaptive transitions.

---

## Register Principle

Operational intelligence records preserve relationships between observed conditions, intelligence analysis, adaptive decisions, and resulting operational states.

---

## Intelligence Fields

Includes:

- operational signal;
- system condition;
- intelligence assessment;
- adaptive response;
- validation status;
- historical record.

---

## Register Lifecycle


CAPTURE OPERATIONAL SIGNAL

↓

CLASSIFY CONDITION

↓

ANALYZE INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

GENERATE RESPONSE

↓

VERIFY OPERATIONAL EFFECT

↓

UPDATE REGISTER


---

## Principle

An operational intelligence register maintains traceability between environmental conditions and autonomous system adaptation.
