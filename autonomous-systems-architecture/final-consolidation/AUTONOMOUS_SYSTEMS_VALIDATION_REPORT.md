## Purpose

Define validation conditions for the ORYNTH autonomous systems architecture framework.

---

## Validation Principle

Validation confirms that execution, governance, operational intelligence, and runtime structures remain aligned, traceable, and architecturally coherent.

---

## Validation Domains

Includes:

- execution validation;
- governance validation;
- operational intelligence validation;
- runtime validation;
- continuity validation.

↓

VALIDATE CONTINUITY

PRESERVE RESULT
