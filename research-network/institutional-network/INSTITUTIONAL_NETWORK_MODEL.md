# Institutional Network Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure for institutional participation, collaboration, and research relationships within the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Institutional Network Principle

Institutions strengthen the research ecosystem through structured collaboration while preserving shared objectives, contribution history, and governance alignment.

---

## Network Functions

Includes:

- institutional engagement;
- research collaboration;
- validation partnerships;
- knowledge exchange;
- applied research coordination.

---

## Institutional Network Lifecycle


IDENTIFY INSTITUTION

↓

ESTABLISH CONTACT

↓

ALIGN OBJECTIVES

↓

DEFINE COLLABORATION

↓

EXECUTE RESEARCH

↓

PRESERVE OUTCOMES

Institutional networks expand research capability through aligned participation and documented collaboration.
