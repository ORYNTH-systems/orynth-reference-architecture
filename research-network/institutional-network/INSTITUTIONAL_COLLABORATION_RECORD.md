# Institutional Collaboration Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Preserve records of institutional research relationships and collaboration activity.

---

## Record Structure

Includes:

- institution identifier;
- collaboration scope;
- participants;
- research objectives;
- activity history;
- outcomes;
- current status.

Includes:

- active collaborations;
- completed collaborations;
- research partnerships;
- evaluation relationships.

## Principle

Institutional records preserve relationship continuity and research lineage.
