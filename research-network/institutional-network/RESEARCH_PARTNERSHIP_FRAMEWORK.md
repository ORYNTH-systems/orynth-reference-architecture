# Research Partnership Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH establishes and maintains research partnerships.

---

## Partnership Principle

Research partnerships require alignment of objectives, contribution pathways, evidence practices, and continuity expectations.

---

## Partnership Areas

Includes:

- joint research;
- validation activities;
- technical collaboration;
- domain expertise;
- knowledge development.

---

## Partnership Lifecycle


DISCOVERY

↓

ASSESSMENT

↓

ALIGNMENT

↓

AGREEMENT

↓

COLLABORATION

↓

REVIEW


---

## Partnership Requirements

Maintain:

- scope definition;
- contribution records;
- communication history;
- outcome documentation.

---

## Principle

Research partnerships create shared advancement through structured collaboration.
