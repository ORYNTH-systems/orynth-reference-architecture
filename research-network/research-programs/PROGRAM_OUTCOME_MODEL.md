# Program Outcome Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how research program outcomes are measured, documented, and preserved.

---

## Outcome Principle

Research outcomes provide evidence of progress, contribution, and ecosystem value.

---

## Outcome Categories

Includes:

- discoveries;
- publications;
- technical developments;
- validation results;
- collaboration outcomes.

---

## Outcome Lifecycle


GENERATE

↓

CAPTURE

↓

EVALUATE

↓

PUBLISH

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Outcome Record

Includes:

- program identifier;
- objective;
- findings;
- evidence;
- impact;
- future direction.

---

## Principle

Documented outcomes convert research activity into lasting knowledge assets.
