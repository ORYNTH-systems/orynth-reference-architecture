# Research Program Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure for organized ORYNTH research programs.

---

## Research Program Principle

Research programs coordinate focused investigation while preserving methodology, evidence, and continuity.

---

## Program Functions

Includes:

- research coordination;
- objective definition;
- methodology alignment;
- evidence collection;
- outcome tracking.

---

## Research Program Lifecycle


DEFINE OBJECTIVE

↓

ESTABLISH METHOD

↓

CONDUCT RESEARCH

↓

ANALYZE RESULTS

↓

DOCUMENT OUTCOMES

↓

PRESERVE KNOWLEDGE


---

## Principle

Structured programs transform individual research efforts into coordinated advancement.
