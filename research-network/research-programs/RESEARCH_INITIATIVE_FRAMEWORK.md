# Research Initiative Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how new ORYNTH research initiatives are proposed, evaluated, and developed.

---

## Initiative Principle

Research initiatives should align with ecosystem objectives while allowing exploration and discovery.

---

## Initiative Areas

Includes:

- foundational research;
- applied research;
- validation studies;
- experimental development;
- collaborative investigations.

---

## Initiative Lifecycle


IDENTIFY QUESTION

↓

DEFINE SCOPE

↓

EVALUATE ALIGNMENT

↓

EXECUTE RESEARCH

↓

REVIEW FINDINGS

↓

INTEGRATE KNOWLEDGE


---

## Principle

Research initiatives provide structured pathways for discovery and innovation.
