# Research Communication Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define communication structures supporting ORYNTH research collaboration and dissemination.

---

## Communication Principle

Research communication should preserve clarity, attribution, context, and evidence.

---

## Communication Channels

Includes:

- publications;
- technical documentation;
- research discussions;
- review processes;
- institutional communication.

---

## Communication Lifecycle


CREATE MESSAGE

↓

VERIFY CONTEXT

↓

DISTRIBUTE

↓

RECEIVE FEEDBACK

↓

REFINE

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Communication Requirements

Maintain:

- source attribution;
- historical context;
- research relevance;
- communication records.

---

## Principle

Effective communication preserves knowledge integrity across the research network.
