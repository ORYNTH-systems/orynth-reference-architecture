# Knowledge Exchange Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how knowledge is shared, organized, and preserved throughout the ORYNTH research ecosystem.

---

## Knowledge Exchange Principle

Research advancement depends on structured exchange of ideas, evidence, methodologies, and discoveries.

---

## Exchange Functions

Includes:

- research dissemination;
- collaborative discussion;
- documentation sharing;
- methodological exchange;
- discovery transfer.

---

## Knowledge Exchange Lifecycle


GENERATE KNOWLEDGE

↓

DOCUMENT

↓

SHARE

↓

REVIEW

↓

INTEGRATE

↓

PRESERVE


---

## Principle

Knowledge exchange transforms individual research into collective capability.
