# Knowledge Transfer Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how research knowledge moves between individuals, institutions, and future participants.

---

## Transfer Principle

Knowledge transfer ensures that discoveries remain accessible, understandable, and reusable.

---

## Transfer Areas

Includes:

- technical knowledge;
- research methods;
- operational experience;
- institutional understanding;
- historical context.

---

## Transfer Lifecycle


IDENTIFY KNOWLEDGE

↓

CAPTURE

↓

ORGANIZE

↓

TRANSFER

↓

VALIDATE UNDERSTANDING

↓

PRESERVE


---

## Continuity Requirements

Maintain:

- documentation;
- contributor attribution;
- supporting evidence;
- version history.

---

## Principle

Knowledge transfer creates continuity across generations of researchers and systems.
