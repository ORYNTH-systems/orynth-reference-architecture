# Researcher Network Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure through which independent researchers, contributors, and collaborators participate within the ORYNTH research ecosystem.

---

## Research Network Principle

A research network expands collective capability through structured participation while preserving attribution, evidence, and intellectual continuity.

---

## Network Functions

Includes:

- researcher participation;
- knowledge contribution;
- peer collaboration;
- research validation;
- discovery exchange.

---

## Researcher Network Lifecycle


DISCOVER

↓

ENGAGE

↓

CONTRIBUTE

↓

COLLABORATE

↓

VALIDATE

↓

PRESERVE


---

## Principle

The research network connects individual contributions into a coherent body of evolving knowledge.
