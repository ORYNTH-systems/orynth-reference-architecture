# Researcher Participation Framework

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define pathways for researchers to participate in ORYNTH research activities.

---

## Participation Categories

Includes:

- independent researchers;
- institutional researchers;
- technical contributors;
- domain specialists;
- reviewers.

---

## Participation Lifecycle


IDENTIFY INTEREST

↓

DEFINE CONTRIBUTION

↓

ESTABLISH PARTICIPATION

↓

CONDUCT RESEARCH

↓

DOCUMENT CONTRIBUTION

↓

REVIEW OUTCOME


---

## Participation Requirements

Maintain:

- contribution clarity;
- attribution;
- evidence records;
- research alignment.

---

## Principle

Participation expands research capability while preserving contributor identity and lineage.
