# Research Contribution Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how research contributions are recorded, evaluated, and preserved.

---

## Contribution Types

Includes:

- theoretical contributions;
- technical contributions;
- validation contributions;
- documentation contributions;
- applied research contributions.

---

## Contribution Lifecycle


SUBMIT

↓

DOCUMENT

↓

REVIEW

↓

INTEGRATE

↓

ARCHIVE


---

## Contribution Record

Includes:

- contributor;
- contribution scope;
- supporting evidence;
- review status;
- relationship to existing research.

---

## Principle

Research contributions become durable assets through traceability and preservation.
