# Collaboration Continuity Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how collaboration history and relationships are preserved over time.

---

## Continuity Principle

Collaboration continuity ensures knowledge, relationships, and contributions remain accessible through future transitions.

---

## Continuity Areas

Includes:

- collaboration records;
- contributor history;
- project lineage;
- research outcomes;
- relationship context.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


CAPTURE

↓

STORE

↓

MAINTAIN

↓

TRANSFER

↓

REFERENCE


---

## Principle

Preserved collaboration history enables future researchers and institutions to build upon prior work.
