# Continuity State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track cross-system continuity states, preserved relationships, transitions, and validation conditions.

---

## State Principle

Continuity states preserve relationships between systems, architecture conditions, transition events, governance states, and resulting preserved configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- continuity identity;
- source system states;
- relationship mappings;
- transition conditions;
- preservation method;
- validation status;
- resulting continuity state;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE CONTINUITY RECORD

↓

CAPTURE SYSTEM STATES

↓

MAP CROSS-SYSTEM RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ANALYZE TRANSITION CONDITIONS

↓

VALIDATE PRESERVATION

↓

REGISTER CONTINUITY STATE

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A continuity state register maintains traceability between ORYNTH systems and their preserved evolutionary relationships.
