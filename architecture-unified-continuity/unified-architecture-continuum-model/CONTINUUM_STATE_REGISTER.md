## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track unified architecture continuum states, transitions, validation outcomes, and preserved lineage.

---

## State Principle

Continuum states preserve relationships between architecture states, continuity conditions, recursive evolution events, governance transitions, and resulting unified configurations.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- continuum identity;
- architecture state;
- continuity relationships;
- evolutionary transition;
- governance conditions;
- validation status;
- resulting continuum state;
- historical lineage.
