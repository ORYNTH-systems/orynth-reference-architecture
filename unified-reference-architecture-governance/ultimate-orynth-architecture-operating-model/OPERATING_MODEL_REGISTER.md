# Operating Model Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track operating model states, governance conditions, operational transitions, and evolutionary readiness.

---

## State Principle

Operating model states preserve relationships between architecture conditions, governance decisions, synthesis outputs, and future operational pathways.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- operating model identity;
- architecture condition;
- governance state;
- synthesis condition;
- operational transition;
- validation status;
- continuity state;
- future readiness.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE OPERATING STATE

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURE CONDITION

↓

APPLY GOVERNANCE RULES

↓

GENERATE SYNTHESIS RESPONSE

↓

VALIDATE TRANSITION

↓

REGISTER OPERATIONAL STATE

↓

MAINTAIN CONTINUITY


---

## Principle

An operating model register maintains traceability between ORYNTH architecture states and their governed operational lifecycle.
