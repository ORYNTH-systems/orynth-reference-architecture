# Synthesis Intelligence Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track synthesis intelligence states, observations, integrations, and evolutionary conditions.

---

## State Principle

Synthesis intelligence states preserve relationships between architecture observations, analytical processes, synthesis outputs, and governance decisions.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- synthesis identity;
- observed architecture state;
- intelligence condition;
- relationship analysis;
- generated synthesis;
- validation result;
- governance action;
- future applicability.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE ARCHITECTURE OBSERVATION

↓

ANALYZE RELATIONSHIP PATTERNS

↓

GENERATE SYNTHESIS RESULT

↓

VALIDATE OUTPUT

↓

REGISTER INTELLIGENCE STATE

↓

PRESERVE EVOLUTIONARY RECORD


---

## Principle

A synthesis intelligence register maintains traceability between ORYNTH architecture observations and resulting integrated knowledge states.
