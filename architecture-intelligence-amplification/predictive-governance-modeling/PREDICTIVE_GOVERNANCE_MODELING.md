# Predictive Governance Modeling

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to model future governance conditions, architecture transitions, and potential system states within ORYNTH.

---

## Predictive Governance Principle

Predictive governance modeling uses preserved architecture knowledge, validated relationships, and historical states to evaluate possible future conditions.

Prediction supports preparation, not uncontrolled transition.

---

## Modeling Domains

Includes:

- architecture state forecasting;
- governance condition analysis;
- transition modeling;
- dependency impact analysis;
- risk assessment;
- reference preservation;
- future readiness evaluation;
- decision support.
