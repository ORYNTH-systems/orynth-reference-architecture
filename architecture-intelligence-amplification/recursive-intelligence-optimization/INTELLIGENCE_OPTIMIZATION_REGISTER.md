# Intelligence Optimization Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track intelligence optimization states, improvement cycles, validation results, and evolutionary conditions.

---

## State Principle

Optimization states preserve relationships between intelligence conditions, identified improvements, applied refinements, and resulting capability changes.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- optimization identity;
- source intelligence state;
- improvement objective;
- refinement method;
- validation condition;
- resulting capability;
- evolutionary status;
- historical lineage.

---

## State Lifecycle


CREATE OPTIMIZATION RECORD

↓

CAPTURE INTELLIGENCE CONDITION

↓

IDENTIFY IMPROVEMENT TARGET

↓

APPLY OPTIMIZATION METHOD

↓

VALIDATE RESULT

↓

REGISTER OPTIMIZED STATE

↓

PRESERVE EVOLUTION HISTORY


---

## Principle

An intelligence optimization register maintains traceability between ORYNTH intelligence processes and their controlled improvement cycles.
