# System Relationship Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to record relationships between ORYNTH systems and subsystems.

---

## Registry Principle

System relationships preserve identity, interaction pathways, dependency context, and operational connections.

---

## Relationship Records

Includes:

- source system;
- target system;
- relationship type;
- dependency context;
- validation status.

---

## Registry Lifecycle


CREATE RELATIONSHIP RECORD

↓

IDENTIFY SYSTEMS

↓

DEFINE CONNECTION

↓

VERIFY INTERACTION

↓

TRACK EVOLUTION

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A system relationship register maintains traceability across expanding architectural structures.
