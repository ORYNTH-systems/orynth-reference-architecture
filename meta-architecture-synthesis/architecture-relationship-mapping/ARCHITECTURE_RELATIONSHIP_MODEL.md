# Architecture Relationship Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to identify, classify, and preserve relationships between ORYNTH architectural components.

---

## Relationship Principle

Architectural relationships preserve context between systems, frameworks, capabilities, dependencies, and evolutionary pathways.

---

## Relationship Domains

Includes:

- framework relationships;
- system relationships;
- capability relationships;
- governance relationships;
- knowledge relationships.

---

## Relationship Lifecycle


IDENTIFY COMPONENTS

↓

MAP RELATIONSHIPS

↓

CLASSIFY CONNECTIONS

↓

VERIFY ALIGNMENT

↓

PRESERVE RELATIONSHIP STATE

↓

UPDATE ARCHITECTURE MODEL


---

## Principle

An architecture relationship model provides visibility into how individual systems compose a unified ecosystem.
