# Dependency Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to identify, classify, and preserve dependencies between ORYNTH systems, frameworks, and architectural components.

---

## Dependency Principle

System dependencies preserve awareness of required relationships, supporting structures, execution conditions, and integration pathways.

---

## Dependency Domains

Includes:

- architectural dependencies;
- operational dependencies;
- governance dependencies;
- knowledge dependencies;
- validation dependencies.

---

## Dependency Lifecycle


IDENTIFY COMPONENT

↓

DETERMINE DEPENDENCY

↓

CLASSIFY RELATIONSHIP

↓

ASSESS IMPACT

↓

VERIFY CONDITION

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A dependency model provides visibility into how ORYNTH components rely upon and support one another.
