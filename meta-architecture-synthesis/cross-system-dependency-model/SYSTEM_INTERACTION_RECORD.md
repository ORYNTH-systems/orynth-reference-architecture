# System Interaction Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how interactions between ORYNTH systems are documented and maintained.

---

## Interaction Principle

System interaction records preserve communication pathways, operational exchanges, and resulting system states.

---

## Interaction Domains

Includes:

- information flow;
- execution flow;
- governance flow;
- validation flow;
- knowledge flow.

---

## Interaction Lifecycle


IDENTIFY INTERACTION

↓

DOCUMENT EXCHANGE

↓

ASSESS DEPENDENCY

↓

VERIFY OUTCOME

↓

RECORD STATE

↓

MAINTAIN HISTORY


---

## Principle

A system interaction record provides traceability across interconnected architectural components.
