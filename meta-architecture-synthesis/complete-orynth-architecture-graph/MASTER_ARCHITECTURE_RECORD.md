# Master Architecture Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the master record structure for preserving the complete ORYNTH architectural state.

---

## Architecture Principle

The master architecture record provides a consolidated reference of systems, relationships, dependencies, concepts, and evolutionary history.

---

## Record Domains

Includes:

- foundational architecture;
- operational systems;
- governance systems;
- research systems;
- evolutionary systems.

---

## Record Lifecycle


CAPTURE ARCHITECTURE STATE

↓

CONNECT SYSTEM MODELS

↓

RECORD RELATIONSHIPS

↓

VALIDATE COMPLETENESS

↓

UPDATE ARCHITECTURE GRAPH

↓

PRESERVE EVOLUTION HISTORY


---

## Principle

A master architecture record provides the canonical reference point for ORYNTH ecosystem structure.
