# Architecture Layer Convergence

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to converge ORYNTH architecture layers into a unified meta-architectural structure.

---

## Convergence Principle

Architecture layer convergence establishes relationships between independent architectural domains while preserving each layer's identity, purpose, constraints, and operational role.

---

## Convergence Domains

Includes:

- foundational architecture;
- execution architecture;
- governance architecture;
- intelligence architecture;
- operating architecture;
- autonomous systems architecture.

---

## Convergence Lifecycle


IDENTIFY ARCHITECTURE LAYERS

↓

MAP LAYER RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ALIGN ARCHITECTURAL PRINCIPLES

↓

VERIFY CROSS-LAYER COHERENCE

↓

ESTABLISH CONVERGED MODEL

↓

PRESERVE ARCHITECTURE HISTORY


---

## Principle

Architecture layer convergence enables ORYNTH systems to operate as interconnected architectural components while maintaining structural integrity.
