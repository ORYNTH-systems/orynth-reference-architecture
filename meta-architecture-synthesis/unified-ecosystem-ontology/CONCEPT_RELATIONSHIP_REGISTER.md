# Concept Relationship Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to record relationships between ORYNTH concepts.

---

## Relationship Principle

Concept relationships preserve meaning, context, dependencies, and interactions between ideas throughout ecosystem evolution.

---

## Relationship Domains

Includes:

- concept identity;
- conceptual hierarchy;
- related concepts;
- semantic relationships;
- evolution history.

---

## Relationship Lifecycle


REGISTER CONCEPT

↓

DEFINE RELATIONSHIP

↓

CLASSIFY CONNECTION

↓

VERIFY MEANING

↓

TRACK EVOLUTION

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A concept relationship register maintains semantic continuity across the ORYNTH ecosystem.
