# Unified Ontology Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to organize shared concepts, terminology, relationships, and meanings across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Ontology Principle

A unified ontology preserves conceptual consistency while allowing specialized systems to maintain their unique operational roles.

---

## Ontology Domains

Includes:

- foundational concepts;
- system concepts;
- governance concepts;
- execution concepts;
- knowledge concepts.

---

## Ontology Lifecycle


IDENTIFY CONCEPT

↓

DEFINE MEANING

↓

MAP RELATIONSHIPS

↓

VERIFY CONSISTENCY

↓

INTEGRATE KNOWLEDGE

↓

MAINTAIN ONTOLOGY


---

## Principle

A unified ontology provides the semantic foundation required for ecosystem-wide understanding.
