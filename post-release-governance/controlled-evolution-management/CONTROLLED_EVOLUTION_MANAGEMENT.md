# Controlled Evolution Management

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to govern future ORYNTH architecture evolution while preserving reference identity, structural continuity, and historical traceability.

---

## Evolution Principle

Controlled evolution enables architectural advancement through governed transitions, explicit impact assessment, and preservation of canonical reference relationships.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- architecture revisions;
- capability expansion;
- framework refinement;
- documentation updates;
- integration changes;
- version transitions.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


IDENTIFY PROPOSED CHANGE

↓

CLASSIFY CHANGE TYPE

↓

ASSESS ARCHITECTURAL IMPACT

↓

REVIEW GOVERNANCE REQUIREMENTS

↓

APPLY CONTROLLED TRANSITION

↓

VALIDATE CONTINUITY

↓

PRESERVE EVOLUTION RECORD


---

## Principle

Controlled evolution management ensures ORYNTH can advance without losing architectural identity or reference integrity.
