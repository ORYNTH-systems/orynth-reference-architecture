# External Validation & Adoption Tracking

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to observe, record, and analyze external validation, adoption activity, implementation feedback, and ecosystem interaction following ORYNTH publication.

---

## Validation Principle

External validation and adoption tracking preserves evidence of architecture interaction while distinguishing observation, evaluation, implementation, and formal adoption.

---

## Tracking Domains

Includes:

- external review;
- technical evaluation;
- research interaction;
- implementation observation;
- ecosystem feedback;
- adoption indicators.

---

## Tracking Lifecycle


IDENTIFY EXTERNAL INTERACTION

↓

RECORD VALIDATION EVENT

↓

CLASSIFY OBSERVATION TYPE

↓

ASSESS ADOPTION SIGNAL

↓

UPDATE VALIDATION STATE

↓

PRESERVE EVIDENCE RECORD


---

## Principle

External validation tracking provides a structured method for understanding how the ORYNTH architecture is received, evaluated, and utilized after release.
