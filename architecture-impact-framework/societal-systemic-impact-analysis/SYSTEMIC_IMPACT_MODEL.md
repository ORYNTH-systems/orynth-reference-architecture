# Systemic Impact Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structural model for analyzing architecture-level systemic effects.

---

## Systemic Principle

A system-level effect emerges through interactions between architecture, participants, environments, and time.

---

## Systemic Impact Model


ARCHITECTURE CHANGE

IMPLEMENTATION CONTEXT

STAKEHOLDER INTERACTION

TEMPORAL DEVELOPMENT

↓

SYSTEMIC EFFECT OBSERVATION

↓

IMPACT ASSESSMENT


---

## Analysis Categories

### Direct System Impact

Effects within implemented environments.

### Network Impact

Effects produced through connected participants.

### Institutional Impact

Effects on organizational structures and practices.

### Knowledge Impact

Effects on shared understanding and future development.

### Cultural Impact

Effects on norms, expectations, and participation.

---

## Systemic Factors

Includes:

- scale;
- adoption depth;
- relationship density;
- persistence over time;
- evidence strength.

---

## Principle

The Systemic Impact Model provides a structured method for evaluating architecture influence across interconnected environments.
