# Systemic Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for systemic impact analysis.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- systemic impact identifier;
- analyzed architecture state;
- affected systems;
- observed effects;
- evidence sources;
- analysis methodology;
- impact classification.

---

## Systemic Analysis Lifecycle


SYSTEMIC EFFECT IDENTIFIED

↓

CONTEXT ANALYZED

↓

EVIDENCE COLLECTED

↓

PATTERNS REVIEWED

↓

IMPACT CLASSIFIED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Systemic Impact States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- ANALYZING;
- OBSERVED;
- ASSESSED;
- VALIDATED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Systemic Status Records preserve accountability between architectural influence and documented analysis.
