# Outcome Validation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structural model for validating architectural outcomes.

---

## Validation Principle

Outcome validation requires comparison between intended state, observed state, and verified evidence.

---

## Validation Model


EXPECTED STATE

IMPLEMENTED STATE

MEASURED CHANGE

EVIDENCE REVIEW

↓

VALIDATED OUTCOME


---

## Validation Categories

### Direct Validation

Outcome demonstrated through immediate evidence.

### Comparative Validation

Outcome demonstrated against a previous baseline.

### Longitudinal Validation

Outcome demonstrated through sustained observation.

### Independent Validation

Outcome reviewed by external evaluators.

---

## Validation Factors

Includes:

- evidence quality;
- measurement reliability;
- temporal consistency;
- causal relationship;
- uncertainty assessment.

---

## Principle

The Outcome Validation Model prevents unsupported claims by requiring evidence-based confirmation.
