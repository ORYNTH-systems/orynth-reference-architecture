# Outcome Validation System

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the system for validating outcomes produced through ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture implementation.

---

## Outcome Principle

An outcome requires more than activity or measurement.

A validated outcome requires:

- defined expectation;
- observable change;
- evidence relationship;
- evaluation criteria;
- validation process.

---

## Outcome Domains

Includes:

- operational outcomes;
- governance outcomes;
- assurance outcomes;
- resilience outcomes;
- adoption outcomes;
- systemic outcomes.

---

## Outcome Validation Lifecycle


EXPECTED OUTCOME DEFINED

↓

IMPLEMENTATION OBSERVED

↓

CHANGE MEASURED

↓

EVIDENCE REVIEWED

↓

OUTCOME VALIDATED

↓

RESULT PRESERVED


---

## Validation Requirements

Outcomes must preserve:

- measurement context;
- evidence lineage;
- validation criteria;
- interpretation boundaries;
- uncertainty conditions.

---

## Principle

The Outcome Validation System converts measured changes into evidence-supported architectural outcomes.
