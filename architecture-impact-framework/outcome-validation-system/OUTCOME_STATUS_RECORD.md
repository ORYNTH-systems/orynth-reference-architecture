# Outcome Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for outcome validation activities.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- outcome identifier;
- architecture version;
- expected outcome;
- observed outcome;
- evidence source;
- validation method;
- final determination.

---

## Outcome Lifecycle


OUTCOME PROPOSED

↓

EVIDENCE COLLECTED

↓

VALIDATION PERFORMED

↓

RESULT DETERMINED

↓

STATUS RECORDED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Outcome States

Possible states:

- PROPOSED;
- OBSERVED;
- REVIEWING;
- VALIDATED;
- VERIFIED;
- INCONCLUSIVE;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Outcome Status Records maintain accountability between claimed impact and demonstrated results.
