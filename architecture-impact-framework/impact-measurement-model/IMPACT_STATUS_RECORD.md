# Impact Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for impact measurement activities.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- impact identifier;
- measured architecture state;
- baseline condition;
- metric definitions;
- evidence source;
- measurement results;
- validation status.

---

## Impact Lifecycle


IMPACT IDENTIFIED

↓

BASELINE ESTABLISHED

↓

MEASUREMENT PERFORMED

↓

RESULTS REVIEWED

↓

IMPACT VALIDATED

↓

HISTORY PRESERVED


---

## Impact States

Possible states:

- IDENTIFIED;
- MEASURING;
- OBSERVED;
- VALIDATING;
- VERIFIED;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Impact Status Records preserve accountability between architectural implementation and demonstrated outcomes.
