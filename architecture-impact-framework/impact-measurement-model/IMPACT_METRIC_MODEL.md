# Impact Metric Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure for impact indicators and measurement criteria.

---

## Metric Principle

Metrics must represent meaningful change rather than isolated activity.

---

## Metric Model


BASELINE STATE

ARCHITECTURE INTERVENTION

OBSERVED CHANGE

VALIDATION METHOD

↓

IMPACT DETERMINATION


---

## Metric Categories

### Efficiency Metrics

Measures resource, time, or process improvement.

### Integrity Metrics

Measures preservation of correctness and trust.

### Resilience Metrics

Measures recovery and continuity capability.

### Adoption Metrics

Measures implementation and participation.

### Knowledge Metrics

Measures contribution and understanding.

---

## Metric Factors

Includes:

- measurement definition;
- collection method;
- comparison baseline;
- confidence conditions;
- interpretation limits.

---

## Principle

The Impact Metric Model ensures measurements remain connected to meaningful architectural outcomes.
