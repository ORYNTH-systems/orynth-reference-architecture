# Impact Measurement Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework for measuring the effects produced by ORYNTH / Morning Star architecture adoption, implementation, and evolution.

---

## Impact Principle

Impact measurement distinguishes between:

- architectural activity;
- implemented capability;
- observed outcome;
- validated systemic effect.

Impact must be evidence-based and traceable.

---

## Impact Domains

Includes:

- operational improvement;
- governance improvement;
- assurance improvement;
- resilience improvement;
- knowledge contribution;
- ecosystem influence.

---

## Impact Lifecycle


ARCHITECTURE IMPLEMENTED

↓

IMPACT INDICATORS DEFINED

↓

DATA OBSERVED

↓

MEASUREMENTS RECORDED

↓

OUTCOMES ANALYZED

↓

IMPACT VALIDATED


---

## Measurement Requirements

Impact measurement must preserve:

- baseline conditions;
- measurement methodology;
- evidence source;
- temporal context;
- interpretation boundaries.

---

## Principle

The Impact Measurement Model provides a structured method for determining whether architecture produces measurable improvement.
