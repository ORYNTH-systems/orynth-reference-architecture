# Impact Status Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for long-term impact continuity.

---

## Record Fields

Includes:

- impact identifier;
- architecture state;
- historical measurements;
- validated outcomes;
- systemic observations;
- continuity assessment;
- future review requirements.

---

## Impact Lifecycle


IMPACT RECORD CREATED

↓

MEASUREMENTS PRESERVED

↓

OUTCOMES CONNECTED

↓

SYSTEMIC EFFECTS TRACKED

↓

CONTINUITY REVIEWED

↓

IMPACT HISTORY MAINTAINED


---

## Impact States

Possible states:

- INITIATED;
- TRACKING;
- VALIDATED;
- CONTINUING;
- EVOLVING;
- HISTORICAL.

---

## Principle

Impact Status Records preserve the relationship between architectural evolution and demonstrated long-term effects.
