# System Evolution Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how recursive system improvements and evolutionary transitions are documented.

---

## Evolution Principle

System evolution records preserve the relationship between prior states, optimization decisions, resulting changes, and future development pathways.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- system state changes;
- optimization events;
- capability improvements;
- validation results;
- future opportunities.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


IDENTIFY EVOLUTION EVENT

↓

DOCUMENT CURRENT STATE

↓

APPLY OPTIMIZATION

↓

VERIFY NEW STATE

↓

COMPARE RESULTS

↓

PRESERVE EVOLUTION HISTORY


---

## Principle

A system evolution record maintains continuity across recursive ecosystem improvement cycles.
