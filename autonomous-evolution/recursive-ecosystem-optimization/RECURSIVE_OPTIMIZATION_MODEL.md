## Purpose

Define the framework used to continuously improve ORYNTH ecosystem structures through feedback, evaluation, refinement, and controlled evolution.

Recursive optimization preserves system identity while enabling iterative improvement through observed conditions, measured outcomes, and validated adaptation.

- operational optimization;
- knowledge optimization;
- ecosystem optimization.
