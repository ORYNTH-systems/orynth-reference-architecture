# Optimization Feedback Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure used to capture feedback signals, improvement opportunities, and optimization actions.

---

## Feedback Principle

Feedback records preserve observations, sources, evaluation results, implemented improvements, and resulting system effects.

---

## Feedback Domains

Includes:

- system observations;
- user feedback;
- operational feedback;
- validation feedback;
- research feedback.

---

## Feedback Lifecycle


CAPTURE FEEDBACK

↓

CLASSIFY SIGNAL

↓

EVALUATE IMPACT

↓

DEFINE RESPONSE

↓

VERIFY IMPROVEMENT

↓

UPDATE RECORD


---

## Principle

An optimization feedback register converts system experience into structured improvement pathways.
