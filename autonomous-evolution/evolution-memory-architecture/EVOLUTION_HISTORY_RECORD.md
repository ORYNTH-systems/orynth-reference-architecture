# Evolution History Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH evolutionary transitions, milestones, and architectural changes are recorded.

---

## History Principle

Evolution history preserves the sequence of system states, decisions, modifications, and resulting capabilities.

---

## History Domains

Includes:

- milestone events;
- architectural revisions;
- governance transitions;
- validation changes;
- operational evolution.

---

## History Lifecycle


RECORD INITIAL STATE

↓

DOCUMENT TRANSITION

↓

CAPTURE CHANGE REASON

↓

VERIFY NEW STATE

↓

COMPARE EVOLUTION

↓

ARCHIVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

An evolution history record provides a persistent timeline of ecosystem development.
