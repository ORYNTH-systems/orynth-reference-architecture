# Knowledge Continuity Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure used to preserve knowledge relationships across ORYNTH evolution.

---

## Continuity Principle

Knowledge continuity maintains connections between discoveries, decisions, implementations, and future development pathways.

---

## Continuity Domains

Includes:

- knowledge artifact;
- originating context;
- relationship mapping;
- transformation history;
- current relevance.

---

## Continuity Lifecycle


IDENTIFY KNOWLEDGE ASSET

↓

CAPTURE ORIGIN

↓

MAP RELATIONSHIPS

↓

TRACK TRANSFORMATION

↓

VERIFY CURRENT STATE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A knowledge continuity register prevents loss of context during ecosystem evolution.
