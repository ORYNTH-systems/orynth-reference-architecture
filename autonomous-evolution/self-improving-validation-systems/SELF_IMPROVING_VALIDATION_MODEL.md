# Self-Improving Validation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to improve ORYNTH validation processes through observation, analysis, refinement, and controlled evolution.

---

## Validation Improvement Principle

Self-improving validation preserves verification integrity while allowing validation methods to adapt based on evidence, experience, and changing system requirements.

---

## Validation Evolution Domains

Includes:

- validation observations;
- verification methods;
- evidence quality;
- assessment criteria;
- improvement pathways.

---

## Validation Evolution Lifecycle


OBSERVE VALIDATION PROCESS

↓

IDENTIFY LIMITATION

↓

ANALYZE IMPROVEMENT

↓

IMPLEMENT REFINEMENT

↓

VERIFY EFFECTIVENESS

↓

PRESERVE CHANGE HISTORY


---

## Principle

Self-improving validation systems enable continuous assurance without losing traceability or verification discipline.
