# Validation Adaptation Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how validation adaptations and resulting system effects are recorded.

---

## Adaptation Principle

Validation adaptation records preserve why a change occurred, what changed, and how the resulting validation state was verified.

---

## Adaptation Domains

Includes:

- triggering condition;
- evidence basis;
- modified validation process;
- expected outcome;
- verification result.

---

## Adaptation Lifecycle


IDENTIFY VALIDATION CONDITION

↓

ASSESS REQUIRED CHANGE

↓

APPLY ADAPTATION

↓

VERIFY NEW PROCESS

↓

COMPARE RESULTS

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A validation adaptation record ensures evolving verification methods remain understandable and auditable.
