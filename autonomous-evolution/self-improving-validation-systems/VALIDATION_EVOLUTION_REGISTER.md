# Validation Evolution Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure for tracking validation improvements, revisions, and evolutionary changes.

---

## Evolution Principle

Validation evolution records preserve previous states, improvement rationale, evidence, and resulting validation capability.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- validation version;
- identified condition;
- improvement action;
- verification result;
- adoption status.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


REGISTER VALIDATION STATE

↓

IDENTIFY CHANGE

↓

DOCUMENT IMPROVEMENT

↓

TEST REFINEMENT

↓

APPROVE EVOLUTION

↓

UPDATE REGISTER


---

## Principle

A validation evolution register maintains continuity between previous and improved validation states.
