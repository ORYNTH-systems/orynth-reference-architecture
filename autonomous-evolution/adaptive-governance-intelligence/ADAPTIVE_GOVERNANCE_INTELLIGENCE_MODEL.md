# Adaptive Governance Intelligence Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to enable governance structures to adapt through observation, evaluation, learning, and controlled evolution.

---

## Governance Intelligence Principle

Adaptive governance intelligence preserves decision integrity while allowing governance mechanisms to respond to changing conditions, evidence, and ecosystem requirements.

---

## Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- governance observation;
- condition analysis;
- decision adaptation;
- policy evolution;
- continuity preservation.

---

## Adaptation Lifecycle
OBSERVE SYSTEM CONDITION

↓

ANALYZE GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

EVALUATE CHANGE REQUIREMENT

↓

SELECT ADAPTATION PATH

↓

VERIFY GOVERNANCE IMPACT

↓

PRESERVE DECISION HISTORY


---

## Principle

Adaptive governance intelligence enables evolution while maintaining structural continuity and accountability.
