# Governance Learning Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the record structure used to capture governance observations, lessons, and adaptive improvements.

---

## Learning Principle

Governance learning preserves experience, evidence, decisions, and outcomes to support future refinement.

---

## Learning Domains

Includes:

- observed conditions;
- governance decisions;
- lessons learned;
- improvement actions;
- validation outcomes.

---

## Learning Lifecycle


CAPTURE OBSERVATION

↓

DOCUMENT CONTEXT

↓

ANALYZE LESSON

↓

IDENTIFY IMPROVEMENT

↓

APPLY CHANGE

↓

UPDATE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A governance learning register transforms experience into preserved institutional knowledge.
