# Adaptation Decision Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how adaptive governance decisions and resulting changes are documented.

---

## Decision Principle

Adaptation decisions preserve reasoning, evidence, conditions, and resulting governance state changes.

---

## Decision Domains

Includes:

- initiating condition;
- decision context;
- evaluated alternatives;
- selected adaptation;
- verification status.

---

## Decision Lifecycle


IDENTIFY CHANGE CONDITION

↓

REVIEW EVIDENCE

↓

EVALUATE OPTIONS

↓

SELECT ADAPTATION

↓

VERIFY RESULT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

An adaptation decision record maintains traceability between changing conditions and governance evolution.
