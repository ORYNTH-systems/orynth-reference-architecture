# Morning Star Entry Ontology

Status: ACTIVE

Version: 0.1.0

Classification: Constitutional Ontology

---

## Purpose

The Morning Star Entry Ontology serves as the governed constitutional orientation layer of the ORYNTH ecosystem.

Its purpose is to introduce an initiate to the constitutional structure of ORYNTH without modifying the scientific authority of the frameworks it presents.

Morning Star governs entry into the ecosystem.

It does not govern the scientific content of the ecosystem.

---

## Constitutional Scope

The Entry Ontology defines:

- constitutional orientation;
- terminology;
- variables;
- taxonomy;
- framework relationships;
- dependency relationships;
- semantic boundaries;
- constitutional navigation;
- provenance;
- reconstruction.

---

## Constitutional Role

Morning Star occupies the constitutional initiation layer of ORYNTH.

It prepares an initiate to understand:

- ecosystem organization;
- constitutional relationships;
- framework scope;
- dependency ordering;
- governance boundaries.

Scientific discovery remains within the individual constitutional frameworks.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

Morning Star shall remain an orientation architecture.

It shall not silently redefine, absorb, or replace the constitutional identity of any ORYNTH framework.
