# Provenance

Status: ACTIVE

Version: 0.1.0

Classification: Constitutional Ontology

---

## Purpose

This document defines the provenance model governing the Morning Star Entry Ontology.

Provenance preserves the constitutional origin of every admitted ontology element.

No ontology element shall exist without an identifiable constitutional source.

---

## Provenance Principles

Every admitted ontology element shall preserve:

- originating framework;
- originating document;
- constitutional scope;
- admission status;
- historical traceability;
- reconstructability.

---

## Provenance Sources

Constitutional provenance may originate from:

- Canonical Models;
- Constitutional Specifications;
- Governance Artifacts;
- Constitutional Execution Records;
- Constitutionally Admitted Amendments.

No other source automatically establishes constitutional provenance.

---

## Provenance Requirements

Every ontology element shall identify:

- originating framework;
- originating constitutional artifact;
- relationship to Morning Star;
- constitutional authority;
- admission status.

---

## Provenance Preservation

Morning Star shall preserve provenance for:

- terminology;
- variables;
- taxonomy;
- framework relationships;
- dependency relationships;
- navigation structures;
- semantic boundaries.

Provenance shall remain intact across constitutional revisions.

---

## Provenance Constraints

Morning Star shall never:

- remove provenance;
- replace originating authority;
- silently substitute constitutional sources;
- merge unrelated provenance chains;
- attribute concepts to incorrect frameworks.

---

## Provenance Verification

Constitutional provenance shall remain:

- explicit;
- auditable;
- reconstructable;
- historically traceable;
- governance compatible.

Missing provenance renders an ontology element constitutionally inadmissible until resolved.

---

## Constitutional Invariant

Every ontology element admitted by Morning Star shall remain permanently attributable to its originating constitutional framework and constitutional artifact.
