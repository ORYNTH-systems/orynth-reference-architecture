# Framework Alignment Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH frameworks maintain alignment when operating as interconnected capabilities.

---

## Alignment Principle

Framework alignment ensures that each subsystem contributes to shared objectives while maintaining defined responsibilities.

---

## Alignment Domains

Includes:

- conceptual alignment;
- operational alignment;
- governance alignment;
- technical alignment;
- lifecycle alignment.

---

## Alignment Lifecycle


DEFINE FRAMEWORK ROLE

↓

COMPARE REQUIREMENTS

↓

IDENTIFY INTERACTIONS

↓

RESOLVE CONFLICTS

↓

VERIFY ALIGNMENT

↓

MAINTAIN RELATIONSHIP


---

## Principle

Alignment preserves coherence across a growing architecture.
