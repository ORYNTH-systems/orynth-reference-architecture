# Resource Coordination Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH resources are coordinated across integrated architecture layers.

---

## Coordination Principle

Resource coordination ensures that information, capabilities, and operational assets are applied according to system requirements.

---

## Resource Domains

Includes:

- knowledge resources;
- computational resources;
- organizational resources;
- operational resources;
- governance resources.

---

UPDATE MODEL

Resource coordination improves efficiency while preserving system alignment.
