# Capability Orchestration Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH capabilities are coordinated into unified operational pathways.

---

## Orchestration Principle

Capability orchestration aligns specialized functions so they can operate together while preserving individual boundaries and responsibilities.

---

## Capability Domains

Includes:

- intelligence capabilities;
- governance capabilities;
- execution capabilities;
- knowledge capabilities;
- ecosystem capabilities.

---

## Orchestration Lifecycle
IDENTIFY CAPABILITY

↓

DEFINE ROLE

↓

ESTABLISH RELATIONSHIP

↓

COORDINATE OPERATION

↓

EVALUATE RESULT

↓

PRESERVE STATE
