# Platform Readiness Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define criteria used to determine whether ORYNTH integrated architecture is prepared for operational use.

---

## Readiness Principle

Platform readiness requires alignment between architecture, capabilities, governance, validation, and operational requirements.

---

## Readiness Criteria

Includes:

- structural completeness;
- validation completion;
- governance alignment;
- operational stability;
- documentation completeness.

---

## Readiness Lifecycle


ASSESS COMPLETENESS

↓

VERIFY REQUIREMENTS

↓

REVIEW VALIDATION

↓

CONFIRM OPERABILITY

↓

AUTHORIZE READINESS

↓

PRESERVE DECISION


---

## Principle

Readiness assessment establishes a clear transition from integrated design to operational capability.
