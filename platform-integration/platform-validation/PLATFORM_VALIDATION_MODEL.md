# Platform Validation Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how the integrated ORYNTH platform is evaluated for structural coherence, operational capability, and readiness.

---

## Validation Principle

Platform validation confirms that integrated capabilities operate together while preserving architecture, governance, and execution requirements.

---

## Validation Domains

Includes:

- architecture validation;
- capability validation;
- runtime validation;
- governance validation;
- operational validation.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


DEFINE VALIDATION CRITERIA

↓

ASSESS INTEGRATED SYSTEM

↓

VERIFY COMPONENT RELATIONSHIPS

↓

EVALUATE PLATFORM BEHAVIOR

↓

DOCUMENT FINDINGS

↓

PRESERVE VALIDATION RECORD


---

## Principle

Validation transforms integrated architecture into verified operational capability.
