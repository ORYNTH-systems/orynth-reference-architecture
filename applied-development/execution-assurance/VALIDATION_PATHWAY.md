# Execution Assurance Validation Pathway

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define validation requirements for Execution Assurance deployment.

---

## Validation Areas

### Authority Validation

Verify:

- source authority;
- authorization state;
- delegation conditions.

---

### Execution Validation

Verify:

- current eligibility;
- environmental conditions;
- dependency state.

---

### Evidence Validation

Verify:

- records;
- reconstruction capability;
- outcome traceability.

---

## Validation Lifecycle


ASSESS

↓

VERIFY

↓

DECIDE

↓

EXECUTE OR PREVENT

↓

RECORD


---

## Principle

Execution assurance requires evidence-based eligibility determination before occurrence.
