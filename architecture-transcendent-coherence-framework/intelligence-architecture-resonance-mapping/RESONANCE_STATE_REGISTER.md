# Resonance State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track intelligence–architecture resonance states, relationships, validation conditions, and lineage.

---

## State Principle

Resonance states preserve relationships between intelligence capabilities, architecture structures, coherence fields, governance conditions, and resulting integrated states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- resonance identity;
- intelligence source;
- architecture context;
- relationship mapping;
- resonance conditions;
- validation status;
- resulting resonance state;
- historical lineage.
