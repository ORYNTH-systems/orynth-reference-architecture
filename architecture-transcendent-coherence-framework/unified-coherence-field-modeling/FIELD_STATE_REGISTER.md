## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track unified coherence field states, relational conditions, validation outcomes, and evolutionary lineage.

## State Principle

Field states preserve relationships between architecture conditions, intelligence relationships, governance structures, continuity states, and resulting coherence configurations.

- field identity;
- participating architecture states;
- relationship mappings;
- coherence conditions;
- validation status;
- resulting field state;
- historical lineage.
