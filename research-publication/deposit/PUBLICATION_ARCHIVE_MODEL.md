# Publication Archive Model

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define long-term preservation of ORYNTH publication artifacts.

---

## Archive Functions

Preserve:

- released versions;
- historical records;
- research lineage;
- supporting documentation.

---

↓

VALIDATE

↓

↓

IDENTIFY

↓

PRESERVE

↓

REFERENCE

## Principle

Archived releases become permanent historical records of architectural evolution.
