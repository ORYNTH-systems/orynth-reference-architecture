# Deposit Structure

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the structure of the ORYNTH archival publication package.

---

## Deposit Package

Includes:

- architecture documentation;
- research papers;
- technical reports;
- metadata records;
- validation materials;
- release history.

---

## Archive Organization


ORYNTH RELEASE PACKAGE

├── Architecture Documents
├── Applied Architecture Records
├── Ecosystem Records
├── Governance Records
├── Operational Records
├── Domain Records
├── Publication Materials
└── Metadata


---

## Principle

A publication deposit must preserve enough context for independent reconstruction.
