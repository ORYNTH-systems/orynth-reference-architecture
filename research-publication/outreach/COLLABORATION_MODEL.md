# Collaboration Model

Version:

v1.0.0

## Purpose

Define how external collaborators may engage with ORYNTH.

---

## Collaboration Layers

### Exploration

Includes:

- discussion;
- review;
- research alignment.

---

### Evaluation

Includes:

- technical analysis;
- validation;
- feedback.

---

### Development

Includes:

- structured contribution;
- applied research;
- implementation.

---

## Collaboration Principle

Collaboration expands capability while preserving system identity.
