# Validation Methodology Report

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define validation principles used throughout ORYNTH.

---

## Validation Domains

### Structural Validation

Evaluates:

- architecture relationships;
- organization;
- dependencies.

---

### Governance Validation

Evaluates:

- authority;
- change processes;
- continuity.

---

### Operational Validation

Evaluates:

- maintenance;
- monitoring;
- lifecycle processes.

---

### Domain Validation

Evaluates:

- applicability;
- requirements;
- deployment readiness.

---

## Principle

Validation preserves evidence before expansion.
