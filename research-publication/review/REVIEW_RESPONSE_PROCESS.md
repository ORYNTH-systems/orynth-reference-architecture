# Review Response Process

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how ORYNTH responds to external review.

---

## Response Lifecycle


RECEIVE REVIEW

↓

CLASSIFY FEEDBACK

↓

EVALUATE IMPACT

↓

DOCUMENT DECISION

↓

APPLY CHANGE OR PRESERVE STATE


---

## Feedback Categories

- clarification;
- correction;
- improvement;
- proposed amendment.

---

## Principle

Review feedback contributes to evolution while preserving architectural continuity.
