# Review Package

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the materials provided for independent evaluation of ORYNTH.

---

## Review Scope

Reviewers may evaluate:

- architecture structure;
- conceptual foundations;
- implementation boundaries;
- validation methodology;
- governance model.

---

## Review Materials

Includes:

- architecture documents;
- technical reports;
- domain applicability records;
- governance records;
- operational records.

---

## Review Principle

External evaluation should examine the architecture according to its stated scope and claims.

---

## Boundary

Review of architecture does not imply endorsement of future implementation outcomes.
