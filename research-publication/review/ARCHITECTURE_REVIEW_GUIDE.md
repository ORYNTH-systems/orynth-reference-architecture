# Architecture Review Guide

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Provide a structured approach for reviewing ORYNTH architecture.

---

## Review Areas

## 1. Structural Review

Evaluate:

- architecture organization;
- component relationships;
- dependency clarity.

---

## 2. Governance Review

Evaluate:

- authority models;
- change processes;
- continuity mechanisms.

---

## 3. Methodology Review

Evaluate:

- assumptions;
- definitions;
- validation approaches.

---

## 4. Application Review

Evaluate:

- domain applicability;
- integration boundaries;
- deployment considerations.

---

## Principle

Review should distinguish architecture design from implementation claims.
