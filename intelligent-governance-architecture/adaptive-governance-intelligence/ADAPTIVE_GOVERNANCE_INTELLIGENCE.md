## Purpose

Define the framework used to enable governance systems to adapt through contextual analysis, changing conditions, intelligence feedback, and preserved constitutional alignment.

---

## Governance Adaptation Principle

Adaptive governance intelligence enables governance structures to respond to changing environments while maintaining identity, authority boundaries, decision integrity, and continuity.

---

## Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- contextual governance analysis;
- policy adaptation;
- environmental awareness;
- risk evaluation;
- decision refinement;
- governance learning.
