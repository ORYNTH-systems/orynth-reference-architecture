# Adaptive Governance Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track adaptive governance states, changes, decisions, and evolutionary conditions.

---

## Register Principle

Adaptive governance records preserve relationships between environmental conditions, governance responses, policy states, and resulting transitions.

---

## Governance Fields

Includes:

- governance condition;
- environmental context;
- adaptive response;
- policy transition;
- validation state;
- historical record.

---

## Register Lifecycle


CAPTURE GOVERNANCE CHANGE

↓

CLASSIFY CONTEXT

↓

ANALYZE GOVERNANCE IMPACT

↓

DEFINE ADAPTIVE RESPONSE

↓

VERIFY TRANSITION

↓

UPDATE REGISTER


---

## Principle

An adaptive governance register maintains traceability between changing conditions and governance evolution.
