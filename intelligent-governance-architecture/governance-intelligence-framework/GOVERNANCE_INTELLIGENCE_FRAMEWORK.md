Version:

## Purpose

Define the framework used to integrate governance intelligence, contextual analysis, decision awareness, and governance alignment across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Governance Intelligence Principle

Governance intelligence enables systems to understand conditions, evaluate decisions, preserve authority relationships, and maintain alignment with governing principles.

---

## Governance Domains

Includes:

- authority intelligence;
- policy intelligence;
- decision intelligence;
- risk intelligence;
- operational governance;
- adaptive governance.
