# Governance Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track governance intelligence states, signals, relationships, and transitions.

---

## State Principle

Governance intelligence states preserve relationships between authority, policy, decisions, operational conditions, and governance outcomes.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- governance condition;
- authority state;
- policy state;
- decision context;
- intelligence assessment;
- transition history.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE GOVERNANCE STATE

↓

ASSESS GOVERNANCE CONDITIONS

↓

ANALYZE INTELLIGENCE SIGNALS

↓

VERIFY GOVERNANCE ALIGNMENT

↓

UPDATE STATE MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A governance intelligence state register maintains traceability between governance conditions and resulting system decisions.
