## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track intelligent governance ecosystem states, relationships, transitions, and governance conditions.

---

## State Principle

Governance ecosystem states preserve relationships between governance intelligence, constitutional authority, adaptive conditions, decisions, and ecosystem outcomes.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- ecosystem governance state;
- authority condition;
- intelligence state;
- decision state;
- adaptation state;
- continuity condition.
