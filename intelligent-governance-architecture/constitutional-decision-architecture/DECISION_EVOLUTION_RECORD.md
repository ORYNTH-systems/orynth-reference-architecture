# Decision Evolution Record

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define how constitutional decision architecture evolves over time.

---

## Evolution Principle

Decision evolution records preserve changes to decision models, governance refinements, authority structures, and future constitutional pathways.

---

## Evolution Domains

Includes:

- decision refinement;
- authority evolution;
- constraint development;
- governance improvement;
- future decision models.

---

## Evolution Lifecycle


CAPTURE DECISION CHANGE

↓

DOCUMENT TRANSITION

↓

ASSESS CONSTITUTIONAL IMPACT

↓

REFINE DECISION MODEL

↓

VALIDATE CONTINUITY

↓

PRESERVE HISTORY


---

## Principle

A decision evolution record ensures constitutional decision structures remain coherent, traceable, and adaptable.
