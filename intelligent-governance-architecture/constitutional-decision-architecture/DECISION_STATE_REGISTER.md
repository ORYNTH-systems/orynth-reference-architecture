# Decision State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track constitutional decisions, states, transitions, and validation conditions.

---

## State Principle

Decision states preserve relationships between authority, context, constraints, eligibility, execution, and resulting outcomes.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- decision identity;
- authority condition;
- constitutional alignment;
- decision status;
- validation state;
- transition history.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE DECISION STATE

↓

ANALYZE DECISION CONTEXT

↓

VERIFY CONSTITUTIONAL ALIGNMENT

↓

ASSESS TRANSITION

↓

UPDATE DECISION STATE

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A decision state register maintains traceability between constitutional conditions and governance outcomes.
