# Coordination Operating State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track coordination operating states, execution conditions, synchronization states, and transitions.

---

## State Principle

Coordination operating states preserve relationships between connected systems, operational contexts, adaptive coordination responses, and resulting ecosystem states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- coordination context;
- operational state;
- synchronization condition;
- relationship status;
- adaptive response;
- continuity record.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE COORDINATION STATE

↓

ASSESS OPERATING CONDITION

↓

PROCESS COORDINATION ACTIVITY

↓

VERIFY SYSTEM ALIGNMENT

↓

UPDATE COORDINATION MODEL

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A coordination operating state register maintains traceability between coordination execution and its operational environment.
