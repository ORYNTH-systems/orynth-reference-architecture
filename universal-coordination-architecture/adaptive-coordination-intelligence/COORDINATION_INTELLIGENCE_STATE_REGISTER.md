# Coordination Intelligence State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track adaptive coordination intelligence states, decisions, relationships, and transitions.

---

## State Principle

Coordination intelligence states preserve relationships between system conditions, coordination requirements, adaptive responses, and resulting operational states.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- coordination context;
- intelligence condition;
- relationship state;
- adaptive response;
- synchronization status;
- continuity record.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE COORDINATION STATE

↓

ASSESS SYSTEM CONDITIONS

↓

ANALYZE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

SELECT ADAPTIVE RESPONSE

↓

VERIFY COORDINATION RESULT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A coordination intelligence state register maintains traceability between adaptive coordination decisions and their resulting ecosystem conditions.
