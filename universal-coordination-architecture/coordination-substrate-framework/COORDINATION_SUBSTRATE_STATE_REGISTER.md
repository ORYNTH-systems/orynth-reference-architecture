# Coordination Substrate State Register

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the registry structure used to track coordination substrate states, relationships, synchronization conditions, and transitions.

---

## State Principle

Coordination substrate states preserve relationships between connected systems, communication conditions, synchronization states, and resulting coordination outcomes.

---

## State Domains

Includes:

- coordination identity;
- relationship state;
- communication condition;
- synchronization status;
- alignment state;
- historical continuity.

---

## State Lifecycle


CAPTURE COORDINATION STATE

↓

MAP SYSTEM RELATIONSHIPS

↓

ASSESS SYNCHRONIZATION CONDITION

↓

UPDATE COORDINATION MODEL

↓

VERIFY ALIGNMENT

↓

PRESERVE RECORD


---

## Principle

A coordination substrate state register maintains traceability between coordination foundations and their evolving operational conditions.
