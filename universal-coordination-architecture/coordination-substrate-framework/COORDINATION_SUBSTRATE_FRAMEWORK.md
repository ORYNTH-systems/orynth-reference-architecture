Version:

## Purpose

Define the foundational framework used to support coordination relationships, communication structures, synchronization mechanisms, and operational alignment across the ORYNTH ecosystem.

---

## Coordination Substrate Principle

A coordination substrate provides the foundational structure through which systems exchange context, synchronize actions, maintain relationships, and preserve coordinated continuity.

---

## Substrate Domains

Includes:

- coordination identity;
- communication relationships;
- synchronization structures;
- context exchange;
- alignment mechanisms;
- continuity preservation.
