# Adaptive Knowledge Intelligence

Version:

v1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Define the framework used to support adaptive knowledge acquisition, organization, refinement, and application across the ORYNTH cognitive architecture.

---

## Knowledge Intelligence Principle

Adaptive knowledge intelligence enables knowledge structures to continuously evolve through contextual understanding, relationship discovery, and validated refinement.

---

## Intelligence Domains

Includes:

- knowledge acquisition;
- contextual analysis;
- relationship discovery;
- knowledge refinement;
- adaptive application.

---

## Intelligence Lifecycle


IDENTIFY KNOWLEDGE SOURCES

↓

ASSESS CONTEXT

↓

MAP KNOWLEDGE RELATIONSHIPS

↓

REFINE UNDERSTANDING

↓

APPLY INTELLIGENCE

↓

PRESERVE KNOWLEDGE STATE


---

## Principle

An adaptive knowledge intelligence layer provides continuous knowledge evolution while maintaining traceability and coherence.
